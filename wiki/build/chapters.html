  • 001 1. Chapter 1

    A gamer from our world wakes up in Night City, 2075, in the body of fourteen-year-old Kusanagi Motoko — stripped of cyberware by Scavs, rescued, and coma-bound for a year. She recognises the Cyberpunk setting and immediately fears what that means. Hot-headed Tyger Claw enforcer Junichiro "Jun-Nii" Kusanagi arrives as her "older brother," swears she is safe, and brings her home to a cramped Japantown apartment.

    Rooting through her old belongings, Motoko slots a shard labelled "Gema / Gamer" and the Cyberpunk 2077 stat screen blinks to life in her HUD — Body, Reflexes, Cool, Technical Ability, Intelligence, all near zero. She begins grinding exercises for XP, studies late mother Natasha's netrunning shards (unlocking Breach Protocol), and falls asleep as a system message glows: *Rested XP +25%.* The addiction has begun.
