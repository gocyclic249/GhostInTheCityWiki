Braindance Catalog

Braindances produced, commissioned, or sold by Motoko throughout the story. Each entry is a full-immersion experience — the viewer lives someone else's memories frame by frame.

BD-001  // Ch.40
Kamikaze Raid
[RELEASED]
One of the original four BDs recorded after Motoko's neural BD implant went live. Judy Alvarez described the senses file as shockingly good — Motoko's heartbeat barely rises while climbing a building, her combat calm translating directly through the recording in a way that made Judy's stomach flip. Ten copies pressed and distributed through Nox on the street. The first proof that Edgerunner BDs could be something different from snuff films.

BD-002  // Ch.47
Raffen Camp
[RELEASED]
A solo silent raid on a Wraith-affiliated Raffen camp in the Badlands. Motoko bets Jun radio control forever that she can ghost the whole camp without a sound, then does exactly that — nine kills by blade in the dark. Rita at Lizzie's watched it before the drop-off and pulled Motoko aside afterwards, genuinely unsettled by how combat-wired she came across. The BD scared Rita more than the Kamikaze one had.

BD-003  // Ch.60
Scav Den
[RELEASED]
The first major scav den clearance — Motoko working through a full den of Scavengers. Handed off to Judy for editing and released through the Mox distribution network. By this point the BD operation has found its rhythm: record, edit at Lizzie's, press at Nox's, distribute through the street network Hiromi is quietly building.

BD-004  // Ch.86
The Only Thing They Fear Is You
[RELEASED]
The yacht heist. Motoko's most intense BD to date — she boards a privately-owned yacht at 3 AM, choke-holds the marina guard, plants a virus in the security system, and proceeds to work through the whole vessel. Nox experienced it at Judy's workshop and ended up on the floor gasping. Judy needed caffeine just to finish the edit. They agreed it was the most intense virtu either of them had experienced. Named after the DOOM Eternal track Motoko trains to. Known on the street as the DOOM BD.

BD-005  // Ch.115
Inner Universe
[RELEASED]
The outlier. No kills, no scavs — a fully live parkour run from a penthouse rooftop through Night City: lantern wires, bridgeways, through a stranger's apartment window mid-stride, across the Jig-Jig Street rooftops, landing at the Sakura Market ramen stand. Set to 'Inner Universe' from the Ghost in the Shell soundtrack. Judy couldn't finish editing it due to her heights phobia — Nox cleared it instead and called it another preem experience. Both of them ended up craving Sakura Market ramen afterward. David Martinez was spotted selling copies outside Arasaka Academy.

BD-006  // Ch.157
Section 9 Scav Raid
[RELEASED]
The XBD operation — Section 9's raid on the North Arroyo scav den used as an XBD harvest site, clearing the killing rooms floor by floor. A full crew op, the first BD to show Section 9 working as a unit rather than Motoko running solo. The Maelstrom encounter mid-raid was edited out of the release cut. Hiromi walked off with the raw footage for a separate project. Rebecca watched the finished BD and glimpsed herself as a motion blur in one shot; she was impressed by the professional-grade editing.
