// Appearance

Ichi gets less physical description than almost anyone in the story — the narrative tends to focus on what he does rather than how he looks. What comes through is someone who reads as steady and unremarkable on purpose: the kind of person who fades into a room until the moment he needs not to. Has a big truck. By Chapter 240 he's been secretly practicing with the Minotaur combat bot in his own time. The details accumulate quietly, same as he does.

// Background

The strategic mind of the original crew — calm and methodical where Malcolm is loud. Ichi was running gigs in Kabuki before Motoko arrived and provided structure to what would otherwise have been a chaotic group of street kids. He was already leading jobs by the time Motoko joined, and his tactical instincts made him a natural fit as Section 9 scaled up.

Present from the earliest gigs through the final chapter, Ichi is one of the crew's most reliable constants. He's not flashy about it — by Chapter 240 he's secretly been using the Minotaur combat bot for practice in his own time and broke its ankle joint without telling anyone.
