Characters

☠ Motoko Kusanagi Netrunner / Assassin Independent A gamer from our world reborn in Night City's deadliest body. Founder of Section 9. Kensei-titled netrunner and swordswoman with a kill count that keeps climbing. ⚔ Junichiro Kusanagi Enforcer Tyger Claws Hot-headed Tyger Claw enforcer and Motoko's self-appointed older brother. Gave her first weapons, first connections, and first cover story — and is usually the last to know what she's actually doing. ★ Hiromi Street Fixer / Crew Independent Motoko's best friend — all mohawk and no brakes. Secret daughter of Arasaka executives who runs hard with the Kabuki street crowd and drags everyone else into trouble first. ► Malcolm Gunner / Crew Independent The loudmouth of the original crew who never stopped showing up. Blunt, opinionated, and usually right — the one who'll tell Motoko what she doesn't want to hear. ■ Ichi Tactician / Crew Independent The strategic mind behind the original crew. Calm and methodical where Malcolm is loud. Has been running gigs since before Motoko arrived and never stopped. ◆ Omaeda Netrunner Independent The netrunner of Hiromi's original Kabuki crew. Present for the early gigs and the H2 loot run, then quietly faded from the story as the crew evolved into something else entirely. □ Sam Crew Member Independent A crew member who died in a Maelstrom drive-by ambush while Malcolm watched. Present somewhere in the early chapters, gone before the story names the loss. ✕ Hayato Nakagawa Martial Artist / TC Heir Tyger Claws Granddaughter of fixer Wakako. Daughter of the Tyger Claws' head. Introduced as a spoiled corpo girl with a Kerenzikov — until she lost, bowed, and came back for more.
