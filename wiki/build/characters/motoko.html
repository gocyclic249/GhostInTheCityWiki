// Appearance

Petite and deceptively slight — the body she woke up in was fourteen years old and fresh out of a coma, stripped of cyberware by Scavs. She rebuilt it into something else entirely through obsessive physical grinding. Her hair is dyed purple and cut short: cropped at the nape with long front bangs swept to the side, modelled after the Major from Ghost in the Shell and worn as a deliberate statement. Her eyes are yellow Kiroshi optics — installed early on — capable of seeing in near-darkness, reading net code at a glance, and registering her face as a blur on surveillance. Both arms are chrome, a constant presence in how she moves and fights. By the story's midpoint she carries visible muscle that has no business being on a frame that small.

// Background

A gamer from our world who woke up in Night City, 2075, inside the fourteen-year-old body of Kusanagi Motoko — stripped of cyberware by Scavs and coma-bound for a year. Recognising the Cyberpunk setting, she immediately began treating her life as a game, grinding stats through the Gamer System HUD that activated when she slotted a shard labelled 'Gema / Gamer.' Starting from near-zero attributes, she rebuilt herself from the ground up through obsessive training, street gigs, and sheer stubbornness.

Over 240 chapters she clawed her way from coma patient to one of the most feared operators in Night City — master netrunner, swordswoman, and assassin. Founder and leader of Section 9, holder of the Kensei title as the youngest ever recognized by the elders of the Chram Denya Jinja — the Shrine of Chrome, Night, and Electricity — and the woman behind a confirmed kill count that keeps climbing. She operates at the intersection of street survival, corpo intrigue, and ghost-in-the-shell-level cyberware mastery.
