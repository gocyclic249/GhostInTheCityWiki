// Appearance

Warm presence, spiritual aesthetic. The story captures her through actions rather than appearance: fortune readings, threading her arm through Motoko's, guiding people to Vik's clinic with genuine care. She radiates calm in a city that has none.

// Background

Motoko first met Misty when bringing salvaged chrome to Viktor's clinic. Misty runs Misty's Esoterica, the shop above Vik's basement operation, and serves as his informal nurse and assistant. She took immediate interest in Motoko, offering spiritual guidance about healing and identity that landed harder than expected.

Misty fills a role nobody else in Motoko's life occupies: genuine warmth without agenda. She reads fortunes for visitors, worries about people she barely knows, and radiates the kind of big sister energy that Motoko never asked for but clearly needs. Her relationship with Jackie threads through the clinic visits, giving Vik's basement the feeling of an actual community rather than just a chop shop.
