Regina Jones

Watson fixer who hired Motoko to kill the Devil of Kabuki. Purely transactional, occasionally frosty, and never short on gigs that need a particular kind of person.

// Appearance

Operates from an office tower in Watson. The story treats her as a voice and a presence rather than a physical description. Professional, direct, and uninterested in small talk.

// Background

Motoko physically infiltrated Regina's office tower to pitch herself as a merc. Regina was intrigued by the boldness and hired her on the spot for a high-profile job: assassinate Jotaro Shobo, the Devil of Kabuki. Motoko executed it clean. The success established their working relationship.

The relationship runs hot and cold. After Motoko pulled out of a difficult 6th Street infiltration she couldn't complete, Regina went quiet for a stretch. Later gigs resumed through corporate contract channels, including Kang Tao work. Purely business, occasional friction, but Regina keeps calling because Motoko keeps delivering.
