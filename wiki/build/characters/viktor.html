Viktor Vektor

Night City's most honest ripperdoc. Former boxer. Runs his clinic behind Misty's Esoterica and became Motoko's go-to for every chrome upgrade that matters.

// Appearance

Former boxer with ripper mods visible on his hands and glasses that never quite sit straight. Operates out of a basement clinic behind Misty's Esoterica. Methodical hands, dry humor, and the kind of steady presence that makes you forget you're lying on a table while someone rewires your nervous system.

// Background

Motoko sought him out early, bringing five crates of salvaged scav chrome as a gift to establish a relationship. Vik was impressed by the gesture and the business sense behind it. He became her primary ripperdoc from that point forward, installing her Kiroshi optics, cyberdeck, and running regular maintenance on her increasingly complex chrome loadout.

More than a service provider, Vik fills a quiet mentor role. He warns Motoko against over-chroming, shows her pictures of people helped by donated cyberware, and keeps his clinic as a neutral ground where mercs like Jackie and Motoko can cross paths without pretense. His integrity is the reason Motoko trusts him with her body, which in Night City is the highest compliment anyone can pay.
