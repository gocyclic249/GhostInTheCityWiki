Ghost in the City

A Cyberpunk 2077 / Ghost in the Shell crossover SI (Self-Insert) by Seras. A gamer wakes up in Night City as Motoko Kusanagi — without memories, without allies, and with a body that was purpose-built to be the best netrunner in the world. 242 chapters of corpo-politics, heists, street violence, and one woman clawing her way from coma patient to legend.
