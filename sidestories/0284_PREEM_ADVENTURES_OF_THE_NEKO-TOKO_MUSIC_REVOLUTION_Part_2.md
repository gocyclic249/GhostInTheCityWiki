 The Lion's Fang sisters skid backwards down the sidewalk, their feet kicking up a spray of dust as their shoes scrape along the concrete. "GUH! THESE… VIBES! THE FANS… THEY ARE DRAWN TO THEM!"

 Motoko dances and strums her guitar, backing me up and giving out the best of sounds!

 "HOW ARE THEY … SO… POWERFUL?!"

 The four sisters scream as they are driven to their knees. They weren't aware just how strong the power of ♡Cuteness and friendship♡ was!

 A mob of ghostly fans manifest and contribute their adoration, cheering and shouting while waving glowing batons all around us. Their power creates a strong array of colorful lights and special effects that increase the power of our song, blasting the four sisters off their feet!

 "WAAAAAAAAAAAH!😭" They cry.

 Our song ends, and I walk up to the prone and shaking forms of the four sisters. "Are you okay? I tried to hold back so you wouldn't be hurt.🙀" I extend one of my soft and cutely slender hands to help Singha, and she stares at it for a moment in confusion.

 "...You're… you're being _nice_ to us? After we insulted you like that?"

 I tilt my head like a puppy, but somehow cuter♡. "Yeah? Wouldn't Evening City be nicer if everyone wasn't fighting all the time? That's why I want the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**, so I can unite everyone and bring ♡peace to the city♡."

 She blinks a few times, the other sisters also seeming stunned at my words. Singha glances at the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**in her hand, and then reaches up to me with her free hand.

 I pull her up and steady her as she wobbles on her feet. Motoko, being all nice and stuff, helps up Carnassial and Raion.

 Singha stares at the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**for another moment, then glances at me. "So… You don't want there to be any fighting anymore?"

 I ☆smile☆ at her, causing her face to turn red for some reason. "Teehee! That's right! If I can help everyone see that music is great, then we can all get along♡❣"

 Singha blinks, a few droplets of water forming in her eyes, then she sniffs loudly, and slowly holds the _**Gilded**_**Microphone** out to me.

 I reach out as the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**'s power calls to me, the feeling of a union long overdue builds as my fingers inch ever closer.

 Suddenly!🙀

 A metal blur fwips past, and snatches the _**Gilded**_**Microphone** from Singha's open palm!

 Everyone shouts in surprise as they watch the blur move up the sheer wall of a nearby building. As it reaches the top, it slows, allowing us to see what looks like a humanoid robot.

 A man walks up beside the robot on the rooftop and takes the _**Gilded**_**Microphone** from the robot's metal fingers.

 "Ah, if it isn't the 'Lion's Fang Sisters'... Or should I say, _just_ 'The Sister's'."

 The four sisters step back and shake in fear. "F-f-f-Fujimujimuramasaoji-sama!"

 Motoko is looking at the roof as well, but only seems to be staring at the robot.

 I point a finger at the old, probably smelly, old man on the roof. "Who're you! And give me back my _**Gilded**_**Microphone**!"

 The old man laughs while inspecting the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**. "Heh-heh-heh… I am Fujimujimuramasaoji-sama. And I am a mysterious old man. And leader of the Lion's Fang Gang. **And those four are no longer in my gang!**"

 The sisters recoil, as if poked really hard. "W-What! But… We've been with your gang our whole lives!"

 Fujimujimuramasaoji nods. "Yes, and you've failed me for the… final time. **You are no longer in my gang!** Come, Jun, we have business somewhere else."

 Motoko twitches as the robot nods and speaks in a dull and robotic voice. "Affirmative, Fujimujimuramasaoji-sama." And they walk out of sight.

 The four sisters fall to their knees, crying biggly tears. "W-w-what are we supposed to do😭?! The Lion's Fang Gang was our whole lives! There's nothing for us anymore!" Says Singha, speaking for her sisters.

 Thinking fast, I decide that the sisters are good people. "Why don't you join us♡? We could use more help in bringing peace to ☆Evening City☆."

 They sniffle and wipe gross snot from their noses. "You'd… you'd do that much for us? Take us in even when we're so… _useless_?"

 I shake my head, sending my immaculate curls sashaying through the air. "You're not useless, he's just a grumpy old man who's probably also a pervert. ♡Together we'll track him down and get the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**back!"

 "...That was Jun…" Motoko whispers, but I hear her because we're best friends❣

 "Did you know that robot, 'Toko?"

 She nods. "That was my brother!"

 Everyone gasps loudly in surprise.

 "Your brother is a robot🙀!?" I ask. Which would be kind of preem, if he were on our side.

 She shakes her head. "He's not a robot, at least… he wasn't when I saw him last… The Lion's Fang Gang must have done something to him!"

 Carnassial steps forward. "Hrm.. Excuse me? That was Jun, Fujimujimuramasaoji's assistant and enforcer. He's the most powerful 'borg in the Lion's Fang Gang!"

 Motoko shakes her head once again. "That doesn't make sense! Jun has only ever loved two things! He'd _never_ join a gang!"

 I prop up my chin on my hand. "Hmmmmmmmmmmm. Then he must be being mind controlled!"

 Everyone gasps.

 "That would explain everything!" Motoko concludes. "We _have_ to rescue him!"

 The sisters nod all at once. "We know where the Lion's Fang stronghold is! We'll take you there so we can rescue your brother!" Raion notes. "And get the _**Gilded**_**Microphone** back!"

 As a group, we all begin running down the street, and after a few minutes we arrive at the nightclub which is the Lion's Fang Gang's main stronghold.

 Dozens of neon-tattoo'd guards stand around the club with keytars, guitars, keyboards, guiboards, shamizens and technozens. All looking ready to jam at a moment's notice.

 Scary.

 But, so long as we have friends, No One can stop us!

 We walk towards the club, the guards noticing our large group immediately, and several finger their tools in preparation, running a few bars to warm up.

 Then, as we step onto the sidewalk in front of the building, they unleash their attack!

 It rushes towards us, and we brace for impact!

 We won't stop until Jun is free and I have the _**Gilded**_**Microphone**!
