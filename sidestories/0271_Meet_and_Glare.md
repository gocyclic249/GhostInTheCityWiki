Meet and Glare​

 "Don't mind Jun, Jackie. Why don't you take a seat for a bit, we have drinks in the fridge if you're thirsty, I just need to armor up," Motoko said before heading off into her room.

 Which the two men present took as their chance to say their respective pieces.

 "So you're working with my sister on this gig then?" Jun asked pointedly.

 "Yeah, even asked for it," Jackie answered. "Apparently she thinks we could be a good team."

 "I don't know you... and I don't like not knowing people who are apparently going to be backing up my sister."

 "Well I know about you, _Oni_. Gotta say, wasn't expecting Motoko to be related to ya. Though maybe that explains how she is apparently so skilled and why Wakako likes her."

 "Wakako is a good fixer, she knows talent when she sees it."

 "So glad you agree with that," Jackie cockily grinned.

 "Just don't do anything that puts my sister in danger, especially if its only to save your own hid."

 "Hey, I look after my chooms. As far as I'm concerned me and Motoko are chooms too. She has nothing to worry about from me. Heck, if I had my way she wouldn't even be on this gig. Kids like her shouldn't be doing stuff like this."

 "She can handle herself, and won't listen to anyone else telling her otherwise," Jun admitted almost painfully. "Though if you being truthful it will be nice to know someone with experience is covering her back... assuming you don't just slow her down of course."

 "Slow her down?"

 "Motoko is a bit of a kamaitachi. You either get caught up in her wind or you get blow over by it. I hope for you sake those muscles of yours ain't for show."

 "I've earned them well, and tangled with more than a few crazy gigs before."

 "Before or after you were a Valentino?"

 "Merda, if this what all this is about I don't run with them anymore, and I got no personal beef with the TC else I wouldn't even be working with Wakako. Everyone knows her and your gang are tight after all."

 "Not as tight as everyone thinks really. Its just her sons are high level members and we respect her since been around longer than most of us have been alive. Still, Motoko isn't TC, no matter how much I think she'd do well in it, so just cause I am don't treat her different cause of that."

 "You say that, but something tells me if I ever do anything to her you don't agree with you'd get a couple of your buddies together and hunt me down."

 "I would."

 "Heh, I believe it too. That's what good siblings do. Don't worry, I ain't no gonk."

 "I hope not, for my sister's sake at least. She can attached to things easily, so don't take advantage of that loyalty at all or you don't deserve it."

 "I ain't her brother, but I think you should trust your sister more to make her own decisions."

 "I trust her completely, just not you."

 A moment later Motoko returned.

 "Okay! All set, let's hit the road! We got gonks to deal with."

 "Sure Hermanita, let's… Go." Jackie said as he left the apartment.

 Motoko looked back at Jun with a glare, which her brother could only shrug in response to.

 "Stupid Onii-chan."
