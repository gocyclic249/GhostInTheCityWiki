* * *

 "Kusanagi! Do you know why you're here?"

 "Erm Sensei? You told me to grab my imouto and make my way down here ASA-"

 "NOT YOU! THE OTHER KUSANAGI!"

 "Me Sensei!?"

 "Yes, YOU. I saw your latest XBD. SLOPPY WORK! Beating the Scav to death with your Carnage instead of breaking his neck with a buttstroke? Letting yourself get caught offguard by the lummox with a Sandy?

 NO! This XBD has thrown away every single bit of technique i attempted to drive into your skull! Clearly it is my failing as a teacher that my lessons did not stick, but i can fix that......Oh yes i can..."

 And as heavy industrial shutters start to fall in place with loud ominous clangs while the rest of the Tyger Claw members made a beeline to the exit while she remained pinned by the sheer intensity of Sensei's glare.... it was at this moment that Kusanagi Motoko realize that she might be in trouble.

 ===============

_"Ichi!'_

 A buttstroke to the throat

_"Nii!"_

 Chrome fingers jab into the eye-sockets of a imaginary foe

_"San!'_

 A brutal thrust at the heart of a target that caused the back of the training dummy to erupt.

_"REST!"_

 Flowing smoothly into a stance, Motoko came to a complete halt. 

 "Passable. Rest for 15 minutes. We shall begin the class on blood splatters when i return."

 As Sensei left without a backward glance, Motoko collapsed into a pool of crumpled rags. Who knew that Sensei had been so serious about fixing her " lack of grace and technique" ?

 Still, it wasn't a total loss. Who knew Sensei mastered so many ways to make Kills look dramatic and cinematic? The teachings on using blood splatter to help create tension were AMAZING! She could totally see his experience as an ex-bloodsport gladiator shinning through!

_Wait till Judy get's a load of THIS!_
