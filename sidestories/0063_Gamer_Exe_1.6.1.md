"Hey Motoko!" Hiromi cheered as she burst through the door in her usual energetic manner.

 "Hi Hiromi!" Motoko replied as she jumped up and down happily, the glowing whisker marks on her cheeks flashing into a cheery neon barrage. The bright and cheerful smile looked so real, it was a shame it was just an ornament... An ornament to a very pretty artificial doll that was so...real that at times she could make the mistake of believing it truly was her friend instead of the empty vessel pulled from that Scav den. "So did you come to play Choom!?"

 "Yup!" Hiromi chirped as she pulled out her gambit, two shards with a simple and useless program, and of course a program to breach it into the main operating system of anything it is put in. "It is really Preem!"

 "Really!" Motoko squealed as she snatched one of the shard cases from her hand, only to frown when she looked at the name of it. "But...this is that lame exercise program... I mean what kind of Gonk would want to use faulty nanites and its lame exercise routines!"

 "A Gonk that found out it lets two people with the same program think and work in sync.." Hiromi teased as she inserted the other shard, knowing it would take the bait... It was unusually interested in getting access to her home, and her mom refused to have it over making lame excuse after excuse.

 "...You will owe me some Ramen for this..." Motoko huffed as she tried to look reluctant in slotting the shard in, immediately it stiffened, and suddenly Hiromi fell into a pit she never saw coming.

 Namely that the Doll, was the ghost of another woman... who died and became Motoko.

 ----Reboot Complete----

 "-ke her and get out!" A young man's voice shouted as booted feet stomped off.

 "J..jun didn't mean it...Motoko..." A young woman's voice stated as he vision shifted to the floor and the sight of an oddly dressed teen girl's body, that she realized detachedly was her own body. She felt she should feel something over the fact that she felt no feeling in her body, but she felt literally nothing.

 ---Gamer Exe 1.6.1 booting up---

 Suddenly she could see nothing but a blank screen, as words scrolled through her vision. After a few minutes of miscellaneous sound, she heard both sniffling, and then a motorcycle starting up. That was the sound she heard for who knows how long, and then it happened.

 ---Motoko "Vicky" Kusanagi Version 2...Soul Killer Engram MK 1. Arasaka Gemini Model 77... Location: Night City---

 "Night...City." Motoko stated quietly as memories were suddenly available to her, just all there. Fragmented memories of a young man who she called brother in Japanese, some flashes of other Japanese young men and a young girl telling what seemed to her to be careful. Then flashes of men and women with strange things on there body, and even mechanical arms...

 The most clear memories after the flashes was there, and she dove in frantically to find out what happened to her...

 ______________________________________________________________________________________________

 "-thought you said this thing would wipe her memories!?" A voice in Russian demanded in the darkness, how she knew what they were saying was answered by the text in the corner saying translating.

 "It was supposed to!" A young echoing voice frantically defended themselves.

 "Yeah well this fucking thing seems to just give these fucks new memories for anyone to peruse instead of giving these fucks hunting us nothing to go on!" The angry voice growled even as it started getting more and more distant and...muffled.

 Interspersed within were memories of what she knew was herself, blood and her staring into her own fading eyes. Then warmth to cold and wet, and a woman's voice. Then some memory flashes of a little child from her point of view, only it was skipping and torn like a bad vhs tape...

 _______________________________________________________________________________________________

 She wanted, no needed to feel something!

 Humanity leveled up... Humanity Level 2.

 And suddenly she felt a sense of distant sadness.
