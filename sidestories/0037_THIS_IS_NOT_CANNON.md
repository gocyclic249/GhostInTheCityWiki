 Hiromi walked up to her friend with a plan, an evil plan. she soon noticed Mokoto was there.

 Good~ revenge for all the times Mokoto said she was a bad driver.

 "Ohiyo guys~ how is everything."

 Ichi looked up from his drink. "We're good Hiromi, how are you."

 "I'm fine, I'm actually here to deliver something."

 Everyone looked at her, perfect~

 "Oh what are you delivering?" questioned Mokoto, tilting her head slightly.

 Hiromi's smile almost turned feral, it was time.

 "Something from Vik for you." She brought out a A4 sized envelop and opened it before Mokoto even moved to grab it.

 Everybody's jaw dropped, hell Hiromi noticed a customer at the bar who was nosing in literally having is jaw drop on the floor. he was scrabbling for

 his jaw seconds later.

 But the envelops contents. A picture of Vik showing of his dad bod in a matching leopard dressing gown and... well you get the picture. This with some words on the back of the picture.

 Which she began to speak out loud

_'Mokoto, your friend told me that you liked me more than a friend. I am sorry but I can not reciprocate, you are too young for someone like me, get out there and find someone better. But Hiromi asked for me to do this for you and for everything you have done, I think it is fair.

 'I will alway be willing to help, Vik'_

 Everyone turned there heads to Mokoto who looked morified.

 "Well Mokoto?" Hiromi said with a grin wider than a sharks "Are you satisfied with Vik's level of '_customer service_?'"

 Mokoto stood up, hands slamming the table and redder than any blood seen on the streets of Night City and shouted.

**"THIS IS NOT CANNON!!!"**
