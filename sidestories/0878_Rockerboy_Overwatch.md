 Three walls down two to go. Data demons flooded towards me, or well the attack I was routing through the fast food joint across the street. Though you really couldn't call it either of those things. It was just way too overpriced and also not very fast at all. So really it was their fault that all the bandwidth they had was being taken up. It should not take 15 minutes to fry french fries of all things, and the soda fountain was out of everything that wasn't bog standard nicola. Seemingly made with actual bog water.

 100 EXP gained.

 100 EXP gained.

 100 EXP gained.

 The world around me split into two perspectives as demons died around me in droves. In the digital I was ripping my way through a data fortress, while in the other...

_"So we want to release-"_

 A man in a banana yellow suit tried to talk optimal album release windows to me.

 In that other world. -The real, I had my eyes closed. Strategically spaced several chairs down from Hiromi, -far out of kicking range. Who was glaring daggers at me if the camera in the room was to be believed. Carefully I tapped out a rhythm on the faux wooden meeting table. Letting the others around me know that I was at least awake.

 Ah the benefits of playing up the aloof rockergirl angle. The exec's in the meeting couldn't even get mad at me, because my behavior made them more money!

 Still I could feel heat creeping up my spine in the digital. It was slightly risky doing this without a chair, and I only had a large takeout cup that had become a slurry of Nicola at this point to cool off with, but there was no way I was going to sit through a _four hour_ meeting and get nothing done. Small mercy that the ice machine at the slow food restaurant still worked.

 So instead of being bored, I killed three birds with one stone.

 "_Fading_ will be our seventh most promoted album for the month of August. I'm sure that's exciting news for everyone in this room to hear, the royalties-"

_A burst of light shot from the palms._

 100 EXP gained.

 The Demon fell apart the code breaking apart and hindering nearby programs with its own digital remains.

 Bird number one, I wanted access to all of MSM's digital infrastructure. I'd already slipped a few shards into the session recording computers to help with that yesterday, but I needed the netrunner in charge of security to be distracted while they worked their cyber magic.

 With a twist of digital space I was somewhere else entirely and laying waste to yet more defenses.

 Bird number the second, my grinding time was being severely hampered by said meeting so I might as well get something for my time here.

_"Realistically, each of you can expect to earn roughly four hundred thousand eddies in that month time period with a tail lasting-"_

 Bird number the third, I had a perfect alibi for where I was during the attack if things were ever discovered.

 Filmed on camera looking for all the world to just be another Rockerboy nursing a killer headache.

 Heh, ninja.

 You can take the Rockerboy out of the ninja, but you can't take the ninja out of the Rockerboy!

 ...Or something like that.

 I'll think about that more later when I'm not nursing this headache okay?

 Hiromi's voice cut loudly through the dirge of dying data demons in my mind.

_"I think this sounds like a good way to kick everything off, what do you think Motoko?"_

 Yep, that was her angry voice alright.

 I checked the timer on the shard programs and saw that they had all finished up. So with a sigh both physical and real I cut the connection.

 Blearily I opened my eyes to the far too bright room to see everyone looking over at me.

 "Yay, August."

 Hiromi's stare could have cut through steel, but I had far too much of a headache to care, and wait actually yeah!

 Bird number the fourth! I was doing this to help Hiromi too. No secret backstabbing corpo deals would escape my watch now!

 I'm doing you a favor here you know!

 ...Wait wasn't it October?
