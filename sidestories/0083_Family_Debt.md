You know what I would really like to see? Motoko taking Jun with her out in the badlands to see Scorpion and the rest of the Aldecaldos.

 "Jun, right?" Scorpion offered him his hand, which Jun ignored completely. Choosing instead to simply stare at the man with narrowed, suspicious eyes. "I, uh..." he chuckled uncomfortably, lowering it after a few seconds of uncomfortable silence. "Motoko mentioned you."

 "I'm sure she did." He replied tonelessly. Folding his arms, he continued "So you're the guy she risked life and limb to save?" He took a moment to pointedly look the man up and down, noting his worn and faded jacket and shirt. Stark in contrast against Jun's more colorful Tyger Claw ensemble.

 "Yeah." Scorpion sighed. "Listen, I bet a guy like you don't think too highly of us Nomads, but lemme tell you this - that job I took your sister on? If it wasn't for her, I would have died." He said with such sincerity that it actually made Jun blink. "She went above and beyond what I expected from a no-name Solo like her. What _anyone_ expected, honestly. Hell, even my fixer was surprised. And that old bat doesn't get surprised over much nowadays, lemme tell you that much!" he chuckled.

 Straightening up properly, he calmly met the eyes of the man in front of him. "She saved me. A FUBAR job like that? I fully expected her to just cut her losses and delta the fuck out of there. But not her." he said, a smile creeping into his voice at the end. "Outnumbered ten to one, she flatlined those Raffen fucks down like they were nothing."

 "Fucking degenerates!" he spat, hands clenched and his face scrunched up in anger. "A bullet's too good for them!"

 Gathering himself back together, he continued "Family means a lot to us Nomads, and the fact that she allowed me to see them again is a debt I might never be able to repay. Hell, next time she shows up I can guarantee you she'll be welcomed with open arms." he said, throwing his arms out to the sides for emphasis.

 "You tell her this." he said, index finger pointing in the direction of Jun's chest "She ever get into any kind of trouble, anything we're capable of helping her with, you'd be damn sure we won't hesitate to do so." He promised, fist clasped over his heart. "I swear on my name."

 Jun looked at him for a few moments before nodding, his tense posture relaxing. Offering Scorpion his arm, he clasped it in a warrior's handshake. "I'll hold you to that."
