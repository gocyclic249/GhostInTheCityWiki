 Omake: Motoko wants the first drink

 Rogue was pleasantly suprised at how quiet the day had been. The rising tensions between the gangs had been making the whole city feel like a cyberpsycho, waiting for a reason to go mad.

 Even the corps had been getting antsy at the increase in violence in even the 'safe' areas of the city. She still hoped the rumour that Arasaka was close to letting Adam off his leash was exaggeration.

 As Rogue contemplated the insanity of Night City she heard something strange from the entrance to Afterlife. A metallic scraping noise, as if a large piece of torn metal was being pulled into the club.

 As she was about to go and have a look she saw a small slip of a girl come in facing away from the club, hair lightly smoking from burnt ends, holes leaking blood scattered across her form.

 As the girl came further into the room, Rogue could start getting a glimpse of the source of the horrid screech of warped metal. At first it appeared to be a blown open engine, twisted metal pointing ever which way. As it was pulled under the light however Rogue noticed it was a humanoid piece of twisted metal. An uncomfortably familiar form.

 As the girl drew closer to the bar, dragging the mangled borgs remains behind her, Rogue lamented ever thinking this was a quiet day.

 The girl stopped to take a breathe, hefted the large form onto the bartop and with the grin of madness asked " Can I have the pleasure of ordering the first Adam Smasher, please?"
