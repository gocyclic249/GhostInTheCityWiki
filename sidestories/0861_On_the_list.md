Jago Szabó took one look at the man coming off the elvator and just knew it was going to be one of those days.

 "McNamara?!"

 Mac wearing an actual suit for once gives him a grin and a pair of finger guns.

 "Hey Jago hows business?"

 "How the fuck did you even get in here?!"

 "My name's on the guest list."

 Jago looks over his tablet and glares at the name on the list.

 "How in the actual fuck did you pull that off, I know for a fact you were not on the guest list."

 Mac gives a shrug.

 "Should have hired me to run net security for the event instead of the Voodoo boys.

 It's what you get for being cheap."

 Jago pinches the bridge of his nose and waves over one of the guards.

 "And what is stopping me from having [Charlie](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Charles_Graham) here just throw you off the building?"

 Mac looks over to Charlie.

 "I'll buy you a few beers at the bar to make up for this."

 "Make up for wha...."

 Mac's eyes glow blue and Charlie [punches himself](https://i.kym-cdn.com/photos/images/newsfeed/000/617/983/4b0.gif) out cold.

 He then looks over at Jago.

 "So you were saying?"

 Looking at the downed guard Jago then glares at the fixer.

 "Hansen isn't going to be happy you're crashing the party."

 Mac hands Jago a data shard.

 "I trust this will smooth things over, I'm sure a man of his vision and one of your talents can make the most of this."

 Jago's eyes go wide after plugging in the shard.

 "How the hell did you get your hands on this intel?"

 The fixer gives the accountant another smile.

 "Jago it's what I do.

 Now I believe this is a party, change the scheme, alter the mood. Electrify the boys and girls if you'd be so kind."

 The fixers eyes go blue again the lights on the floor change and a different music track plays.

 Chester Bennett walks over to Jago.

 "The fuck is he doing here, Colonel Hansen was rather clear about who was and was not on the guest list."

 Jago hands Bennett the data shard.

 "He just made the Voodoo boys look like script kiddies and that shard is his payment for an invite."

 Bennett plug in the shard and has a similar reaction to Jago.

 "How in the absolute fuck?

 The Colonel's been trying to get this intel for weeks."

 "It's what McNamara does."

 "What does McNamara do besides crashing another of my parties and why the fuck in Charlie laying on the floor?"

 The two Barghest members look over to see Kurt Hansen standing behind them giving them a look that demands answers immediately.

 Jago is the first to speak up.

 "Sir he slipped his name onto the list despite the added net security from the Voodoo boys and offered us a data shard with the Intel on project Silent Sparrow we've been trying to acquire."

 Hansen is handed the shard by Bennett and plugs it in and after a moment gives them a nod.

 "I want to have a word with the Voodoo boys after the party, I don't like paying for prime rib and getting expired beef jerky in it's place.

 Let McNamara stay but keep an eye on him and who he talks to, see how soon our opening act can start and get charlie off the damn floor."
