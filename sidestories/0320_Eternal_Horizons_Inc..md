 *Cue, the happy jingle starting and Motoko wearing an Isabelle outfit walking around the slagged remains of a Greenhouse cluster in the Biotechnica Flats*

_We here at Eternal Horizons Inc. have been hard at work developing a healthy and sustainable way for people fighting with cyberpsychosis symptoms to live out their urges in a positive fashion, helping themselves and doing their part to help develop a healthy alternative to all the indigestible slop we are served as fast food here in Night City._

 *Cue Adam Smasher breaking down another wall of a "greenhouse" with Bionechnica employee guts hanging of him and somehow smiling happily into the camera*

 *Seemingly reeling back into the past the trailer now shows unhappy young Motoko watching in horror as her brother Jun is scarfing down unending amounts of XXXL Burritos and this visibly impacting his health and disposition.

 Same as with her, Adam is also shown with shoulders down overhearing colleagues whispering hurtful things behind his back and unleashing his rage upon them tears in his eyes.

 This leads to Motoko going on her own killing spree and meeting Adam Smasher who was also "visibly" suffering from cyberpsychosis and both of them just crying at each other about how sad they were*

_After considerable research we have concluded that the bad quality of synth foods is one of the main leading causes for prolonged mental health issues, which in turn are one of the building blocks of cyberpsychosis. Thus our company was created to fight both of those at once by allowing our patients to unleash their pent up anger at deserving targets whilst simultaneously investing their energy into growing good food which would not impact their health negatively._

 *Cue Adam and Motoko both being shown carnaging through another "greenhouse" which is then converted to fertile plantations with 'toko ripping an epic solo on her guitar whilst Adam lends his gravel and guzzoline voice for the growl part of the jingle whilst he is pulling an entire rotary tiller to infuse the blood and gore of the defenders into the ground, animated fruits and vegetables with happy faces instantly sprouting from the ground to show a job well done.*

_We at Eternal Horizons Inc. are happy to announce our test run to be completed and are currently reviewing the first batch of patients to be inducted into our long-term care program.

 Special thanks goes to Biotechnica for their donation of workspace and acceptable targets._
