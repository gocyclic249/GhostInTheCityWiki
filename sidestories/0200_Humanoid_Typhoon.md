"Mr Welles, i demand a certain...level of skillsets in the Mercs i take on. As this is your first job with me, i will be sending an auditor. I must warn you to not look down on her because of what she looks like. "

 Gulping at the naked threat in Wakako's voice, Jackie followed the direction of her hand into a dimly-lit room with naught within but a table and a high backed Office chair. The kind that Corpo bigshots loved to use to flex dominance over their minions.

_Stay cool Jackster, this is your shot at the big leagues!_

 Still as the rotated oh so slowly, Jackie could feel the tension build and build. His palms start to sweat, the aches from that boxing session he had with Vik and the kid making their presence known.

 "So...Mr Wells, Wakako got me to babysit your ass...Now tell me are you worth me losing a night's work to babysit?"

 The voice that rang into his ear was something straight out of a Action BD. Sexy with a hint of danger that told him to watch the checks his mouth might issued.

 "I...I'm ready for the big leagues Mam!"

 "Well, that's what you think. You have to prove it dear boy, now tell me your skillse-"

 Suddenly dimly lit room blazed white as celling lights turned on revealing to Jackie that little kid he saw in Vik dressed in an oversized trenchcoat and floppy hat midspeech. As they both froze at the awkward situation, an irritated voice cut in.

 "OK, that's enough Motoko. The promised 10 mins you traded for with that favor from putting down the Borg is up. Now scoot. You and Mr Wells."

 "No wait Wakako! I wasn't done and you just RUINED everything!"

 "This is me not giving a damn. Now Scoot! And you Wells! Don't screw up my job and don't let this One get distracted! I'll add another 1000 eddies if you make sure she comes back without stealing anything!"

 "That's RUDE! Stop treating me like i'm a magpie or something!"

 "Shush brat. Don't bother me when i'm giving Mr Wells the info needed for the role he'll play if he pass the test."

 Despite the whirlwind of events before him, Jackie caught that and managed to squeeze out a question.

 "The role?"

 "Yes, if you pass the test, i'll put you on a retainer to babysit this Brat and help me clean-up the mess she caused. Whenever she does something call me. You be very busy, but i'll need the headsup to handle things on my end before news of her accidents hit the public sphere."

 "You..you're treating me like some kind of Humanoid Typhoon!"

 "Think of it as Insurance on my part against your ability to spontaneously cause a mess."
