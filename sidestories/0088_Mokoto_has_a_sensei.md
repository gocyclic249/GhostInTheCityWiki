**Mokoto has a sensei**

 Mokoto couldn't help but feel existed as time went on, sure at first she had her...dislike for the idea at first, given that after a few weeks she felt like she learnt a lot, more than she would of if she was still doing things on her own.

 That sweet EXP just kept coming in.

 But right now she was heading to her Sensei house, he had something for her to do.

 Soon she was there and knocked the door and was quickly meet by her Sensei.

 "Ah good, you have arrived. please, come in."

 Mokoto entered. "Yes Sensei, may I ask why you called for me?"

 "Yes, please follow me." her Sensei said leading her through his house until they arrived at a garage with a classic car in it.

 "I wish for you to wax my car. please don't scratch the paint."

 She got existed "Is this a Karate kid thing? You know wax on, wax off?"

 "Karate kid? I don't even- no, I just want to wax my car. I have a hot date tonight."

 Mokoto looked confused for a second "Huh? but what about my training?"

 "That's Friday, now. Chop-Chop! Wax on Mokoto!"

 Her brow twitched with annoyance. Forget what she said, she hated having a Sensei.
