And another one! I'm on a roll

 Listened to Crab Rave and had this mental image

Spoiler: Crab Rave

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/embed/LDU_Txk06tM)​

 The mini-Tachikoma's are called yonkoma (4 koma) because of a recommendation I saw (I forgot who said it first)

* * *

 I said I was going to use it for scouting and reconnaissance, that's true, but that's not why I built it.

 Putting the finishing touches on the quadcopter I ran a few preflight checks before uploading the pre-built flight path and the song.

 The hard part of the design was not making a small quadcopter, but making it possible for the drone to record BD virtues.

 At the same time, I sent a command to the flathead prototype that I stole and finished. It was a barely functional model when I took it, now it has everything but the optical camo.

 It reached its arms up and started clicking its claws together to a certain beat, bouncing its body up and down.

 The quadcopter panned and showed the yonkoma tapping their little claws against the ground, drumming away at the beat.

 Then the real reveal happened as several garage doors opened across the street and out poured a swarm of yonkoma getting into position, where they started to also bounce up and down, clapping their claws over their heads.

 The quadcopter then focused on the flathead as the beat dropped and it broke out into energetic dancing.

 The majority of the motions for the yonkoma were programmed by the fledgling AI I am making to control the yonkoma. It can barely comprehend text and only program motion sets for yonkoma at the moment though.

 As the song finished I pulled the BD from the quadcopter and headed over to Lizzie's so that Judy can edit it.

 After reassuring Rita that I wasn't going psycho I went to Judy and handed over the BD virtues.

 "As promised here's a couple BDs with no violence to make up for The Only Thing They Fear is You."

 "This isn't some kind of sick joke right? They're normal BDs?" She looks at them skeptically, especially the second one.

 "Yeah, the first one is freerunning and parkour. I tried to amp up the feeling of freedom and speed as much as possible. The second one is special."

 "Special? In what way?"

 "It took an AI to coordinate, and you can't let anyone else see it."

* * *

 Later (when Judy finishes viewing the second BD)

 *Motoko WTF was that!?! That wasn't a BD! How did you even run that in a BD wreath!?!*

 *I made a drone that can record BDs, it uses a virtual environment to reduce the dissociation for the viewer while still letting them see only the drone's POV*

 *I can kinda see why you called it Crab Rave, but where did you get all those robots*

 *I made them myself, just like my grenades.*

 *YOU MAKE YOUR OWN GRENADES!?! That's it, I'm telling Rita*

 *You tell Rita and I'll stop delivering your caffeine*

 *I'll have Nox do that*

 *NOOOOO!*

 Before I could do more Jun came in, and he looked annoyed.

 "Imouto, why is there a swarm of robots dancing in the middle of the apartment?"

 "OH NO! IT BREACHED CONTAINMENT!"

 "WHAT DID YOU DO MOTOKO!?!"

 "I _may_ have been training an AI to be the primary personality of a swarm of robots."

 "YOU WHAT?!?"

* * *

_Later_

 After making sure the ai wasn't going to run off and get me hunted down by Netwatch I went over to Hiromi's.

 While she thought the parkour BD was preem, I did tell her that the second BD was a gift to her.

 "This one I made for you, it has a song with it, but it's not violent or anything."

 Hiromi blushed, probably thinking about the last BD I made. As I grabbed her hand to put the BD in it I saw her eyes started shifting in color. Looking closer her eyes were swirling an array of colours that could probably only be seen by enhanced eyes. Her blush deepened and I realized how embarrassing this situation is, it's like that friend who is always trying to sell their mixtape.

 After she finished viewing the BD she looked at me and asked, "Is that first drone what I think it is?"

 Nodding I responded, "That's right, taken straight from Militech's labs that there is the prototype Flathead drone."

 "It's been a year and a half since we met, or re-met, and I thought you would like it."

 Reaching down I pulled out the case that held the Flathead.

 "So, here you go, it's all yours."

 Immediately she leapt into a hug, burying her face into my shoulder.

 "Motoko, you're unbelievable. This is… I don't even know what to say."

 I patter her back gently and chuckled. "Just don't tell Netwatch, I had one scare already"

 Suddenly Hiromi's hands were on my shoulders and she was staring me in the eyes.

 "Motoko, what did you do?"

 Refusing to answer I kept my mouth shut.

 "Motoko you gonk! WHAT DID YOU DO!?!"
