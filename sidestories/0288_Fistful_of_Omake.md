 Omake 1. Darker alternate emo and anger driven Motoko.

_When she woke up. Not only her chrome were gone. Some of her flesh bits are klepped too and never recovered.

 When Jun found her, she was missing her lower jaw and her ribcage. Difference of being late by some minutes.

 It was a miracle she is still alive.

 3 months later._

 "Sis! Where the fuck have you been?!" ,Jun rushes forward to hug Motoko, she stands there like a statue as her brother curls his chromed arms around her.

 "Been doing ...stuff." , Motoko's cyberjaw vocalize emotionlessly. Her thermal katana still warm in it's sheath.

 "Have you heard? They call me Raiden now. My onii-chan the Oni. His sister, Raiden..."

_Flashback to 3 hours ago

 Motoko's blade slices through enemies like hot knife on butter.

 While she dashes around , cuts up scavs and their weapons into pieces.

 Her mind kept playing a song she heard from a place far away....

 'Memories broken, the truth goes unspoken

 I've even forgotten my name

 I don't know the season or what is the reason

 I'm standing here holding my blade...'

 Her mind hums as she parries the bullets fired at her she cannot avoid, hitting it back to their owners in lethal ricochets.

 'The only thing I know for real..'

 She leaps up slashes downwards into a Scav who looks up in fear to see her bloodthirsty cyberjaw grin before they are bisected into bloody halves.

**'There will be blood shed!'**_

 "Now buy me something to eat gonk!"

_Minutes later_

 David Ramirez is scratching his head in severe regret of several things

 One, he is late to attend Arasaka Academy

 Two, his Eddies is rather empty now.

 Three, his mom is gonna cry and kill him for the above problems.

 All because of the crazy girl who is now shoving food in her mouth that he paid with his mom's hard earned money.

 Wiping the food stains with her sleeve, she now kidnaps him to elsewhere

 "Since we are dating, I'm bringing you to see my family!"
