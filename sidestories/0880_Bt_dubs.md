 "How far along are we in the 'real life' scenario. This "Star Gate SG-1" If you will?"

 "Have you guys gotten stuck in Atlantis yet?"

 "Or wait,was that a separate team?"

 "Oh! Have you all met your android copies yet?"

 "Ah, shut your dumb mouth Motoko!" The cyborg girl slaps her checks twice.

 "I'm sorry I only ever watched your show as background noise while playing on my phone! *_whispering quietly as if so only she can hear._* "_Way to go Motoko, a head full of every song you've ever heard and you end up in the media you used as chill background entertainment._"

**Later**.

 "Dude this isn't even the first piece of fiction I've found myself suddenly in."
