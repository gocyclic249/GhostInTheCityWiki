 Motoko visits Judy.

 Motoko was flipping and flopping through the building, tugging Judy after her back into the basement and robbing her of one of her few cigarette breaks.

 Motoko being her exited and bubbly self as usual chitters to her: "Hey Judy this one will be a banger! You have to watch it right now!"

 Judy who had already stopped struggling and lost her cigarette on the way just sighs.

 Motoko blushes:"I was extra careful this time to not call your name, so you dont have to edit that out again!"

 "Well atleast you learned from last time." Judy says tiredly not knowing what was coming this time.

 Motoko beams with pride: "Yeah I'am amazing! This one has way more action than the last BD, it is a little different from my usual style. I hope you are gonna like it!"

 Judy shivers, her legs feeling alien again.

 Judy:"Yeah sure let me take a look." She says with all the enthusiasm she can muster, her smile almost looking genuine.

 Judy takes a deep breath and puts on the BD wreath.

 Just when she was about to lower the settings Motoko stops her.

 "NO!!! You cant lower the settings!! I used my self made song which is ultra cool, you have to feel the vibe!!!" Motoko pleads with her puppy face.

 Judy knowing she will not win this one takes another coffein shot to fortify herself.

 "Fuck me" she resings herself.

 When she puts on the BD she or rather Motoko feel ice cold almost instantly.

 *Well nothing new here, still sucks* She thinks, the perfect control being impossible to get used to, even after the other BD's.

 Thats when everything went wrong.

 In the other BD's there was atleast a semblance of humanity left, this time there was only bloodlust and hate.

 There was just no way she could keep a coherent thought, being fully immersed in the focused bloodlust and loosing her sense of self, while violence and slaughter, washed away the last smidgen of what was left.

 The music in the background was only amplifying the feeling of carnage.

 After what felt like hours or only minutes, she couldnt tell, of brutal and unfrogiving slaughter the BD was over, the music stops.

 If the loss of control still bothered her, she couldnt notice this time, the only thing she noticed, were the ragged breaths, shaking limbs, and all consuming panick.

 Never before has she felt something even remotely like this, so much hate, so much bloodlust, just thinking about it, made her throw up, half of it landing on the chair she was lying on, because her body was shaking too much.

 She knew that Motoko had a inhuman focus, what she didnt know was that she could focus on emotions and dial them up to 100. 

 Thats when she noticed the person who was responsible for this, looking worried but also expectantly at her.

 If before she was a little intimidated of her she now was outright terrified, even though she knew, or should know, that she was safe, or should be safe.

 Her body and mind being pushed to their limit after just being a living slaughter machine made it hard to think.

 Motoko pouts and says sadly: "Hey Judy are you alright? Are you ill? Did you not like the song? I really thought this was going to be a hit!"

 Now she was sure, Motoko is a vampire.
