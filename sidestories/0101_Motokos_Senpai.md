_Dear Adam Smasher.

 I write to you to show my appreciation towards the work you've been doing so far.

 I thought I was the only one dedicated to the Path of Cosplay, feeling that my efforts were unappreciated, until I learned in detail about you.

 I've been murdering gonks like the raffen, scavengers and maelstrom goons in various jobs I could get my hands on, trying to scrounge up enough eddies for a full body conversion just like the one you have. I've been doing it for a few weeks now, though sadly, I'm not a chad front-line fighter like you, instead preferring to murder people from the shadows without them noticing, but still! Your example was a great inspiration to me, and I'm grateful for the glorious example you've set up for all of the aspiring full-Borg hopeful conversions like me.

 Now then, don't get me wrong here. I'm not exactly a fan of indiscriminate violence you seem to prefer to engage in. Violently murdering unaffiliated civilians seems like a sloppy work to me, but then again, who am I to question the Master of Violence? My preference is targeted violence towards targets I feel deserving of it, but what do you think? Do you feel like it's more fun to just murder everyone even tangentially involved? Or is it more challenging and fun to restrict one's violence just to predesignated targets? Let me know what you think if you could, please!

 Anyways, thanks for reading this message! I hope to hear from you soon, and if the stars align we'll meet each other on the opposite sides of a field of battle! Not like you would see me coming anyways ![Image 24: :p](https://forums.spacebattles.com/styles/sbforums/smilies/tongue.gif)

 With sincere regards:

 The Major_

 ------

 Adam re-read the message once again.

 On the face of it, it was somewhat amusing.

 A simple, albeit somewhat creative, introduction of some idiot solo's credentials, professing admiration, swearing to meet in battle, yadda yadda.

 He witnessed hundreds of such things in his long life.

 Still... few have managed to leave such a message written in pigeon blood upon human skin and taped to his forehead to be noticed just when he was coming out of a diagnosis and recovery cycle.

 Really, there was precious few replies he could think of that would properly carry through his thoughts on the matter, so he settled on the classics.

 "Heh" Adam spoke out loud, crumpling the bloodstained parchment made from human skin in his cyber fist "Bring it on"
