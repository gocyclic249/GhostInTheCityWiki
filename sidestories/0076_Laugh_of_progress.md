"I choose to discover a new element in this decade and do the other things, not because they are easy, but because they are hard." I say, standing triumphantly in my kitchen trying to figure out where I'm gonna get a big enough amount of pure Lead and Dysprosium to smash together. Then I'll have to figure how to actually make the laser. Hmmm. Science is hard, why can't I just have someone else do the boring part and take the credit.

 "That sounds so boring that I'm going home if you actually start doing that." Hiromi says. She's right. It's super boring. Ugh. I really want my Motokoium tho. It'd be so cool, but I have too many projects on the back burner already. I still haven't managed to make HMG-chan'a final form. Her ai isn't close to being done, and there's all my half finished attempts at making a robot maid to clean my edgerunner clothes when they get covered in scav blood, although I've mostly been targeting maelstrom lately, worthless borgs. I ought to see if bombing them to kingdom come counts as kills that give experience. You fuckers took my arms, I'll take your everything. Hehehehe.

 "Motoko you gonk, I'm going home." Hiromi says, giving me a light slap.

 "No wait. I was just thinking of unleashing ultraviolence on maelstrom, not nerd shit." I beg, Hiromi come back. I need social interaction or I'll start going insane and put in an ai in the microwave.

 "Then let's do something fun. Or at least just watch tv. There's a new episode of Man vs. Machine. They got a world champion boxer against Atom, a militech prototype bot. It's probably a few years outta date from the real prem shit they got in their secret labs, but it's still top of the line tech." Hiromi is practically vibrating with excitement as she continues to talk about all the super secret hidden science projects that corps are doing. It's still nerd shit, but Hiromi only cares about the outcome not the process.

 Wait a second.

 "Hehehehehehehe." I can't help but laugh. That's it. Why waste my time doing all the hard work when I can build a bot that can synthesize Motokoium for me. I am a genuis. Hahahahah.

 "Ok. I'm going home. You're doing your pervert laugh. Catch you later." Hiromi says while walking out the door. Who needs her. Progress waits for no man. We set sail on this new sea because there is new knowledge to be gained. And I will be the captain.

 Wait.

 "Wait. Hiromi! Stop. We can watch tv." I shout, running after her. The captain can't be all work and no play. Plus I'll need to grind up my tech skill before I'll feel comfortable letting a robot handle potentially fissile material.

 "And it's not a pervert laugh! It's the laugh of progress!"
