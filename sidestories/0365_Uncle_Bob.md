Motoko stood in her shot-up apartment staring at the tv, remembering her thoughts about the movie before the Scavs and Maelstorms shot up her couch. Then she looked at her music box. Then her tool kit. 

 "I wonder how much chrome the Tyger Claws have laying around?"

 It was a very, very sizable amount. 

 A few days later, Motoko finished coding and upping her Tech skill as she made constant use of it while crafting. She stepped back and looked at her new creation with a smile.

 Some time afterwards, a tall well built man walked into a known Maelstrom club wearing ill-fitting clothing and a stretched out tiger-riding-on-a-unicorn t-shirt that looked like it used to be owned by a thirteen year old tyger claw fangirl. Which it was.

 Every weapon in the place was immediately aimed at him, but none of that worried the stranger. 

 The tall man that resembled an old Austrian bodybuilder who, in this world, was laughed out of Hollywood, just strode towards the bartender and spoke with an accent, "I'm looking for Natalie Desanto, also known as GearSlot."

 All the weapons started firing. 

 Not having any organic material, the android's Sandevistan never needed to shut off as it found the most efficient path to slaughtering everyone that was shooting at him except the bartender. 

 Moments later, only the bartender was still alive and pleading for his life, "I'll tell you all I know!"

 The tall android nodded and turned off his holographic cloak so that he now reselmbled an imposing red-eyed metal skeleton.. wearing ill-fitting clothes and a stretched out tiger-on-a-unicorn t-shirt as he looked the terrified bartender over, "I'll also need your clothes, your boots, and your motorcycle."

 "Great job, Uncle Bob!" Motoko chirped from outside as she waited near her own bike. 

 "Do not contact anyone about this. If you do," the terminator glared at the terrified bartender, "[I'll be back.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CnQm2_cAZvo&pp=ygUQdGVybWluYXRvciB0aGVtZQ%3D%3D)"

 "I don't understand why you needed to build this thing," sighed Jun as he pulled up next to Motoko, "I could have done everything it did."

 "Stop ruining my dreams, Jun!"
