Hmmm... Omake time.... Mr Anderson or should I call you Mr Wick? Part 1

 Motoko stared at the gunbarrel pointed right at her head without blinking, the owner of said gun was breathing slightly heavily as he one handedly adjust his ruined suit.

 "So. Mr Anderson, mind I ask why you are after my client?" Chirped Hiromi from the side sitting on a couch looking for all the things in the world as chilled as ice.

 Thomas Anderson spared a glance at the other kid sitting there, musing if the person that sent the hit out on Brother was really this kid cowering behind the couch. Something he finds very odd, but his netrunning skill led to this as one of the only possible lead he has.

 "Someone put a hit out on my Brother. And all the leads I have led to that little girl cowering behind the couch. So unless you can prove otherwise, I suggest you kids take a hike. I don't want to kill you if I can avoid it" growled out Anderson as kept a wary eye on the purple haired girl Infront of him. The other 2 boys groaning slightly in the hallway from where he knocked them out.

 "A deal then, Mr Anderson. We at section 9 will help you find your Brother's killer and you let our client go if she's innocent. It's our speciality." Offered Motoko as she continues to stare down the John Wick look a like.

 Anderson mused over the suggestion, it's not like he has anything to lose and shooting kids will make him feel bad about it especially if they didn't do it. His lead here were dubious as it is and having some helping hand is better than none. A test then. "You still have to convince me to lower this gun first." He shot back.

 Motoko smirked as she lowers her gun away and gave a small chuckle. "Mr Anderson, you can wave that gun all you want but it won't do you much good. Or should I call you by Mr Wick?"

 Anderson look slightly shocked for a split second, before he lowered his gun. "Impressive. You have my attention. Kid"

 Hiromi ever the corpo, "then have a sit Mr Wick, and we'll hash out a deal like proper civilized people." Even as she held back the chill in her spine on the fact that Motoko found our their target was actually an Alias, still the contract is to get Mr Anderson or Mr Wick as he is now found out to be off Asuka. Said client slowly peek out from behind the couch, her shotgun with her earning an amused snort from Mr Wick as he sat down on the couch opposite of Hiromi.

 "Now let's talk."
