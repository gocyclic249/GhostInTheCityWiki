***Months later.**

 Jun was just about to bite into his Burrito XXL when the sound of footsteps coming to a halt right behind him caught his attention. Putting the burrito back down, he turned to faced the motley crew fully geared for war staring at him.

 " You don't want to do this. Walk away now and i'll pretend nothing happened. "

 " SHUT UP ONI! You got this coming! Once we takedown the feared Oni, WE'LL BE THE NEXT TOP DOGS IN NIGHT CITY. "

 The sound of an engine started to purr, the growl getting louder and louder causing the faces of those standing before Jun to pale.

 "Tha-, That's the Oni Engine!! Be ready Guys, he's going to attack us ANY MOMENT!"

 "I- I'm sorry, i can't do thisss!!"

 "Me- Me too!"

 "You're on your own Bob!"

 With shrieks of terror, the crew infront of him threw down their weapons and fled leaving behind the spokeman who despite trembling, raised his Cut-O-matic and pointed it straight at Jun.

 "I- I won't give up! I'm the man who survived the Gladiator Tournament out in the Badlands! This is nothing, NOTHI-"

***BOOM**

 As the man collapsed to the ground with a hole in his chest, Jun turned back to his Burrito and tried his best to ignore the rumors that were once again spreading.

 " Did you guys see that? The Oni punched the guy so fast that he didn't even notice!"

 " Look at his hands!! They're still so clean! The blood from that guy wasn't even able to land on his fists!"

 " Man, that is one wicked Sandy he has. I mean i'm using a Krenz but i didn't even see him move at all!"

 " Well, that's the Oni for ya! Didn't you hear the stories of how he took out that Scav base in the time it took for the rest of the Kamikaze to park their cars? "

 " WOW! They say that the Oni Engine is a sign on how geared up he is for a fight. Considering how mild it was just now, the Oni wasn't even taking them seriously! "
