Melissa breathed out and started the snippet of the BD over again. It was the only section she cared about. She'd watched the whole thing, felt a brief amount of grim satisfaction at the Scavs dying—stupid assholes got Tony killed, and ripped him off over it too—but this was the only part she really cared about.

 Walking up behind Tony. Idiot. Stupid. Stole a car you shouldn't have. Slipping behind him as he bounced along carefree with eddies in his pocket, probably thinking about buying her something. The dumb gonk. Ghost was so fucking pissed at him. She was so fucking pissed at him. Why'd you do it Tony? Why'd you have to do it?

 She plunged a knife in his neck.

 Melissa ripped the BD headset off. Fuck. She sat in the dark for a while, head in her hands. She didn't sob anymore. Didn't cry. She'd watched it enough times to move past that. If she felt a little wetness on her fingers it was just from her eyes being too dry.

 They hadn't needed eddies that badly Tony. You didn't have to buy me things, just stay with me. Fucking gonk.

 Tony. Tony.

 Melissa looked at the BD headset. Slowly she put it back on and breathed in and out. She hit play on the only snippet of the BD she cared about.

 It was the only thing she had left.
