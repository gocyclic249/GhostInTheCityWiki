 The only thing was she couldn't find his angle, going out to a slaughter made sense, but why was he wearing a tie, and why did he have a bunch of anti-personnel grenades in a bouquet?

_Adam Smasher_

 He was on his way from his latest slaughter when his path was blocked. Scanning the meat that stood in his way he saw a girl wearing an Arasaka Academy uniform and holding a body pillow.

 "YOU CRADLE ROBBING BASTARD! STAY AWAY FROM MY MOTOKO!"

 With that warcry, the girl charged forward. Quickly activating his sandy Adam stared horrified as the girl's advance sped up instead of slowing after his speedware activated.

 Unable to move in time to stop her he could only watch as she swung the body pillow again and again.

 According to all known laws of physics, there is no way a body pillow should be able to damage Adam Smasher. Its fluff is too soft to affect the advanced composites that make up his body.

 The body pillow, of course, wins anyway because Hiromi doesn't care what humans think is impossible.

 As the initial charge knocks Adam back, sending him skidding across the now cratered concrete, she keeps ranting.

 "WHO! DO! YOU! THINK! YOU! ARE!" Her every word is punctuated with another slam, sending damage reports across his OS and crushing armour plating.

 "You think you're special because Motoko invited you to kill Scavs with her!?!" Adam's arm stopped responding, he held it up to try and block her attacks, but that barely slowed her down after she bashed it into scrap.

 "Motoko asked Me out first! ME!" Amidst the barrage of body pillow blows, Adam Smasher's frustration was now mixed with bewilderment. He had faced down countless enemies, but this crazy girl wielding a body pillow would be the one to defeat him.

 Finally the little tidbit of information that was only noticed by his unconscious mind clicked into place and everything suddenly made sense, except for Hiromi's physical capabilities. The image of the body pillow was a very familiar girl wearing a leotard and half jacket, in fact, it was the same girl that invited him to go out and slaughter Scavs.

 While that brought context to the situation that still didn't change the fact that there was nothing he could do as his frame was slowly pounded into scrap. At this point, his audio receptors were destroyed so he couldn't hear her continue ranting.

 Just as his biopod was about to begin ejecting he saw out of the corner of his eye the counterintel agent that came with him in the AV, While part of him wanted her to do something about this, he knew that it was both too late, and nothing that she could do against ~~mea~~monster.
