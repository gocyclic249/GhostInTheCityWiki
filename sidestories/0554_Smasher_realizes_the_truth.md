 Adam Smasher smashes through a wall and grabs Motoko by the throat. "**YOU THINK I WOULDN'T KNOW? I WOULDN'T REALIZE?"**

 Motoko chokes, unable to say anything even as Adam continues. **"THE SNEAKY MAYHEM, THE MURDER, THE GOD DAMN MUSIC? YOU EVEN MADE IT LOOK LIKE A CHARACTER FROM AN ANIME! MOTOKO KUSANAGI, OR SHOULD I SAY, MORGAN BLACKHAND?"**

 The pressure finally eased on her throat, and she was allowed to slip to the floor as she gasped for breath.

 "**TRYING TO BAIT ME WITH A TRAP LIKE THIS, MAKING A FAKE BODY AND IDENTITY TO LURE ME IN, DON'T MAKE ME LAUGH."**With that, he grabbed her by the skull and lifted her up again, so she could look him in his hate filled eyes. "**I JUST HAVE ONE QUESTION FOR YOU, MORGAN."**

 He leaned in, until all Motoko could see was a single eye. "**WHY A TEENAGE GIRL, MORGAN? WHY A TEENAGE GIRL? I'M NOT THAT TYPE OF WEEB, ASSHOLE. YOU'VE GOTTEN FUCKED IN THE HEAD SINCE THE LAST TIME WE MET. I'M FUCKING INSULTED."**

 His voice fell and he released Motoko to the floor again. "Get yourself a proper body and just fucking ask if you want to throw down again. Jesus dude." He walked through a different wall than he entered through, shaking his head clear of the rubble. "Fucking Ghost in the Shell bullshit, just use a phone and call me next time, fucking hell."
