Crafting Glory!​

 Mitch was starting to think his 'apprentice' was legitimately crazy and only acted mostly sane.

 How could he tell? From her latest 'babies' she wanted to show off to him.

 When Saul told him that Motoko wanted to learn more about his Overwatch and how to tinker with impressive gun mods like it had he had been all for it. She had done a lot for both Scorpion and the whole clan after all, and honestly he liked the idea of teaching an up and coming fellow techie. She was a great student too; attentive, eager to learn, very polite to him, and even inspiring him to find creative ideas to add to his weapons.

 Why not use explosive rounds since using a silenced weapon after all?

 Of course, once Motoko actually started crafting mods and guns herself wholesale things got... interesting.

 Namely, in front of the camp's impromptu gun range with various weapons sitting on a fold out table.

 "Okay Motoko, what new war crime have you made today?" Mitch said only half-jokingly.

 "So glad you asked!" Motoko said with an excited grin as she picked up a rifle and showed it off to him. "Behold, the Lancer!"

 What was before him as a heavily modified DA8 Umbra, being far different from the usual 'fire and forget' rifles you normally see. Namely, this one had a full overhaul that made it [look](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/gearsofwar/images/9/94/Mark_3_Lancer_transparent.png) tougher, bulkier, packing more ammo and firepower, and of course one couldn't ignore what was attached beneath the barrel.

 "Why does this rifle have a chainsaw attached to it?" Mitch asked in concern.

 "When borgs get in close with katanas, gorilla arms, or what have you!" Motoko answered in a way that made it sound like she should have added 'duh' at the end of that sentence. "Really good at cutting through subdermals and tougher chrome. Has option to heat up or electrify the chain as well! Wanna see?"

 Before Mitch could say no Motoko held the Lancer up high, ran over to a target dummy, reeved up her gun and brought it down on it. Mitch watched as the crudely built but still tough dummy was quickly reduced to pieces while Motoko just laughed.

 "What you think?!" Motoko asked with bright eyes.

 "It's..." Mitch looked over the pile of dummy pieces. "Effective."

 "Right! Oh, you'll love this!" Motoko said before putting down the gun and then picking up a [grenade](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/readyplayerone/images/b/b9/Holy-Hand-Grenade.png) that looked like it was plated in gold and jewels, with even a cross as a pin. "Behold, the holy hand grenade of Antioch!"

 "Looks like something a Valentino would like," Mitch noted. "What makes it so special?"

 "Its a special blend of conventional explosives and incendiary, but taken to the max! Was blessed by priest and added a special speaker to it that when thrown sounds like a church choir! I even added a script on it!" Motoko said as she pointed it out on grenade before reading it outloud. "And Saint Attila raised the hand grenade up on high, saying, 'O Lord, bless this thy hand grenade, that with it thou mayst blow thine enemies to tiny bits, in thy mercy.' And the Lord did grin. And the people did feast upon the lambs, and sloths, and carp, and anchovies, and orangutans, and breakfast cereals, and fruit bats, and large chulapas. And the Lord spoke, saying, 'First shalt thou take out the Holy Pin. Then shalt thou count to three, no more, no less. Three shall be the number thou shalt count, and the number of the counting shall be three. Four shalt thou not count, neither count thou two, excepting that thou then proceed to three. Five is right out. Once the number three, being the third number, be reached, then lobbest thou thy Holy Hand Grenade of Antioch towards thy foe, who, being naughty in My sight, shall snuff it.'"

 Mitch just stood in silence at that.

 "So, want to see what it can do?!" Motoko asked.

 "How big is the yield?" Mitch asked cautiously.

 That made Motoko's grin drop as she thought about it, looked around, and then sighed and placed the grenade down.

 "Too big, so I guess another time," Motoko said sadly.

_'How big is it?!'_ Mitch couldn't help but think if the Badlands was 'too small' to test it.

 "Moving on, here is the handcannon!" Motoko said with pride as she showed Mitch... a [foam finger](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/xhU6zDzQPVI/maxresdefault.jpg).

_'Where does one even get a foam finger nowadays?'_ Mitch thought. "I'm kinda afraid to ask what it does."

 "Watch!" Motoko said as she pointed the foam finger down range and Mitch watched as each time the thumb of the foam finger went down it fired the finger and a target down rage _exploded_ as if hit from an explosive round. "The perfect infiltration weapon!"

 "Think the finger would raise eyebrows." Mitch pointed out.

 "Hmmm, outside sporting events that is most likely true," Motoko admitted as she put down the foam finger and picked up what Mitch was sure was a sound system of some kind, but the presence of a trigger told him otherwise. "Now this, is the [dubstep gun](https://i.ytimg.com/vi/KF5Heg8XTp0/hqdefault.jpg)! It fires pure soundwaves of destruction forces!"

 Sure enough, as soon as Motoko pulled the trigger Mitch watched as brightly colored lasers and sound blasted the targets while electronic music played, which Mitch noticed made a few family members dance out at the catchy beat, himself included. Certainly made his teeth shake like he was in a club.

 "Not subtle at all," Mitch pointed out.

 "I know! Isn't it great?!" Motoko said as she put the unwieldy weapon down.

 Mitch was starting to think that Night City was doomed if Motoko kept going down this path of insane and deadly weapon making.

 "And this is [Mr. Toots](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/redfaction/images/9/9b/Mrtoolsmay3.jpg)!" Motoko said with pride as she gently held what Mitch thought was a stuffed unicorn toy, only to watch how it seemed to nuzzle into Motoko's arms lovingly. "I programmed him myself! Wanna guess how you use him?"

 As Mitch watched the demonstration he corrected himself.

 Night City was most certainly doomed, and he helped make it so.
