"Good evening, citizens of Night City! We're here today at 'Badass Weekly' to discuss the most prominent rising star of our beloved city - Motoko Kusanagi. Here, in our studio for an interview."

_*turns to Motoko*_

 "So, Ms. Kusanagi, let's start with the question that's on everyone's mind: what's your secret?"

 "Scav hunting."

 "…scav hunting? So… you're good at hunting scav?"

 "Good? Bitch, I'm great! I'm always ready. Even now. What, did you think that the two HMGs on my back, these grenade bundles across my hip, the Burya in my left hand and the Malorian in my right are just for weight practice?"

 "Yes, I was wondering actually…"

 "There is nothing to wonder about. I can detect a scav from 10 miles away just by the smell of their farts. They reek like they're in dire need of a quick death. Which I deliver. Always. Remember that."

 "O-okay, I will. I definitely will…"

 "Why are you so nervous? There is nothing to be worried about, unless you're a fucking scav, then you have a hell ton of things to worry about. Most importantly, ME. Why the hell are you even more nervous now, I just sa— Wait a minute. Are you… a scav? Hm? *sniff-sniff* You smell of… piss. Just like a fucking scav…"

_*suddenly, an entire platoon of MaxTac operatives simultaneously crashes through the studio's window. Yet before they can do anything, they all die under a suspiciously well-timed and supernaturally accurate storm of gunfire.*_

 "See, that's what I'm talking about! Look at these fucking scavs! Where the hell did they get all this fancy military equipment? Who the hell knows! But one thing is for certain. They adapt, they evolve, they consume… But so do I…"

_*The host doesn't really have anything to say to this. Mainly because she's fainted*_

 "I tell you, this is an infestation of the highest order. Just the other day, I got tipped about a possible scav den. And guess where it was? At the top floor of the fucking Saka tower! Can you imagine? But well, a good scav is a dead scav, so I instantly got down to work. Infiltrated the place like a pro. Like always. Anyways, there I am, at the top floor of Arasaka Tower, and guess what I see? Two fucking scavs. One in a business suit, the other is some borg whom the first guy called Smasher. Fucking _Smasher_, can you believe it? That's the most stereotypical scav name I've ever heard in my life! Might as well call yourself Skidmark!"

_*takes a deep breath*_

 "Anyway, I started blasting. And like any other good scav, they got their spirits broken and their bodies torn under a fire from some good old anti-tank handguns. That's two less scavs to terrorize our innocent _*distant explosion in the background*_ citizens."
