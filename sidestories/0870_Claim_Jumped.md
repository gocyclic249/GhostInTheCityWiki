Mac look up from his terminal to see one of his crews come in.

 "Okay why do you all look like someone kicked your puppy I didn't hear any alerts from the Militech band about the heist."

 A 3ft tall man in a cheap suit spoke up.

 "Yeah well getting in went like clock work but when we went to klep the items you sent us in for they were already gone."

 Mac types furiously at his terminal looking up shipping data.

 "Gone, the new drone hard drives and updated paraline models were gone?

 That can't right those weren't due to be shipped for a couple more days?"

 The twins spoke up next.

 "Boxes were gone/manifests said they should still be there.

 Perhaps another runner hit the place/someone jumped our job."

 The fullborg in a samson frame and cowboy had spoke up next in the worst fake Texan accent Mac had the misfortune of hearing.

 "Didn't see no bushwhackers come or go from the scene who could have hornswoggled us, all that stood out was that little darling singer ghost in the shell."

 "Ghost in the shell, you mean Motoko Kusanagi?"

 "Is that her name well shucks I didn't know that, girls got a real talent for music."

 Macs face falls at that comment.

 "And for theft, she's an expert at stealth and netrunning.

 Here I go getting you janitorial access cards and uniforms to get into the employee areas without attracting attention and then she just waltezs in and kelps everything.

 Did you at least install the program into the buildings mainframe?"

 Cheap suit man nods at him.

 "The program was uploaded unnoticed and we left without further incident."

 Mac forewords them half the jobs payout.

 "Well no loot means no pay for that half though not through any fault of your own, you still uploaded the program and didn't attract attention.

 So at least it wasn't a total wash, I'll let you know when another job comes up."

 The runner crew walks out looking less than thrilled by the smaller than expected payout.

 "Dagnabit we got claim jumped by a little girl?"

 "Bullshit!/Horseshit!"

 "Now now guys that's just the nature of our profession, as is finding out where our little thief took her ill gotten gains and engage in a Strategic Transfer of Equipment to Alternate Location.

 I'm sure Mac's client will still be happy to purchase the goods."

 "Hawt Dawg we going to rustle her cattle this time and see how she like them cow pies!"

 "Must you use the accent/Seriously Jean Paul that's total gonk."

 "Guys let me make a few calls, see where she might have taken the goods and we quietly liberate them and make a few eddies."

 [**_one failed heist later_**]

 Mac looks up from his terminal to see the very battered crew walk in.

 "Tried to hijack the girls stolen goods didn't ya?"

 "Mistakes were made/We done goofed."
