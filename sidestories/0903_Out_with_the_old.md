**Out with the old**

 "Are you checking out my new arms?", Motoko asked, staring glibly at the MSM upper manager.

 "They don't look... New...", replied the manager, as he slowly attempted to push further through his chair.

 "They're great arms! And on brand! You wanted approved brand!"

 "Yes, but where did you-"

 "I've got a guy. He supplied the arms."

 "A guy?"

 "Your guy."

 "My guy?"

 "Alex. He's your guy, right?"

 "Oh, Alex! Alex got- Alex... got... You these arms?"

 "They're Alex's arms!"

 By now, blood had begun to pool on the table where the arms were resting. As it dripped onto the floor of the manager's office, he cringed back further-

 "You were right Mr Manager man! You said I could make use of him!"

 Edit: No Alex's were armed in the making of this omake.
