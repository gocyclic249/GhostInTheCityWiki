TC Tikes​

 Smoke lingered in the air as the smoker nearly finished with his call within his perfectly kept office.

 "Make sure Okina-san gets the care he needs. No cost is too great," the man said firmly.

_"Of course, Saito-sama,"_ the man on the other end said with respect before Sota ended the call.

 "An assassination attempt so soon after peace is made... still hasn't beaten the record," he noted to himself as he set down his pipe and brought up the reports.

 They had already identified the would-be assassin, Leo Dolby, more commonly known as 'Truck' on account of him being as large and subtle as one. Known ties to Maelstorm, but nothing direct of course, and the fact he brought an LMG to an assassination mission was proof enough of that.

 Still, he was not without skill given his ability to infiltrate the building and hide in the trunk of a large car, waiting until his target was vulnerable to strike. He would have succeeded was it not for Okina's quick reflexes and aid of two unlikely sources.

 Jun and Motoko Kusanagi.

 The former was a low level grunt turned Kamikaze, making a name for himself as 'The Oni' during the recent gang war and surviving relatively intact. The latter had not yet been inducted in truth, but aided Kamikaze during the conflict as well and had been slowly making a name for herself as a Solo in Wakako's employ. Both were legacies, their parents being TC as well and having made names of themselves before their deaths.

 The elder brother had apparently brought his little sister to the party to introduce her to the leadership since she was a relative unknown, and had left early because she had... eaten too much sushi.

 Saito could not help but laugh at the poor discipline in one so young and inexperienced, but apparently quite skilled.

 Anyhow, they were in the right place at right time to help prevent Okina's death, though not of his escort's, and slay the Solo instead.

 An impressive feat considering the amount of military grade cyberware the man had according to an autopsy by one of their rippers, including a sandevistan. From the report the siblings had slowly disabled the man's ability to effectively fight until the elder Kusanagi finally ripped his head off.

 From there the security from the party above the parking area had finally reacted to Okina's call for reinforcements and found them battered but alive.

 Jun would have to be rewarded for his loyalty and skill, especially considering he went into the fight unarmed, and maybe offer an official invitation for Motoko to officially become part of their ranks rather than merely a poser as the reports stated she was not long ago.

 Of course, that was _before_ her encounters with Scavs and year long coma. Since then she seemed less interested in the Tyger Claws and more in striking out on her own.

 That was fine, not all children followed their paths right away and needed to experience the world until they returned home. Besides, she was already working for Wakako and the TC always had need of more discrete agents. She may play Solo for as long as her heart desires so long as she does nothing against the TC's overall interests.

 Until then, she would be one to keep an eye on along with her brother.

 Mayhaps in time they will honor their parents well.
