War Runs​

 "Just keep to the back roads and take it slow," Ichi reminded Malcolm as he drove them through the back alley streets and other tight but lightly traveled areas.

 "This is like the fourth time you've told me dude, I got this," Malcolm waved off his concern.

 "We're in the middle of a gang war with the Maelstroms, I'm not taking any chances," Ichi reminded him as he idly looked in the back of his friend's car and the boxes of supplies within. "Especially not with the goods we are moving."

 "Its just spare ammo, meds, and a couple guns, something I'm pretty sure the Maelstrom has plenty of already," Malcolm said with a shrug. "Not like we are moving cyberware or something, _then_ I'd be worried."

 "Shobo-sama made it very clear how important these supplies are you Ghonk," Ichi hissed at him. "Logistics are just as important as soldiers when it comes to winning wars, and this is most certainly a war. These supplies could mean life and death for a number of Tyger Claws and those under their protection, especially as the fighting when Maelstrom kicks up."

 "If its so important why leave it to us?" Malcolm asked curiously as they carefully coursed a near deserted street.

 "Cause actual Tyger Claw members are needed on the frontline to blunt the Maelstroms and they are too borged out to even think straight most of the time to even consider hitting us," Ichi said as a fierce smile overcame his face. "Besides this is the perfect chance to prove our worth in a time of crisis to the gang and can lead to bigger and better things for us down the road. We just gotta make sure we don't fuck it up.

 "Should have invited Motoko to help, then no one could possibly fuck with us," Malcolm idly said as he kept on driving steadily through Night City's lesser used streets on their way to a Tyger Claws strongpoint.

 "I was gonna ask, she even offered at first, but apparently her brother called her and told her to keep her head down tonight," Ichi explained as he kept a lookout through the windows with his tech shotgun at the ready.

 "Damn, really? First time she gets in contact with him in who knows how long and he tells her to sit this one out? Fucking Gonk," Malcolm shook his head. "Still, knowing her she'll likely _try_ to listen to Jun-san if nothing else. At least she is totally locked up like Hiromi or totally bailing on us like Omaeda."

 "Her parents are corpos, so of course they were gonna lock her up during a gang war," Ichi shrugged. "And this is way outside of Omaeda's skillset anyhow."

 "I guess... still, other than some sounds of fighting in the distance we've haven't actually seen anything," Malcolm pointed out, the sounds of gunfire and muffled explosives in the distance punctuating his point.

 "Like said, cause the jacked up borgs are too stupid to think strategy and just know how to hit Tyger Claws head on, which is why we'll win this," Ichi said with confidence.

 "Still, sure Motoko will be upset she missed this shit, its what edgerunners live for after all," Malcolm chuckled as they came upon one of the strongpoints, a couple Tyger Claws waiting for them as the garage opened up.

 "I'm sure she will appreciate a boring night for once," Ichi said as he got out of the car and started to unload the supplies.

 After all, what idiot went out on a night like this totally alone?
