 CyberPsycho

_mad flies, mad flies buzzing all around

 Let em talk, can you hear? The world is upside down

 Roaches run, Hoppers hop, voices around your head

 Pull off your hand, load up your arm, time to make em deaaaaaaaaaaaaaaad_

 BOOM

 It's all here in the city!

 Where every day it's Night

 All the guns lookin pretty

 All the blades lookin tight

 All the fun in the city!

 How you die is how you live

 So drink the names of the fallen

 Down your throat like a shiv

_Beams of light falling down, insects underfoot

 Crunch of boots fill the air, breathe the ash and soot

 Grab your friends, find the rings, throw em all away

 Mad flies, Mad flies, time to make em paaaaaaaaaaaaaaay_

 BOOM

 Another world in the city!

 A whole planet made of bones

 Spoiled milk for the kitties

 Blood for the skull throne

 Always work in the city!

 Kill a year in a week

 Roast a pig by committee

 Find the prey that you seek

_Quiet now, buzzing down, ropes from dragonflies

 Machines that talk, silent screams, with nothing in their eyes

 Losing weight, fill the holes, bake a second batch

 Death in chrome, Chrome is flesh, time for the final maaaaaaaaaaatch_

 BOOM

 A fresh start in the City!

 Old clothes on new toys

 New gigs for the kiddies

 Time to try out new joys!

 Nothing ends in the city!

 Fresh seeds for the birds

 Klep and flat, chrome and muscle

 Find the beat, sing the words
