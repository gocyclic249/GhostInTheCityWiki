[" The KING is back, BABY!"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zWDcot2YezA)

 With that announcement, Adam Smasher let loose with the HMG in his hands, the recoil from the gunfire reverbing up his body and towards his perfectly coiffed hair so that a small cowlick broke loose in a manner that framed his smoldering eyes perfectly.

 " Just a hunka, hunka BURNING LOVE!"

 Taking cover from the rain of bullets coming in tune with the very nice baritone singing from Adam Smasher, Rita grabbed Motoko by the collar and start shaking her violently.

"Lord Almighty, Feel my temperature rising"

 " LOOK WHAT YOU DID MOTOKO! Why did you have to diss his fashion sense!? When i suggested you get a hobby, i didn't mean be a fashion critic for the Solos in Night City!"

"You gonna set me on fire"

 " I had no choice RITA! It's a burden i must bare as the only person in Night City with excellent taste!"

"You light my morning sky, 

 With burning love"

 The sheer sincerity behind Motoko's line forced Rita to take a good look at Motoko once more, just incase something had changed from the last time she had saw the girl.

![Image 19](https://i.pinimg.com/originals/6d/54/9a/6d549a31fc2d68142acd1bfb00bc6c00.png)​

 Leather Jacket. Legless Netrunner suit. Shades of Purple. CHECK

"Burning, burning, burning, 

 And nothing can cool me"

 " Yes. Clearly you're the essence of good taste. Now can the ESSENCE OF GOOD TASTE convince Adam Smasher to stop SHOOTING AT US!??"

 " OK FINE!... I see what i can do. "

"Feel like I'm slipping away

 It's hard to breath"

 " HEY ADAM! YOUR STYLE ISN'T SHIT!! NOW IT'S SO OUTDATED THAT THE DINOSAURS ASKED FOR THEIR CLOTHES BACK!"

 " I THOUGHT YOU SAID YOU WERE GOING TO SEE WHAT YOU COULD DO!"

" You light my morning sky

With burning love

With burning love"

 " I AM! I'm telling the hard truths no one dares to tell him!"

 " DO YOU NOT REALIZE WHY THAT IS THE CASE??"

" I'm just a hunk, a hunk of burning love

Just a hunk, a hunk of burning love

Just a hunk, a hunk of burning love"

 As the rich baritone of Adam Smasher's singing and the RATATATAT of Heavy Machine Gunfire got closer to the spot they were arguing in, Rita put a pause to the argument to seek out better protection from the dissatisfied victim of one of Motoko's vicious STYLE teardowns.

"Just a hunk, a hunk of burning love

 Just a hunk, a hunk of burning love"

_I should not have suggest she get a hobby._ That was the thought running through Rita's head as she cowered behind one of the booths. Wincing at the feeling of bullets chewing away at the cover she was hiding behind, Rita couldn't help but wonder if things would have gotten better if she had just left the kid to continue murdering her way through Night City.

" Just a hunka, hunka BURNING LOVE!

Just a hunka, hunka BURNING LOVE!"

_YEPPP. Should have kept my big mouth shut and let her stay a XBD Star._
