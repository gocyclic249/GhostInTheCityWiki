**_Dimensional Contact Established_**

 "Man, those things almost zeroed Adam Smasher!" Malcolm moaned as he looked at the field of ugly fucking things.

 "...How did you know they had that acid blood?" Hiromi asked Motoko who was...twitchy.

 "...I had a dream that turned into a nightmare..." Motoko replied in a cryptic manner, while cooly staring into the distance.

 "And these motion trackers you insisted we use?" Ichi asked as he pondered the somewhat wrecked ship they had rushed towards after it barely landed.

 "Again it was part of my dream...of another life." Motoko replied trying to keep up her cool act.

 "..Corporal Moore?" Spoke up one of the people they managed to save, one of the ones that Vic didn't need to perform surgery to remove one of those things from. "Victoria Moore? But you died blowing up their nest..?"

 "...Okay, so..." Hiromi muttered while looking at Motoko with an inscrutable look. "When did this Moore woman die?"

 "About a week ago...I think..." The person, Lt. Takanaka replied as she stared at Motoko like she was a ghost.

 "That was the same time Motoko..." Malcolm trailed off with a look at the others.

 "Come get some!" Rebecca roared as she fired from the line with a couple Marines from this ship at the small amount of creatures that still popped out.

 It was kind of chaotic in that both Miltech forces and Arasaka forces were here, and both fighting these creatures.

 ----------------------------

 "Fuck!" V cursed as she kicked the side of the truck. "He does know that the damn computer of this other wreck lists just how many times these idiots at Weyland -Yutanni tried the same fucking thing, and lost whole worlds to them!?"

 "Will you shut up and do your fucking job!" Jenkins snarled as he jerked her around to face him. "Do you know what successfully pulling this off will do!? It will guarantee my rise-"

 "Baka..." Yorinobu muttered as he holstered his gun, leaving Jenkins a corpse to rot on the ground. 

 "...Thanks..." V said, though she was kind of confused why he did it. Maybe that stupid order was...

 "Washington DC has gone...silent." Yorinobu stated bluntly. "We have intercepted transmissions from the area of creature resembling these abducting people and infesting whole neighborhoods."

 The whole area around them became silent, as his words were broadcast over both the airwaves and through speakers. The thought that the capital of the NUSA had gone dark was, alarming.

 "We have also heard that other areas have also had sightings of these things, so far largely in the NUSA." Yorinobu continued. "It has only been a few days since these things arrived, and they have spread so far through greed. I have read logs from this...Atomos, logs that detail how whole planets have been lost to these things in spite of military intervention, and many, many attempts to study and breed these things as weapons against others..."

 The silence only broken by weapon fire was...like the grave. The knowledge that these...aliens could possibly overrun the planet, was chilling.

 "Every attempt failed disastrously." Yorinobu looked at each person around him, locking eyes with V for a moment. "Now...now it is our turn to deal with these things, and yet just like these fools from Weyland-Yutani! Our own corporate masters see these things as...profitable, as foolish things to use, and think they can control them despite the information that has been shared with them!"

 "...Wait a minute!" Solomon Reed, one of the men who showed up with a Militech group spoke up. "There is no way that Militech would try something like that-"

 Suddenly a barrage of rockets shot out, shooting down two AV's. One Militech, and one Arasaka. From the wreckage of both scurried the spider like creatures, and a few grown ones that had broken free.

 Before anyone could react, they were cut down with accurate gunfire, and the grown ones were hammered with not only bullets... But explosives.

 "I don't care whose ass you kiss!" Motoko roared angrily over the comms and the speakers. "But no one is causing us to lose this fucking planet to goddamn Aliens under my watch! So either be part of the solution in wiping these things out, or get out of my fucking way!!!"

 "..." Solomon Reed just stared at the wreckage that had contained those packages he had loaded up under orders.

 "...Well said..." V replied with a sigh of relief.

 "I will see about getting us some supplies..." Hiromi muttered as she quickly got to work, hopefully before Arasaka started working against them.

 "A strong passion..." Yorinobu muttered in approval, while Adam Smasher was being repaired, and growling angrily that he had to be saved by rookies.
