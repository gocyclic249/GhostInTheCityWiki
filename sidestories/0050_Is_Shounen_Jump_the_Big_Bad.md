I guess? I'm not to familiar with the Cyberware in the Cyberpunk setting beyond what was in-game. It was really a excuse to weeb it up via converting Kakashi's signature move into CP2077 terms.

 That being said, it be amazing if the Tyger Claws is just chock full of people that are anime references that only Motoko recognizes. 

* * *

 Motoko stared in shock at Jun who now eerily looked like Briareous from the Appleseed series as he waved at her from the entrance of a hole-in-the wall Izakaya.

 "MOTOKO HERE! I'm so glad that things have calm down enough that i can introduce you to the people i work with."

 Stepping through the doorway of the Izakaya, Motoko found herself doing a double-take. Most of the people within seemed to look like Cyberpunk versions of popular Anime franchises from her previous life.

_I'm sure it's a coincidence! The Tyger Claws are Japanese so i'm sure some appreciation for the same asthetics most have bled through! Don't overreact Motoko!_

 "Come on! Since this is a party, i'll allow you one cup of sake!"

 Curiosity about how Cyberpunk Sake tasted compelled her to take a sip, the overpowering sweetness however meant that it took a enormous effort of will not to spit it out.

 "Motoko! This is Kakashi San. He used to work with Tousa-"

 *PFFT

 Half-gagging from her impromptu spit-take, Motoko found herself confronted with a walking trademark violation in the form of Naruto's Hatake Kakashi, who had gracefully dodge her projectile weapon with just a minor raise of his sole eyebrow.

 "Hmm.. Jun Kun, I think you're sister isn't ready to handle alcohol yet. Still, nice to meet you Motoko Jou-chan."

 "Well, that went better then expected! Now let me introduce you to the rest of the people i work with. LUFFY SAN! OVER HERE!"

 Motoko felt as if she was watching a train crash as yet another walking trademark violation turned towards her brother's shout with Straw hat on his head and with a "Shishishishishi."

 The night passed into a blur after that, but some of the remaining Trademark violations that stood out to Motoko included:

 - Kurosaki Ichigo (_One of the best swordsman in the Clan Motoko! He can wield that oversized sword he has on his back with surprising speed_.)

 - Yagami Light (_He's really smart, his plans saved our ass more then once._)

 - Edward and Alphonse Eric (_I know they have gaijin names, but they're some of our best weaponsmiths. They're pretty good in a brawl as well_)

 - Natsu Dragneel (_He's hard to work with. Very much a pyrotechnic. He likes setting himself on fire and charging at people_)

 Hours later after carrying a drunk Jun back home and dumping him on his bed, Motoko found herself putting her head in her hands as she wondered aloud.

 "Am I in a Multi-cross? Is Shounen Jump the Big Bad? ~~Seras ~~Me, are we going to get a DMCA?"
