**Motoko meets her match**

 Jun let out a sigh of relief as he drank his Nicola. I had been two hours since his little sister told him and Akira to and quote 'get lost so she could fix this mess!'.

 No skin off his back, he couldn't make sense of the numbers, it was all Greek to him.

 Seriously, there were Greek letters in the spreadsheets for some reason.

 Either way Jun could relax for a bit whilst he watched the new episode of Watson Whore, he had thought-

**_"AAAAAAAAHHHHHHH!!!!"_**

 He was going to miss it...

 Motoko came charging into the room with a such look of panic on her face he wondered if he was going to get a panic attack by proximity as she grabbed hard.

 "Motoko! Wha-"

 He was interrupted.

 "No time! Call you bosses now or **_WE! ARE! DEAD!!!_**"

 "Wha-?"

 Again interrupted

**_"THE TYGER CLAWS HAVEN'T BEEN PAYING TAXES!!!!"_**

 "...What?" thankfully, he wasn't interrupted.

 "You. Don't. Get it! The IRS is still a thing! No one messes with the IRS! Nomads pays its taxes, _Arasaka_ pay its taxes, hell as far as I know from netrunning _Maelstrom_ pays its taxes! The only groups who don't pay their taxes are dead! Just like the Inquisitors!"

 "...Who?"

 "Exactly! I'm maybe brave and Hiromi may say I'm a _'little'_ crazy but I'm not that crazy! You are either going to pay your taxes or your out of the Tyger Claws! I am _NOT_ going to have a IRS liquidation squad knocking at my door!!!" she shouted in his face as she started to shake him!

 It looked like he had found the one thing that could beat his sister, and that was taxes...Truthfully, understandable. Taxes scared the shit out of him too if they could make his sis like this.

 To quote a Militech exeat that he once saw on TV. _'Taxes are truest of Lovecraftian horrors.'_
