Second war with Maelstrom.

 "Get ready the Maelstroms will be here soon." I heard Jun over the coms.

 The TC gonks prepared themselves.

 I also inspected my Nekomata and felt my Burya at my waist, it became almost instinct by now.

 We were defending a casino of the TCs, which will be attacked tonight. 

 Well the TCs were, I only wanted to kill more of those freaks.

 I was on the roof of the neighbouring building and had a perfect view on the street where they would come from.

 I rested my Nekomata on the roofs edge while I hid in the shadows.

 I activated cool and became still, completely focused on the task ahead, killing Maelstroms.

 I already saw the lights from their cars, Maelstroms weren't known for being silent, they would make a full on frontal attack.

 I adjusted my scope, making sure I would hit the first shot.

 Just a bit and the first car will be in range, then I will shoot the drive-

 Cars blearing "The only thing they fear is you" 

 Not even Cool could stop the RAGE I was feeling.

 THEY USED MY SONG TO HYPE THEMSELVES UP FOR THE FIGHT!!

 "THATS MY SONG YOU FUCKERS!!!" 

 A girl could be heard screaming, drowning out the preem music of a dozen cars in the night.
