Netchatting​

 Within a basic apartment Megabuilding H8 a young man was working away at a machine that most would have considered ancient, which they wouldn't be totally wrong since it was decades old.

 Omaeda typed away at his old clunker of a cyberdeck as his crappy cyberware one connected to it to augment each other to some kinda basic level. The second he earned enough eddies he was gonna buy and chip in for a good cyberdeck since as an aspiring netrunner his current state of equipment was downright embarrassing. It was too bad the Scav Den Motoko knocked over didn't have any good cyberdecks among the chrome they cut out of people, only the low-end civilian grade stuff that he was stuck with.

 Speaking of which...

 Omaeda quickly made his way over to a private and encrypted chatroom to get in contact with a fellow netrunner, this one being much better equipped and experience, who had been teacher and friend as he made his way into the community himself.

 4N7: *Just checking in, find anything interesting on data I was able to klep from what remained of those Scav computers?*

 BL155: *Not much, most of it was fired after your edgerunner friend exploded most of it, and since it was Scav gear wasn't exactly top of the line anyhow.*

 4N7: *So nothing good?*

 BL155: *Nothing your crazy choom would want at least since no data on other Scav dens. The parasites don't really work like traditional gangs, no real leadership or hierarchy and shit. Just run around city in independent cells and do their own thing. Not uncommon for Scavs to fight each other for chrome after all.*

 4N7: *Guess that means she'll have to hunt them down the old fashioned way. Shame, since last payday was pretty good. She might be kinda nuts but she gets results.*

 BL155: *Just be careful about edgerunners dude, those guys don't fuck around and people in their orbit tend to be offlined sooner rather than later.*

 4N7: *Will keep that in mind... so what data was recovered? Just curious.*

 BL155: *Nothing huge. List of chrome taken, potential buyers, personal messages about random shit, tons of porn, and the usual crap. Corpos, Scavs are not.*

 4N7: *Guess I was just curious about what the heck Scavs of all people do on their computers and such since worst of the worst.*

 BL155: *About the same as everyone else. First thing you learn as a netrunner, nearly everyone's online history is the same.*
