# She's here again

*Source: https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-2352#post-112225775*
*Words: ~150*

---

Rebecca bumped my shoulder.  
   
 "You're fuming, Crush," she said.  
   
 "I'm not fuming!" I replied.  
   
 "Uh huh." The annoying half-ad intoned.  
   
 "She's here again." I muttered.  
   
 "Who?"  
   
 "Her! Shotgun girl!"  
   
 "Oh. *Her*." she emphasized.  
   
 "She's always here! Every show!"  
   
 "Yup."  
   
 "And she always gets into the front row somehow!"  
   
 "Sure does."  
   
 "With the shotgun!"  
   
 "It's the shotgun alright."  
   
 "And she stands there, caressing it! Staring at Motoko!"  
   
 "Please don't be jealous of a shotgun, Crush."  
   
 "I'm not jealous of a shotgun! She just shouldn't be bringing it to the shows, distracting Motoko!"  
   
 "Yes, I'm sure Motoko is very distracted by the shotgun, she loves shotguns."  
   
 "That's the problem! Motoko ***remembers*** her! She remembers the shotgun!"  
   
 "It was a memorable shotgun."  
   
 "I should get them to ban weapons at the show."  
   
 "You're fuming, Crush."  
   
 "I'm not fuming! I'm seething! There's a difference!"  
   
 "Uh huh."  
   
 Rebecca cackled.
