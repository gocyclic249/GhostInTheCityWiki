Ichi: "Motoko, I was looking for the broom earlier and couldn't find it, but what's this?"

_*Ichi gestures to a door that has been partially opened. Grenades spilling out from it into the hallway of the SLS basement.*_

 Motoko: "Oh, that's just my grenade storage room. I build a lot of them as practice and I can't use enough of them to get rid of the surplus. Nobody wants to buy them either."

 Ichi: "Motoko, doesn't this seem a little risky? You know, to have in the basement of the building you work out of?"

 Motoko: "God Ichi, it's perfectly safe until you introduce an electric current. Otherwise I wouldn't keep them there."

 Ichi: "...But there was an outlet in that room for one of the old cleaner bots right?"

 Motoko: "It would take a large and frankly wild power surge for any current to spark outside of an outlet."

 Ichi: "Okay, well what if one of the dumb kids upstairs forgets to turn their safety on and fires straight down?"

 Motoko: "I just told you Ichi, grenades don't work like that. The kid is just going to be missing his toes."

 Ichi: "Why do you keep assembling so many grenades if you aren't going to use them Motoko!"

 Motoko: "You don't understand Ichi! _The resin must flow!_"

 Ichi: "The hell does that even mean?"

 Motoko: "It means don't come crying to me the next time you need another custom cup holder for your center console!"

 Ichi: "But-"

 Motoko: "Out! And take another crate of grenades for your van! I know you've been skimping!"
