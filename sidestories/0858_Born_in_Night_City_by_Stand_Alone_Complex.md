I had an idea for a song Motoko could put together inspired by a real one. Do a Night City rendition of Born in the USA by Bruce Springsteen. So many people don't get the satirical protest the Boss intended it to be because they only know the chorus, and it doesn't take much of a twist to make it suit Night City. Here's my twiston the song:
