Omake: The File and The Clause

 -- A few moments after Motoko gets off a call with Hiromi --

 Motoko: "Listen... Alex. I have two questions for you that I feel are rather important."

 Alex: "Helping you out is what I'm here for! Ask away!"

 Motoko: "My first question is if you can tell me the top... Lets say 10 most important facts of my file that I'm sure MSM has on me. Before or after I joined, doesn't matter."

 Alex: "Well I have noted down here that you prefer real food, you prefer next-gen cyberwear, you..."

 --One list of preferences later--

 Motoko: "Ok, well to start off with, I'm going to request that you fire whoever wrote my file as they are ether incompetent or are attempting to sabotage you. I'd sugjuest contacting Wakako and asking for _her_ file on me. Especially as I'm pretty sure any file she gives is going to contain some relevant info that file is missing. S-"

 Alex: "Why don't you just tell me what we messed up with and I'll get your file fixed right now!"

 Motoko: "Because I'm already rather annoyed with you and want this conversation over as fast as possible. Now second, I'm interested if you actually read _my contract_."

 Alex: "I am familiar with the standard MSM talent contracts, yes."

 Motoko: "Ahh, so that would be a no since my contract has a modified clause."

 Alex: "And what would that be?"

 Motoko: "If I have to tell you, then I'm going to use it. So you should really just read it yourself."

* * *

 Omake note: The clause was something Hiromi had put into the contract stating that any death, dismemberment, trauma, or lost production time due to MSM employees' antagonizing, needling, or otherwise crossing Motoko's boundaries is fully the fault of MSM. She fully knows that while Motoko will take a lot, she is also _**fully**_ capable of going Cyber Psyco. She's seen the BD and she did _not_ want to be blamed for any... _incidents_.
