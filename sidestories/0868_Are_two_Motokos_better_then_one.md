**Are two Motokos better then one?**(...maybe?)

It's the first thing that popped into my head. I need professional help don't I?

 =========

 The electronic chirp of a doorbell rang through the apartment interrupting the carefull calibration and conservation work Motoko was doing to her latest loadout. Her fingers freezing in place, the delicate gun part she was carefully manipulating inches from slotting into it's proper place.

_"...grumble..."_ She slowly lowered the piece of tech on the weapons bench and looked at the door with some annoyance.

 "Who the hell is it now?" she grumped to herself as she rose to open the door. "Too soon for Jun, Hiromi has classes and the Guys are doing their gigs they think I don't know about." She pinged the security of course - she wasn't that big of an Idiot. 

 In the back of her mind she swore she mentally heard Hiromi shouting at her to be carefull and not take chances. 

 Nothing. There was no one on the other side. Everything was clean.

 Motoko immediately stopped in place and pulled the Burya aiming it at the door eyes sharpening.

 The door chirp repeated.

 Slowly and carefully she silently ghosted to a new position with some cover while mentally preparing herself for trouble. Whoever this was, he or she was an Idiot to target her in the apartment she modified for secutity.

 She gave the signal to open the door and...

 ...empty space greeted her.

 Without a nanosecond of hesitation she immediately knew what she should do. Using her top Sandy assisted speed and Fast Drawing the Burya she twired behind herself while aiming...

 ... her vison narrowed at the hand holding an identical Burya. It was aimed at her head. Her own monster of a gun was raised just slightly above chest height at the stranger holding the gun. A million thoughts flashed in her head, the main one being - _stupid Motoko._

 She should be dead. A full second passed and no flash of weapon fire. She took another stupid risk - instead of pulling the trigger like a normal person she flashed a look at the face of the mysterious assassin...and froze.

 Her own face greeted her. Much less Chrome, ganic hands and other smaller differences but otherwise..._and the eyes._ Somehow she knew without even knowing. She lowered her gun and holstered it without letting her eyes leave the strangers own.

 The second Motoko put the weapon away a half second later.

 They looked at each other in silence for a full minute. Microexpressions and blinks was the language of choice.

 Then Motoko opened her mouth and spoke. "...Fuck em ROBs am I right?"

 "Yep." The second Motoko answered with a slightest of tremors in her voice. It was noticed.

 Motoko's posture relaxed and without hesitation she engulfed herself? in a hug. A moment later it was reciprocated.

 "...how long?" was the first question of many as the two Motokos started exchanging facts about their current circumstances. The exchange took a good while as each of them took turns asking questions about the most important stuff first. 

 "Was everyone ok?" "_Wait...who is your father?"_ "How is Jun, Hiromi and the rest of the guys?" "_But seriously wait...who is your father?"_

 "And what about Vic..."_"Motoko Arasaka! What in the shit?!"_

 It took them a while.

 As they were sitting on the couch they both finally looked at each other with identical gremlin grins as Motoko asked the obvious question.

 "Of course you know what we have to do now right?" her eyes sparkled with mischief.

 The answer came immediately. "Of course I do! I'm basically you."

 They both grinned wider.

*****a few hours later*****

Opening the door Jun wanted nothing more than to eat a quick Burrito - in viewing range of his gremlin of a sister - and watch terrible Night City televison while relaxing on a comfy couch.

 He froze on his way from the fridge, XXL Burrito in hand.

 On the main couch sat his sister. That was normal. Beside his sister sat her nearly identical twin, sans most of the chrome. _That was not normal._ Both of them were looking at him with the same creepy expressions some cult members had when they tried to induct another sucker into their cult.

 When they spoke their voices were identical. "Hello brother Jun." Both of them blinked at him at the same time. Their eyes were intently boring into him. Then they slowly lowered to his burrito. Both of them frowned in an identical way and spoke. "The Burrito is unhealthy for you brother Jun, you should eat something better." The synchronization was pich perfect.

 Jun was silently freaking out. Slowly and carefully he returned the Burrito to the fridge, his eyes never leaving The Motokos as he made his way back to the door.

 The door opened and without even saying a word Jun left the apartment.

 A few seconds after they heard the sounds of running the girls erupted into laughter.

*****Later in the day*****

 Heheheheee. Hiromi was a happy girl as she wandered the hallway to her Motoko's place. Finally, now that the boring classes are over she can enjoy some quality time with her ~~cr~~_good friend._ Even Martinez wasn't too much of a gonk today. Yes life was good she thought to herself as the door opened for her and she entered. She suddenly stopped a foot from the door.

 There were two Motokos relaxing on the couch looking at her with identical smiles on their faces.

 Hiromi's brain gave up the ghost and fizzled. She came up with the only logical conclusion that made any sense to her.

 "...oh, It's one of those dreams again." her voice muttered dreamily. "Good! Those are my favourite ones."

 She slowly begun to take her clothes off as both Motokos blinked at each other in confusion. Then at Hiromi again. And at each other.

 "...what?"

*****Nearly evening time*****

 Malcolm was a little tired from the busy day of doing small gigs with Ichigo but finally could do a proper chillout with his chooms, eat snacks and enjoy the chaos that followed in Motoko's footsteps. He was still a little out of it as he stepped through the door to the Monster Sibling's place.

 His eyes looked at the people gathered and blinked once.

 Then he started walking again giving a single nod each to both Motokos and a very red faced Hiromi occupying the seating area.

 "Motoko. Motoko. Hiromi." He greeted them on the way to the fridge to grab some snacks and after rummaging for a while he grabbed a bunch tossing them at the central table and quickly sitting himself at one of the empty places between the girls. He took a remote and turned on the TV while already munching on something.

 After a while he looked at them and calmly asked. "So, what's new?"

 Both identical girls looked at each other. Even Hiromi stopped impersonating a tomato for a bit. Then they smiled at each other over Malcolm's head.

 "Malcolm is just such a awesome choom isn't he?" asked NC Motoko.

 "Yep. You said it." answered Arasaka Motoko nodding seriously.
