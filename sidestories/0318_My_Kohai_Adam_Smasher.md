 Adam Smasher was not a complicated man. He was good at killing, and that was kind of it. His body was steel, and fire was his blood. The borged out mercenary didn't really do things like 'having long term goals' or 'thinking too hard.' Decades of violence, the creation of a living legend, and at the end of it all.. it didn't really matter.

 Adam was just going through the motions. He always had been. Oh, sure, occasionally someone would pop up that would -- momentarily -- catch his interest.

 And then he'd kill them.

 It wasn't always like this, though. There was a time, long ago, when Adam appreciated other things. Creative things. Art, and music, and consumable media. Hell, his _name_ wasn't even his real name, it was a play on some old school comic book character.

 Nobody ever got the reference. Hell, most folks old enough to just weren't alive anymore. His boss's dad was one of the few left, and that prick wasn't the kind to enjoy _anything_ from the good ole' US of A.

 "Adam," his boss called. "I've got a job for you. A potential hire. It will require.. some investigation, though." His boss passed him a nondescript shard. "This BD is being distributed all across Night City, and, somehow seems entirely authentic. Watch it. Find me some clues. If we can track them down we might have an excuse to head to Night City sooner, rather than later."

 Adam simply accepted the shard and stomped to the corner to relax, before slotting it in.

 --

 Several hours later, after many, many repeated viewings, Adam returned to the land of the living. His boss, noticing the giant mechanoman stirring, raised an eyebrow. "Well? What do you have for me?"

 "It.. was beautiful," muttered Smasher.

 "What."

 "If I still had eyes, boss, I would be crying. I'm one of the greatest killers on the planet," stated Smasher. "I have ended almost all the rest. Not one of them, ever, was an artist. There was no love in their craft. It's always.. perfunctory. Until now."

 Yorinobu Arasaka blinked at that. Since when did Adam Smasher know a hundred eddie word like that?

 "I will find them," Smasher added. "And I will ask them to teach me."
