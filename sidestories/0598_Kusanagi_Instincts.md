**_Kusanagi Instincts_**​

 "Who the _fuck are they!?!?"_

 Jun let out through gritted teeth. Either ignoring or unaware of the metal cup that was now warped beyond recognition in his hands and still spilling its contents all over his hands.

 "Well Jun-Kun~, they're hopefuls to your teeny, tiny, pretty, pretty Imouto's be-"

 "Stop that!" Rita snapped, far too late as Akari's words processed in Jun's mind.

_"What the **fuck is happening?!"**

 "_ Jun-" Rita tried again only to stop as the Tyger Claw's Oni mechanically turned his head to her, like an ancient clockwork piece, as his hair burned a furious red cluing anyone in to his mood if he murderous expression and spinning flame themed optic Irises didn't do so, that is.

 She would have to be really careful and diplomatic with this...so that meant she was fucked in a horrible way. Why couldn't there be another Mox girl nearby that had more experience with things like this? Then again how many had border straddling potential cyberpsychos in this situation?

 "It's normal for a growing - er - young woman to attract, tho-, _attention._ I mean _attention_-"

 "**What?!?!** Since _**when did that apply to Motoko!!!??"**_

 She wasn't sure if his gripping his hair so hard was a good sign or not. It kinda showed restraint?

 The Mox's Borg quickly slapped a hand over the other Tyger Claw Borg's mouth, even if she was already way too close to her peace of mind. Akari was leaning her chin amicably over her shoulder, for some reason, as she lounged sideways behind her and Jun on the spine of the couch they were sharing.

 It was supremely hard keeping still even as she got another pair of wild eyes focused on her from her opposite side, ones that were also unfortunately closer than the other pair. The crazed grin she could feel in her palm and the blade of the Claw's foot that appeared below her neck wasn't helping her by any means but she was already in this mess. Might as well keep helping Motoko.

**_"Since when did that happen!?!? It's too soon to anyone be looking at her like that!!"_**

 Rita cringed internally as she gestured Jun down and to her astonishment, as well as very, _very,_ appreciative relief he seemed to listen to her even if he still didn't let go of his hair.

 She moved her hands to gently pry Jun's hands away from his Tech Hair-

 She got distracted.

 Akari did not.

 "Ara~, Araa~~!" Her voice was a honeyed song filled with the undertones of way too many things together, "How bold! Trying to go for the grope!"

 Jun froze, all of the earlier signs of his feelings going along with him.

 Rita despaired, her body freezing as well.

 And Akari giggled gleefully as she grasped the immobile Mox, reaching beneath her arms in a hold that she turned into a pseudo hug as she tightly grasped her. Making sure to return her chin to its earlier position in a mockery of affection.

 Naturally, Jun chose that moment to move. His hand raking his hair back slowly, gaining a pulsing color the farther along it went and his tattoos, irises and accessory lights syncing with the display.
