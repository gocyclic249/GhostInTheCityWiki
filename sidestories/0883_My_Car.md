 Reading through old posts and found this and had to write something.

 Adam Smasher had been enjoying the fight, but now that the Meat was trying to escape it was time to put an end to it. 

 He could see the Meat's car and decided to do a superhero landing on it right in front of them. The look of terror on their faces when he crushed their escape were always great.

 Next thing he knew he was flying through the air instead of smashing into the car. Looking around he saw a massive explosion... and that his legs were gone. He hit the ground hard, his arms were barley responding. A quick check and yep, everything was fucked.

 "My car."

 Adam Smasher looked and saw the Meat he had been chasing.

 "You destroyed My CAR!" she yelled as she turned to him with a look of fury.

 Honest question, what would happen if Adam Smasher landed on her car and set all of her explosives off?
