Montage Time!​

 Ever since Motoko trained a bit with Vik and Jackie she was aching for more. She got so much experience just listening to Vik about how to properly fight where before she had to spend hours randomly punching at the gym!

 To say nothing about actually sparring with him!

 Vik was a former heavyweight boxer who finished second in the Watson Boxing Grand Prix. He could have totally gone pro and continued career, but decided on quieter life after that. Still, Vik still loved boxing since he constantly watched it, commented on it, and even gave lessons to people he liked, which Motoko was proud to count herself among said list. Which is why it didn't take make to convince him to train him.

 Sure, everyone used guns, bombs, swords, cyberware weapons, hacks, and other crazy tools to kill each other in Night City every day, but knowing how to throw down with your fists was just good sense after all.

 That and Motoko might have alluded to the idea of being professional local fighter if nothing else, and that seemed to excite Vik a bit.

 Besides, every up and coming rookie boxer needed a veteran coach. It was tradition!

 Which is why as the weeks of training went out Motoko made sure to follow all kinds of traditions...

 ---

 Vik stood in the corner of the freezer as his student trained in a rather... interesting manner, namely beating a large hanging piece of synth-meat studded with random pieces of metal.

 Her argument being she needed to know how to punch ganic and non-ganic at some time, which made sense given you could never be sure how chromed some folk were and had to adjust how you fought sometimes.

 Though her insistence to wear sweatshirt, sweatpants, and a headban all while humming a [song](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=_YYmfM2TfUA) that made him feel older than usual was odd. But it seemed to pump her up so didn't go against it.

 Her form still needed work though.

 "Keep at it! Don't stick in one place! Move around!" he told her while helping adjust her hits.

 ---

 "We could have gotten a treadmill for this you know?!" Vik yelled as he biked slowly behind Motoko as she ran through Night City's streets at a fast and even pace.

 At least she knew the important of training her endurance, but this wasn't a work out for him!

 She just ignored him as she sung out another song out loud while having her music box thing, which she was carrying on her back, gave her lyric musical accompaniment.

 "[Live to win](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WKyZyBe3DbE), 'till you die, 'till the light dies in your eyes!"

 He really hoped some asshole NCPD cop didn't pull them over for disturbing the peace or anything, but it seemed like people randomly cheering Motoko on as she ran were having fun with things at least.

 ---

 Vik watched as the sparring bot he rented and programmed with some of the best training programs and recordings of past champions decked it out with Motoko, whom was doing a good job clashing against it over time even though it was tireless and more skilled than her overall.

 Still, it was a robot running off a program so was predictable and nothing like a real fight, but it taught her to see patterns in her opponents, how to handle tough and strong borgs, and she found the idea of boxing a robot too good to pass up.

 And all the while she was singing again.

 "[Never Surrender!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QxD5Tc2PmIE) Never Say Die! You Got The Heart Of A Hero!"

 Vik had to admit, she certainly chose good ones that fit the theme of their training sessions if nothing else.

 ---

 "[You're the best! Around](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=E-WHW-QNswE)! Nothing's ever gonna keep you down!" Motoko continued to sing even as she pumped iron with Jackie as her spotter, the young man egging the girl on and evening singing along at some points.

 All the while Vik couldn't help nodding his head to the beat.

 ---

 "[Rising up to the challenge of our rival!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=btPJPFnesV4)" Motoko sang out enthusiastically as she very quickly went at Vik's old speed bag until the thing was a blur.

 Misty and Jackie were cheering her on to go faster and faster.

 Vik was mouthing the words as he pointed the radar gun at Motoko, curious to know just how fast she was going.

 ---

 "[You got the Touch!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A52--FKUQgU)" Motoko said as she landed a knock out blow on Jackie that had the poor boy fall back and hit the ground like a ton of bricks.

 "Oh... you got the touch alright," Jackie moaned on the floor as Misty saw to him while Motoko just kept singing with her arms raised the air in victory.

 Vik was starting to think these songs were dangerous at this point considering Motoko's progress so far. Hopefully she didn't know much more.

 ---

 She had more, though Vik was starting to think she was running out of them.

 "[You need a montage!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vK4gv11PTI8) Even Rocky had a montage!" as she ran up and down the staircase of the building while Vik stood nearby.

 "I'm starting to think you should take a break, Motoko," Vik admitted, starting to worry about his student's sanity at this point even as her skills improved.

 ---

 "Yeah, its getting bad," Jun admitted as he and Vik stood in the former's apartment and they watched as Motoko went wild cooking in the kitchen... while singing.

 "[It's a piece of cake to bake a pretty cake!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zFfqE5ob888)" sang Motoko as she was indeed baking... _lots_ of cakes.

 "I think I broke your sister," Vik said.

 "Naw, her going nuts over an obsession is pretty normal... but I do need to sleep sometimes," Jun pointed pointed as he chugged an energy drink to stay conscious.

 Vik could only sigh as he brought out his medical bag. "I got the sedatives."

 "I will not be silenced!" Motoko finally stopped singing as she fought off her brother and doctor to make her finally shut up and sleep, using the same fighting methods Vik had taught her to do it too!

 Well, at least he was a good teacher.
