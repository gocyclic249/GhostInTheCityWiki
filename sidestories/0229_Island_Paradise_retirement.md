You know all this talk about GITS SAC reminds me why it's my favorite incarnation of GITS, it doesn't end with the Major suddenly deciding to merge her consciousness to some gestalt entity and 'ascend' to the net. And even that got iffy with Solid State Society. And don't get me started on 2045 and its bizzaro world ending that makes absolutely no sense if you think about it.

 All of which is to say. If this story ends with Motoko merging her mind to some AI beyond the blackwall, well it would be on brand, but uh she doesn't need to cosplay _that_ hard I hope.

 Maybe she could do the one thing absolutely no merc in Night City has ever accomplished. Retiring to some island in the tropics and enjoying life.
