I mean the last time Motoko had an actual challenge it took totally not Neo throwing her out of a building. Special forces this guy might be, but he certainly isn't the one.

 Motoko is about to get some big names interested in her. One can only imagine the horror a data analyst would experience trying to put together a dossier on her.

* * *

 "So what's the story?"

 "Okay so there's this teenage street kid, wannabe mercenary."

 "Heard it all before."

 "Okay, but then, get this she's also a netrunner."

 "Unusual, but everyone has hobbies."

 "No like an actually _good_ netrunner."

 "Still just sounds like a kid whose just gotten lucky to me."

 "She's also a crack sniper."

 "I've got like twelve snipers, it's not _that rare_ of a talent."

 "Stealth specialist! Can't forget that, we know of some jobs she's supposedly taken but never been seen on."

 "So she has more patience then the average gonk?"

 "Keeps a rocket launcher under her bed."

 "You don't do that?"

 "Made it into the afterlife."

 "...Do they just name a soda after her if she dies too young to drink?"

 "She records all of her gigs and sells them in school parking lots."

 "So she's a real go getter then? Honestly sounds like something we could be doing."

 "Oh yeah, a real go getter, had her arms ripped off in a gang war. Then earned the respect of the gang who ripped them off, _by killing them._"

 "What gang was this again?"

 "Maelstrom."

 "That'd explain it."

 "Makes a habit of raiding and then looting anything not nailed down in the place she raids."

 "...That just makes good financial sense."

 "Flaked out on an opportunity to be a street racer."

 "Didn't we all?"

 "Blew up a boat with a rocket launcher."

 "Sounds like a fun Tuesday to me."

 "Killed twenty men while armed only with a sword."

 "...Do you think we could have her give lessons?"

 "...No, Okay get this she's also, -also a musician. Even has some of her songs on the top downloaded charts, and they aren't even half bad."

 "Alright well now that's just weird."
