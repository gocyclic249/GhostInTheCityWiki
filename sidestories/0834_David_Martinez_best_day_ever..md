David Martinez best day ever.

 David decided this was the day. He squatting in an alley outside of a restaurant. No more looking at Ghost's BD's for the hundredth time. Worst was when his mother found him. After using his BD wreath all day after school, drool coming from his mouth and clothes soaked in sweat. He had gotten such an earful from his mother. He thought she'd take his BD wreath from him! Luckily, he needed it for school. She wouldn't have been so merciful otherwise.

 He looked at a store window's reflection in front of him. Hair good, jacket good. Oh yes. He was on fffire today! The only thing they fear is you! He'd be sure to meet Ghost later. And he was her greatest fan! He knew! Because he always got her BDs first! Well, first selling them. Which was the most important part!

 He adjusted his hair again. 'Focus on the mission David. You got this!' he told himself while pointing at the mirror. Then he did finger guns, pew, pew.

 "heh."

 He knew Hiromi, his bossy "boss", would meet Ghost for lunch. She would always grin like a moron and be over the moon when she'd meet her after school. Like clockwork. And he noticed her grinning like that when leaving for lunch. This was one of those days. He knew it. She would meet her for lunch!

 So he did what the Ghost would do! Sneaking sneakily after Hiromi, he would time it perfectly so they would meet! Then he'd ask for him to join Ghost's crew! It was a gonk proof plan!

 He had gotten A's on strategic planning and operations. He knew his stuff.. Although he should have gotten someone else to do the sneaking. Sadly, he was Solo today. Just like a merc. Which in turn was actually good; he was no gonk. Operation sneak like how Ghost sneaked up to that gonk who tried stealing her car commence! No need for contractors!

 As he was squatting in an alley, eating a burrito. Operators needed their lunch as well. Considering how he had gotten here. He always just followed what others have told him. His mother, his teachers and everyone else. But Edgerunners.. That was something he wanted to do himself. He'd be a badass who would flatline people who got in his way. So cool.

 Becoming a corpo never sat well with him. The school uniform that looked like a suit. Never fit him, he looked so gonk with it. That's why he always made it look sloppy. It unironically looked better on him that way. Not that it made him fit in with the rest..

 After he finished his burrito, he took a quick peek through the window of the restaurant they had entered. And there was the Ghost! She was so cool! She had this cool military look about her. While she was cute and cool around Hiromi. She was a killing machine if she wanted to be. So Nova..

 He shook his head to clear his thought. He had to focus! Now was the time to strike. Time to casually walk into the restaurant. Play to cool, like he accidentally entered when they finished eating.

 He then entered the restaurant, squealing like a schoolgirl inside all the while.
