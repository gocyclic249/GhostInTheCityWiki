I read this completely differently. See Jun needed to pay off his character point deficit from all the cyberwear he got installed and he apparently was out of girlfriends to throw on the tragic backstory pile so he used the fact Jun's idiot player lead the borgs to his house as someone was following him. His sister was suppose to last just long enough for Jun to see her die ingloriously in the street outside Jun's appartment. This would have started a awesome fight that got fully a third the party killed off on average and would let them have a grand war story.

 The problem is Motoko did not agree to this. I mean she is just some player's morality pet dependent in the TTRPG they are playing so why would she get a vote? That is crazy talk. Also Jun was to busy grinding to notice his sister was also grinding and not being a proper, expendable, moe blob. I mean he didn't even notice she managed to shrug off that bedridden thing is less than a week. Jun was suppose to have to care for her and gain some humanity off her. She NOPED those plans by training off the negative body score like freaking Rock Lee... because she has exactly the same opinion of forced bed rest.

 This let Jun start off the Borg invasion as a combatant and nerfed the damage from it while derailing his character growth(or at least his humanity score farming). So he got an expendable girlfriend for the arc. Who he already killed off for character points. So Motoko ends up his expendable dependent in his back pocket, even if she already vetoed being ill girl. So later in the giant Pacifica debacle kicks off due to some crazy background rolls stacking the plot arcs. 

 This let the Game Master punish Jun for making perception his dump stat. So he sticks a Maelstrom tail on Jun and he leads it straight to his apartment and his 'moe blob' dependent. He leaves like five minute later and the tail calls in the borg ill team to set off the kidnapping arc to try an bind Jun to his derailed character arc.

 Mr. 'Perception is My One True Dumpstat' entirely missed what she was actually doing to make them mess, He missed the amount of guns she had. He entirely missed that she was pointing a higher quality than normal antiborg shotgun at him, when he burst in or what was making the mess he wanted her to clean up. Then he just left leaving her annoyed.

 So they send in the borgs, only Motoko is already outside the building and that derails the timer where all these NPCs manage to get killed off by borgs as they search for Oni's apartment with his dependent inside. This rather thoroughly throws off their time table, so Kamikazi only shows up twenty minutes after they already left. So they have to search for clues and eventually they stumble into her hands and they go to track down the borgs.

 Cue Kamikazi having to actually do tracking checks and use skills no one leveled. The Game Master attempts to throw them a bone for all the giggles this is giving him and then Motoko wakes up like five hours ahead of schedule and agrees to mortal combat. The Game Master stalls the borg long enough so they can actually find the place. They get there just in time for her to be throw in the pit, only she manages to off the borg with no arms and a pocket knife. This kind of baffles and entertains the characters so they get Jun to agree to wait until she screams as they throw her to the ripper as its worth even more humanity points. He agrees.

 Only she glares at the ripper instead of screaming and buzz kills the whole event for the borgs. She's no fun anymore. Realizing the Moe blob isn't gong to scream they hold Jun to his word and use the non-screaming time to spend hours doing prepared action checks. This goes on for hours as the ripper starts taking Motoko not reacting to his vivisection personally and tries every check the ripper can to get the scream. This lets the preparation checks go on for way, way to long and the borgs are going to lose horribly and ignominiously. Two hours later the arms are installed in the literally worse intentional botch job in history.

 Motoko soaks that without screaming. The ripper is now actually gaining humanity points just so it can feel adequately freaked out by her. The ripper actually stops as its Cool stat has taken enough damage its failing checks to avoid being creeped out and is rather shaken. The ripper failed a roll to continue hard enough the torture is finished so the cyberpsycho ripper can get away from the creepy little devil girl. That was suppose to be a mocking title not a challenge.

 So the borgs are reminded Motoko the unscreaming exists and toss her back into the pit with enough negatives on her rolls she has to crit to remain standing. GM is in full morbid fascination mode. Kamikazi is running out of ideas to prepare without selling off their gear for the insane prep bonus. Jun? Jun has fretted so much he cascade critted himself 7 points of humanity.

 At this point Motoko starts yelling at the crowd about how her left elbow smells like the sound of psychedelically backed tartan with stripes the color of a 1973 Volkswagen beetle winning at Jeopardy on a question about a crystalline pine trees tax returns and its effect on global mash potato asphalt. THe audience is arguing its was a 1963 model and one of the Kamikazi declares that counts as screaming.

 So the borgs die to about twelve times the prep bonus needed to stealth kill them all and Jun tries to farm more humanity off this... at which point his sister headbutts him so he can stop the badly dressed elder horror from trying to sculpt her brain meats into a cactus doing the Russian hat dance in Braille for vampire goats.

 Meanwhile the Kamikazi sees all these good guns hanging out on this random table and start dividing that sweet loot.

 Cue the next arc, Motoko getting her guns back from Jun's stupid party members.
