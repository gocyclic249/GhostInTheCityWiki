 Waving away Fujimura Sama's offered hand, Jun got onto his feet and took the offered knife. Stumbling to the corpse of the Borg, he thrust it in give it a slight tug forcing the corpse to shift and spill out a trio of Cyberware parts that were strangely unblemished and intact despite the intensive combat that slew the Borg.

 A quick look at his loot revealed that he luckily managed to snagged the Sandevistan the Borg was using, Subdermal Armor and reinforced Gorilla Arms. 

 " I claim the Sandevistan. The Kamikaze can have the other two parts, i like to trade them for the upgrade for my Katana."

 " Very well Jun. You know the procedures. Drop by to see Mizoko when you're free with the Sandy and she'll reboot and install it for you.

 Now go back home with your sister. The Kamikaze once again thank you for providing us with the Kamuy's Blessing."

 ==============

 "Irasshaishimasu Jun! I heard from Fujimaru Sama. Here to install the Sandevistan?"

 " Yes Mizoko San. I would also like my Subdermal Armor to be enchanced. I got some new parts off some Street Trash that tried to jump me on the way here."

 " Sure thing Jun kun! Lemme just take a look at the parts....Yep, just like always. A perfect match to your current Chrome. It still amaze me how all the Chrome parts you get from the Kamuy's Blessings can work seamlessly with your current parts."

 " Who's to question the Kamuy? Still with the war dying down i forsee a drop in 5 * requests from the Kamikaze Mission Hub, i might need to start working on Edgerunner Jobs to get more 5* Requests. i'm very close to getting to 6* quests"

 "Oh I'm actually pretty curious about this Jun kun. How exactly does this "star" system work?"

 " When i look at missions or gigs, there's a numerical star indicator on them that indicates how many stars they are. As long as the Stars on the mission don't exceed the Stars on my Hunter Card, i'm usually able to complete the Mission."

 " Hunter Card?"

 " Yeah. i have a capture of the Card when i first got it after the Maelstrom raid of the Casino i was working at."

![Image 19](https://www.monsterhunter.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2021/04/introcard_img_rise14.jpg)​

 "Oh wow, that's pretty interesting! And it changed after you killed your first Borg and got your Gorilla Arms? I heard the stories from Fujiwara Sama!"

 " It did. It's pretty embarrassing now but the entire reason i found out how the Kamuy's Blessing worked was because i was so overcome with rage that i was slashing the Borgs body with my blade when his chrome just popped off his corpse."

 " Well, your Blessing did help in cutting down the time and resources needed to establish Kamikaze...So Good Job! Now you should head to Sensei, he has something for you."

 ==============

 Entering Sensei's Dojo, Jun made his way to a locked room and opened it with a flash of his Hunter Card. Entering the room, Jun found himself surrounded with racks after racks containing Nanomachines of various hues and colors.

 It had been an extremely weird experience for Jun to figure out that some of the scraps he got from the Borgs he hunted could be turn into Nanomachines that were utterly different from what was available on the market. Said Nanomachines were inert on their own, but when combined in different portions could turn into Ammo, Cyberware InstantFix Gels and even Toxins that could be applied on blades.

 The whole thing had been so out of his comfort-zone that he had just left the whole thing to Sensei to explore, who had in-turn churn out Rounds, Toxin-coated Throwing knives and even Aerosol Grenades that the Kamikaze eagerly pounced on to use.

 " Sensei? Mizoho san said that you're were looking for me? "

 " Yes Baka Deshii. I heard from Fujiwara that you and your sister got into a fight with an enemy Borg without being armed? "

 " That's not really our fault Sensei! It was an ambush and we weren't the target."

 " FOOL! That's no excuse! At least your Sister was carrying around some weaponry! What about you, you useless lump!"

 Rolling his eyes at Sensei's Tsun behavior, Jun just waited Sensei's rant out. Anyytime now Sensei would go into Dere Mode, he just needed to wait out the Tsun.

 " ......and i heard she was using plain blades! Plain Blades after all the weapons i crafted with the Kamuy's Blessing? HERE! Give these to your Sister and tell her to use these if she wants to put a Borg down with knives!"

 Looking at the packages handled to him, Jun saw that one was labelled "Paralysis Throwing Knives x5 " while the other one was labelled "Tranq Coating"

 " Sensei...Does Fujiwara Sama know about this? I thought they're being treated as important gear for the Clan's Armory?"

 " Baka Deshii! You think Fujiwara will care about such minor items when you're the one providing the ability to even build them? Go and give them to your Sister!"

 " Hai! Arigatou Sensei!"

 ==============

_Life,_ Jun mused. _had certainly become strange since he got the Kamuy's Blessing_. While originally it was something he prized thanks to how it allowed him to avenge Kasumi, now it had become something more important.

 It had become the only way he could keep up with Motoko's freakish growth. Her growth since awakening from the Coma was scarily fast and if he didn't keep up he might not be able to help if she landed herself in another mess.

 Just the thought of her getting into a Gig too much for her to handle and him not being strong enough to assist send shivers down his spine. He couldn't afford to lose her as well.

_I think i'll reach out to Wakako Sama and see what Gigs she has to offer. I can probably trade the Chrome i don't need from the Gigs to Fujiwara Sama for more specialized parts to upgrade my gear._
