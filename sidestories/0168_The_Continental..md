Payment on successful completion of the job, and if it's achieved to my satisfaction I will consider renewing this partnership beyond a one-off contract. Clearly things have gone well for you since the unfortunate passing of your former employer, young Ichinose. I expect news soon.

 Ichi disconnected the hardened comm attached to the secure landline system in the formerly abandoned Havenford Hotel. Once upon a time it had probably been quite the establishment - the luxury hotel having fallen into disuse when the rival across the street (a three-star bunkspace called The Advocet - the original building and not the derelict in Valentino territory) had pushed it out of business to become the only tourist trap inn for anyone wanting to stay in Downtown Upper Eastside Night City back in 2038.

 Now, with a cash injection from a certain gonk-brained Solo/Rockergirl/Netrunner he'd been - and still was - chooms with, his crew was set up in the remodelled place. Four star luxury with a dedicated Delamaine setup (not sure how Motoko pulled that off, unless it was the work of Hiromi pulling weight as their Corpo backer) as a front for what was a safehouse and mission control for just about any Op the Kusanagi siblings were working on. At least, when it wasn't being used as a venue for one of Motoko's band performances or for club nights.

 Getting up out of the Receptionist's chair at the front desk, Ichi turned while brushing lint off his expensive suit (and wasn't that a turn up for the books - him of all people dressing like a Corpo!) Good thing it acted as a hell of a solid armorjack when idiots decided they could try and mug him at gun and knife point - because it had saved his life a couple of times. Made him glad the little Ninja BD star had personally told him to wear it when on the job despite his reservations.

 At least, the good thing was it wasn't some stuffy Neo-Militaristic thing he'd never be caught dead wearing. He looked like a stylish and wealthy obayun or kumichō, and holographic fibers allowed him to display his Tyger Claw markings if he so wished. That and it nicely disguised his two hand cannons. Hidden iron was a great plan, and the built-in bio-monitor and tracking unit on the suit would make sure he'd be taken care of if anything did go horribly wrong.

 But now it was time for discretion. Off went the holo effects, and on came the sound dampeners. Not quite as quiet as Motoko pulling a Fuma Kotaro the 7 th​ - but more than enough to get the drop on people between him and his clients.

 "Omaeda, the call was legit, right? We're picking up Maine and his crew today?"

 "Looks like it. Me and the Spiders can handle the hotel, you and Malcolm can go with a couple of Mercs to do clean-up. If it comes to it, Hiromi's due back to stay at the Penthouse tonight and The Oni's downstairs in the Dojo. Just don't forget to loot the place or Motoko's going to pout again." Chirping electronic voices from the little blue 'bots giving their confirmations.

 "Geez, don't remind me. Time for us to kick ass. We're taking the 'truck.' The one Scorpion and V got us."

 "Ooh. Have fun and try to get me Sacha's or Lucy's agent number!"

 "Having dreams is Nova, man - but I doubt your chances." Omeada didn't respond verbally. His single-finger salute was response enough.

 Anyways. Time to get to work.
