 Motoko: "I made a few upgrades to my arms, Hiromi. Now I can get the remote from all the way across the room!"

 Motoko holds out her arm, palm out towards the remote and yells out, "GET OVER HERE!" as a long chrome tendril lashes out, snags the remote with three slim manipulator fingers, and retracts quickly until the remote is in Motoko's hand.

 Hiromi: "..."

 Motoko: "I managed to put three of them in each hand!"

 Motoko drops the remote and extends three tendrils from each hand and waves them around hypnotically. 

 Hiromi: "I'm an Asian girl and I don't know how to feel about this."

 Motoko: "Check it out, I'm spider girl!"

 Motoko launches a tendril into the ceiling and tries to swing from it, only for the cheap construction-grade ceiling to fail at holding her wight.

 Motoko from the floor: "I'm okay!"

 A loosened light fixture falls down onto Motoko's head. 

 Motoko: "Still okay."
