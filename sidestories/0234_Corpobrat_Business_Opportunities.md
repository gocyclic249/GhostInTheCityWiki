Corpobrat Business Opportunities​

 Nox felt a little uncomfortable after Motoko's friend, this Hiromi girl, dragged him off after they dropped her off at the ripperdoc clinic and took him up to Corpo Plaza to 'have a chat' at one of the more expensive looking restaurants. It was the kind of thing you saw in movies and BDs before some corpo hit squad came out of nowhere to waste those eating.

 Didn't help that Hiromi was a corpobrat apparently, and while Nox was willing to give her a chance since she was Mokoto's best choom and all, she still made him nervous.

 "First, let me properly introduce myself. I'm Hiromi Mitsunashi, Motoko's best choom and her minder since while she is the most badass person I know she doesn't always think things through," Hiromi started off the second they sat down and digitally made their orders.

 "Uhhh, I'm Nox the Mox," Nox said awkwardly. "Would you believe me if I said that Nox was my actual name before I joined up?" he attempted to joke.

 "Eh, I heard worse," Hiromi shrugged. "Sure you get teased enough by your fellows Moxes about it already."

 "Yeah, though it helps that Anna takes half of it," Nox chuckled. "Her last name is Nox," he explained to Hiromi following her raised brow.

 "You two must be a bundle of laughs when you get together," Hiromi chuckled.

 "I try not to show up at Lizzie's when she is on shift. Kinda annoys her," Nox admitted.

 "I can imagine, cause I'm a little annoyed with you too Nox," Hiromi said as she leaned forward, her elbows on the table and her hands folded in front of her.

 "Uhhh, sorry?" Nox apologized in confusion.

 "No worries, its an easy fix after all. I want a cut," Hiromi demanded.

 "A cut of what?" Nox said in honest confusion.

 "A cut of your BD business. How about half of your half?" Hiromi offered. "Motoko can keep her half since she does the hard part, but I refuse to not have a hand in her her merchandising rights, especially since you could be earning much higher profits than you are currently doing or could even do on your own."

 "Hey now! Those BDs are preem and-"

 "I'm not doubting the quality of the BDs, in fact they are easily comparable in quality to the ones made by high end studios that it makes me want to meet the editor at some point," Hiromi cut him off. "And we both know the content of them are just as good if not better. I don't even want to imagine just how intense the virtues are either. I'm doubting the distribution network since as far as I can tell its _just_ you selling them on street corners and other small circles."

 "I'm widening the net! Word of mouth is going around and people want to see more of Motoko kicking ass! To feel that edge she feels every time and see how real Edgerunners get it done without the overly flashy gore or other unpleasant emotions," Nox defended himself. "She's slowly becoming a real star and I want to see it rise higher since she deserves it."

 "On that we both agree," Hiromi said easily. "Still, your clientele are still small fries and you are making at most a couple hundred eddies on each sale, right?"

 Nox could only grimace at that, since she was right. People wanted their high end BDs as cheap as possible after all.

 "That's what I thought. So what if I could tell you I can get you access to more and higher paying customers?" Hiromi said with a predatory grin.

 "...How?" Nox asked as he leaned forward in morbid curiosity.

 "I go to Arasaka Academy with other corpo kids, those with parents from lower management all the way to high executives. And you know what each and every one of them wants? _Excitement_," Hiromi explained. "They lived their whole lives so far stuck in tight shirts, formal parties, and living with the hard gazes of their 'rents constantly staring them down to not make them look bad. I complain about them a bunch, but my own 'rents are pretty easy going all told compared to some of the martial law horror stories I've heard before. I'm actually thankful for the Silver package for Trauma Team after all, instead of 24/7 GPS tracking and total lockdown to only certain parts of city or just in my own home. That is why so many are _desperate_ for an escape or a new experience of some kind, and their fat allowances and poor sense of money value means they will pay through the nose to get it."

 Hiromi reached into her pocket and placed a shard onto the table between them, one Nox recognized as one of Motoko's BDs, namely the Maelstorm raid. Hiromi tapping her finger against it.

 "Can you imagine just how hooked we can make some of those kids to Motoko's experiences? What kinda BD on the market right now makes you feel more in control than hers and isn't some kind of torture porn crap? Who can get into the Academy or send the kids directly to you to get their fix of control and confidence that they rarely get themselves? Maybe even hook their parents who feel the same in their own damn job environment?" Hiromi questioned, tapping the BD shard each time. "We could clean house... assuming you are interested of course?"

 Nox could only stare at Hiromi as he imagined her in a black suit and a window to her back overlooking the whole city, and totally believing it could be a reality in the future.

 Why not make corpo connections now? Especially if Motoko was their bridge point.

 "You got a deal," Nox said as he stretched out his hand, which Hiromi smiled and grabbed.

 Then she pulled him forward to whisper into his ear. "You know she is way out of your league, right?"

 "Of course," Nox easily agreed before smiling. "Same about you too."

 "Maybe... but at least I got to her _the voice,"_ Hiromi bragged as they let each other go and sat back down, right as a server came by to give them their high class meal.

 "What voice?" Nox said with a great deal of interest.

 Hiromi just stuck her tongue out at him in smug satisfaction.
