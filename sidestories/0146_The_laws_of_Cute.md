 The dark alley is filled with numerous flavors of junk and debris, old cardboard boxes, old _metal_ boxes, even old boxes made of plastic. This alley has it all, Baby! Even has its very own mercenary.

 "Are you sure this is going to work? I feel like they'll just shoot me the moment I go out there." Motoko mutters, seemingly to no one, while peering out the alley's exit. Her eyes tint with mild distress as she takes in the sight of the joint Maelstrom, Raffen, and 'Meatmen' hitsquad, the Meatmen being a fairly recent addition to the 'people that want Motoko dead' party.

 They all seem to want her dead _so much_ in fact, that they have dedicated no less than eighty two street thugs, twenty seven full borgs, five armored cars, and a captured NCPD Minotaur, which someone had decided to weld a truly massive tangle of yellow cables to, right on its head, in a rather accurate imitation of an afro, clearly for reasons incomprehensible to man.

 "There's nothing to be afraid of-desu♡!-" Motoko's rifle chirps with an electronic voice. "-They're all _very_ uncute. The laws of Cute>un-cute dictate that you can never lose. Hmm-Hmm." Nekomata declares seriously, her voice somehow portraying a cutesy double head-nod while also revealing not a single drop of doubt.

 A small thoughtful hum rife with a biting chill emanates from under Motoko's arm. "В дерзости есть прибежище."

(In audacity there is a refuge.)

 "See? Even Burya-kun is telling you to be confident❣ Don't worry Jen-_Motoko-chan,_ with my new upgrades our Puru-Puru-Neko-Neko, and Brrr-Brrr Bang-Bang powers combined will dazzle them and no one will fight anymore♡! Go For The Happiness😻"

 Motoko runs a hand along the new additions, and notable deductions, from Nekomata's design. Gone was her stock, her grip, her trigger, and even her scope. The rifle hardly seemed like one with those iconic features missing. Much more likely now that someone would mistake her as an instrument at a glance, what with the six strings running along her barrel and the small keyboard built into the side of her receiver.

 The mercenary strums a simple chord on her cute rifle-guitar monstrosity and visibly squashes down her worry. "I don't know… This is my first time playing with an audience… What if I mess up?"

 The wintry electronic voice once more speaks up from her shoulder harness "Если все ваши враги мертвы, это провал?"(If all your enemies are dead, is it a failure?)

 "You're right Burya-kun❣ Even if not everyone likes your song, your loveu-ness will shine through to their hearts anyway♡! Now, say it with me-" Nekomata squitches, (dunno. I can't think of a word to describe her voice so I made one up. Huh, internet says 'squitch' means "old fashioned couch-grass"… Tha hell does THAT mean?) revealing that she had no idea what Burya _actually_ said.

 Motoko nods in agreement, seemingly with herself and her weapons, and begins to speak in unison with the guitar-gun.

 "Very-Very Loveu-Loveu-force, push into their souls with the power of love!"

 "Very-Very Loveu-Loveu-force, push into their souls with the power of love!"

 Motoko raises her arm in a circular arc and brings it down across the strings. Sparks fly from Nekomata's capacitors as the power chord roars down the alley, shaking dust from the rooftops above.

 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 "Where'th tha fuck hast bee that little Tee Cee Ninja female-dog!? Our band'th been waiting upon this street for h'wat feels akin to a lifetime!" A rather uniquely dressed 'Meatman' indubitably declares with one leg raised as if he is propping it up on something… but there's _nothing there_.

 A Maelstrom goon turns to look at the odd man. "Why are you talking like that? Just speak normally."

 "**I SHAN'T DO SUCH A THING, NO-NO-NO-NO**! Our public relations bureau has concluded that the Meatmen have'th the need for a strong and verbose recognizable image! I have come'th up with the'th idea of this accent to estable'th our Brand!"

 The Maelstrom goon obtains a flat expression. Then the entire hitsquad's attention is grabbed by a conspicuous power chord screaming out from a particularly dark alley nearby. As in, every other alley is brightly lit by neon lights and there is a single alley that is veiled in darkness.

 "Hey!" Shouts a Raffen-Ruffian. "That's probably the girl we're **GAAHH!**"

 "Why did you scream?" Asks another Raffen.

 "Sorry, I thought of something scary. As I was saying. That's probably the girl we're-"

Spoiler: But then, a song!

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ae_lCDuBAFQ&ab_channel=djM2fiesta)​

 A flash of color leaps from the darkened alley and sours across an eight-yard gap to the roof of a parked car nearby, slightly denting its hood in the process.

 "One, Two, one two three four!" The figure begins to dance and sing, a large head of hair with a frankly ridiculous amount of volume sways moments behind her movements, as if caught in a time dilation field that delays all action taken upon it. But not really, it just seems that way.

 The hitsquad are struck dumb by the audacious display, many of them nodding along to the brisk song.

 "Man, this chick can play!"

 "And Sing!"

 "I really relate to the lyrics!"

 "**IT SPEAKS TO MY'TH DISCONTENT WITH THE STATUS-QUO! WE MUST REBEL FROM'TH OUR SOCIAL CONSTRUCTS AND ASSEMBLE A NEW LOVEU UTOPIA-DESU!**"

 The oddly accented fellow immediately grabs a nearby folding chair and _utterly thwacks_ the man beside him. As if it was the sound of a warhorn, all chaos breaks hell… All breaks chaos loose… all hell chaos… wait… all hell breaks-_you get the idea_.

 Bullets and robotic fists fly, beating flesh and bone into pulp on the random street in Night City. Many daring residents lean out from windows to get a better listen to the music and violence on display, heedless of potential ricochets.

 And Motoko dances through it all, sometimes forced to dodge an enemy or two that had seen through the hypnotic haze of her song's influence. Periodically the guitar would flare with light and sound and "Subtly" send a humongous chunk of metal into an attacker.

 After only a minute of two of this madness, all combat comes to a screeching halt as a robotic roar reverberates through the air. "**THAT'S ENOUGH OF THIS SILLINESS!**"

 The golden-haired minotaur stomps towards Motoko, who continues playing regardless, and points a stray noodle of its large afro in her direction somehow. "**YOUR TRICKS HAVE TRICKED MY TRICKY BUNCH INTO BEING TRICKED BY YOUR TRICKY TRICKS!****AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAANNND**that was very rude…"

 Motoko blinks for a moment at the robot, not really sure what to do other than keep singing.

 The robot lurches forwards with a rattling 'CLANG' as a metal folding chair bounces off of the back of its head.

 "**NOW WHO DID THAT! I'LL HAVE YOU KNOW I HAVE A DEATHLY ALLERGY TO METAL FOLDING CHAIR! YOU'LL HAVE TO TAKE THIS UP WITH MY DOCTOR, DOCTOR NOSE HAIR!**" The robot's 'face' turns, revealing two small openings, although they are shuttered, with a small wooden sign hanging on the front that simply says "Gone fishing, sorry!"

 "**OH NOOOO! MY MOST POWERFUL ATTACK! I GUESS I'LL JUST HAVE TO USE MY OTHER MOST POWERFUL ATTACK!**Comere _koochie koochie koo!_"

 As the robot wades into the fight, waving its gun mounts around in a way that _somehow_ looks like a groping gesture, Motoko just gets more and more disturbed.

 "...Yeah, this is too weird for me." She mutters between lyrics and slowly starts shuffling backwards, hoping to remain out of anyone's notice.

 "No❣ Their loveu-ness is almost at PEAK!" Her rifle protests.

 "Yeah, no. I'm leaving."

 Motoko slips back into the only darkened alley.
