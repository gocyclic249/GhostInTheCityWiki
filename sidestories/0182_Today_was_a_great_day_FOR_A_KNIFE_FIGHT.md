 Motoko behold the carnage before her and smiled as it was good. Almost nonchalantly [she placed the Wakizashi held in her right hand into the crook of her elbow and gently tugged it through](https://media.tenor.com/r7vVvBUZ7CMAAAAC/ghost-of-tsushima-cleaning.gif) with a flourish she learnt from binging on Jun's _Chambara_ BDs.

 There had been a severe lack of Gigs from both Wakako and Regina recently, which meant the only way for her to maintain peak BRRR status was to do more _Creative Requisitions_. That being the purpose of her hunts meant that she had to stick to using the plethora of Blades that found themselves in her coat, car and pockets, afterall Cyberware was apparently worth more if they were sold without any holes through them.

 She had the whole thing down to an Art at this point. _Sitting Targets_ found their throats gently slit from behind as she moved like mist, _Standing Targets_ found their backs introduced to her Blade point-first while _Aware Targets_ found throwing knives hitting their hands and throat in a staccato beat before a blade was inserted into either eyes or heart depending on which Cyberware was more valuable. 

 Honestly, things were getting a mite too boring for Motoko, only the gentle BRRR from seeing her numbers inch incrementally up kept her going. The Eddies from the sale were just a number that she barely even used considering her blades were all that she used at the moment.

 "Hey Ichi, you can bring the Van over. I got em all."

 Deciding to take advantage of the time, Motoko moved to explore the Den and see if there was anything interesting she could Yoink. Sadly, room after room revealed none but the usual Scav detritus that were barely worth the effort of stuffing into Ichi's Van.

 That was until she got to THE ROOM. Motoko knew it was THE ROOM because it was radiating THE ROOM Vibes such as being placed in a pitch-black corridor with the only light above the door, having blood stains glimmer wetly on the floor as well as being a thick ass door that even Gorilla Arms would struggle to force open.

 Motoko looked at the Door that screamed BOSS FIGHT and looked back at her current loadout of Knife, Knife, Wakazashi, Throwing Knives. Clearly she should return to her car, pick up HMG Chan and burst through the door firing it while calling out in her best Italian accent "SAY HELLO TO MY LITTLE FRIEND." 

 Then, Motoko looked at her Blades EXP and the teeny tiny gap before the bar was full. There was clearly no doubt on which was the superior option. Stepping up to the door and pressing the OPEN button had it open with a menacing hiss revealing a strangely bare room populated by a single man facing the door with an arsenal of knives laid before him.

 "You are here Baba Yaga. My name is Olegz. The Bratva very unhappy with how your operations stop Cyberware supplies. I am here to fix problem. No you, No problem."

 Getting up smoothly from the ground, the man grabbed a Cleaver and a Stiletto from the armory before him and pointed it at Motoko.

 "I hear you good with Blades. I good with Blades also. Door to Room no open till only 1 heartbeat inside. Shall we dance Baba Yaga?"

 The hand darting to her coat and flinging a dagger that was easily parried was Motoko's reply as both parties broke into a sprint. The thrust at her heart from Olegz's stiletto found itself parried by the Ka-bar knife Motoko's left hand had quickdrawn from back while the slash from her Wakizashi to his throat glanced off the flat of the Cleaver blade before it.

 Almost simultaneously, both sides took a step back after the first clash. The parries proving without a doubt that both sides were experienced knife fighters who were ready to guard against the quick fatal blows that were the hallmarks of a Knife user. 

 Circling around each other, quick stabs and brief cuts were the probing blows used to get a sense of their opponent. Bait meant to open up each other's tight guard to land a blow that could decide the fight in an instant. Parries became quick darting affairs meant to knock a blade away while minimizing openings.

 Olegz Style was pure offensive. Heavy chops from the Cleaver accompanied by vicious stabs from the stiletto ensured that Motoko had to constantly make the right decision on how to block his attacks. The stiletto stabs could be easily parried away while the weight of the chopper meant that a direct block could shatter the blade in her hand

 Motoko Style however was more balanced. The Ka-bar knife in her left hand serving as a Main-Gauche while the Wakazahi in her right hand launched a plethora of cuts that flowed unpredictably with flicks of her wrist. The size of the Cleaver proved to be an advantage in guarding against said cuts, the flat of the blade serving as an excellent shield that meant Olegz only needed to get it within the target zone of what Motoko was cutting at.

 Back and fro the duo went, the exchanges getting faster and faster as they each gradually got familiar with the other's style. Little cuts and nicks started to appear on both parties, as fatigue and exhaustion start to slow them down. The clashing of steel against steel turned from a melodious harmony into a the rough shriek of steel scraping against each other as the blades start to get chipped.

 Then it happened. A mistimed block thanks to a feint on Olegz part had Motoko directly blocking his cleaver with her Ka-bar knife, the weight of the chopper concentrated on just one part of the already stressed steel meant that it shattered into glistening shards. A moment that came out of nowhere to both parties who had been lulled into a rhythm that now found itself broken.

 Before Olegz could recover from the sudden lack of resistance, Motoko moved. A quick downward thrust with the broken blade in her left hand pinned Olegz foot to the ground and had him screaming from both the sudden shock and pain. A inward facing cut from the Wakazashi in her right hand cleaved through Olegz left forearm causing both hand and Stiletto to fall to the ground with a thud.

 Quickly rolling away from Olegz desperate and fury driven cuts with the Cleaver in his remaining hand, Motoko took a moment to assess the situation. Olegz was now pinned to the ground via the dagger in his foot and the loss of his right hand meant that he was unable to tug the dagger out unless he dropped the cleaver in his hand.

 The best strategy in this situation would be to wait Olegz out and let him bleed to death, however Motoko did not have the time to do so. Going by her mental clock, she had maybe 5 - 6 minutes before reinforcements showed up, which meant she needed to take action. 

 A brief attempt at landing a lethal blow found itself parried by Olegz who was spitting out vicious curses in Russian while throwing knives found themselves either intercepted by the flat of the cleaver or caught with the stump of his arm, the sheer desperation of a cornered animal driving away the pain of doing so.

 As such, making full use of her mobility Motoko begun to circle Olegz. Forcing him to constantly turn and face her using the dagger in his foot as a pivot, an act that basically caused him to turn the veins and tendons into mincemeat. Motoko knew, no matter how tough he was it was impossible for him to keep doing that without pause.

 Then it happened. A slight flinch. A brief pause. Motoko seized that moment and darted forth like a diving swallow. The Wakaizashi held in both hands and doing a picture perfect _Hidari ichimonji Giri_ that sliced along Olegz wrist and shoulder causing the top portion of his arm to slide off as if she was filleting a fish. 

 Recovering from the cut, Motoko looked at the Bleeding Meat before her and ended his suffering with a decapitation strike that sent his head flying before[doing a brief chiburi](https://s3.amazonaws.com/prod-media.gameinformer.com/2020/07/15/dc638e1a/cleaning_blade.gif)to shake off the droplets of blood staining her blade. 

 The solemn moment however found itself interrupted as a[BGM started to play](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MO9pW3sfbTY). A memorable tune that meant the Risk she took in facing the Scav Champion with just her blades was worth it, that her goal was achieved and the BRRR Gods were satiated with her actions. 

**Today was a great day!**
