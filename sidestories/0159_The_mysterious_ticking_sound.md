The mysterious ticking sound

 -Tick, tock, tick, tock…

 "Mhhh", Jun hummed as he entered Motoko's room, " what is that mysterious ticking noise?".

 He inspected the door, "not over here", then her desk, "not over there".

 -Tick, tock, tick, tock

 "It is, kind of… catchy".

 Jun looked around, to make sure his sister had not yet returned from her latest gig, and seeing the coast clear, he began to sing.

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 'Maybe I should also take singing as my new hobby', he thought

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 So absorbed he was in his own lyrics, Jun didn't notice his sister until he heard her join his tune with a loud-

 -"Motoko!"

 Startled, but undeterred, Jun continued to sing.

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 "Motoko!"

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 "Motoko!"

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 "Motoko!"

 It was at that moment that he noticed a flash of green by the doorway.

 And then chaos ensued.

 "HIROMI, I'M HIROMI, YEAH HIROMI, WOOO!!!"

 "HIROMI, GO HIROMI, YOU'RE THE COOLEST, CHOOM!"

 Jun felt his eye twitching, and did the only thing that he thought would stop this derailing.

 "JUN!" "HIROMI!""JUN!!" "HIROMI!!""JUN!!!!" "HIROMI!!!!" "MOTOKO!"

 -Tick, tock, tick, tock

 Maybe, in hindsight, trying to outshout the little gremlin wasn't the best idea, but at least Motoko had restored the order, and they could continue.

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 "Motoko!"

 "Jun, Jun, Kusanagi Jun"

 "Motoko!"

 "Hey guys!" Hiromi interrupted "I think I found the source of the ticking sound! It was the Uragan rocket launcher under Motoko's bed"

 *explosion noise*

 -the end
