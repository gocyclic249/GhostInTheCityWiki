**Mokoto's School Trip**

_Outside Araska academy_

 Motoko was bored as of right now whilst she waited in her car. She had been asked by Hiromi's parents to drive back home after another Cyberpyscho attack that happened (Like that wasn't a common occurrence in this city). But she had came out earlier than she had originally wanted to because Jun was watch one of those future shows that not only sucked, but made her braincell's want to commit non-alive. now normally she'd do something to distract herself from Jun's questionable shows but he had been getting on at her for last programming binge.

 Okay maybe forgetting to sleep is a bad thing, but Jun didn't need to be on her back, she had only done it twice... _this week_.

 Anyways she got out of the apartment so she didn't have to deal with her brother, Leaving her here a good number of hours before class ended. now you maybe asking why not do a gig to pass the time or just hang out at Lizzies. well two reasons.

 First is that the fixers she knows have no work for her at the moment, second is that Lizzies in doing 'renovations' after a couple of gonks tried to cause trouble.

 Blood is a bitch to remove from carpets.

 Either way she was bored. As she turned her head she noted a can of red body paint that someone from one of her last jobs had her transport, some indie Hollywood type that wanted to make a action movie without CGI or _actually_ killing people, which wasn't surprising as she had played through the crucifixion questline. She got the can as a bonus along with the pay, no idea what she was going to do with it though.

 And then it hit her.

 She had recently got her ultimate chrome for her... 'Kusanagi build', the optical camo system! It was a pain to get but it was so~ worth~ it~! But she hadn't really had a chance to... play with it. 

 Now she did though. Revenge for Hiromi's prank with that pic from Vik... _it still isn't cannon!_

_Inside Arasaka academy's cafeteria_

 Hiromi picked at her lunch bored out of her mind, class was boring, her classmates were boring and worst of all! Her friends who didn't go to this place of boredom were out doing whatever they wanted! Ugh! Stupid mom and dad wanting her to be a productive member of society! she already was helping Mokoto deal with all the chrome they 'liberate' from Scavs and giving them to ripperdocs all around Night City... Yeah sure the rippers have to pay for them, but at a discounted price! And don't tell Mokoto but when Vik sent her a pic of a kid who needed chrome to fix their disability she may of given extra large discount to Vik, after all Vik was doing a service to Night City why not her too.

 Again, don't to Mokoto, she had her rough reputation to keep after all.

 "You're in the way street rat!" was all she heard before felt weight shoved into her into her meal. Turning saw her lower classmate David Martinez had been pushed into her by that brat with some _'high ranking'_ Arasaka dad. Katsuo or something.

 Man was that face _punchable_.

 "The hell you doing!" she shouted

 "Oh your shouting at me? Why it was this street rat that bumped into you."

 "Bumped?! You! Pushed! Him! into me!"

 He gave her a look before giving a scowl "Oh? And? Actually aren't you that Tyger Claw wannabe? You are just as bad this Street Rat, not knowing their station."

 "Um, Tanaka-Sama. You might want to be careful, there's rumours that she has she has a Edgerunner bodyguard." One of his stooges said with worry with his tone.

 "Ha! Some girl who think she's a gangbanger wannabe! She should go to the Scav gonks so they can-" before he could finish his sentence Hiromi heard a angry but familiar voice.

[**_"Hey kids! wanna see a dead body!"_**](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=51DjxGrnZzE&ab_channel=AdmiralStoicRum)

 As those words said Katsuo's face began to be smushed as a fist manifested into it, launching him and making people scream as the invisible figure now manifested with a angry face and covered in what seemed to be blood.

 Hiromi though smiled seeing Mokoto, although she wished she was the one to punch that face!
