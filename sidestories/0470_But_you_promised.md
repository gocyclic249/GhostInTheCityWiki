 "Fine. I promise not to be mad at whatever gonk thing you did."

 "Also you can't ask how."

 "Motoko..." she glared at the girl.

 "Fine. I wont ask, just get it out already."

 Motoko turned and an emty car drove up...

 Panam's hand went to her gun, but the Solo pulled out the shard, and its trunk popped open.

 "Motoko. Where did you get a car?"

 "Found it, not important. Look at this!"

 She pulled out a large dull grey, heavy plastic case and opened it.

 Panam felt her heart lunge up into her throat in fear, and what she would later convince herself wasn't excitement.

 "Motoko! WHERE DID YOU GET A ROCKET LAUNCHER?!" she screamed.

"YOU PROMISED!"
