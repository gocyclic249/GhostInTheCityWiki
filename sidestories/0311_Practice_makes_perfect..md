So this is something I've been working on for a little while that's different from my normal sketches. Just wanted to write something a little more slice of life. So here is Motoko on an 'off day' no gigs, no work, no Jun.

_Practice makes perfect._

 She was excited today, having finally found all the gear she needed to perform some test runs. It was time, the sun was out, temperatures were high, and air pollution was down thanks to a strong Northwestern wind off of the Pacific.

 Typical Night City Mentality. Why try to improve the air quality when everyone can just hope that a windfall would sweep in and take care of it for a few days? Loud music and shouts of revelry echoed down from the tops of skyscrapers. Since going to the beach was obviously out of the question for any NC resident save the fully borged, both public and even some private pools would open their doors at the top of buildings. Letting the Night City populace forget the gang wars and corporate grind for a day. The scent of synthmeat was in the air as BBQ's across the city roasted whatever they had on hand, hoping extra profit would grease their palms.

 It was amidst all of this that she parkoured up the sides of H10 with nothing more than a lexington, her clothes and a backpack, she had a destination in mind. Handholds became footholds as she carefully ascended the mega structure, Athletics and her Condor's keeping her going where any normal woman would fall. Still the pipes and section dividers offered her easy rest stops that she took advantage of. Stopping at one of the dividers and cracking open up a packet of instant ramen she had purchased from a vending machine on the way over. -She had sprung for the deluxe package, slightly pricier than a normal packet as this one actually came bundled with a bottle of boiling hot water. A 'luxury' in Night city where clean water was at a premium.

 She expected these machines wouldn't be long for this world. The water was worth far more than the machine in the eyes of the homeless. Still taking a page from the NC playbook, -she'd enjoy it well it lasted. Noodles successfully rehydrated. She dug in using the complimentary chopsticks and sat there for several minutes humming a tune and enjoying the smog free air and view. Ad's scrolling across clear blue skies and Av's humming away to their destination. She loved this world.

 Still all good things, -and all that. She began her ascent once more leaving her empty bottle and ramen cup as testament to her climb.

 It was only another hour before she finally reached her destination. An empty but well cared for home rested atop the mega building. V's endgame apartment unoccupied and just waiting for a legend to call it home. She unslung her backpack and left it by the stairs before hacking into the place and setting the cameras to loop.

 The ice was decent but it only took ten minutes for her to have disabled all the security and have the AC and entertainment center turned on. Lazily she stretched out across the sinfully long couch, one leg hooked over the end. She laid there for several minutes basking in the luxury that was a fully functioning air conditioning unit. She had already hacked the H10 registry the day before just to be certain, and sure enough nobody was currently 'leasing' the place.

 A monthly letter was even sent out to residents practically begging for _someone_ to please rent the place. Politely asking those who worked for a megacorp to tell their bosses about the house. Promising discounts on water and amenities for the 'advertiser' in question.

 Standing up and stretching she went off to explore the place in detail, finding most of the place empty except for a surprisingly well kept pantry and fridge. Cracking open a white and gold can of whatever a 'Nikola prime' was she gulped it down and immediately found it nostalgic, it reminded her of a pepsi more than the cough syrup like taste of its normal cousins.

 She walked around for a while, sipping the drink and heading up the stairwell, admiring the way the black and white marble of the place reflected the sun coming in from all of the windows. Seriously, three quarters of this building were just windows! Privacy thy name isn't V, well at least the building height prevented any peepers. Sighing she tossed the mostly empty can onto the upstairs bed before heading back downstairs and through the 'front door.'

 The 'lawn' was probably the real selling point of this place. Despite being entirely fake it was likely more grass than the average populace of Night City saw in a month. Complete with both a small pond and a pool. Retrieving her backpack she made her way to the pool before opening it up and finding the swimsuit she had purchased a week before.

 Soon enough she had changed and was floating in her own private and free pool. Basking in the cleanish air and quiet serenity of the place. She stayed there for a few hours. Alternating between swimming to help build her athletics and just…Floating, finding the activity to be very calming. She hadn't really just swimmed since…Well, since coming to this world she supposed.

 It was nice, no distractions, no gigs, no Jun-

***Ring,* *Ring.***

 Speak of the Oni…That was his caller ID. Still, even her brother couldn't ruin this for her! This was her lazy day! She blocked his call before sighing and kicking her way back over to the edge of the pool. The sun was starting to go down and it was time for the main event. She took the time to dry off in the sun before changing back into her normal outfit. Sticking the now damp swimsuit into the backpack before opening another pocket and grinning at the contents.

 Inside was a belt harness that you could easily fit around your waist along with several D-rings and a 150 meter long spool of a thin yet strong wire, -which had been the whole reason for bringing the pack in the first place. Not only did carrying it up here help increase her strength, but it also allowed her to leave in style! She went over to the glass railing and carefully set up an anchor point. Making sure both the railing and her knot were both secure and could take her weight, before clipping it to her new 'belt.'

 She looked over the edge grinning at the two hundred meter drop to the ground before backing up. Making sure to grab her backpack. (Because the swimsuit had set her back more than she'd like to admit.) She strapped that on as well before doing a few stretches, breathing in and out as she did so.

 Well practice makes perfect…

 She sprinted off towards the railing where her anchor was before jumping onto and then off of it. She _had_ wanted to do a flip, but the wire would have gotten tangled. So instead she had to settle for falling at terminal velocity. Letting out a loud "Whoooop!" as she descended.

 She used her Condor to grab the wire and slow her descent as she neared the bottom, stopping just twenty meters away from her destination before slowly swinging out to an overpass and unclipping herself.

 Calling her Qaudra,(and causing a major traffic jam in the process) she proceeded to head home. Grinning all the while.

 Best, day, ever.
