Jun finally marched his way up the stairs to his apartment for what might be the last time. Stepping inside he took a look at the empty shrine before wincing and checking the katana. He returned it to the now disorderly shrine. _'He would have to take it all down soon.'_ A traitorous thought crossed his mind. The sword was returned chipped and damaged, and he offered a short prayer to his parents, before with a sigh moving over to the shower, grateful that Motoko wasn't back yet. Taking a moment to peel his shirt off, (He never fully undressed after Motoko had walked in on him once while showering and it had been awkward.) he reached to press the button-

 "You have reached your daily allowance of water. Further use will result in a 50 Eurodollar surcharge per liter of water. Thank you for choosing Night City Water."

 Freezing cold water shot out from the spout and rolled down his shoulder blades, burning harshly wherever his skin met metal.

 He stood there under the spray as he watched the numbers on the monitor in front of him spiral out of control faster then a gas pump when he refueled his bike.

 He slammed the 'OFF' button as fast as he could before a primal roar tore itself from his throat.

 "Motokoooooooo!"
