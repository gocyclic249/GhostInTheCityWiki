# I purge the wicked.

*Source: https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-1072#post-94597248*
*Words: ~640*

---

*I purge the wicked.*   
   
 My blade leaves a trail of red in the air as it cuts through bone, muscle and chrome. Half of it is from the glow of its thermal edge, the rest of it is blood. Part of my mind makes note of the barrel of a gun aimed my way and with a simple flick of the wrist the head of the Maelstrom gonk I just flatlined goes flying, perfectly timed so that it catches the bullet instead of me. Through the smoke and the blood and grey matter I have just enough to time to catch his expression of muted horror mixed with fury just before my blade bisects him from shoulder to hip.  
   
 That den dealt with, I spare no time looting the bodies. There are more to hit, more scavs to kill.  
   
 *This impious madness must end.*   
   
 The way there is a blur of streets, darkness and neon lights. Not important. I barely pay more attention to it than I must in order to avoid collisions. Next thing I know, I'm right there again. Back in my element.  
   
 My blade sings as I slice my way through the reinforced door, as well as the guy standing on the other side of it. The Burya in my left hand roars its fury as it blows fist-sized holes through a trio of 'strom standing around an operating chair holding their latest "donor". More come out from my right and front, but they are moving so slowly. As if the air was replaced with molasses.  
   
 I do not.  
   
 Bullets whizz by and around me, never hitting their mark as my blade flashes out again. Severing arms and heads in a dance most macabre.  
   
 *I shall be the instrument of Armageddon.*  
   
 This place turned out to be a lot larger than anticipated. Rooms and hallways pass me by like rain and I actually have to stop a few times to reload my huge fuckoff revolver. It doesn't matter. I'll wear them down with my Lexington if I have to. But looking at the even huger gun now held in my hand, this might not be needed. A massive automatic pistol, *Magnum Opus 14mm* carved into the slide in stenciled letters. Firing it sends a shockwave all the way down my chrome arm and into the rest of me that leaves me with a strange ache in my bones, but I ignore it in favor of watching cyborg assholes get torn nearly in two by it.  
   
 *The end is near.*  
   
 Eventually it runs dry so I tuck it in my waistband in favor of my next ~~pilfered~~ requisitioned weapon. Hm. Maelstrom must be getting desperate if they're already bringing out the flamethrowers. Shaking the blood off of my thermal katana before the heat bakes it in, I direct the superheated let of liquid fire through one of the broken windows of the abandoned apartment complex they were squatting in. The fuel tank lasts me another villa-turned-scav den and a trailer barrack before it finally runs out of juice. Doesn't matter.  
   
 *The Apocalypse is here.*  
   
 My katana makes a noise like a guillotine slicing through a cast iron frying pan filled with sizzling bacon as it cuts the arms off of a gangoon equipped with mantis blades. Ignoring his screams I swing a second time, releasing the blades from the arms and then proceed to bicycle kick them through the air like razor missiles right between the eyes of two men who were just about to open up on me with a pair of assault rifles. Grabbing both I tuck the stocks under one arm and, with a bit of work, manage to get my finger on both triggers just in time for their buddies to come into view.  
   
 *Rip and tear until it is done.*
