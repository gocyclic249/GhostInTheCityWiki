Understanding fully that you can't _actually_ get a rant like this going on people like Maine because those people will interrupt, or ignore you, or kick you out of the safe house, possibly with actual kicking...

 "Maine, do you understand how bones break? They aren't magical willpower conduits, they're physical materials with specific limitations. You break bones by exceeding those limitations, and no amount of some imagined _toughness_ is going to stop it."

 "Yeah but-"

 "No, shut up about the chrome. I'm making a point here. Once you break the bone you can _maybe_ push through the pain and keep doing whatever, assuming it wasn't something _critical_ and you just _crippled_ yourself, and you can probably call that "tough". But since you are _actively accumulating more unnecessary damage by exasperating the original issue_, I'd really rather call it _fucking retarded_. Ruining your health to satisfy your ego."

 "ye aren't talking about _bones_ here to begin with-"

 "**Nerves are exactly the same you dumbass fucking gonk**! Physical materials! Specific limits! When someone tells you "this is the limit", exceeding that limit builds up damage! Maybe a little at first, but you keep making things worse with your gonk-ass decisions and eventually you'll be rolling into a fight half dead! Working with half-broken equipment - and your body _is_ fucking equipment - gets you fucking killed!"

 "If your body breaks you just replace the broken part with more chrome! I can-"

 "**_THAT IS HOW YOU GO CYBERPSYCHO_**."

 Everyone paused for a moment to let that settle in.

 "It's not about the amount of chrome you have jammed in your gonk-ass half dead body. It's about everything _else_ cascade failing because you kept abusing the parts that worked until they stopped working, then added _even more_ stress, in the form of extra chrome, to half-broken ganic connections, and then kept abusing them until _they_ broke too.

 "The only way; _the only way_, you bypass all that... is scooping your brain out and putting it in a full-body replacement. _Then_ we'll see how _tough_ you are, because your willpower will be the only thing _left_ for you to break. 

 "You will note that a full-body conversion like that has _incredibly high_ cyberpsychosis rates. Because as it turns out, you can't just drop a person into an entirely new shell and expect that to work out well. They need the experience with normal chrome first.

 "You're looking at cyberpsychosis either way... _unless_ you let your gonk-ass body heal from the shit you shove in it between adding new parts. And since the ripper is _the expert_ in how that ganic shit breaks and how to _avoid_ it, how long it takes for ganic parts to heal and acclimatize with their _specific material properties that continue to **not** be magical willpower conduits,_ you listen to your fucking ripper."

 Mokoto shrugs.

 "Or not, and then I guess I'll see you sometime in a landfill after you burn out."

 Mokoto looks around, noting she is outside the safe house and nobody is around.

 "... wait, when did that happen?"
