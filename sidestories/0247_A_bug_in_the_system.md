_Probably not a good idea to use game physics exclusively for this story.

 This is why._

 ---

 Jun was having a shitty day, he was being run ragged by his bosses and forced to put in overtime.

 He thought he would finally be getting some sleep tonight, but it would seem his imouto had other plans.

 "Jun-nii! Thank goodness you are here! Help I am stuck!"

 Jun just rubbed his eyes in frustration. Before his eyes, his imouto was crouching between a wall and a metal staircase.

 From a certain angle, it would look like she got stuck between the stairs and the wall, but when he got closer to take a look, there was enough space for her to walk out.

 "Imouto... Please tell me you didn't wake me just to mess around." As much as he loved his imouto, even he has his limits.

 "Look, I know it looks like I can get out of here on my own, but trust me, I really am stuck! Don't believe me? Try and touch the air there!" Jun followed his frantic sisters eyes as they pointed to the empty space next to her.

 Jun sighed, he had already driven for a good 30 minutes through traffic, 12 minutes sneaking into the building his imouto was at, and took another solid 10 minutes to climb up the stairs of said building to get to her because the elevator was down for maintenence. It was now 3 AM in the morning, he was tired, cranky, and hungry but he was already here. Might as well do what his imouto says.

 Jun kneeled next to his sister and motioned his hand to touch the empty foot of space between his imouto and the wall. His eyes widened in disbelief when his hand felt the sensation of a concrete wall.

 "W-what the hell?"

 "See! I told you! I told you! I am not crazy!"

 "Imouto... How?"

 Motoko's cheeks flared in emberrasment, "I don't know! I used the Camera Control quickhack on the adjacent building's cameras and when I focused my optics back into my position, this is where I ended up. Now please get me out of here!"

 Jun looked between Motoko, the building adjacent to them, and the empty wall then sighed in resignation.

 The things he did for family.

 ---

_Btw, this actually happened to me in game. I was sneaking around in Arasaka Industrial Park Warehouse and I got stuck between the wall and the stairs after using Camera Control on the security cameras to avoid being spotted.

 I only managed to get out because I reloaded a previous save.

 A feature which poor Motoko lacks._
