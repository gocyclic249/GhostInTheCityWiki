"Ah man! That was so cool! Villain speeches, awesome kungfu battles! I fought the Major! The fight was so cool, they would have to spend at least half the animation budget to have handled it!" I cried out to the sky far too excited to do anything but squee at my recent accomplishments. 

 Japan was burning down in the background. The riots alone were causing some serious problems. The prime Minister would likely get replaced by the total cutie Prime Minster that I remember from SAC 2nd gig. I wasn't sure if she was really a good choice what with all the corruption coming to light, but eh.

 I took three steps when I realized it.

 "You're about ten minutes too late?" I muttered then stopped. Dead in my tracks.

 "I did it thirty-five minutes ago! FUCK! I could have made my villain speech like a million percent cooler with that! Dammit! Okay no it's okay. No one knows." I grabbed my Burya. "I just need to kill everyone and then myself, and no one will know... What's the current population of Japan? Eh. That actually sounds like a lot. I'll just go eat Ramen and cry about it instead." Nodding to myself at that wise decision I faintly heard a strange ding like an alert sounding. But that was silly! I maxed everything already so my system hadn't sent me a level up alert in years!

 I checked my agent and snorted at the spam message, something about Wisdom? I deleted it. Not important.

 Besides, everyone knows Wisdom isn't a stat in Cyberpunk!
