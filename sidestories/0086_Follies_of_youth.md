 "Yo, Wakako, I'm fixed up and ready to go" her most recent infiltration specialist called out of the blue "Got any jobs for me? A bit of warning though, I'm only accepting gigs involving wholesale slaughter of Maelstrom gonks"

 "Ah yes, I have heard a word or two about your... adventure" the old lady of Japantown replied

 "Preem. So you know I'm itching for vengeful slaughter"

 "Indeed I do" the spider replied "Sadly, I don't usually deal with that group, seeing as they usually operate out of Watson and Northside; out of my area of influence, you see"

 "Wakako, you are busting my imaginary balls here" Motoko replied "Sure, I know it's a bit unusual for you to get involved with the metal gonks, but see, here's the thing. For one, I know you got some offspring amongst the Claws who are chomping at the bit to flatline some of the stormy chrome junkies. Two, I know you know of a fixer or two that operates in the area who could set me up with a gig to slaughter some chrome-jobs, just send them my resume or something"

 "How bold of you" Wakako replied "Not only have you barely survived your recent string of... 'surprise surgeries', but you are now demanding your elderly fixer to give away her hard-won contacts just so you could take your work elsewhere. Am I suppossed to starve to death, just to satisfy your ambitions young girl?"

 "If you are so worried about that, I'm perfectly fine with coming down to you office and stuffing a loaf of delicious fresh all-organic bread down your undoubtedly fine throat, you old and experienced lady I love to work for so much."

 "Ah, to be young again..." Wakako smiled to herself "Fine, I will give you a contact information for the local fixer, as well as my recommendation. Don't squander it, girl."

 "Wouldn't dream about it, old hag"

 "What was that? My comms system is quite ancient, you understand, and I sometimes get these little glitches here and there. Why, I almost called up MaxTac onto your location by accident just now."

 "I said, 'wouldn't dream about disappointing your expectations', and that I'm thankful for your continued trust and support for my measly endeavors"

 "Thought so."

 "Yep, have a nice day" Motoko replied "...old hag" she finished the call then and there.

 Wakako smiled.

 'Ah, the follies of youth'.

 "You come in highly recommended, by the old spider of all things, so here's the deets" Regina Jones said over the comms "We have a Maelstrom trade deal with the Scavengers. The metal-heads are negotiating a wholesale chrome trade deal with the scavs. I need you to infiltrate the area and see what the final negotiated deal is about, my client wants to know what's being traded, for what, and where and when the transfer of goods is taking place-"

 "I got it, Regina-sama" Motoko replied with a serious tone of her voice "Slaughter everyone and leave no survivors, got it"

 "No. I just said, infiltrate the area, gather Intel, and skedaddle without being seen-"

 "Ah, I get it now. My apologies" Regina's gamble of an newest operative replied "Mission objective acknowledged. Leave no survivors to report me as 'being seen' optional objective confirmed. Commencing Virtuous Mission"

 "Wait! Motoko, for the love of- wait!" Regina tried, but the comms stayed silent "Motoko? Motoko! Motoko!!!"

 Quickhack the camera.

 Take it over, look through its lens.

 Use it to hack another cam, then disable the alarms.

 Take the view of the new cam, glance at the minefield.

 Remotely disable the mines directly on the path towards the presumed security station.

 Maelstrom gonk just vacated the toilet, grab him by the head and break his neck, stuff him back inside the septic tank.

 Another gonk that was waiting in line to use the toilet, almost made you, Quickhack his optics, move behind and stab him in the brain with a screwdriver he was using just a few seconds before to please himself.

 Don't ponder the question.

 Stuff the body down the septic chute you stuffed the previous gonk inside.

 Move along.

 Another camera.

 Take it over.

 Through its lenses, take over a cleverly hidden turret, set it to idle in an endless diagnostic cycle.

 The gonks are riled up, didn't receive mandatory report from two of their members.

 They are on high alert now.

 Fuck it.

 Approach the turret you got stuck in an endless diagnostic cycle, tear out the Heavy Machine Gun.

 Maelstrom on high alert now.

 Too bad.

 So sad.

 You have a Heavy Machine Gun with hundreds of rounds of ammo.

 The enemy has a bunch of gonks barely waking up from browsing BD's and an infiltrated security system.

 There's only one thing you can do.

 You squeeze the trigger as if it was the gentlest of lovers.

 You yell out in joy as the heavy rounds penetrate the makeshift sheet-metal defensive reinforcements as if they were nothing more than paper.

 "Gyahaha!" You yell out in primal joy, as the heavy mechanism of your gun's recoil do their best to break your arms "Dakka dakka dakka!"

 For the first time in your life you feel truly alive.

 For the last time in theirs, so do scavs and storms.
