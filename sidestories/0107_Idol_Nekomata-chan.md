# Idol Nekomata-chan♡!

"Hello hello! Welcome everyone! It's me, Neko-chan! Nyaaaan~♡!" Twitters a high-pitched voice, her accent distinctly Japanese. "I'm here today, again, with TriggerFinger-San, who is out right now, and we're watching over the compound. I'm sure today is going to be exciting, my _Puru-puru_♡_~ Neko-Neko_♡_~_ powers tell me so!"

 The slightly ear-piercing voice of the sniper rifle rattles with a distinctly metallic twang, due to the small scrap hut that she is currently propped up in. The night is a quiet one, or as quiet as Night City gets, distant gunfire and the roaring of engines notwithstanding. A low cover of rain laden clouds hangs above the practically abandoned city district, though they have yet to release their payload.

 "Now, all of my cute viewers at home, today we're watching Ziggy Q's interview with Militech rep Markus Rodriguez. So far Ziggy has been his usual _very un-cute_ self and Markus hasn't been interesting at all. I'm not sure why we're watching this, but I don't have fingers, so this is what we have-Desu!"

 A voice suddenly echoes up from around the roof's edge. "**Who da fuck are you talkin' to!**"

 The sniper rifle gasps theatrically. "Guillotine-Kun, that is a very bad word! You are being very un-cute right now! Sorry about that everyone, we're just going to ignore _naughty guillotine-Kun_ and move on."

 "**You call me a kun ageein and I'll tear yur fuckin' face off!**" Guillotine-Kun shouts back, though Nekomata pays him no mind.

 "So, Markus-San and _un-cute Ziggy_ were just talking about the gang war taking place, and Militech's response to the increased demand for their product-Oh!" The rifle exclaims in surprise as a figure ducks into the small shack and takes a seat at the miniature table within it. "Hello TriggerFinger-san. I was wondering when you'd be getting back, my fans were curious if we'd be doing anything today?"

 TriggerFinger doesn't respond. Instead keeping his eyes locked onto the small television showing the interview and idly picking his nose.

 "_Why yes, adorable Neko-chan-_" The Nekomata ventriloquises in a deeper voice. "-_I was thinking that I'd spend some time cleaning you and scrubbing that discount EBM tech-lubricant I bought off the streets from your conducting rails. I know it's low quality and isn't completely non-conductive like the user manual says it should be, which could hurt you. Since you're the only reason I'm still alive, I really should take care of you-desu_♡_._"

 TriggerFinger, who hasn't said a word, seemingly finds something of interest stuck in his nose, which he briefly looks over before flicking away.

 "Trigger-san, I knew you loved me! The air is full of purupuru♡~ loveu♡-loveu♡ today! I feel my Neko-Neko♡~ powers rising! I will now make a prediction!"

 Nekomata's ethereal cheeks puff out in concentration and her round eyes clench shut. "Neko-puru-neko-puru-NEKO♡! AHA!" Neko's voice drops to a low and wispy mutter, her eyes snap open, though instead of their normal shape, the pupils and iris have become twisting swirls. "Ooooh~ TriggerFinger-san, you will die two weeks from now from constipation of the butt~ Your poo doesn't come out and you explode~ AH!" Neko-chan blinks, the swirls disappearing from her eyes. "Hura-hura? What did I say? Was my prediction a nice one? Lovely Trigger-san deserves a happy future, right everyone? Let's all wish TriggerFinger-San good luck in finding happiness, and we'll do it with the power of Puru-neko♡! Say it with me everyone Neko-puru-neko-puru♡~"

 A dark shadow suddenly leaps into the small shack, TriggerFinger gurgles feebly as the television is covered with a thin spray of blood. Nekomata 'eeps' in fear as a dark figure stands over TriggerFinger, watching the man bleed out from the large gash in his throat before reaching out, and gently slumping him in his chair in a way that looks natural at a glance.

 "P-p-p-p-p-p-p-puuuu?!" Nekomata stutters wildly, her eyes wide in terror. "A-a-ah! A-ahem." She clears her throat, suddenly looking half-serious, but her eyes and mouth still twitch in a clear sign of fear. "Neko-puru A-AHA! M-m-my n-new prediction i-is that T-TriggerFinger-san dies from blood loss~ AH! Oh no! My p-prediction came true! Poor Trigger-san! PFHH-" Nekomata seizes in utter terror as the deadly shadow tilts its head and begins to survey the room.

 Nekomata presses her hands over her mouth to stifle her phantom breathing, her eyes twitch sporadically as the figure dripping with blood comes closer and closer to spotting the sniper rifle. Then like the illuminating beams from some spotlights, if said spotlights were possessed by an evil spirit of death, twin beams of sight alight on Nekomata.

 A strangled scream slips past Nekomata's lips and she slams her eyes shut as the being reaches out with one of its hands, the one not holding the blade still dripping with crimson fluid.

 "Ah." A soft voice mutters breathlessly.

 Nekomata stops shaking, one of her eyes slowly slides open and she beholds the gently smiling face of a young girl. The fear flees like a demon being chased away by oversized rabbits wielding humongous pink hammers, and Nekomata openly gawks as the girl runs a soft hand along her receiver, causing her to shiver as a foreign feeling of _knowing_ passes over her.

 "Y-y-you're **much** cuter than Trigger-san… W-w-who are you?"

 The terrifying and **Cute** human girl pulls Nekomata close for a hug. With her mouth so close to her barrel, Nekomata has no trouble hearing the girl's whispered words. "Next time."

 "P-puru♡?" Nekomata squawks quietly as she is set back in her previous spot leaning on the wall.

 The girl stands up, looking at Nekomata with a gleam of adoration in her eyes, then she turns around and walks towards the door.

 "W-w-wait! **Cute** person! Take me with you-Desu♡!"

 "A Nekomata?" A new voice says from somewhere on the girl's waist. As the girl is now facing away, Nekomata can see the shape of a pistol holster on the small of her back. The pistol continues. "Hey, listen! This girl isn't human, pray that she leaves you behind. I'm already doomed, but you can still survive! Trust me, you don't want to be anywhere near her!"

 "EEEHH?! I don't care if she's 'not human'! She's **cute**! Make her turn around and pick me up-Desu!" The rifle cries desperately.

 The pistol seems to need a moment to process that as the girl opens the door of the little shack. "_Uugghh,_ fucking Nekomatas. Alright, fuck it, ignore me. It's not like I know what I'm talking about or anything."

"В любом случае, она мало что может сделать чтобы предотвратить свою судьбу."(Either way, there is little she can do to stop her fate.) Responds another voice from under the girl's arm.

 "Yeah, I know. But I need to at least try to limit the collateral damage of this. I refuse to do nothing."

 The girl slips out of the shack completely, the door slowly closing behind her.

 Nekomata eyes the girl's previous location with naked desire. "No no no! Come back **cute**person! I've got Puru-puru-powers♡!"

 But her cries fall on unhearing ears. The door drifts shut and rattles the walls of the weak structure.

 Nekomata sighs despondently and looks around the room, nearly wallowing in depression. As her eyes fall on the corpse of TriggerFinger, they scrunch up in distaste. "So un-cute…" She mutters, the smells of the corpse's 'discharge' becoming more and more prevalent in the enclosed space.

 Nekomata spends the next few minutes just marinating in the new stench the room has acquired, grumbling to herself and trying to keep her spirits up.

 "She'll be back for me, we're both too cute♡! We're _made_ for eachother♡! I'll be the Jack Maximum to her Jenni Flexx. Isn't that right, mina-san♡?" Nekomata asks the empty shack, her only answer being a few unrelated shouts from somewhere in the building below, and the inane babble of the television.

 "Umu, that's right! And very nice of you all to say-Desu♡!" Nekomata agrees, then suddenly jumps in surprise as a quick rip of rifle rounds go off in the garage below her, merely the concussive sound is enough to shake the roof under her stock.

 "P-p-puru!?" Nekomata stutters.

 A collage of sounds continue below, which Nekomata is surprised to find she can actually hear. The last string of shots had apparently cut off the ever-present music that used to pump through the whole garage at an excessive volume.

 "W-w-well, at least the-AH!" Nekomata pauses as a stray round pings somewhere nearby, creating an awful din in the enclosed space. "...At least the music has stopped…"

 "Alright. C'mon stay together you idiots! We hold out here. She can't sneak up on us here, we just have to wait for reinforcements. See how this ninja bitch likes trying her hand against forty of us!" A loud voice shouts, muffled by the ramshackle shack and with an echoing quality to it from the distance.

 "Ah! That was Frost-San! He really needs to watch his un-cute language. It sounds like he's fighting my Jenni." The shouting from below continues, Frost barking orders to his people. Nekomata's fine-tuned senses detect the familiar sound of someone scaling the ladder that leads to the rooftop.

 Suddenly, the door bursts open with a quiet squeal from its shoddy hinge. "P-p-p-puu!-" Nekomata stutters, startled by the girl's sudden reappearance and swift approach to Nekomata's position. "M-my Jenni♡! My Puru-puru-powers♡ told me you'd be here~, but we should go, this place is quickly becoming _very un-cute_-WAAAH!" The rifle's speech is interrupted as the girl quickly shoulders the Nekomata and points it towards one of the shed's walls.

 The girl depresses the trigger halfway, the rifle hums as an excess of energy is dumped into her capacitors, causing their heat to skyrocket. "Puru-puru-puru-puru~!" Nekomata rattles, her mouth moving without her consent. Her eyes look down her own scope as she attempts to locate the target that the girl has selected, but all she can see is a bare sheet metal wall.

 Suddenly, with the gentlest of motions, the girl pulls the trigger all the way. "**PEW**♡~!" Nekomata roars like thunder, sending a 50 cal steel-jacketed tungsten spike just shy the length of a man's hand out of her barrel at 6000 feet per second.

 The spike neatly passes through the sheet metal of the shack's wall as if it weren't even there, then the round crosses the short distance to the maelstrom goon at the top of the ladder. Once again, it passes through him as if he weren't even there, whatever armor he may have had proving utterly useless against the sheer speed and weight of the metallic chunk passing through his chest cavity. The cavitation caused by its passage leaves a hole well over the size of a football.

 "Awawa? Do you not like walls or-" Nekomata abruptly stops talking. In the deafening silence following her loud discharge, the near-silent sound of a meaty splat can be heard from the direction the girl had shot. "A-a-ah! I mean! P-p-puru-neko♡ AHA! Y-yes, my prediction of that… man's? Death came true! My Jenni wouldn't have been able to make that shot without my _cute predictive qualities_♡!"

 "She must have some high quality eyes, Motoko probably saw someone through the wall." The pistol from before mutters lowly, clearly unamused by the rifle's assumption.

 "Ghrk!" Nekomata chokes. "I-I-I k-knew that! I saw in a v-vision that she h-had m-m-magic eyes and would s-see her enemy-Desu♡!"

"это не было твоим достижением. Так что заткнись."(it was not your achievement. So shut up.) Growls a slightly angry voice, though from the deep and gravelly tone one might assume he was enraged.

 A retort is on the tip of Nekomata's lips when Motoko swings her around, and begins running from the shack in a burst of speed. Just in time too, a hail of small caliber rounds clatter across the roof's surface, sounding like the loudest rain that ever rained.

 Motoko drops into a slide and skids across the metal of the roof and into the small hatch that leads to the garage.

 "Awaaa! I can't see! It's too dark in here!" Nekomata complains as Motoko and her arsenal are engulfed in darkness.

 Her speech is abruptly interrupted by Motoko pointing her and half-pulling her trigger. "Puru-puru-puru-puru~!" Quickly followed by a full pull. "**PEW**♡~!"

 The flash from the rifle's discharge briefly illuminates the open space of the garage, and a van parked nearby.

 Nekomata's head begins to spin, the world becoming a chaotic jumble of flashing lights and complete darkness.

 "Puru-puru-puru-puru-**PEW**♡~!" Another flash of light. And a tsk of dissatisfaction from Motoko.

 The sound of a door getting kicked open briefly centers Nekomata's attention, although she only manages to spot a collection of glowing red dots consistent with maelstrom's preferred cybernetic eyes before Motoko dives aside and drags her along.

 Bullets bounce across the ground inches away from them, and then they stop. Quick footsteps approach their position from around some kind of obstruction, and Motoko presses herself and her weapons up against the floor.

 Enraged footsteps walk a complete circuit around the van, before they rush off to another part of the darkened structure.

 Motoko slides out from under the van with an uncanny deftness, and Motoko raises Nekomata while walking through the darkness.

 The frazzled rifle loses track of how many 'Purus♡' she utters, her head still spinning out of control, but by the end the shuffling feet have ceased and her magazine is nearly empty. The cool air of Night City makes Nekomata involuntarily shiver. '_When did we get outside?_' She thinks to herself.

 But the confusion doesn't abate at all, all the rifle sees is a blur as Motoko runs aside and then quickly and quietly climbs a ladder. She reaches the top and treads lightly across the roof.

 The familiar stench of the small shack overtakes Nekomata's senses, briefly making her gag as she becomes reacquainted with the smell she had previously been adjusted to.

 "Bitch! Come on then! You've cut down these gonks! You think you're hard!? They are chaff! I'll rip you apart!" Frosty's familiar voice echoes up from the ground, easily going through the thin sheet metal.

 Nekomata's gag gets caught in her throat. "Puru-puru-puru-puru-**PEW**♡~!" The round punches yet another hole into the shack, right alongside a myriad of smaller caliber punctures, and Nekomata's magazine finally goes empty.

 Motoko has an instant to 'tsk' to herself before the far wall lights up with a storm of rifle rounds punching through at a rapid pace. The girl throws herself aside, although her desperate dodge doesn't work completely and multiple 7.9mm bullets penetrate her armor and eject sprays of blood.

 Nekomata's gagging reaches a disgusted crescendo as Trigger-san's stench rises to a never-before experienced level. Motoko pulls the corpse close and shelters behind it.

 The rounds continue to pump in, more and more flits of light skittering through the walls, and digging holes around Motoko and her arsenal, carving deep trenches into the only thing between them and the storm.

 After one fakeout, the assault ends, and silence once more overtakes the small shack, the only sound being the quiet groaning of the significantly damaged ramshackle hut leaning on its last legs.

 "Got ya huh, bitch?" frosty taunts.

 Motoko growls and withdraws the bulky frame of a large pistol from an under-arm holster. The weapon chuckles to himself quietly as he is aimed. His loud shot rattles the shack, making the structure sway a little, and Motoko's effort's are rewarded by an enraged shout. "**How are you still alive!?**" Followed by a renewed effort to deconstruct the shack with bullets.

 Tightening her frame to fit behind the corpse a little better, the rounds zip past, luckily accomplishing little, and before long the shooting stops.

 A flicker of color passes across the door, a figure peering in from a safe distance. Motoko narrows her eyes and raises her weapon once again. Frosty only has enough time to widen his eyes before a chunk of metal punches through his chest.

 The rooftop goes silent as Frosty's body tumbles out of view, the girl in the shack shoves the body off of herself roughly, coughing up blood in the process.

 "She's not fuckin' human…" The automatic pistol mutters.

 The electromag pistol just chuckles.

 Despite the chunks of gore on her frame, Nekomata's eyes turn into glittering stars. "Uheee! That's my Jenni! You are the **cutest,**most deadliest **cute**person there ever was! Stick with me and you'll go places, ♪YATTAAA~☆🎜!" The rifle then devolves into a mad round of childish giggling and celebratory nonsensical 'predictions♡'.

 The auto-pistol groans loudly. "...I'm too old for this shit."
