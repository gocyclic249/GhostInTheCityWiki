***20 years later**

 "Look at that Jun! Night City is as much of a cesspit as it has ever been. You think Motoko has everything ready? I did give her the Gig pretty late."

 Turning to look at the hulking suit of armor that followed behind her, Hiromi nodded in satisfaction at Jun once again succeeding in scaring away the heaps of flunkies that wanted to arrange a "coincidental" run-in with Arasaka's head of Section 9.

 "Has my sister ever let you down?"

 The deep rumble of Jun's voice and the matter of fact manner in which he presented the statement calmed her fears and reminded her that Motoko had always fulfilled the Contract once it was accepted and that she was just letting pre-mission jitters ruffle her.

 "Yeah, you're right Jun! Motoko has never accepted a Gig she couldn't succeed in."

 Moment of weakness over, a snap of her fingers gathered her waiting entourage, all who knew that staying within earshot when Director Hiromi wanted some personal time was reason enough for the "Oni" the Director's personal bodyguard to throw them out a window. After all it was a proven fact that any place visited by the Director had a 500% increase in the expenditure budget for windows.

 "Prepare a car."

 "Yes Director!"

 As the minion ran off to fulfil her orders, Hiromi used her waiting time to review the multiple files her entourage handed to her, a nod of her head or a single statement deciding the life and death of hundreds.

 "Car's ready Hiromi. We should go."

 Nodding in acknowledgement to Jun's words, a wave of her hand send the rest of the waiting flunkies scattering as she made her way towards her readied vehicle.

 "Shall we see the result of Motoko's work? It did take us over a decade after all to get to this point."

* * *

_***10 years ago.**

 "So what you're saying is that the source of all the Training Sims, Special blueprints and even drugs you've been handing to me for my Section 9 Department were a result of a Nanotech Shard you slotted into your neck when you just woke up from your coma? Also, again why do i have to call my department Section 9? We have more then 9 members and what are we a Section off? "

 "YEP! I think it's some kind of Super Soldier Program. It's why i can learn so quickly. ANYWAY, the important thing is that i got enough levels now to unlock the Blueprints to make and i quote here... "GAMER Shard (degraded). Also, Section 9 is a great name and you signed a contract where i get to name your department if i provided you with the things you need to build it. Stick to your contracts Corpo!" 

 "I'll show you, you STREET BRAT! RARGH!!"

 Sighing at the now familiar sight of his Sister and his boss once again squabbling and hairpulling like they were teens again, Jun reached out and grabbed the both of them by the collars before pulling them apart.

 "JUN! AS YOUR BELOVED IMOUTO, I COMMAND YOU TO DROP ME AND HOLD HIROMI SO I CAN GIVE HER A NOOGIE!!"

 "JUN! AS YOUR BOSS, I COMMAND YOU TO DROP ME AND HOLD MOTOKO SO I CAN DRAW ON HER WEIRD CLOTHES WITH MY SHARPIE!"

 "YOU FIEND!"

 "Ladies, focus. We are here in this Den Motoko keeps in the Wasteland for a reason. Motoko, please continue with what you were saying about the Shard."

 "Oh right! Ok, so i made 1 Shard which i want to give to Jun and if it works, it could totally help with Hiromi's dream of building up enough forces to go independent from Arasaka and become the Top Fixer in the WORLD!"

 Without any hesitation, Jun picked up the provided Shard and slotted it in.

 "I'm getting a message saying Gamer System Initializing."

 "Oh oh! I Got that as well! Ok, you then need to move to access your System and you'll see a new menu with Gamer."

 " Got It. It's giving me a Stat Screen with Body and Reflexes. Accessing it brings up options like Athletics, Muscular strength, Cardiovascular Might, Adrenaline Booster etc"

 "That's it? Also, that seems very different from mine!"

 "Yes."

 "Ohhhh so that's why it's degraded! The Shard only allows modifications to the user's body!"_

* * *

 "The Biotech we were able to reverse engineer from analyzing your body was useful though. It being all built on enhancing a body's biological function allowed us to give all Section 9 members a Sandevistan equivalent without the drawbacks beyond exhaustion."

 Despite the full face Oni mask he was wearing, Hiromi could tell Jun was rolling his eyes. He had not been a fan of the multiple prodding and examinations he had to undergo after enhancing each stat.

 "Come on! Don't you think it was cool that you're been called the Biotech equivalent of Adam Smasher? I still remember that Biotechnica guy trying to poach you saying that they can provide you with better Bioware then Arasaka!"

 "You do realize that the guy kept sending Edgerunners after me to catch me _Dead Or Alive, but Intact_ right?"

 Giggling slightly at the annoyed tone Jun was speaking in, Hiromi looked out at the rolling skyline of Night City before speaking again.

 "I mean, you should be thanking that guy! If he didn't sent them after you, how would you be able to earn the reputation of being equal to Adam Smasher via taking out all the Hunters solo? ANYWAY, looks like we're here!"

 Feeling the car slowing down and coming to a halt, Hiromi put back on her Director Face and once more became Director Hiromi with her Bodyguard Oni in tow.

 Exiting the vehicle and sending the driver away, Hiromi entered the nondescript warehouse and past layers of doors guarded by bots and security turrets before finally entering a underground stadium like complex.

 Looking out at the sight before her, Hiromi found herself looking down at squad after squad of identical soldiers going through drills in eerie synchronicity. Despite the armored visors lending them all a uniform appearance, the movement and instinctual actions screamed Motoko to both Jun and Hiromi.

![Image 14](https://i.pinimg.com/originals/81/f9/8f/81f98f22a233f800ea4ae85fc1ad901a.jpg)​

 "What you think Hiromi? Like the Bioroids? I do good work considering you only gave me 2 years to train them! Oh Hi Nii San! How's being a Corporate sellout treating you these days?"

 "You got me this Job Motoko! And i distinctly remember you begging me saying that you don't trust anyone else to keep Hiromi safe from the Office politics or Corporate Shad-"

 Raising an eyebrow as the speaker of Jun's Oni mask cut off, Hiromi looked around to see if she could spot Irritating Best Friend Motoko before she got glomped.

 *GLOMP

 "Hi Motoko, nice to see you. Thank you for training the Bioroids with your weird fancy Gamer Shard."

 "Don't forget to thank me for stealing the _How To Bioroid_ from Biotechnical as well!"

 "Motoko, that was a Gig. I paid you for it, it comes under you being hired by me."

 "Wow, you sure sound Corpo there Miss Director!"

 " You know, you have alot of sass towards the person running your Bank Account. Do you not want your eddies back or something?"

 "GASP! THAT'S THEFT!"

 "GASP! I'M A CORPO! STEALING FROM CUSTOMERS IS MY JOB!"

 Considering this happened everything they met, even the Deja Vu of breaking them apart was getting stale. A part of Jun couldn't help but wonder when was the exact moment he got so jaded by the duo's shenanigans.

_Was it when they blew up Arasaka Tower and managed to deflect the blame to some poor kid in a Cyber-skeleton?

 Was it when Motoko interrupted Yorinobu's attempt at Patricide due to crashing into the wrong floor for Hiromi's Birthday?_

 "Focus. I believed Motoko has a report to give. Hiromi perhaps you should listen to the report and decide on the next step to take."

 "Oh right! Boss Lady Hiromi! It is my pleasure to inform you that the training has been a success! Each Bioroid now has a chip containing skills i distilled from my vast experience as a solo that will make them more then strong enough to face any corporate force even if outnumbered 2 to 1!"

 Hearing Motoko's verbal confirmation sent a surge of courage up Hiromi's spine, letting the grin on her lips appear she made her declaration of War.

 "Very well, as of now Section 9 is now independent from Arasaka, and like any good Fixer i should have my own place of power don't you think? Konpeki Plaza seems like an excellent choice as well as would serve as a excellent severance package.

 Major Kusanagi Motoko, ready your troops and get me my Hotel!"

 "Aye aye Mdm Director Hiromi!"
