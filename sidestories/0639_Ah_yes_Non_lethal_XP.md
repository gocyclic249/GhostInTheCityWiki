**Ah yes, "Non lethal XP"**

* * *

Motoko: "Man I can't believe I nearly forgot about this!"

 Yoko: "What hack did you use to disable these men?"

 Motoko: "Err, I punched them?"

 Yoko: "...And then dropped some sort of hack on them correct?"

 Motoko: "I mean, I guess I used short circuit on a few?"

 Yoko: "Another of your specially tuned hacks?"

 Motoko: "...No?"

 Yoko: "Motoko, you are a terrible liar. None of these men have woken up yet. They've just continued to writhe on the ground for days now."

 Motoko: "...No, no. I just knocked them out."

 Yoko: "If you had just merely 'knocked them out' Motoko, then they would have woken back up within minutes. _Not days._"

 Motoko: "...Well at least they're still alive?"

 Yoko: "Motoko, _what hack did you use?_"

 Motoko: "Err... Trade secret?"
