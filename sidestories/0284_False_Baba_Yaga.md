The Scav's NEED to respond, or die... And there has to be SOME sort of conversation going on in the background between cells... At SOME level.

* * *

 "This can't go unanswered." was the first thing said as the news blared over the speakers on the small basement. Smoke filled the air and a small fan slowly rattled along on the ceiling, simply spreading out the noxious cigar smoke throughout the room more than cooling anything off. "We have a... 'face' to go with this false Baba Yaga."

 One of the other men snorted, his companion to his right giving him a distainful look. 

 Nine figures, seven masculine, two feminine, sat around the small card table, eyes and faces static and X eyed. 

 "You disagree?" one asks.

 The man who snorted gestures to the tv. "нет... Just amused that we have a.... hunter." he explains. "A few taken out, a few recorded, and then this. Our storage... This 'Laughing Man' is a joke on us.... A FUCKING JOKE!"

 "успокоиться." the first one says, motioning as they turn off the tv. "We are here to come up with solutions."

 "коммуникация" one of the females mutters, her companion giving her a nod. 

 A few others hesitate, look to one another untrusting...

 The first man stands though, once more, "Arina is right, unfortunately." walking around he grabs a bottle of clear liquid, pouring shots one after another. "We stay separate for the most part, we work is silent cells. And we are getting picked off.... One." a shot of glass goes down in front of one. "after another." another glass clinks down, "after Another." 

 One by one the scavs get their drink, metal and realflesh grabbing their shot glasses.

 "It's time for a change comrads. It's time for us to unite... It's time to make a joke of the Laughing Man..." Shots disappear one at a time before the standing man drinks. "And I have a plan..."

 The last glass goes down onto the table.
