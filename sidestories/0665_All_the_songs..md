Every chapter that Motoko even _thinks_ about singing a song will without fail inspire at least three pages of responses about other songs she could sing.

 Not that I'm complaining, but think about it from poor Seras point of view. Each song they write into the story is at least another 600 to 800 words!

 Well, unless we get creative.

* * *

 Hiromi: "So what song are you working on today Motoko?"

 Motoko: "None, they're all done."

 Hiromi: "You finished writing multiple songs in a single afternoon?"

 Motoko: "I finished writing _all_ the songs over the last three months while you've been at work selling XBD's and Ichi's been busy with his truck job."

 Hiromi: "So you just sat in your room for the past three months and wrote a bunch of songs?"

 Motoko: "All of the songs."

 Hiromi: "How many songs exactly though?"

 Motoko: "_All_ of the songs."

 Hiromi: "Jun? Do you know what she's talking about?"

_*Jun removes earmuffs.*_

 Jun: "What? I needed to get new ears because of all the noise! Doc said I have to keep these on for at least another week! Did you ask me something?"

 Hiromi: "How many songs did your sister write?"

 Jun: "I don't know? At least, more then twelve? I haven't been able to hear very well. This is my third pair of ears!"
