Motoko Deals!​

  
 Motoko's Discount Rates/Favor Trade Rates:  
   
 10% off in exchange for aid in a prank against a friend/family member.  
 20% off in exchange for help in getting tickets/access to public event and/or event item  
 30% off in exchange for day off/mental health day/sleep in day/no pants day/no I totally didn't forget something important day  
 40% off in exchange for access to fluffy animals to pet and cuddle with  
 50% off in exchange one free phone call with Wakako just to shoot the breeze and hang out  
 60% off in exchange for one free unaltered photo of Wakako honestly smiling  
 70% off in exchange for one free unaltered photo of Wakako happily hugging one of her grandchildren  
 80% off in exchange for one free unaltered photo of Wakako's wild younger years  
 90% off in exchange for a day of fun with Wakako in place of Motoko's choice  
 100% off in exchange for one honest hug and 'atta girl' from Wakako ![`:D](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7 "Grin    `:D")  
   
 Wakako could only stare blankly at the contents of the shard before she looked back into the smiling face of the Solo who came over to her office to give it to her.  
   
 "You can't be serious," Wakako said simply.  
   
 "You asked for my rates!" Motoko pointed out.  
   
 "I did, didn't I," Wakako admitted. "I suppose a foolish request deserved a foolish response."  
   
 "One of these days I'll get that hug!" Motoko yelled out loudly for anyone in the Pachinko Parlor to hear, but were wise enough not to react to, as she left the building.  
   
 "She's one of my best agents, she's one of my best agents..." Wakako silently chanted to herself as she exercised all her hard-earned self-control to not put a hit out on the girl.  
   
 It was a challenge every single day.
