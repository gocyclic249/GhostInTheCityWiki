**_"Of course I wouldn't murder you Hiromi. I wouldn't do that. Ever. You aren't allowed to die after all."_**

 "Not sure if I'm scared or... Yeah anyway, Jun made sure to make sure I asked... What happened to all the XXL burrito vending machines? I mean, I know it was you and they are all destroyed... but why? Jun was crying you know?"

**_"THOSE THINGS ARE NO LONGER ALLOWED TO EXIST! THEY ARE MY NEMESIS MY GRUDGE WILL NOT END UNTIL THE SUN ITSELF DIES. NOW THEN. Do not concern yourself with such petty things, you have a four o'clock meeting with Arasaka, he wishes my assistance in handling the completion of his pathetic Soulkiller V.0.1 You may take from him whatever you want for the information or not Hiromi. After all. No one can harm you now."_**

 Hiromi looked away from the computer that Motoko's voice was coming out of, in her penthouse office, the Arasaka building was now Hiromi's domain. She was the ruler of Night City now, not that she had to do much with Motoko the AI goddess controlling everything around her, but it wasn't like Motoko was too overbearing..

 Most of the time.

 Now she just needed some way to calm Jun down. He had refused to leave his room and his pile of XXL burrito wrappers that he had collected. Apparently the smell was the only thing that brought him any comfort? It was pretty gross though, and Motoko would go ballistic if Jun died.

 Hiromi was going to have to buy out what was left of XXL burritos and make XXXL burritos... Although that might be something else. She would workshop the name... Without Motoko's help this time.
