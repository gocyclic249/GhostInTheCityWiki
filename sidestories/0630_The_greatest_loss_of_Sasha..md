The greatest loss of Sasha.

 ----------------------------

 Sasha had slunk into Yoko's den as she was invited for a talk with her. She was well aware of the scornful looks of her fellow netrunners.

 They talked about what happened and Sasha felt they cleared the air a little bit.

 As the meeting came to an end, Yoko said: 

 "One last thing, Sasha. Motoko was my apprentice before she met you. After what has happened, you will lose..."

 "No, stop, don't continue!" Sasha yelled and fell to her knees, tears gathering in her eyes, guessing already what Yoko wanted to say.

 But Yoko continued with her fox like smile, taking immense pleasure by doing so. 

 "Yes, you will lose your rights to be called 'Senpai'!"

 After that, only a loud "Nooooooo!" could be heard from the room, drowning the well known "Hohohoho" of female laughter from Yoko.
