 "How did you get so fast, Motoko?" Hiromi asked.

 Motoko shrugged. "Oh, I had some cyberware installed. Can't you see?"

 "All I can see are Gorilla Arms. Did you get some hidden work done on your legs?"

 "No. Just the Gorilla Arms." Motoko said, flexing.

 "What?"

 "Uh... I can pump my arms faster while running, so my legs go faster to keep up?"

 "That's not how that works, Motoko."

 "What's that? I can't hear you!" Motoko shouted as she ran away at Olympic speeds.

 "You can't keep running away from my questions!" Hiromi shouted after her, riding her Kusanagi after her escaping choom.

 "Fuck." Motoko said as she was quickly caught up to. Maybe she should have sprung for the cyber eyes after all, maybe then she could outrun all her problems.
