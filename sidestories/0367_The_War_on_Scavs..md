This thought refused to leave me alone, unfortunately, I couldn't figure out a way to finish it in a satisfying way before 2 am so it deteriorates and ends abruptly. 

 The War on Scavs.

 "In a surprising turn of events the winner of this year's election is Motoko Kusanagi! After declaring Scavs as 'Public Enemy Number One' and spearheading the War on Scavs campaign she has single-handedly dropped the crime rate by 30% and reduced missing person cases by a staggering 43%. We're outside of Mayor Kusanagi's apartment right now for an exclusive interview."

 "Bwuh?" Totally not falling off the couch in surprise I stumbled closer to the TV gaping at the news. 

 Somehow I managed to win the election that I didn't even notice was happening without even running. 

 Rushing outside I was immediately assailed by a swarm of reporters.

 "I'm Gillean Jordan with N54 News. It is said you started your 'War on Scavs' campaign after being attacked in your own home, could you elaborate on that?"

 "Is it true that you were attacked by Scavs and put in a year-long coma?"

 "One at a time! Yes, Scavs attacked me and my brother while we were in this very apartment. Yes, I was previously attacked by Scavs before and was left in a coma with amnesia." Cutting the reporters off before they could continue I tried to gain some control of the situation.

 "Mayor Kusanagi! What is your plan now that Scavs have been pushed out of the city?"

 Panicking I searched for a solution, and said the first thing that came to mind.

 "I will build a great wall, and the Scavs will pay for it!" Freezing for a moment I processed what I just said, kicked cool into high gear, and doubled down.

 "The proceeds from recovering cyberware in Scav dens will all go toward Scav eliminating initiatives."

 Tripling down on the memes I announced, "I will make Night City great again!"

 The reporters erupted into excited chatter.

 "I have a Dream! That one day someone could walk through Night City without fearing for their life. That Night City will be a land of the TRULY free, dammit."
