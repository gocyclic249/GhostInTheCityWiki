The 'Talk'​

 After dropping Motoko off at her place Ichi and Hiromi drove off in his van, making their way back to the club with Malcolm's and Omaeda's vehicles following shortly behind them. The two were silent as they were still processing how the day went and the part they had in it, even if a relatively small part compared to a mutual friend of there's apparently going all edgerunner and taking out a Scav den all on her own, only calling them up to help loot their place before giving nearly all said loot away to some random Riperdoc.

 At least said Ripperdoc didn't _want_ to totally cheat them and paid 10,000 eddies to Motoko who then split it among the crew, and even Malcolm getting lucky and getting a free Smart Link install. But then again they couldn't really complain what Motoko did with said loot considering fact she was that earned it and they were her moving team at best.

 But what really got them all was just how... chill she was during the whole thing, before and after. Which brought up some understandable concerns.

 "Before you say anything, no Motoko is _not_ going cyberpsycho," Hiromi spoke up the second she saw Ichi start opening his mouth, causing him to snap it shut. "For one thing, she never had a bunch of chrome in the first place, and even less after the Scavs grabbed her. She is like, the most ganic person I fucking know! She's not normal crazy either, just... Jun is being a total gonk and she needed to let out some serious steam on the one group _no one_ would be against getting flatlined and just so happened she has a serious and totally justified hate for."

 Ichi just sighed.

 "I don't think she is going cyberpsycho or crazy, just that... she is way different than she used to be."

 "I repeat, I want to see _you_ come out the same after Scavs slice you up and wake up from a year long coma with your memories fried," Hiromi fired back, which Ichi had no good response too so just kept quiet.

 Not long after that though, both Malcolm and Omaeda called them both up for a group chat.

 *This Smart Link is fucking preem guys!*

 *Still think its a waste without matching optics and actual smart weapon to make the most use out of it.*

 *Says you. There are tons of things I can use this baby for beyond just smart guns. I can connect it to all kinds of smart stuff, maybe even my ride if I can upgrade it later.*

 *Gonna need several big paydays like this to make that happen... Speaking of, this gonna happen again anytime soon?*

 "Naw, pretty sure Motoko is gonna take it easy, what with her arm and all, for awhile. Will give her a couple of days and then check back in to see how she is doing," Hiromi said. "Should be weeks at least, assuming she doesn't just use her eddies to get herself a new arm, but she would totally tell me if she did something like that. Besides, she promised that the next time she does something crazy like hit a Scav den she will call us up beforehand to back her up... in whatever way we can at least."

 *Man, Motoko has gotta real scary since she woke up. She is totally becoming an Edgerunner at this rate.*

 *Not sure if that is a good thing for our own long-term health... but I can't deny this is the best payday we've had ever. I'll be sure to share with you guys if any of the data shards I clipped have info on other Scav dens the same time I tell Motoko, make sure we know the deets beforehand and all that before she asks us to do anything crazy.*

 "Come on guys, she wouldn't do that," Hiromi waved off their concerns. "Like said, she will be bedridden for awhile and promised she would keep me informed about important stuff as it comes up."

 "Motoko is very much changed from the friend you used to know, Hiromi," Ichi pointed out.

 "I'm not doubting that, but she is still my choom and chooms stick together," Hiromi said with finality.

 *Hey, if I can get more sweet chrome I am at least open to crazy ideas from time to time.*

 *So long as I'm not directly in the line of fire, since that is not my strength, I am not against working with Motoko more in the future either.*

 Ichi just grumbled into his steering wheel before sighing and nodding his head.

 "Then its settled! We earned good eddies today for basically doing nothing, so no more complaining!" Hiromi ordered.

 *Could have gotten more if we slowly worked through that chrome through various sources and such...*

 *But then we wouldn't get a Ripperdoc's good side. He did say we would get discounts on chipping in and chrome! Besides, if nothing else Scavs will horde chrome like rats and we can get in on that action.*

 "Let's not plan anything right now," Ichi interrupted. "Let's just take it easy and do our usual gigs for now since those are much more predictable and steady."

 "And boring," Hiromi couldn't help with the jab, earning her a glare from Ichi.

 "You want to walk?"

 "I'll be good," Hiromi giggled as Ichi just sighed again.

 *You sure that Motoko won't do anything else for a bit, Hiromi? She doesn't seem like type to sit on her butt for long anymore.*

 "Please, after all that action at the Scav den, what kinda trouble could she possibly get into until she is 100% again?"

_Somewhere in the Badlands outside of Night City_

 "This fucking sucks," Motoko muttered to herself as she bled all over the sand as the storm whipped all around her.
