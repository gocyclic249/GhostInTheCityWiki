 "Well glad to know it's not one of my girls doing this for once."

 The [Caterer](https://comicbook.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/4/2024/02/895f95d8-0c57-4953-b144-5da85038a801.jpg) looks on in muted horror as Motoko demolishes the Sushi platter.

 Hiromi gives her a deep bow.

 Mrs. McNamara I apologize for my client she's not quite accustomed to formal business settings.

 Melissa shakes her head in pity for the Caterer then redirects her attention to Hiromi

 "Oh nonsense my dear this is hardly anything,

 My youngest daughter used to do the same thing to food platters offered at any company party I had to take family to attend.

 My granddaughter also...ah [here she is now](https://images-wixmp-ed30a86b8c4ca887773594c2.wixmp.com/f/25530c1b-5c89-4c85-8b65-8602af58c383/dftt6aa-b2e0528d-3275-42c0-ac82-72f923e7bb4e.png/v1/fill/w_894,h_894,q_70,strp/enid_cyberpunk__1__by_punkerlazar_dftt6aa-pre.jpg?token=eyJ0eXAiOiJKV1QiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9.eyJzdWIiOiJ1cm46YXBwOjdlMGQxODg5ODIyNjQzNzNhNWYwZDQxNWVhMGQyNmUwIiwiaXNzIjoidXJuOmFwcDo3ZTBkMTg4OTgyMjY0MzczYTVmMGQ0MTVlYTBkMjZlMCIsIm9iaiI6W1t7InBhdGgiOiJcL2ZcLzI1NTMwYzFiLTVjODktNGM4NS04YjY1LTg2MDJhZjU4YzM4M1wvZGZ0dDZhYS1iMmUwNTI4ZC0zMjc1LTQyYzAtYWM4Mi03MmY5MjNlN2JiNGUucG5nIiwiaGVpZ2h0IjoiPD0xOTIwIiwid2lkdGgiOiI8PTE5MjAifV1dLCJhdWQiOlsidXJuOnNlcnZpY2U6aW1hZ2Uud2F0ZXJtYXJrIl0sIndtayI6eyJwYXRoIjoiXC93bVwvMjU1MzBjMWItNWM4OS00Yzg1LThiNjUtODYwMmFmNThjMzgzXC9wdW5rZXJsYXphci00LnBuZyIsIm9wYWNpdHkiOjk1LCJwcm9wb3J0aW9ucyI6MC40NSwiZ3Jhdml0eSI6ImNlbnRlciJ9fQ.qJvB6iPt1mOq3VyDaK3ZyZTR2Ej9YZbTK0ZSi-cD_C8).

 Said girl stares at the rapidly vanishing offerings of sushi.

 "Hey don't eat it all, I want some too!"

 Motoko looks up from the platter her mouth full of sushi.

 "Huh?"

 The other girls tech hair transitions through a number of colors as she takes some chopsticks and ties to snatch some sushi from the serving platter before Motoko devours it all.

 Hiromi Blinks in surprise as she sees the new girl.

 "Wait Traci Kensington is your granddaughter?"

 The now named Traci Kensington was currently dueling Motoko with their respective chopsticks trying to get the last portions of the sushi.

 The other members of the gathering were making amused comments at the display with more than one lawyer making bets on who would get the last piece of sushi.

 Melissa shakes her head before letting out a sharp cough.

 Traci stops he efforts to beat Motoko and looks around the room her face becoming more pale.

 "Oops, I'm Sorry Grandma i got a bit carried away."

 Letting out a tired sigh Melissa McNamara gestures towards Hiromi.

 "Perhaps you can introduce yourself to the young lady I am in negotiations with for the services of your opponent?"

 Traci Blinks in surprise at seeing Hiromi.

 "Hiromi Mitsunashi?"

 Motoko looks between the two.

 "So how do you two know each other?"

 Hiromi raises an eyebrow at Motoko.

 "Traci also attends Araska academy, I know her parents and aunt worked for Danger Gal and her grandfather was some kind of Information specialist.

 I was however unaware her grandmother was Melissa Logan."

 "It's McNamara these days, Logan was my maiden name and L.A.W. was a better name for the firm instead of M.A.W. despite what lawyer jokes and comments about Jeri would claim otherwise."

 A [woman](https://i.pinimg.com/736x/b6/7c/0f/b67c0fde7a69d3108f2d3539af154067.jpg) walks over to them with an amused look on her face.

 "Oh I don't know about that, M.A.W. does have a certain ring to it and puts the fear of god into our opponents in court.

 I see Traci has some new competition for demolishing Takahashi's sushi platter this time."

 Melissa chuckles at the other woman's comment.

 "Hiromi Mitsunashi this is Jeri Anderson the most cutthroat lawyer you'll ever meet in Night City."

 Jeri smirks at Melissa.

 "Keep saying sweet things like that and I'll have to steal you from your husband."

 Melissa gives Jeri a teasing smile in return.

 Now now Jeri you know very well you'll have to share if you want this.

 Motoko leans over to Traci

 "So are they always like that?"

 Traci sighs then snags a piece of sushi when Motoko isn't looking.

 "Yup pretty much, I can never tell if they're joking or not."

 "What kind of Law firm is this place?"

 "The finest in Night city, we count many high profile celebrities among our clients and our success rate is second to none."

 Both Motoko and Traci look over to the [tall woman](https://i.pinimg.com/736x/73/b5/0d/73b50de5b16ffed3d05460f69eadf200.jpg) talking to them.

 "Hello I am Anastasia Wick and you must be Motoko Kusanagi, our information & security consultant gave you high praise in his recommendation.

 I also see Traci has demolished our sushi platter yet again."

 Traci gives the lawyer an indignant squawk and points at Motoko.

 "She ate most of it, I didn't demolish it this time!"

 Anastasia looks over at the Caterer.

 "Is this true Takahashi, did Miss Kusanagi manage to actually beat our little Traci to the punch and match her bottomless pit of a stomach?"

 "Yes Ma'am the hungry kitten now has another rival besides her aunt, I fear we will have to take out the second platter early."

 "Very well bring out the additional platter and a yardstick to rap Traci's hands if she reaches for it.

 Miss Kusanagi if you and miss Mitsunashi would be so kind as to come with us to conference room three so we can discuss the task we wish to hire you for."
