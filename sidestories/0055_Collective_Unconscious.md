"So what does this have to do with my Sister!?" Jun asked as he paced the Tyger Claws owned bar.

 "You might have to face the fact that your sister is not your sister..." Netrunner hired by Wakako stated serenely, after telling them about some kind of Corp experiment that his sister was apparently caught up in. 

 "What the hell is that supposed to mean!?" Jun demanded angrily, as he glared at this...bitch.

 "What do you remember happening-" The Netrunner said before listing off a date and time, some time before he was told his sister was awake.

 "I was staring in the mirror and wondering how I could still be...human?" Jun stated immediately and with some confusion as he remembers being on the couch mourning over his sister, only to suddenly see himself as a Borg with an almost bunny-like head. The name Briareos echoing in his head momentarily.

 "I remember the blood dripping from my blades," The Netrunner stated calmly as she calmly cut a row of glasses in half without breaking them and with a speed that shocked the room, especially since all they could see was her drawing the Katana a bare inch before pushing it back in. "I grew up a street rat and never touched a Katana before that day, and yet when forced into a corner by some Scavs I grabbed this broken Katana and cut them down as if it was as easy as breathing..."

 "What are you saying exactly?" Dansuke who looked somewhat pale and haunted as if he also had a similar experience, judging by the startled and somewhat pale looks of others. He wasn't alone."

 "...Have you ever heard of the Collective Unconscious..."

 ------------------------------------------------- 

 A Terse Explanation Later...

 "So you are saying some Corpo's decided to use coma patients and those in a vegetative state to try some kind of experiment to stimulate the brain and fucked up!?" Jun demanded, the explanation that apparently the flash he had was from a previous life of his going a little over his head.

 "According to your sister's confidential file..." The Netrunner, Koga said in a serene tone. "The Doctor's examination made it clear that if your sister woke up the amount of damage to her brain would have left her a blank slate incapable of getting better, and yet she not only fully recovered with only amnesia... But she is in fact very able to take care of herself."

 "What are you implying..." Jun growled as his hand closed very firmly onto the back of a chair, the dark thought that they had been only keeping his sister alive to bilk him of money all of this time flashing across his brain. "Cause it sounds like you are saying my sister never should have woken up without this experiment and this Collective Unconscious..."

 "I am stating that your sister essentially died in that Scav den," Koga stated with a sigh. "right now you are basically dealing with a memory back up of her previous life. Thankfully unlike the Subject calling themselves Alex Mercer, she is relatively normal instead of a walking bio-plague that the Corpo's managed to put down by killing every living thing is a four block radius..."

 "...She is still my sister..." Jun stated grimly and confidently, it didn't matter if she may have been someone else before.

 "I came here to inform you of this so that you will know that you should not trust her unconditionally," Koga replied. "personally I believe her actions show that she is very loyal to you and if she truly was treacherous you would have found out through her actions by now. She might be somewhat...weird from what I have heard, but she is very competent."

 "What do you mean she is very competent!?" Jun demanded as he crushed the back of the chair with chromed out arms. "What has she been doing that a Netrunner thinks she is competent!?"

 "You do know that she now works for Wakako as a Edgerunner right?" Koga asked with a raised eyebrow.

 "...WHAT!?" Jun shout could be heard throughout the bar and in fact around the block.
