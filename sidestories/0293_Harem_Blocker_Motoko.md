 Harem member A: "Is it done?"

 Harem member B: "The Door is locked."

 Harem member C: "Windows are shuttered."

 Harem member A: "Target?"

 Harem member B: "On his way back from the vending machine."

 Harem member A: "Good wait one minute then unlock the door, where are the candles?"

 Harem member C: "By the nightstand, what's the status of 'Lithium falcon?' "

 Harem member A: "I paid Wakako good money to distract her with a gig tonight. BD's?"

 Harem member C: "One pay per view of 'max steel' and another for 'Watson whore' later tonight."

_*Suddenly there is a knock on the door.*_

 Jun: "Hey! Why'd you lock the door? I was only gone for two minutes."

 Harem member B: "Coming, We have a surprise for you!"

 Jun: " A surprise? What surpris-"

_*Suddenly an armored Quadra comes crashing through the window crushing the candles and carefully arranged burrito's that spell out 'We love you Jun.'*_

 Motoko: "Cough, cough...Uh so much dust, Jun?... If you're here, this isn't my fault!"

 *Jun breaks down the door.*

 Jun: "What the hell!?"

 Motoko: "Jun I need my grenades, the wraiths decided to follow me home. Stupid Wakako couldn't even tell me which camp to hit."

 Jun: "Motoko our home!"

 Motoko: "Tell that to the wraiths! One of them has a rocket launcher Jun! -Oh hey girls! Gang meeting?"

 Harem member ABC: "..."

 *Motoko sifts through the remains of her room before finding a box and pulling out a grenade.*

 Motoko: "...Don't suppose we could crash at one of your places for a couple days?"

 Harem member ABC: "Oh yeah of course!/Anything for my little sister/ No problem!"

 Jun: "Motoko we can't impose on them like that! I'll just get some tarps from the 25/7."

_*All the girls stare at him.*

 *Motoko chucks a grenade out the window.*_
