**Mokoto: The Inner Weeb Awakens**

 Jun looked upon his sister in shear disbelief, in had been a fairly normal day before he got home. Talk to some friends, shook down a shop owner, got a burrito and killed a Scav. You know the day-to-day activities of living in Night City.

 But as soon as he got home he was in for a real sight.

 His sister in a looonnnggg black coat (emphasis on _**long**_), a just as black fedora that seemed to always shadow her eyes and her covered in katana's that he was sure were stolen from the shop down the street.

 Oh also she was standing underneath a broken fire sprinkle that he was certain was meant to be her pretending is rain.

 "Uh, Mokoto? What are you doing?"

 She barely looked up as she spoke "Ah, brother you are here. I apologize, I was in deep constipation."

 he blinked "Constip- do you mean contemplation?"

 She flinched for a second "Um- yes! A small twist of the tongue!"

 he gave a flat look "huh-huh, sooo.... Why are you.... '_contemplating?_'

 She regained some of her bravado back. "Ah! I have been brought before the great temple in Night City and been made a sword saint! With this act I have achieved enlightenment! For you see, while you were hanging with your chooms, I was practicing the sword! While you were eating your awful burritos I was-"

 He drowned out her monologue and messaged Hiromi, the girl was more likely to tell him what was wrong with his sister.

"Hiromi, sis is acting weird. Y?" he thought typed.

 I took a minute before replying "She overused her sandy deflecting bullets, let me guess? Practiced my sword speech?"

 He looked at his sister still going on about the sword "Yep."

 After a second "Get her to bed, should be better tomorrow. We have the holos to embarrass her to not do what she did too much in the future."

 He paused hearing that "Send copies please" And after a second he got some files. After that he looked at his sister, he knew what to do in this situation. Seen it happen plenty of times before.

 Jun walked up to Motoko who was still talking under the broken sprinkler.

 "-You were riding the metro, I was practicing the blade! While you were-"

 "Ahem!" He interrupted, making her pause and glare. "Sis, you what to know how to empower you...abilities."

 He nearly shudder when he said that last word, he had to deal with this situation more than he'd like. Sensai usually had him deal with situations like this, he hated to deal with gonks.

 But his sister although glaring from the shadow of her hat seemed interested. "Oh? You think you have wisdom that I don't have? Please then, inform me of my lack of technique so I can hone my blade further!"

 "You name your attacks like this." he said bring his arm up "Brother! Chop!" and brought it down on her head.

 "Gah!" was the only sound she made before she dropped on to the wet floor.

 He just shook his head. "Damn Chuuni stages, hopefully this is just Sandy induced." as he picked her up, he looked at the broken fire sprinkler making him wet. "How am I gonna fix that?!"

 Ugh! His little sis could be a gonk sometimes!
