Jun

 Jun startled awake, unsure what he just heard. "What?"

 "Ugh, I asked if you and your umm friend, wanted breakfast!" Jun looked around even more confused.

 "Motoko what are you talking about? No one else is here?" He half asked wondering if someone was on their couch? Man he was tired, he had helped some tyger claws set up a lot later than he wanted but everyone was having fun, drinks coming long into the night. Motoko peeked her head in and looked around trying to spot whoever she made up in her mind.

 "Jun, you're sure you didn't ask someone to hide or something?" She kept looking around but didn't move from the door.

 "Motoko, I'm too tired for whatever this is, my head hurts and I just want to go back to sleep!" He closed his eyes and took a bite of his XXL burrito trying to tune out his brat of a sister, at least she didn't jump on him this morning.

 "Jun... you, the burrito?" Her voice came out confused and disgusted but Jun just kept his eyes closed as he tried to sleep. "Jun, you were saying some very.. colorful things and making some umm kissing noises... Did you really confess and make out with a burrito from last night in your sleep?" Oh, oooh, part of his dream came back to him then, it wasn't about an XXL but, Motoko would never let him live this down! Just pretend nothing happened!

 "I'm sleeping! Please go away!" It really didn't help that he had to chew before speaking!

 "Piifffffffffffff" The brat wheezed before bellowing out a laugh. "Do I need to have the birds and burrito talk with you Jun-nii? Wonder if they make onesies in XXL! Will your wedding have a flower girl or a napkin boy?"

 I jumped up and started chasing Motoko, my hair, eyes and cheeks red but I had to shut her up! "Motoko I swear I'm..."

 "At least I can see a wrapper so we don't have to worry about XXS burritos under foot anytime soon!" Maelstrom could have her back!
