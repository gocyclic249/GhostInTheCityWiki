 *Motoko and Jackie are on a gig, Jackie won the ro-sham-bo so they're going loud, but the compound got some extra unexpected security.*

 "Hermanita, we're gonna need some bigger guns."

 "Yes! Finally! I've been waiting for this moment my whole life! Or, at least since Tuesday when I set this up!" Her eyes glow gold and she looks down the street, bouncing on her feet (and getting several inches of airtime.)

 A MaiMai drives around the nearby intersection. It's entirely blacked out, windows and all. It slows to a halt nearby.

 "Your gun guy drives a MaiMai? Choom. This is beneath you. You can do better."

 "What? No, this isn't my gun guy." They walk up to the trunk and Motoko pulls it open. Every surface is stocked to the brim. Guns, grenades, ammo. Revolving racks fill the space where the seats should have been, displaying Unity's and Burya's like a cornerstore would magazines, though he notices plenty of those too, for guns of all types. A rock with googly eyes attached Jackie quickly decides to pretend he never saw looks over them.

 "This is MaiMai armoury."
