_Some time later..._

 Motoko: "Uhhhhh!"

 Jun: "What is it now?"

 Motoko: "I just got another job offer from Wakako. Apparently everyone wants to hire the 'next Morgan Blackhand,' or the 'second coming of Spider Murphy,' she's getting annoyed. Keeps cursing at me about how she isn't my secretary. So now I'm annoyed."

 Jun: "Can't you just turn down the jobs?"

 Motoko: "You try responding no politely to 774 job offers a week. Especially when they ask for you as the unholy love child of Spider Murphy and Morgan Blackhand."

 Jun: "Well if you joined the Claws-"

 Motoko: "Jun, at this point I don't think hiding behind the claws rep would work. Face it _I'm bigger then them._ If anything they want me so that they can hide behind mine."

 Jun: "Motoko the claws are family-"

 Motoko: "No their _your_ family Jun. You where on your own looking after me for a long time, I get it. You wanted somewhere to belong and they took you in, but that doesn't mean that I want them to be _my_ family. I have you, and Hiromi, Vik, and Misty, the boys... All I need, You've never been outside of the gang. Not like I have, believe me when I say your life is worth far more then they think it is."

 Jun: "I'm respected."

 Motoko: "No you're _useful._ That's all you'll ever be in the eyes of the gang. Just remember that the next time they congratulate you for taking a bullet for another brothel owner."

 Jun: "...Is this really how you see everything?"

_*A data chip is tossed in his direction.*_

 Motoko: " The solo's manual. words to live by. Just promise me you'll give it a read."

 Jun: "...Fine imouto I'll read it."

 Motoko: "Good."

 Jun: "...Why is the bookmark on page four?"

 Motoko: "...I like that passage."

 Jun: "...It's a manual for how to properly tie your shoes before going on a gig."

 Motoko: "...It's very important."
