 "Fujimura already gave you the apartment before you ever tried to convince me to move didn't he?" I accused him, eyes narrowed as I prepared for the answer I already knew.

 Jun blinked realizing he was suddenly standing in the middle of a minefield.

 "Nooo?" He said.

 "It doesnt matter," I said. "I made my own place and it's mobile too!"

 "You have your own..." He hesitated for a moment. "Wait, did you say 'made'?"

**~**[**One hour later**, middle of the badlands****](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MXcMV-d_2Js)****~****​

 "It's really cool right?" I said trying not to be too smug.

 "...I have questions." Jun spoke in an almost eerily calm manner.

 "Like?" I asked.

 "How? When?"

 "I already told you, I made it." I scowled at his lack of reaction. "And in my spare time." My house was cool damnit!

 "And more importantly..." He took a deep breath. "WHY THE FUCK MOTOKO!" Jun suddenly shouted pointing at [my mobile house](https://i.imgur.com/yAfNUPV.png).

 "IT'S COOL AND AWESOME JUN-NII, THAT'S WHY!" I shouted back, he was just jealous my home was cooler than his.

 "IT LOOKS LIKE IT CAN WIN ALL FOUR CORPO WARS _AT THE SAME TIME!"_

 "Nooo?"

 Jun glared. And pointed really hard at my creation.

"For the honor of the regiment!"
