 OR....

 The sound of feet rushing down the stairs startled Vik up from his Boxing match. Quickly looking around and grabbing a nearby shotgun, he readied himself for what was likely to be a Biotechnical Black Ops team here to "recruit" him.

 Make peace with his likely future, he pulled up his contacts and started firing off last words, a list that was incredibly short and contained people he had not spoke to for years. As he reached the " K" section, two names stood out to him.

 Two new people that he had allowed himself to open up slightly to, the young solo who despite all her skills and secrets, had a very kind heart and her brother who despite being a member of the gangs which Vik despised had one redeeming quality, his affection for his sister.

_[Motoko, i'm going away on a business trip to another city to source for some Cyberware. Don't know when i will be back, but here's a list of Rippers you can approach if you need maintence or new cyberware installed. Take Care and Stay Safe.]

 [Jun, please take care of your sister. You know how reckless she can get. Try to watch her six when she goes on jobs and prevent her from biting off more then she can chew.]_

 Messages sent, Vik readied his shotgun and aimed it at the doorway, ready to fire the moment the Biotech Black Ops team came into range.

***SLAM**

 That was the door. Gun held steady, finger curling on the trigger finger...

 "DOC! VIK! HEY VIK YOU HERE! NEED YOUR HELP WITH GETTING A NEW CYBERWARE MAN!"

 The foghorn shouts of Misty's paramour brought Vik to a halt. Jackie was a nice guy... Good for Misty, but damn that boy could be loud when he got excited. And since Jackie was utterly unable to betray someone for profit, it seemed that he had been overthinking things, which was a mite embarrassing.

 "I'm here Jackie, what do you need?"

 Even as he shouted a reply, Vik watched as Wannabe Solos started flooding into his clinic. The over-emphasis on "Solo" Clothes which were carbon copies of outfits worn by Solos who had made their way onto Afterlife's Drinks menu screamed to him about their inexperience.

 " OH HEY VIK! Do you have this new preem Cyberware "The Minor" is using? We've checked with all the other Rippers we knew and none of them heard of it. But i remembered Misty saying something about a kid who looked like "The Minor" chatting to her about Ghosts, so i thought she might be a client of yours!"

 "Jackie, even if she is i won't reveal my client's information to anyone. And what Cyberware? I haven't heard about something new on the market thus far."

 "Oh Doh! Here lemme show you a short bit i captured from the newest series of BDs from the Moxs"

 The Moxes, that struck a particular chord with Vik. Viewing the short vid from Jackie revealed that his suspicions were right, said series was starring Motoko and a quick glance at the choice bits Jackie had shown him revealed the new "Cyberware".

 The numberical overlay over each action done and the dopamine released on hitting certain milestones. The professional part of Vik's brain started churning as it started thinking up how it could be done.

_Build a database of actions sourced from those at the top of their field, assign a value to it and show the user the value based on how close they imitate the Professional Act. Imitate it enough and it becomes ingrained muscle memory, at that point release dopamine to reward the user._

 " I've never heard of it before Jackie, but i think i know how it can be done. Why don't you and the folks here head back now. I'll ask around and see if i can get any news about it."

 "Thanks Vik! You're the best! Okay boys and girls! the Doc has no info at the moment but he'll see what he can find! I'll let you all know when i get an update from him. In the meantime... Let's head to Lizzie's for shots! We might even be able to sneak a preview of the unreleased BDs!"

 As the crowd of wannabe solos made their way out chatting about the coolest bits from the BD, Vik pulled his contacts and headed to the "K" Section once more.

_[Motoko, i'm not mad. Just disappointed that you never thought to reveal to me you had such a dangerous bit of Cyberware installed for your BD series. Do you know the risks involved? That people could very easily be addicted to dopamine just as well as drugs? Does Jun know? Please drop by my Clinic ASAP so i can take a look at this cyberware. ]_
