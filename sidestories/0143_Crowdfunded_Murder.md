It was moments like this that made Wakko feel her age. Closing her eyes and taking several deep breathes to keep her blood pressure down, she focused on the unrepentant ~~Edgerunner~~ BRAT standing before her.

 "Kusanagi Motoko. Why i am finding a crowd-funding page to kill Jotaro Shobo on the Net?"

 "Well, you know i couldn't find that Regina person you directed me to, there was only this cool looking Netrunner who slammed the door in my face when i asked for Regina. So i thought, that if all that is needed is a Gig, i'll just help with the process of getting one started!

 I mean my XBD production team is already receiving pre-orders!"

_It was the tone of her voice_. Wakako decided, that innocent tone of "what's wrong" with dashes of "praise me! Aren't I SMART!" that was driving her to yank open the drawer of her desk and take out the bottle of Shochu she had stashed within.

 "You do realize that Jotaro Shobo now knows that there is someone gunning for him and that he thus use all of his pull and any favors he's owned to guard both his club as well as search for the source of the bounty?"

 "Pffft! He wishes he can find the person who posted this page who is clearly not me! And more people guarding him means more BRR- I MEAN more targets to take out in the XBD! You know how it is right? I'm just starting out as an XBD Star, so i need to up my game to be able to draw in the viewers!"

 ".......Go. Just go."

 " Sure thing Wakako! I'll be sure to drop you a copy of the XBD when it's done! You can help me share them with any prospective employers! BYEEEEEE!"

***SLAM.**

 As the door to her office slammed shut, Wakako looked at the bottle of Shochu on her desk and the tiny sake cup next to it. Calmly, she returned the sake cup back into the drawer of her desk before opening the bottle of Shochu and letting the smell of raw spirits fill the air of her office. 

 " Kikyo Chan? Please cancel all appointments and meetings i have today, i fear i will be indisposed."
