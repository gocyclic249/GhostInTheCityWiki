 Because i couldn't stop thinking about it. Have a Proof-Of-Concept/Rival Pitch of Revy in Cyberpunk2077 as a kind of Dark Mirror to Motoko. I spent way to much time thinking up what a Cyberpunk Revy would be like.

* * *

**Dark Mirror**

​

 "Hey Pops! I'm here! Are my Cutlasses ready?"

 "Damn it Revy! My door! Stop slamming it open with those steel mitts you call hands!"

 Rolling her eyes at the Old Man's usual routine, Revy made her way to the counter ignoring the aghast looks the nearby suits were giving her as they watched a Street Kid Solo plop herself down in one of the Premier Gun shops of Night City as if it was her home.

 The Kerenzikov she had installed meant that even waiting for the Old Man to finish with his current client was a bore that she could only relieve via scaring the Suits who wanted a Custom Malour piece for bragging rights. The constant clenching and release of her metal fists and steady drumming of her steel legs on the unyielding floor of the shop creating a dissonant din that had them flinching at every screech of steel against steel.

 "Will you quit it with that din Revy? I'm coming over already."

 "Finally Pops! Now show me the good stuff!"

 "Here brat. You should count yourself lucky i stopped making true customs ages ago. "

 " Lucky? You're the one who promised to make me a gun after I saved your gonk ass from being shot. And look at my chrome, i spent the last 5 years getting the chrome you mention i needed to wield one of your pieces."

 " I was trying to bargain you down brat! I was going to offer you one of the pieces in the shop that i'll tuned up for you when you protested! How was i to know that you're so gonk ass stubborn that you went all the way to Atlanta for the eddies needed to get the chrome?"

 " Cmon Pops, don't you like the new badass me?"

![Image 10](https://i.imgur.com/77nNiMp.png)​

 Eran Malour looked at the mouthy street brat before him, looking beyond the confidence, the chrome limbs he could still see shades of the tiny brat who distracted the Edgerunner after his life long enough for him to zero the hired killer.

 "Hey Pops? You getting dementia or something? Where are my guns?"

 "Rude brat. Here."

 Slamming down the custom case on the counter, Eran opened it with a flourish revealing a pair of sliver dual Malorian Arms 3516s as well as 6 loaded magazines of rounds.

 "As you requested. Codenamed Cutlass, built based on what i did for Sliverhand but harden enough to deflect blades and almost entirely mechanical in nature to prevent any chance of it being hacked. Even switching it to fire in Penetration Mode requires use to toggle it via the Fire Selector manually.

 Now, you sure your chrome can handle the recoil? This is still a Anti-Borg gun y'all know?"

 "Pfft, no takeback Pops, they're mine now!"

 Moving towards the culmination of 5 years of effort, Revy picked up her Cutlasses from the case solemnly and under Eran's watchful eye dissembled the pistols before putting it back together. Loading the pistols with a click, she reverently put them away into the shoulder holsters she wore before focusing on the rest of the magazines resting in the case.

 A brief command to her Cyberarms had them open up revealing a built-in Quickloader that she quickly slotted the Magazines into.

 "Thanks Old Man. Everything seems good. Here's the rest of the eddies! I'm off to test these babies out!"

 Revy managed to make her way to the door leading to the outside before the Old Man recovered enough to shout at her.

 " Brat! What's with the Eddies, didn't i promise to make the guns for free as long as you proved you could wield em? Take the eddies back!"

 "Pftttt. Just treat it as if i'm buying you a drink Pops!"

 With that parting shot, the doors to the outside closed yet again with a slam leaving the interior of the shop to once more resume it's atmosphere of one of the Premium Gun Shops of Night City.

 Before any of the suits could recover from Revy's hurricane like visit, Eran quickly made his way to the back of the shop leaving his employees to deal with the news of "Eran Malour making a Customized Piece" after decades of inactivity.

* * *

_Brick: Hey TwoHands, got a Gig for you. Need you to clear out a Tyger Claw Safehouse. You do that and the debt for getting you the Kerenzikov is squared. I still have no idea how you can manage that by the way. 

 Revy: Deal. Location?_

* * *

 The place Brick had sent her to was a wreck. Clearly the Claws were trying to rely on the strategy of making the safehouse look so shitty that no one would guess it was one.

 Shame that they got discovered because now she was going to make the place look even shitter with blood and death while putting her new pieces through her pace.

 Walking towards the hole serving as a door, Revy slowly drew her Cutlasses from their holsters before her hands moved in a blur as she snap of a series of shots at the Claws that came out of hiding to scare the "Civi" away.

 The sound of the gunshots seemed to have kicked off a hornet's nest as before her first victims could even fall to the ground, Claws burst out of the wreck with Sandevistan enhanced speed that she could only catch due to her Kerenzikov enhanced nerves.

 Dodging the blade coming at her with a backsway, Revy's Cutlasses sang as they spat fire at her assailant. The massive rounds putting fist sized holes into the ambitious claw and dropping him to the ground even as Revy did a backflip that had her drawing a bead on another 2 targets charging at her with blades drawn.

 Displaying how she earned the moniker of "Twohands", she fired at them with pinpoint accuracy despite the bobs and weaves the duo were doing to get her into blade reach. The accuracy of the shots turned their smooth sprint into a uncoordinated tumble as death took them away before their bodies could react.

 Yet while she had eliminated the Claws charging at her with Sandevistan enhanced speed, they had brought enough time for the rest of the Claws within the Safehouse to arm up which they were now putting to use as a barrage of fire rained down at her.

 Her Kerenzikov meant that despite the barrage of fire coming down, she was able to seize the little seconds between the shots from different guns and move from cover to cover towards the entrance of the Wreck all the while letting her Cutlasses sing as she return fire.

 A flick of the Cutlasses' Fire selector had it set to Penetration Mode with a faint electromagnetic whine accompanying each shot as the rounds blew their way through the flimsy wall the Claws were hiding behind, forcing them to cut short their barrage and back away from the windows they were using to shoot at her.

 As she finally entered the wreck, Revy found herself ambushed by a borged up member of the Claws, the judicious usage of his Sandevistan for brief bursts of movements meant to throw her aim off indicate his status as a veteran and likely a member of the Claws famed "Kamikaze" platoon. But she wasn't a rookie either, and so biding her shots Revy waited for him to get too close to dodge all the while firing shots meant to lure him into a sense of false security.

_3. 2. 1! Got you now fuc-_

 Once again proving his skills as a veteran, the Kamikaze borg broke the established rhythm with a sudden Sandevistan boosted charge at Revy, laying his bet on the fact that she would be too surprised by the change to be able to draw a bead on him before he reached her.

 And he did bet right, she wasn't able draw a bead on him fast enough to shoot. But proving that despite being nicknamed "Twohands" and making a name with her skills with a pistol did not mean she was helpless without them, Revy threw a kick powered by the full might of muscle kinetics meant to allow her to leap between buildings.

 Despite the chrome the Kamikaze borg had on him, the full force of the kick channeled into the center of his chest threw him backwards with a visible dent in his subdermal armor. But most importantly, it brought Revy enough space to let her Cutlasses express her displeasure at being tricked by him as she emptied the rest of her clips into him.

 A brief mental command had the quickloader built into the forearms of her Cyberarm pop open with fresh magazines for her pistols as she reloaded them with click as the non-speedware enhanced members of the Claws finally reacted to the end of her fight with the borg.

![Image 11](https://i.makeagif.com/media/1-19-2023/DcmgZi.gif)​

 Almost belatedly, the katanas they wielded swung at her. Yet without any speedware enhancing their movements, it was child's play for Revy to bat away their blades with her pistols as she crushed throats and shattered skulls with brutal kicks the Claws could barely see let alone stop.

 The lack of any other borgs or sandevistan equipped claws appearing to stop her meant that she had took out not just the chrome equipped members of the gang but also their entire command structure. Leaving nothing behind but the canon fodder that had not even earn a place with the Claws like the ones she had just put an end to.

 Usually this was the part of the Gig where she left and allowed the Client to wipe up the fodder, but the current Gig called or her to clear out the entire Safehouse before contacting Brick.

_Ergh. Having to clear out the fodder is such a waste of ammo._

* * *

_Please don't find me. Please don't find me.Pleasedon'tfindme.pleasedon'tfindme_

 Mentally chanting his plea and forcefully clamping down on his own mouth, Takuya held his breath and pleaded for **_Twohands_**to just go even as he watched her get closer and closer to the security room he was cowering in.

 She wasn't even making an effort to hide from the cameras wired into the Safehouse. She strolled as if she was doing a needed but boring chore as she shot his friends regardless if they hid, they fought or they begged.

 There was absolutely no tension nor worry on her part, even he could tell that her Kerenzikov boosted reflexes meant she was able to spot and fire first the moment any of the remaining survivors even poke their guns out of cover.

 It seemed like such a adventure when he volunteered to help play Netrunner for the Claws. A way to put the screws to his parents' attempt to force him into the 2nd Generation Corpo box.

 Right now, the monitors were showing him the likely future of his adventure. Holes the size of a fist turning bodies unrecognizable, dismembered limbs showing what happens when Anti-Borg rounds hit limbs, Bullet holes oozing blood as the rounds go through Inch thick cover as if they were paper.

_SHITSHitShitshit why did i take this job!? What's so good about joining the Claws. I don't want to die!_

***Thud. Thud. BOOM. Thud. Thud BOOM**

 The sound of footsteps and gunshots started to get louder and louder as she got closer and closer.

***Thud.**

 The footsteps. It stopped. Utterly frozen with terror, all Takuya could do was freeze as he saw the door leading to the security room start to gradually swing open before it came to a sudden halt.

 "Oh Brick? What's up? Me? I'm just checking the place to make sure i got all the Claws in the place. Oh? You want me to go check out a Safehouse of yours that went quiet even after you sent 3 trucks worth of men over after an alarm was sounded?

 Sure i can do that. It'll cost you though. More if i have to fight as well. Alright, we have a deal. Send me the eddies. Pleasure doing business with you."

***Thud. Thud.**Thud. **Thud.**Thud.********

 Takuya felt his heart start to beat again as the sounds of **_Twohands_**footsteps recede into the distance.

_I swear i'll never do this again! I'll utterly stop meeting up with those of my friends who are in the Claws. Arasaka Academy's Top scorer that's where i'm headed fo-_

* * *

***BOOOOOOOM, CRASH**

 As the grenade she tossed into the Safehouse's armory went off and proved to be the straw that broke the camel's back for the walls holding the wreck up, Revy found herself nodding in satisfaction as the wreck started to crumble into itself.

 Today had been a good day for her. Not only did she get the guns the Old Man had promised her, she was able to take them out on a decent test run while earning some eddies for a easy job.

_I wonder if Rock that wuss found any new jobs for us?_

 So Mirror aspects.

 Motoko: Generalist in terms of weaponry, Revy: Specialist with her Custom Pistols

 Motoko: Stealth Specialist using assassination, Revy: Frontline assault relying on basically outdrawing the people shooting at her

 If Motoko is the Ghost for stealth assassinations, Revy is TwoHands for basically quickdrawing and shooting as if she has two hands to her opponent's one.
