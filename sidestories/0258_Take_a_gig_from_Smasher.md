 Some time in the future. Adam Smasher growled irritably as he was forced to smash through another bunch of ganger that somehow got their hands on heavy anti armor weaponry... Corpo grade weaponry.... Specifically Arasaka Corpo grade weaponry. Looks like he will have to pay another visit to management and set it's straight with the meatbags that they don't fuck with Adam fucking Smasher.

 Grumbling as he smashed the last ganger into a meat pancake, he debate which of the fixer to send the deets of his job offer to. With a slight Shurgs he picked one.

 "Adam Smasher, to what do I owe the pleasure with?" Ask Wakako, her eyes sharp even though the vid.

 "God a job I need done. Can't involve any Arasaka meatbags. Need you to find who painted a target on my back so I can smash them. I don't care how much just get me the info. Here's the deets" Grunted Smasher as he stomp his way back to HQ.

 A few hours later....

 An insistent ring woke Makoto from her nap, the specific ring tone she set for Wakako. "Matoko, still lazing in the afternoon I see" came the disapproving voice of her main fixer." I have a job for you, the client wants absolute discretion. Most likely will require your skill set. Interested?" Makoto gave a nod before the deets flash before her eyes. " Wakako, the client doesn't have any issue with how I get the intel right?" Ask Makoto as she read through the deets one more time. Wakako was silent for a few seconds before saying" just the Intel don't touch the target." Makoto gave a mischievous grin "Sweet Thanks Wakako" before noticed the comm was disconnected..... Again. Wakako did it again!

 Two days later Adam Smasher tense in his Dragoon frame as his audio pick up the sound of squealing rubber tires. From the sounds of it 4 wheels but the weight he estimate to be a small pick up truck doesn't match any vehicle in his database though. And it's seems to be coming from the roof. Odd no carparks up there. He decided to wait and see considering this was where Wakako arranged for him to discreetly pick up his requested Intel.

 There was a slight bang before what looked like a giant mechanical spider dropped down in front of him, hydraulic squealing as it readjust the height of the main body. Adam Smasher stare silently as he recognized the design anywhere.... Who was the weeb that made this? Can't be Subaru Arasaka, too busy with soul killer. A quick scan confirmed the thing Infront oh him to be purely metal. However bit was the voice of the bot that confirmed it for him.

 A Cheerful child like voice sounded" Special delivery for Mr Smasher! Please confirm identity with the previous agreed upon voice line!" Even while asking for confirmation the bot bobbed around excitedly just like a child..... Just like how Adam remember them from the Anime before the data crash...... Shaking his head slightly, he Growled out:" I'm Adam fucking Smasher meatbags".

 The bot stop slightly to analyze his voice to the one recorded before cheerfully reaching into its cargo hold on the back and deposit a high quality data chip in Smasher's waiting hand. "Delivery completed. Sayonara Smasher-san" and the bot wheel away zipping through the back alley. Slotting the data chip into his secured data drive where everything on the chip was triple checked by Black ICE before accessing he look up the Intel he paid for.... Surprisingly detailed and well made for a meatbag. Huh efficient despite the geeking out. Maybe he will ask Wakako to add this one to his list of not kill who knows, the owner might even be a Major cosplayer. That train of thought made him gave a snort of amusement before closing the window tab, that's for later now he has a Meatbag to kill.
