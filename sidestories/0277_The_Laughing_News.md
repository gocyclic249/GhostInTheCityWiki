The Laughing News​

 "[Network News 54](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Network_News_54)! Breaking News!" the announcer says before the screen cuts to the program's main host, [Gillean Jordan](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Gillean_Jordan).

 "This just in, the NCPD have confirmed the death of [Bartolomeo Mordellini](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Bartolomeo_Mordellini) not long after wreckage of his yacht was found down the coast from Night City. Mr. Mordellini, a well-known party planner among Night City's rich and famous, was declared missing and possibly kidnapped after reports came in of his yacht being brazenly hijacked from the Gold Beach Marina earlier in the day."

 A picture is brought up of Bartolomeo and his status of deceased.

 "Investigators at first believed that the hijacking of the yacht was a pretense to kidnap and ransom off Mr. Mordellini, but his death and the intentional destruction of his yacht have caused many to believe that this was a targeted assassination of the entertainer, though the motive is unclear. A less likely theory is that the hijacking was the main goal and the hijackers were not aware that Mr. Mordellini was still on this yacht during the theft and silenced him to remove witnesses."

 The flaming wreck of the GEIshhhhA is pulled up on screen.

 "However, our own investigators can confirm the use of military grade weapons to destroy the yacht, which heavily hints towards premeditation. However, what has grabbed most people's attention is the means by which the ship was stolen in the first place. According to reports, not long after marina security noticed the yacht start making an unscheduled exit and they attempted to respond, most security personnel, marina employees, and even guests were subject to a mass hacking event that temporarily disabled their vision and even when rebooted showed only this logo."

 A picture of the logo in questions comes up on screen.

Spoiler: Logo

![Image 14](https://ih1.redbubble.net/image.711315619.3557/pp,504x498-pad,600x600,f8f8f8.u2.jpg)

 "All optics, cameras, and other security recordings were subject to this hack, later revealed due to an intrusion and backdoor program installed into the marina's network. Only those with no cybernetic optics were unaffected, but none of those individuals were able to provide any form of ID on the thief. All security personnel and other witnesses who can say caught a glance at the thief can only say this logo completely obscured their face and had no other noticeable identifiers."

 The screen changes to show an ancient copy of literature titled _The Catcher in the Rye_.

 "The phrase on the logo is an excerpt from a novel known as _The Catcher in the Rye_, a book written well over a century ago by J.D. Salinger. Investigators are still looking into the significance, if any, this phrase could have to the culprit or if it is merely a calling card to identify themselves. NCPD and corporate programmers have already scrubbed the infiltrated systems and people infected with the hack, but warn just how quickly it spread and the potential it could have on a large groups of people. They ask that anyone with relevant information please step forward."

 The screen then focuses directly on Gillean Jordan.

 "For now, there are no leads on the mass hacking incident perpetrated by what the NCPD have decided to call, the Laughing Man Hacker."
