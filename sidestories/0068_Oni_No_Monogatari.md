 It allowed him to put away thoughts of Motoko, of Deunan, of Tousan and Kaasan.

 It was only when he got off the highway that he had to slowdown, the lack of having to concentrate bringing up the suppressed voices once more from the depths of his mind.

_Nii San! Nii San! HUG!
