 It would be funny if she received a time critical mission and didn't have time to change...

 "Don't worry," Jun said to his chooms as they hid behind some cover from the gunfire, "I contacted Wakako and she is sending over a stealth assault specialist pronto.

 That was when the gunfire abruptly stopped and Jun peeked out to see why. 

 "Motoko?!" Jun blurted out, "What the hell are you wearing?"

 It was his sister. His sister who was wearing a mini-skirted sailor fuku school girl outfit with a huge bow ribbon and a tiara. Who, Jun wasn't sure, either apparently decided to just go with the flow or possibly lost what was left of her mind, "It is I, Pretty Solo Merc Sailor Scout Motoko, here to punish you in the name of Love and Justice!"

 "What the fuck?" said one of the Maelstrom gangoons who was not exactly sure what he was looking at.

 "Moon bullet escalation!" Motoko shouted and used quickdraw. Eight bullets shot out in less than two seconds and all the Maelstrom thugs dropped at the same time. 

 "Pretty princess double tap!" Motoko shot all the bodies again in the head for good measure. 

 "Motoko! What the hell? How are you a stealth specialist?"

 "Hi Jun! No witnesses if there's no one left to witness. Also, I was taking refuge in audacity."

 "That's not taking refuge. That's proudly immigrating and then building a summer cottage there. Why, Motoko?"

 "Sorry, brother. I tried cosplaying as a different character."

 "That doesn't make sense," Jun said.

 "Uh... substitution no jitsu!" his sister shouted and threw a small pebble down on the ground.

 They both stared at it. 

 "Pretend it was a smoke pellet!" Motoko shouted and ran away. 

 Everyone just stared as she scampered up a the side of a building and ran off along the roof. 

 "So," one of Jun's chooms started, "how long have you left her alone and unattended?"

 Jun just groaned.
