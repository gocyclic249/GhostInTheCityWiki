 What? Who was that? Was it big bro, making fun of me again? I was a video game genius! I never lost! My thumb slipped! Stupid sweat!

 I turned around to give 'em a piece of my mind, and blinked in confusion at seeing the strange, blood covered girl standing there, in an even stranger pose. What was it even supposed to be? Did she think she was an anime character or something?

![Image 18](https://forums.spacebattles.com/attachments/editphotomode_10012023_210432-png.86693/)

_Wait, blood covered?_ "Who-" She placed a finger on my lips, making a gentle shushing noise, "No tears, only dreams." Her voice? It was far too husky for her form! I didn't resist as she took the controller from my limp hands, restarting the game.

 Did someone bring in their little sister? Or was she some kind of baba hiding in the body of a teenager? I'd heard about this before, strange old ladies that couldn't handle the concept of aging, and pretended to be eternally young. I was so distracted with my internal thoughts, that I nearly failed to see the next existential conflict right in front of me. She... she'd surpassed my score?! What!

 I went to grab the controller, but she just kicked me in the face without missing a beat, just who was this brat! By the time I struggled to my feet, the others had responded. I watched, dumbstruck, as they threw themselves at her. One at a time, or multiple at once, it didn't seem to matter. She didn't break stride either, fingers tapping furiously as _she killed them all with her legs.

 OH MY GOD!_ She just ripped out big bro's throat out with her teeth, but she didn't look away from the screen the entire time!

 I didn't realize I'd fallen back to a seated position, transfixed, as she finished the session with the highest score I'd ever seen. She was a monster, she couldn't be human! Nobody could do what she'd just done! It was almost disappointing when she let out a pleased sigh and shut the system down, giving it a gentle tap, "Yep, still got it."

 The spell seemed to break then, as I realized that she just killed what looked like everyone in the compound. No! I was surely going to be next! I raised my hands in surrender, thoughts moving at lightspeed. There had to be a way I could get out of this! And then it dawned on me, as I found myself on my knees, hands clasped before me. I struggled to get the words out, what seemed like rivers of sweat streaming down my forehead, "Please! Teach me your ways!" I wasn't even lying, I truly had to know, "The ways, of the true gamer!" My forehead kissed the floor, as I waited for my inevitable demise. Surely this wasn't enough, surely she'd kill me now. I'd never be able to beat a monster like her.

 Her voice surprised me then, still as husky as it was before, "Arise, young padawan." _Pada-what?_ I blinked in confusion, my plan had worked? I could hardly believe it! I looked up, to find her watching me with a smug expression, "Or at least, that's what I'd say if you weren't a filthy scav." My eyes widened a final time, as my screams echoed through the compound.

 Elsewhere, Hiromi shuddered, "Hey, did you hear that? Sound like ORA-ORA, or something."

 Malcolm shook his head, "No, must have just been the wind."
