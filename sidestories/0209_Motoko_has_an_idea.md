After having to wait outside Regina's and the Afterlife debacle Motoko had figured out her problem. Despite her Ninjutsu being low her Ninja was strong! Nobody even knew who she was! Which was good, you know, cause' Ninja.

 It did however make it difficult for new fixers to take her seriously. So what does a plucky young Ninja do when nobody knows who they are?

 Be even more Ninja!

 Which is why she was currently leaning over a sleeping Sebastian Ibarra, aka Padre, with a small camera in her hand.

 Throwing up a peace sign and smiling at the camera she took a selfie. It had been a bit of work to modify a miniaturized printer and link it to a handheld camera.

 As the picture printed out with her contact info on the back she knew she had come up with a real winner of an idea.

 The Major

 SECTION 9

 Infiltration Specialist

 Reasonable Rates

 With some contact details included as well. Not that she had forgotten to add those to the card for the first fixer she had visited tonight. Then had to sneak back in and replace the photo or anything...

 She smiled at the photo looking at her pride and joy, her baby HMG-Chan leaning against the headboard of the bed.

 Anyone can sneak into a heavily fortified, well guarded house to take a selfie without alerting anyone. Hint: No they cannot, but Motoko was thankfully unaware of this seemingly obvious fact.

 No, it took a real Ninja to do so while bringing along something as amazing as her HMG-Chan.

 Now she just had to pin down where Dakota was exactly out in the badlands. Well with the sheer number of Raffen out there it would be productive either way she guessed.

 Loading her beloved HMG-Chan into her car she kept trying to get Jun-nii to understand why this was important!

 "Motoko, I was sleeping, you should be sleeping, that is a thing people need you know. Sleep."

 Oh yeah, people needed that for things other than healing up from grievous bodily harm.

 "Uh, yeah, totally need to sleep Jun-nii, yeah..."

 The prolonged silence was beginning to stretch as she started the car and began driving back to apartment.

 She had promised Jun-nii she would bring him if he wanted to after all.

 "Motoko when was the last time you slept?"

 She pulled up the calendar app and checked the date. Okay, she hadn't been injured and needed to sleep since the Borg had smashed her so badly.

 "Well I slept off the night after that fight with the Borg at the party."

 He had this really exhausted sound to his voice, must be tired from waking up, as he responded.

 "Motoko, that was almost ten days ago. No more work, come home, sleep."

 The whine in her voice was pitched just right to draw the most sympathy she could get.

 "I need to do this Jun, otherwise no one will take me serious!"

 "Explain."

 So she did, as she drove home to get her brother she explained how none of the fixers knew how awesome she was. Because she was too awesome of a Ninja. Kinda a self-inflicted success story there to be honest.

 She had also had to explain that awesome meant preem, only even more preem than preem was. Stupid Jun-nii having no understanding of true culture.

 "So you have spent the last five hours since I went to sleep driving around town. Sneaking into places where Fixers are sleeping..."

 "Or working, that one was fun, he didn't even notice me or HMG-Chan at all! Just sitting there at his desk like a total gonk as I took the selfie."

 "...yes, and that. Which you decided was the perfect idea to fix your problem of being a 'Completely badass ultra cool super amazing ninja'."

 "Yep!"

 "After not having slept at all for almost ten days."

 Unsure why that was relevant at all to the discussion she replied with a hesitant yes.

 "Yeah..."

 "Come home, shower, and go to sleep. No leaving the house until you have slept for at least twelve hours."

 She could hear the tiredness in his voice. Silly Jun was still healing up the last of the hurts from the attack. He just wanted her home until he was done sleeping off his injuries.

 The things she would do for her brother.

 "Okay Jun-nii, but soon as you feel better I want to go find her, deal?"

 "Sure sis, just get some rest okay."
