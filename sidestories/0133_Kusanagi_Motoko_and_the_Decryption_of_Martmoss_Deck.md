 "Ladies! We are gathered here today for a Job of absolute importance. Our success will shake the core precepts of Netrunning to their core! The risk involved is high and both the Corps and any other Netrunner will kill for the information involved. There is no monetary payout that will be equal to the risk at hand, but upon success we will earn ETERNAL GLORY!

 You all the best netrunners i could find, each handpicked by me to be part of this great enterprise. Everything we did together as team was leading up to this mome- "

 "Motoko. Can you get to the point? All you told me was that you're calling in the favor for saving my life during the Biotechnical Job."

 The sound of Sasha's blunt statement made Motoko deflate from her puffed up visionary speech as she looked at the three very non-amused Netrunners she was meeting with. It was the first time she had met Lucy and Kiwi beyond faint memories of her life before, but judging from their body language she could tell that they were irritated at having to show up for a non-paying Gig no matter her hyperbole. 

 " Look. Lucy and I are only here because Maine told us to come. The only reason he did that was because Sasha here begged him to it so that she could pay off the favor she owned you. Once this is over, all debts are squared, so let's get this over with. Why do you need 3 Netrunners? What's the Gig? And this time, say it without all the bullshit."

 Feeling incredibly cowed by the piercing glares levelled at her, Motoko decided to quickly get to the matter at hand. Reaching into the bag by her side, she took out a security case and unlocked it revealing a battered deck.

 " Okay, so way back when i was just starting out as a solo, i ran into this freezer at the Night City Landfill...."

* * *

 ".......and so i need your help with cracking Bartmoss' Deck. So what do you say?"

 Lucy looked at the idiot standing before them with shining eyes and after brief glances at the other two Netrunners reacted the only way possible.

 "No."

 "No, this is idiotic."

 "No. Motoko, a favor is not a suicide pact."

 Without hesitation, Lucy slammed what little remained in her shot glass down before standing up from the crouch and made her way to the door with the others before they were interrupted by a frantic plea.

 "Waitwaitwait! Don't go! I have a plan! JUST HEAR ME OUT!"

 Trading brief glances again, the trio returned to their seats to allowing the desperate Solo to make her pitch.

 " I could have gone with any other batch of Netrunners if all i needed was raw numbers. I mean it's just good luck that i'm able to get all three of you with one favor, but i need all your expertise if i'm to stand a chance at cracking the deck.... 

* * *

_.....Kiwi, i heard through the grapevine that you're an expert in mapping out Data Fortresses, sniffing out the weakspots and using that to turn the defenses against the original host. So what i need from you is your help with mapping out the structure of the Deck. I need to know the places where the ICE is thickest, so i can find a viable route that won't require me to bash my head against all the defenses when i infiltrate._

 Motoko couldn't help but gulp as she looked the the looming ziggurat before her. Some quirk of Bartmoss' defenses at established the first layer of ICE on the deck into the structure the moment she ran the Mapping Tool Kiwi had handed to her.

 "Kiwi, is the Tool supposed to be working like this? Do i enter the entrance?"

_Well, you might not have a choice. My scans are reading that the weakest part of the ICE is at the entrance. Any attempts to punch a hole through the wall will fry your brains. You need to enter it and use the Tool again to find the next spot if you intent to crack this deck._

 "Ok, here i go then."

 Entering the provided entrance and running the Tool, Motoko found herself presented with the sight of a labyrinth. Path after path branching out into the darkness without any indicator on where she should even start.

_Take the path on the left._

 "Kiwi?"

_Yes. I'm running an analysis program base on your Data Feed. Follow my instructions, i'll guide you through the paths with the weakest ICE on them._

 "Thanks Kiwi"

 =================

 Beyond the all encompassing darkness that surrounded her, the nature of Netrunning itself meant that Time could feel nebulous. All Motoko could do was follow the instructions given to her as she worked her way through the labyrinth, Kiwi's voice assisting her past the traps Bartmoss had littered this section of the deck with.

 =================

_Strong ICE ahead, you might wanna avoid it_

 A quickly generated Daemon found itself sent forward and impaled as spikes of strong ICE pierced through the wall before her before derezzing, allowing Motoko to squeeze her way pass it before it could be recompiled.

 =================

_Layer of ICE ahead, but you can probably get through if you create a layer of waste code it can eat away as you move._

 Activating a prebuilt program on her deck, Motoko had her Netrunner Avatar wear a coat consisting of Waste Code as she forced her way though the flickering sea of flame-like ICE before her. Doing her best to make it across as the flames burned away at her coat guarding her avatar, finally making her way across just as the last shreds of the coat shattered as the ICE rendered it into nothingness.

 =================

 Alright kid. From what i can tell this is the final stretch. My tool states that are just two more areas once you clear this bit, but the ICE there seems to be different from this bit. The ones there seem more like a security system over this current bit.

 "Thanks Kiwi. Don't think i could have made it through if it wasn't for your help with mapping the structure and telling me where the bits of Strong ICE the path was littered with are."

 You're right Kid. You couldn't have done this without m- MOTOKO MOVE! A very strong piece of ICE was just generated behind you and it's moving fast to your position!

 Quickly turning her head back, Motoko found herself confronted with a rock barreling towards her Avatar. 

 "Bartmoss you fucking hack."

![Image 13](https://media.tenor.com/iQCHmQ7I8p0AAAAd/indiana-jones-indiana-balls.gif)​

 Tapping into the RAM on her Cyberdeck, Motoko forced her Avatar to speed up making it metaphorically sprint for the entrance leading to the next section of the Deck , and leaping into it with mere moments as the ICE behind it destroyed the Labyrinth she had just traversed before derezzing back into the ether.

* * *

_....Sasha, what i heard through the grapevine is that you're really really good at stealing data. You're not only able to crack some of the software the Corps are using, you're able to do it stealthily enough that most of them don't even realize the data's gone till your Client starts using it against them. From you, i need your help with helping me figure out and crack the locks Bartmoss might be using on his deck._

 Motoko found herself in a room. A relatively blank room filled with nothing but code scrawled across the walls, a puzzle that needed to be solved to unlock the way forward to the data stored within the deck.

_Yeah..You need Sasha for this. Handing the feed over to her. Kiwi Out._

 "Hey Sasha! Talk to me! What do i do?"

_Ok. First, i'm gonna need you to examine the walls so i can determine if the code there contains any hints._

 "That's easy! What do i do after that?"

_We'll try shit out till we either locked it so badly that the Deck get's fried or we cracked it._

 "Oh. Erm, Gogo Sasha! You can do it ?"

 =================

_Ok..Let me see, some this Code seem to be based on that old Java language. I'm gonna compile a key for you Motoko, try using it on the pedestals i'm highlighting._

 Grabbing the key that just appeared, Motoko moved towards the highlighted points Sasha was referring to before inserting the key and giving it a turn. Despite being in the Net, there was a rumble that resounded through the air as the pedestals flashed gold before melting into the floor and turning into a mini copy of the Ziggurat that the Deck contents were appearing as in the Net.

 =================

_WHY ISN'T THIS STUPID LOCK DISAPPERING!_

 "Maybe you should take a break Sasha, we tried over a 100 different ways at cracking this Ziggurat so far. That's probably very tiring and exhausting considering you coming up with the solutions from scratch based on some old shit Bartmoss might be in to. With his rumored ego, who knows what other weird stuff he's in to."

_Wait. What did you just say? Repeat that last bit Motoko!_

 " Erm, Old shit Bartmoss might be in to? With his rumored ego, who knows what other weird stuff he's in to? Which bit are you referring to?"

_YES. YES! That's it! Bartmoss has a huge ego and is proud of old weird shit that only he knows! OK, i'm gonna compile a new key for you, use it!_

 A bit confused by Sasha's outburst, Motoko complied with her order. As instructed, she raised the newly generated key towards the Ziggurat and almost dropped it in surprise as the key began to glow before shooting out a white beam towards the top of the Ziggurat.

 As she watched, the beam began to flow down the Ziggurat, along the flow and even up the walls as everything around her start to turn a blinding white.

![Image 14](https://i0.wp.com/roomescapeartist.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/04/Indiana-Jones-Map-Room.gif)​

 "That..That was so cool Sasha! How did you figure it out?"

_Your words gave me the hint. Bartmoss had a massive ego and was into weird old shit. So what could be more impressive then the usage of binary the most basic computer language and the phrase "Hello World" that old skool Netrunners used to use as their first bits of code?_

 Shaking her head at the leap of logic Sasha took that was somehow right, Motoko strolled through the newly generated entrance in what was an empty room and entered the next area.

* * *

_....Lucy, what i heard was that you're pretty ace at doing hacks in a live combat situation. Beyond just cracking ICE and using a prepared Quickhack to target your enemies, you're skilled and quick enough to crack the ICE and do a custom One Shot Hack that will put them into the ground. That's what i need your help for. Coming up with One Use Daemons i can throw to eliminate the more lethal ICE that is within the Deck._

 Entering what the Mapping Tool had mentioned was the last piece of ICE in the deck, Motoko found herself confronted by roaming ICE in the shape of beasts and monsters straight out of mythology. The route itself to the prize appeared to be a straight one, just guarded with what were clearly Lethal ICE serving as gatekeepers.

_Looks like Lucy's up. Sasha Out._

 Almost instantly after Sasha's sign-off, a Whip and a Overture Revolver appeared in Motoko's hands.

 "Lucy? Is this you? Do i just point the trigger at the ICE?"

_No. You need to hit them with the Whip first so that samples of their code are captured allowing me to analyze them before i can create a Custom Daemon bullet in the gun that you can use to wipe them out. Go ahead and start whipping the first target._

 Feeling slightly cowed by Lucy's brisk manner, Motoko moved towards the ICE in the shape of a Minotaur and gave it a Whip. An act that she quickly regretted because it drew the ICE's attention causing it to charge at her with a bellow.

 "Lucy?? I need the Daemon Bullet now please!"

_Dodge it. Don't get hit till the bullet is done._

 Left with no other choice, Motoko could only do her best Matador Impression as she dodge lithely away from the ICE for what seemed to be an eternity before the go-ahead finally arrived.

_Bullet's done. Just point and pull the trigger._

 *BANG

 As the first of many many ICE de-rezzed into nothingness. Motoko found herself panting despite not having actually done any physical activity.

_Please move to the next target. I want to get this over with as soon as i can._

 "O..Okay, Lucy just lemme catch my breath."

 =================

 Motoko's world turned into a blur after that. Just the respective actions of Whipping a piece of ICE, dodging it till the Daemon Bullet was generated and firing it to get rid of the target before moving to the next one. The monotony only broken up by Lucy's calls of _Bullet Done_ and the focus needed to stay away from the ICE until it could be eliminated.

 As Motoko got closer and closer to the end-goal, the ICE started getting tougher and tougher for Lucy to crack. In some cases requiring Motoko dodge for what felt like eons before a Bullet was done, in other cases requiring Motoko to Whip the ICE repeatedly so that more sample data could be gather before a Daemon Bullet could be generated.

 In the end, with some close shaves Motoko found herself confronting a piece of ICE in the shape of what she was pretty sure was a Balrog from the Lord Of The Rings Movie. Eager to get this over with, Motoko sent her Whip darting forward only to find it parried by it's fiery opposite.

![Image 15](https://31.media.tumblr.com/1f12dd05c1353a8f3192dd8b8f440e4e/tumblr_mqpyq3lmE71sx2peuo1_500.gif)​

_All i'm getting from the Whip is scrap code. Looks like you need to actually hit it for it's data to be captured._

 "O..Okay, i'll just get right on that as i faced down the scary looking ICE out to tear me to SHREDS!"

_Yeah, pretty much. The intelligence built within it is scarily good, it intentionally generated that bit of code purely to parry your whip. Looks like you got your work cut out for you._

 " THANK YOU SO MUCH FOR THE VOTE OF CONFIDENCE LUCY! "

 Ears ringing with Lucy's encouragement, Motoko charged into combat and called upon every little bit of Netrunning Skill and Perks she had accumulated thus far. This was the culmination of her journey as a Netrunner! Taking down this final beast of Bartmoss and proving her superiority over him as she gnaw upon the sweet sweet payload it was guarding!

 Daemon after Daemon was compiled and sent towards the ICE Balrog. Code sizzled and clashed as Motoko dueled with the beast, chipping at it's defenses bit by bit so that she could gather enough data for Lucy to create the literal Magic Bullet needed to send the beast to it's rest.

 Finally after overclocking the RAM in her Cyberdeck to the point of almost ruin, she heard the magic words from Lucy.

_It's done. Finish the Job, Ghost._

 A sweep of her hand had the few remaining surviving Daemons leap at the ICE, grappling and holding it down. The countermeasures ensured that it would only stay down for a second or two before the Daemons were fully eaten away by the ICE, but that was all she needed. 

 Pointing the Revolver at the struggling ICE, Motoko pulled the trigger putting an end to the entire adventure. All the ICE was eliminated, all that needed to be done was for her to stroll up on the metaphorical throne before her and claim it.

 "Thanks gals, i couldn't have done this without you all. I'll be more then glad to share what i got from the Deck. Call it payment for services rendered."

 Sitting on the throne, Motoko found herself presented with a screen not unlike that of a status screen from a video game with the following message inscribed on it:

![Image 16](https://i.kym-cdn.com/photos/images/newsfeed/000/186/610/thankyou.png?1318726198)​

_Something's wrong Motoko! The Mapping Tool is now displaying that only 1% of the ICE on the deck has been cleared. There's still 99% left and they all seem to be similar in scale to the Ziggurat you just cleared!_

 " BARTMOSS YOU FUCKING TROLLL!!!!!"
