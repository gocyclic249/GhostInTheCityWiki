 Please be warned I have no beta so proceed with caution.  
   
   
 *The offices of Logan, Anderson & Wick conference room three.*  
   
 The three senior partners of L.A.W., Hiromi and Motoko all sit at the conference table.  
 The rooms windows deactivate revealing they were [CrystalDome](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/CrystalDome) units.  
 The entrance of the Room is sealed by a blast door and a high pitched whine fills the room for a moment before subsiding.  
   
 Motoko blinks as her eyes visual feed briefly fills with static then clears up.  
 After running a quick diagnostic she notices her BD recorder is offline and can't detect any net access.  
 "That's an impressive jamming system."  
   
 Melissa smiles at the comment.  
 "An NW-54 countersurveillance unit, made and used exclusively by Netwatch's special tasks division."  
   
 Hiromi gives an appreciative nod.  
 "How much did that little toy set you back?"  
   
 "Two times of giving what the medical profession hilariously calls "natural childbirth"   
 and being married fifty years to a loveable gonk of a husband who lived long enough to retire as a captain in Netwatch."  
   
 Jeri rolls her eyes at Melissa's comment.  
 "Yes yes Wallace is an amazing husband & father blah blah blah,   
 I'm pretty sure our prospective contractors would rather hear about the job than stories about married life."  
   
 Anastasia pinches the bridge of her nose and sighs in exasperation.  
 "While you make a valid point Jeri your jealousy is showing, now then on to brass tacks."  
   
 The lights in the room dim and a holographic display of a building is projected from the table.  
 "This is the Night city headquarters for Asukaga & Finch.  
 The Job is delicate and requires the utmost discretion,   
 we need you to infiltrate the building, make your way past security and gain entrance to the secure storage vault.  
 Once inside you'll find lockbox 451 which will contain a high capacity encrypted data module,   
 exfiltrate with the unit and deliver it to the pre arranged drop point."  
   
 Motoko looks over the display, an impressively detailed blueprint of the A&F HQ   
 full specs on the locations of all security turrets, camera's, potential system access points and guard posts.  
 What had her concerned is the large blank space that occupied the lowest sublevel.  
 "I assume the blank area is the secure storage vault?"  
   
 Anastasia nods.  
 "The whole Vault is basically a hardened bunker, it's own self contained power supply and computer system, absolutely no outside net access.  
 The Vault door is a 25 ton one meter thick slab of titanium tungsten alloy that opens only through a special hydraulic mechanism.  
 To operate the mechanism you'll need two special operation keys,   
 these are physical keys with embedded coded id chips making use of both digital and mechanical unlocking of the controls.  
 While it would be possible for us to machine our own physical keys the data chips inside cannot be copied,   
 they are only fabricated as needed and encoded at a facility located in Ostermundigen.  
 As such you'll have to liberate two of the three keys existing for this door, one belongs to the A&F regional vice president,   
 one in the hands of the head of the night city division's security and the last one is with the branch director.  
 We have full layouts of their homes, security systems, psych profiles and places they are known to frequent outside work."  
   
 Hiromi looked over the data.  
 "This is all very impressive but I am forced to ask, why my client?  
 If you have the ability to gather all this intelligence you surely have assets that can preform this mission."  
   
 Jeri smirked at the girl.  
 "We do indeed have contractors that could execute the mission, the issue here is almost none have miss Kusanagi's versatility.  
 Skilled in several forms of combat, highly adept in Stealth and of course exceptionally proficient in Netrunning.  
 Since we are trying to avoid any detection in the acquisition of the data drive the smaller the infiltration team the better."  
   
   
 Motoko looked over the blueprints of the various key holders homes.  
 "Any details on their private security forces?"  
   
 Melissa waves her hand changing the holo image to the [Lazarus](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Lazarus) logo.  
 "Last year they swapped out their security provider from Helix to Lazarus, apparently Helix has withdrawn from the Night City market.  
 Modest weapon and cyberware load out for the majority of their teams,   
 these aren't their Tier 1 operators though there is an ACPA at the residence of the regional vice president."  
   
 "I'll have to do some of my own reconnaissance to confirm the intel but I don't see a problem with this job."  
   
 Hiromi nods at Motoko and then looks at the three other women, her expression changing to what Motoko loves to call Hiromi's corpo mode.  
 "Very well it seems my client is agreeable to the contract so we shall discuss terms and compensation."  
   
 The three women quickly discuss amongst each other and then forward a contract to Hiromi.  
 "Twenty five thousand euro dollars up front for expenses and One million euro dollars upon successful delivery of the data drive,   
 should you manage to remove the drive without detection we are also willing to match that payment amount in services from our firm   
 and give full letters of reference to any of our clients you should wish to do business with.  
 you have a fifteen day time frame to retrieve the drive as we will need time for our expert to decrypt it and prep the intel for our client.  
 all relevant delivery details are provided on this data shard."
