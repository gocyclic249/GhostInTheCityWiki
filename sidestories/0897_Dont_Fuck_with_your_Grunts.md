**Don't Fuck with your Grunts**

 "_-bleugh-huegh-ha_, hooo," Mikey coughed out, ripping the BD wreath off of his head. He blinked heavily, in rapid succession, shaking out his head as he murmured, "What in the actual fuck, what the _fuuuuuuuck_"

 As his racing heartbeat and electrified nerves returning back to normal levels, he refamiliarized himself with the room he was in, a trained reflex he developed to recover quicker from BD vertigo.

 Now, normally, that was because the BDs he was watching were roughly chopped virtus that were recorded on substandard scrollers. By the nature of his work in the in house background check department of MSM Records, he rarely experienced properly polished BDs.

 This one, though, was _fucking wack_.

 Mikey's eyes darted around the room, catching on all the comforting features of his dark, windowless room. A tower containing hundreds of BD cases, a lightweight Netdiving setup, a shoddy mini fridge containing low quality energy drinks.

 He was back "home".

 As he focused back into reality, he reread the "handwritten" label on the case of the BD he just ejected from, the script obviously printed to match the original music chip from the hellscape he just witnessed.

_The Only Thing They Fear is You_

 And _fuck_ if Mikey didn't fear Motoko Kusanagi.

_Shit_, he could still feel eyeball gunk under his thumbnails.

 The initial background searches for Stand Alone Complex were easy, two of the members were scop nobodies, likely to get pushed out at some point if the band began to falter as MSM would tear out all the "valuable" pieces and cobble together another group to sell as a new project.

 The valuable pieces were where things began to get interesting.

 Alice Fujimura, the cared for niece of a high ranking Tyger Claw, was someone warranting a note in a file to not push _toooo_ far. Gangs tended to leave corps alone, so long as they didn't truly fuck over the connected talent, and gang connections could be sold in advertising if the story was right.

 Mikey figured that Alice was the main risk for MSM, the only other major sticking point in the other lead girl's story was being the sister of a Tyger Claw working under Fujimura's uncle. Same note, lower intensity.

 But.

 But but but but _but_.

 His ol' buddy Donny, a real BD guy if Mikey ever saw one, had let him know that there were BDs being sold of Motoko Kusanagi performing her set at some shitty dive bar.

 Not a huge deal to MSM, especially cause the BD producers were small time. Just threaten the owners, get the rights, and sell a "limited run" of the BD if Stand Alone Complex got big enough to sell "legacy" merch. Standard procedure.

 The only problem was what Donny said _after_ he handed the BD over.

_It's kinda weird, bro, I thought Ghost was just like, a merc XBD girl. Super nova that she's a dope rocker too._

 Sure enough, the producers of the music BD, "Straight Lane Shooters", dumb fuckin name, also sold a catalog of edgerunner XBDs, under the name Ghost.

 And that led him here, in his dank cavern, dripping through his shirt with ice cold sweat, settling down after witnessing some of the most brutal killing he'd ever scrolled.

 Motoko would get a major note in her file, and the advertisers would be raring to go to hit the ground running to showcase her as a dangerous woman, not an angsty girl.

 And Alex would get all the credit.

 Stupid fuckin' Alex, and his shitty ass blackmail. Didn't even have the skill to find _real_ blackmail over him, just some shitty manufactured shit that would never fly on someone with real cred in MSM. But alas, Mikey was just a grunt, scrolling BDs and surfing the shallow Net for background checks.

 Never to rise in the ranks, cause Alex liked having grunts in different departments to bully for priority work.

_Unless……_

 Mikey snapped the wretched BD in his hands, wiped the last hour from his logs, and in a flurry of fingers, sent off the work-in-progress draft of the Stand Alone Complex background checks.

 If he was lucky, Alex would behave as Alex does, trying to slowly pressure the group more and more into digging in deeper and deeper into MSM, Motoko Kusanagi would snap, and the "Straight Lane Shooters" would have another XBD to sell.

_I sure as shit won't be watching it though_, Mikey thought as he reached for a disposable fork to scrape the phantom eye juice out from his thumbnails.
