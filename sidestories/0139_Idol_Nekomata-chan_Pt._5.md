"Puru-neko-puru-neko-puru-neko-puru❣" She chants, her tone steadily rising and her face screwing up in concentration.

Although that's all moot anyway because so far all we've got is Motoko's word.

 I don't know about this one kid, seems like either too big of a fish, or maybe none of our business. Could be a corpo compound for all we know. Wouldn't want to step on the toes of any paycheck writers now would we?

We never found anything. We all thought it was like they just floated off to Neverland… Where would Nekomata have heard that name? No, she couldn't have heard that name.

 The steady and quiet grinding sounds of the turrets rotating in their cases is briefly overshadowed by the unusually intimidating squeak of a door opening. Motoko's skin seems to chill against my frame as a man steps out on the porch.
