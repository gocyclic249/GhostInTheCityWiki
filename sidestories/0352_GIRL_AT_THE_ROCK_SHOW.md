**GIRL AT THE ROCK SHOW

 -----------**

 Hiromi was having a good day, a lunch date with Motoko school was uneventful and her lacky/minon one David Martinez was starting to catch on to how she hustled BDs soon she would ask him to see if felt comfortable enough to expand mayb-

 Her thoughts were interrupted by an call from Motoko.

 "Hey Toko wh-"

 "Hiromi, Choom get over here Jun-nii is being a Gonk, with a Capital G!"

 "What did he do this time?" Hiromi asked not really wanting to get inbetween another Kusanagi sibling war.

 "He flirted with Rita!" Motoko exclaimed.

 Hiromi took a minute to process that, rationalizing this as Motoko needed support and not an excuse to have another not date she sent back an affirmative and made her way over to Toko's place.

 -------------

 After she yelled at Jun-nii he left to do some thinking, that worried Motoko. Jun thinking lead to burrito expenses doubling she would be finding stashes of the things every where, those spots where for her explosives!

 Putting her frustration and disbelief into action she quickly wrote a song from her many memories, specifically one with clear vocals and that would embarrass Jun-nii.

 Thus Pop-Punk was born into this world, pulling from the many radio bugs/jams of the rarly 2000s and a respectful thank you to the band that originally made the song, she was ready to get Hiromi's input on it.

 "What's the game plan Toko?" Hiromi yelled as she opened the door

 "Good timing" Motoko walked over and navigated Hiromi to the couch, getting a startled 'wha?' from the girl.

 Hiromi gave her a look, "what did you do?" She asked

 "I made a song to tease him with" Motoko answered bouncing literally to her music box set up she forgot to put away im her mad scramble to think of a way to tease her brother. "And I would like your advice on it"

 "Ok choom, wait how fast did you write this?" Motoko ignored the question booted up the music box, no holograms this time due to the time frame but the instruments should work. So much was her manic energy into trying to use her hobby to mess with her brother and tying the song to how Jun started to flirt that Motoko, slightly miscalculated on song choice, taking its lyrics and connecting them to the situation that happened earlier.

 Hiromi missing that context, was not prepared.

 Motoko shredded out a poppy guitar intro with rapid beats of drums leading into the first verse.

 "~_Hanging out behind the club in the weekend, getting drunk acting stupid with my best friends~_" she started to sing.

 Hiromi was pulled along with the lyrics as a positive sounding twist to what she would call classic samurai style punk though jarring created a almost similar energy, but as the song progressed her face started to heat up and her cheeks reddened.

 "~_I fell in love with the girl at the rock show, she said "what" and I told her that I didn't know. She's so cool gonna sneak in through her window, everything's better when she's around, I cant wait till her parents go out of town, I fell in love with the girl at the rock show!~_"

 Motoko jamming out didn't notice Hiromi start to blush and try to hide behind her hands, until the song started to fade as she ended the session, of course seeing a blushing catatonic Hiromi on her couch worried Motoko that her rather impulsive and quickly written song was bad.

 "Was it that bad?" She asked getting no answer she walked over to Hirmoi and poked her shoulder, the girl jumped a bit being startled, Motoko asked agian if the song was bad.

 Hiromi at the moment was having a crisis of '_was that for me!? Oh god what do i say!?, Quickly Hiromi tell her you like her, invite her to a proper date!_

 "Eep!" She squeaked.
