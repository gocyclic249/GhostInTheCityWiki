Well Motoko now has the scariest rep in the entire badlands. Nomads are a prideful lot, even Dakota took one look at the Wraith camp and just nope'd out of doing anything about it despite all of the heat it would drag out upon the rest of them.

 However instead of the proverbial Calvary she eventually expected to see sailing out from Night City she was instead meant with a group of teenagers who acted like they where hot shit.

 Then in two days a small but annoying wraith camp was destroyed and a small army with a fortified encampment was wiped from the map.

_That_ is Morgan Blackhand levels of leading a small strike team to destabilize an entire corporation and or small country.

 It doesn't matter that the targets where wraiths _this time._ The rest of the Nomads are going to start looking at Night City a little more wearily seeing an entire camp disappear without so much as a peep in a single night.

 What sort of black magic Arasaka bs produced this tiny murder machine? They'll ask themselves. Dakota will likely be very interested in working directly with Motoko in the future. There is no way she didn't have someone watching this from miles away recording a video of Motoko gleefully sniping everyone from the rooftops without even looking.

 For the Nomads at least? She's earned a legend. _She is the wraith._ : P

 The listening post? Keep it, tap into it so that you at least have a better idea of when the main plot is going to kick off. Destroying it or burying it would also bring eyes to the situation that you don't want.

 As for super secret spy Solomon Reed? Well he's too busy working his day job as a bouncer and fantasizing about Mexican food to care. He's currently very upset at the NUSA himself. It isn't Myers silver tongue that gets him to work with them again. It's his misguided paternal instincts to Songbird, and potentially V and the rest that motivate him even if they are twisted towards patriotism.

 We can only hope he never meets Motoko, for many, _many_ reasons.

_*Reed appears from the shadows.*_

 Reed: "We don't leave anyone behind."

 Motoko: "You just leveled a city block!"

 Reed: "Yes, so that we can extract you in the chaos, and bring you home."

 Motoko: "This is my home! You're standing in my apartment!"

 Reed: "Motoko you're wasted in Night City, do you really want to stay here killing people at the drop of a hat for loose change? or-"

 Motoko: "-Yes that's exactly what I want to do! I love murdering people for loose change, it's my calling in life! _The NUSA is boring._"

 Reed: "...Okay we tried the easy way, bring in the extraction team."

_*Motoko Pulls a carnage out from under the sink.*_

 Motoko: "Ah ha! I learned my lesson from last time! Now die free XP!"

_That day Solomon Reed died. Motoko was at first very worried that she just broke the plot of the game, but as it turned out a lot less people ended up dying for no reason, and a potential Corporate war masterminded by AI's was averted... Well one of them anyway.

 Of course Motoko would never know any of this, and would occasionally continue to freak out for the next several years. Paranoid that she'd kickstarted the apocalypse early._
