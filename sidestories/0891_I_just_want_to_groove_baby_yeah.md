I just want to groove baby yeah!

 "Hey Toko, you ok? I heard you've been running around for hours?" Hiromi was somehow the only person on my contact list who didn't need me for something today.

 "Today has been a day of all days. Everyone has had an issue or emergency or NEEDED my time right now! But now, after 10000 years, I am free! I just want to get some noodles and groove baby yeah!" Ugh brain could you not right now? I get giggles back from my choom at least.

 "Should we message Alice and Co or?" Hiromi didn't push them on me, she really is the bestest.

 "This is why I love you Romi, not sure I want to do like music music but maybe something will strike my interest. Could you let them know I'll be at Denny's studio in an hour?" She squeaks out a 'Sure!' "Preem, you should join as well if not too busy!" I hang up and jump in my 66, heading to hunt down some noodles.

 ______________________

 Hiromi

 "I love you too!... she dropped the call?! Son of a..."

 _____________________

 I've been looking over my shoulder all throughout dinner, not sure why but feel like someone is pissed at me? Par for the sports ball I guess, hum let's start with some drums!

 Sence this isn't a normal sess I just grab stuff at random, no think just do! Let's see, 2 bongos, 4 foot petals.. maybe some think? No, let's work it!

 _____________________

 Denny

 The little shit is back and has ignored me for three trips to pull more drums. The worst part is, she isn't even pretending to not see me! She really is just in her own world. So, gotta scoop this out. Takes a bit for her to set up and for once I'm glad I'm not the only one being ignored. The boy's Jared? Klen? Whatever their names are, just watching her from the booth as well. "Shouldn't you be, I don't know, in there setting up as well?" The one who normally does the drums just turns to look at me.

 "Yeaah, we asked and she just walked on by. Hiromi did tell us Motoko was looking to just jam out so we waiting to see what she wants from us." He whispers the next part, as if I don't have some of the best ears you can buy. "And I don't want to try whatever the hell that is." Mood.

 Then Motoko just starts Drumming, and keeps on Drumming, and fuck if it's not amazing. At some point Alice comes into the booth as well, nearly has a panic attack at seeing me, but just turns to watch and listen with the rest of us. None of us say a word, just watch as this barely out of diapers girl just... Fucking DRUMS.

 A few minutes later she finishes up but we don't leave the booth but Jared? I should learn their names, speaks up. "Yeah, fuck all that." I snicker at that but that is some real shit.

 "We need to make that a song!" Alice finds her voice but the boy's just look at her like she's nuts. But before they can get into an argument the tiny terror starts to play the guitar and I'm reminded she isn't a one trick, not even close. Sure most musicians can play a few instruments but not like this. No, this isn't just playing. This is goddamn fuckin Art. And bullshit, it's also some goddamn bullshit! I own shoes older than this girl! But fuck if it's not a joy to watch her. Her guitar solo ends and both the boy's are just looking smugly at Alice as she shakes her head. "Ok yeah, fuck all that."

 Motoko finally notices us all and blushes behind a cough. "Heeeey all, sorry was kinda in the zone." There is a pause as she raises an eyebrow at me but looks back at her band. "Sooo I have some things I wan..."

 "NO!"

 "Fuck that!"

 "I would rather die!"

 At least the rest of her friends are sane.

 Hiromi nearly kicks the door down as she runs into the booth with the rest of us.

 "BITCH!"

 Ok maybe not all her friends are sane?
