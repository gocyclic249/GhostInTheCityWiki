To mention the thing about Motoko's rep vs her contacts, from perspective of someone like Jackie it's still outright ridiculous.

 J: "Fuck yes, finally chance to work with Wakako! You're climbing up in the world, Jackie!"

 M: "Yo, Wako asked me for a favor so I'll be your handler for the next few gigs, see how you do, evaluate your performance, and all that rot"

 J: "What."

 M: "Frankly I think she's just annoyed since I keep giving her headaches and she hopes this assignment will keep me out of trouble and out of her hair for a few weeks"

 J: _"What."_

 ------

 J: "I don't like this chica, those are Nomads there. They are dangerous. If they saw what we're smuggling-"

 M: "What are you talking about?" *turns away towards the approaching armed nomads "Scorpion my man! How are you doing my choom!"

 S: "Motoko! Good to see you!" *they fistbump* "We doing another barbecue over the weekend. Wanna drop by?"

 M: "Sure bet! I even practiced my singing voice!"

 S: "I sure hope so!" *they both laugh*

 J: "What."

 ------

 J: "We're fucked" *banging on the door is heard*

 Police officer: "NCPD! Open up! We know you are there!"

 M: "Wait- Detective Stints, is that you? It's me, Motoko! From that Meat Man case!"

 Stints: "Huh? Okay men, stand down. How about you open the door and we can resolve this misunderstanding peacefully?"

 J: "What."

 ------

 J: "Motoko, wait! Don't approach them"

 M: "Huh? Why?"

 J: "Those are the _Kamikaze_, girl! They are a bunch of Tyger Claws borged out cyberpsychos, dangerous as all hell! They will kill you if you look at them funny!"

 M: "That... makes no sense" dashes and hugs one of the Kamikaze "Jun-nii! I never thought I'd run into you in a place like this! How are you doing?" *Jun laughs and headpats his little sister, other Kamikaze look at them fondly"

 J: "What."

 ------

 J: "Your friend is here?"

 M: "Yup. She should be finished in a few minutes."

 J: "Does she, like, work here or something?"

 M: "Huh? No. She's a student. Her classes should end in a moment, then we will go to the drop-off point and finish the deal"

 J: "The deal... the deal we killed all those people for was organized by a corpo schoolgirl?"

 M: "Yep. Best friend I made in my entire life"

 J: "What."

 -------

 Jackie sat alone by the bar, drinking away his confusion. The last few days were... something. Something else, certainly. Working for Wakako proved to be much different than he expected it to be. Working with Motoko was... eye-opening, perhaps? About as much he still needed to go before getting that drink in the Afterlife.

 It's why he was at Lizzie's Bar in the first place after separating with the girl for the evening. To drink away some of his sorrows, to contemplate things quietly and alone. Get some thinking done, without the specter of the crazy girl hanging over his shoulder.

 From the corner of his eye he saw one of the dangerous looking Mox bouncers with a wide smile on her face and escorting a girl with a familiar mop of purple hair into the restricted area of the bar.

 Jackie put down his drink and just stared into the swirling amber liquid for a minute.

 "What."
