 Ho-Oh Club a Month After Last Chapter

 It was a regular day at the Ho-Oh Club, the boss had returned from his almost a month and a week trip to pick up some new merchandise that fell into his contacts hands. It seemed like everyone and everything was going the way it always did, then the sound of a guitar started up outside the Club. With the sound system being worked on, it was actually quite loud.

 Some of the men opened the door to the sight of a Young Asian man with dark hair in a strange hair style (Poumpador), a preem Guitar, and a couple of amplifiers. He was also wearing dark black pants and a jacket with minimal designs on it, some rings on a couple fingers, and a strangely electric air around him.

 "He ya'll I figure you lot could use some entertainment..." The young man drawled, before starting to strum his guitar to the confusion of the TC Bodyguards and the crowd. Mostly because the gang members were wondering whether they should stop this crazy Rockerboy or not.

**With Motoko**

 "Now I have you Gonk!" Motoko grunted as she took out nearly a month and a half of frustration on him for disappearing before she could slot his gonk, serial killing ass.

 Though it was kind of cool that she managed to find what she did in the interim, who knew using songs to net run would find an old archive of Elvis in the old net that she would accidentally copy. When doing it again she either failed or managed to find gold, like the prototype Gemini hidden in the badlands that could create a personality engram to pilot it based on inserted data, and she had a lot of data on this Elvis.

 Honestly it was amazing that not only did the hybrid infiltration/combat Gemini Android still held up even though it was nearly fifty years old, but she also got Preem blueprints, crafting specs, and an old state of the art 3d printer that could make even advanced parts...with some adjustments.

 She just had to hurry up before the TC decided to hurt her little boy...

 What, no! She wasn't attached to the Gemini Android she put together piece by piece over a month, and based off her dna to make the fake skin that was really fucking advanced. And maybe she let him call her Mama, but that is just because he was key to her plan...

 Those gonks better not have hurt her Baby Boy...

 "Hey Uncle Jun!" The disheveled and covered in lipstick marks young man stated as he walked past Jun into the apartment, behind him came his sister who looked murderous.

 "Motoko!?" Jun asked as he looked at his scratched up sister, who was sporting a black eye and bloody nose.

 "Not now Jun!" Motoko declared. "I need to make sure that those fucking gonk harlots didn't hurt my baby boy!"

 "...Baby Boy!?" Jun declared with heat in his voice, the fire in his gut surging forth to demand who dared touch his sister, and who was that kid who called him Uncle! And when was his sister even pregnant!? Also what did she do to that body she had in her room!?

 Notes: Inspired by the Elvis movie, Motoko's strange ability to plan, and the fact that she is now a Rockerboy. That and Adam Smasher was said to once sport a gemini body that looked nearly identical to Elvis.

 So this time Motoko has an android version of a Gemini who is damn near human in his makeup, technically advanced enough he could be mistaken for a human with cyber implants. His dna being a hybrid of Motoko's and digitized dna of Elvis stolen from belongings of his, technically making him Motoko and Elvis's son.

 His personality was built using an archive of Elvis content from videos, interviews, and so on. He was also treated to his momma's BD's to help him be able to protect himself, with her training him after he was finished to make sure he could take care of himself...
