"Heeeey Jun-nii you busy?" I try to sound as cute as I can after he picked up the call but..

 "Ugh what did you do this time? I swear.."

 "I'm fine! Just.. Well.. A bit stuck.." I hear a sigh but nothing else so I keep going. "I was just scouting out a scav den and while in the closet that had their server, someone put a box or something against the door." That gets a snort from the Oni. "They must have just come back from something because no one was here when I started scouting."

 There was a laugh from his end "Yeah yeah I'll be right there, should I grab others or can I." I cut him off.

 "No! Please Jun no one else, I'll owe you one but.. pleassssse!" I tried not to whine but my chooms can NOT find out about this! "I'm in their security system and take their eyes and guns, it will be a cake walk!"

 "Siiiigh fine, but if you want me keeping quiet you'll owe me a new song!" I give him an ok because that really is a smaller price to pay than what I thought he would ask for. "Hold out for me, I'm heading out now."

 Well... That was a thought.

 ____________________________________

 Watching the cameras Jun had let me know he was close. I start a BD and sing.

 "~Where have all the good men gone

 And where are all the gods?

 Where's the streetwise Hercules

 To fight the rising odds?

 Isn't there a white knight upon a fiery steed?

 Late at night, I toss and I turn

 And I dream of what I need~"

 The timing was just about perfect as the camera has him arrive on his bike. I had to change some words around cause no way was I gonna make this a love song, but the tone and setting was just great!

 "~I need a hero

 I'm holding out for a hero 'til the end of the night

 He's gotta be strong, and he's gotta be fast

 And he's gotta be fresh from the fight

 I need a hero

 I'm holding out for a hero 'til the morning light

 He's gotta be sure, and it's gotta be soon

 And he's gotta be larger than life

 Larger than life!~"

 Right when the first scav would have seen him I pluck his eyes and follow Jun as he cuts body after body, trying to judge how fast he's moving with how fast I sing. Changing from one view to the next behind the last scav Jun's blade splits him down the middle. I sing the last few lines a bit louder so he knows where I am now that there is only Kisanagi's here.

 "~I need a hero

 I'm holding out for a hero 'til the end of the night~"

 He opens the door and I pounce hug him as he smiles down at me I end the BD.

 ___________________________________

 "Did you.. why am I asking. Of course you made a brain dance of the rescue. Imouto if you don't want people to know you got stuck in a closet why did you film it?" We had grabbed some noodles on the way home and Jun just had the oddest look on his face after I told him why I was singing.

 "Because no one will believe you now if you try to blackmail me with this later!" That gets a big Guff! "And, please don't let this go to your head, you are sometimes my hero Jun-nii." I lean into him a bit as we eat.

 "You were bored huh?"

 "SO BORED!" He pushes me away but we are both laughing so good ending yeah?
