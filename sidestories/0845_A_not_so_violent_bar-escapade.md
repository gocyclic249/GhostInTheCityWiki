A not so violent bar-escapade

 Few days ago

 Violent Hemorrhage band members were sitting in a bar, drinking their voes away with scop drinks. Wondering how they ended up as the followers of a teenage girl as their main singer and leader. Their old band but a memory in the graveyards of flatlined bands.

 "Alice, when are we going to become famous?" Klein said.

 Jared looked up from his beer and said. "Why aren't we famous yet? Bet it's our naming sense.. Remember when Alice cut herself on a coffee tray? If that didn't happen, we would already be famous."

 "Screw you Jared, just because I came up with the name Spilled Blood for one of our songs, you bring that up again! The band name isn't why weren't doing well," said Alice, throwing a can of half finished beer at Jared.

 "Auch, hey! At least finish what you drink before throwing it! I got beer all over my shirt now!"

 "Look a new song title. Spilled Beer!" Klein snickered.

 "Can't tell any difference between the puke and whatever the colour is." Alice said.

 "Hell no, that's neo yellow colour!" Jared defended vehemently.

 "No way, it's too weird. Have you ever washed that shirt since you bought it from that alley vender?"

 "Not really, but I don't roll that way… Klein Back me up man!"

 "No way man, leave me out of your shirt. I am glad you're the only one who has contact with that shirt, and I want it to stay that way.."

 "We should have called our band, ba-dum-dum." Alice said, grinning at Klein.

 "Just because I am a drummer and I like what I do, very funny Alice. I am not going to mention your vocals, because I don't sing so low."

 "Oof" Alice cringed.

 "Good job Klein, you did it anyway." Jared said, cheering with his beer and finishing it.

 "We're now ghost in the shell band crew anyway." Klein mused.

 "What about our NeoRock Electrosmash roots!? All these songs are great, but they lack the- the- smash parts!" Jared slurred, half wasted already.

 "Maybe we can make some good NR-ES songs with Mokoto. We are a whisper away from becoming big; I can feel it in my bones." Alice said.

 "Jared mumbles in his sleep and drools. You sure you're not just heard it from him? I swear I heard him mumble through half one of our songs in his sleep, which was impressive to be honest." Klein said.

 "What's wrong with you Alice? Are you like always staring at me when I sleep? Are you coming onto me??" Jared said, bug eyed.

 "You were drunk and sleeping on the floor when I heard it. You're a lightweight," Alice said, laughing while recalling his stupid face he had made. He had the same expression Jun makes all the time.

 "Maybe if we're lucky, you'll be singing the new songs we have learned from Mokoto. And I'll actually enjoy your sleep mumbling."

 "That would be an upgrade! We could all sing together with you, lets hope it happens! Cheers!" Klein said.

 "Cheers!", "cheers!"

 "How is our choomba doing anyway?" Klein asked.

 "She's been doing some Netrunner stuff as far as I know." Alice said, shrugging.

 "Huh, netrunning. She's a merc, rocker and a Netrunner? Makes sense, I suppose. In the first place, I can't wrap my mind around her."

 "True that." Jared said, raising yet another new beer can.

 "Hopefully, we can get some producers to listen to us in one of our gigs. Maybe we can become big from that."

 "You wish, well. I wish as well. I feel it's just a question of time." Klein said.

 "Imagine, someone like the producers for GH-Bandits!" Jared said.

 "Yeah, right! Like someone like that would appear." Klein scoffed.

 "I've never even heard of anybody who's heard them play live! Maybe they are ghosts?" Like our small ghost?"

 "With Mokoto at our lead. We could become Ephemeral ghosts! No Ghosts in the jars! Ghosts in the suits!! Ghosts in the tight leotards!!!" Jared said. His genius self evident, only to himself, eyes swimming.

 "No way I am wearing a leotard. I'll leave that you Jared." Klein said rolling his eyes.

 ".. Jared passed out." Alice said.

 "Oh. How long until he mumble sings, you think?"

 As supporting characters, the band doesn't get much alone screen time, so I thought of writing this scene where they get drunk in a bar. Kind of accidentally made Jared into a drunk, silly guy here, which is ironic as he comes off as the most reasonable of the bunch imo.
