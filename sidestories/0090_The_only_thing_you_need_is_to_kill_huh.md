 The only thing you need is to kill huh?

 KILL

 Jun really should have paid closer attention but with the Maelstrom and Tyger Claws demanding more of his attention he could be forgiven for missing the message from his sister.

 Right?

 Which is why he was driving pedal to the metal through traffic to the latest 'strom hideout that was hit by... something. Something about a crazy chick with a sword.

 As the car squealed in protest and he stepped out of the still rocking vehicle he nodded at the Claw.

 "She here?"

 The Claw shook his head. "Nothing but dead 'strom. Netrunners are pulling footage cause... well." he pointed a thumb at the warehouse.

**MANKIND IS DEAD**

 And the painter's brush was slid down to the ground. 'Meat crayon indeed.'

 Jun grit his teeth as he made his way in to the Netrunners through the charnal house.

 Motoko didnt come home that night.

 ---

 Another day

 Another dead hideout.

 Jun stared up at the signage.

 "Sheesh they really fucked her up."

 He glared at the Claw coming up behind him.

 "What? Oh come on, you think anyone normal would do this?"

 He gestured at the sign above. "**BLOOD IS FUEL**"

 "Like who does this?"

 His fist balls involuntarily.

 "That's my sister you're talking about."

 The Claw starts before apologizing, "Alright alright sorry just... nerves. Anyways, runners pulled the footage again. Gonna be a while before someone can put it together."

 Motoko didnt come home that night either.

 ---

 "**HELL IS FULL**"

 The blood sign taunted him.

 "Fuck!" The curse pulled him out. The Claw previously calm was now trembling. "Just heard from the runners. She's Still. Here."

 'Motoko!'

 He didnt wait for the rest of what the Claw was saying, he was already tearing through the door.

 It felt like he blinked and now he was behind her. The broken skylight letting the sun light in like a spotlight.

 "OOooh Niiii-saaan." Her voice whispered.

 "Motoko!"

 She turned her head tilting backwards, something dripping something **red**

 "Nii-san. I'm better now. I've _adapted_."

 She turned revealing a still struggling Maelstrom in her grip as as her sword pinned him to the ground through his neck.

 "But I think I need one last experience." She rips the sword out splattering the ground crimson.

 "Would you kindly test me."

 "Motoko, please. Stop."

 "uh uh. That's not what you're supposed to say. You're supposed to say Challenge Accepted."

 "Challenge Accepted?"

 She laughed. A deranged maddening laugh.

 "Oh boy. Here I go! KILLING AGAIN!" She screamed as the sparks flew from the trench the the sword was leaving.

 And then Jun didnt have time to think any more.

 Only act.

 ---

 "Okay seriously? This isnt edited?"

 "Nope."

 "She's-"

 "Dodging bullets? Yup."

 "And then-"

 "_Punching the bullets_ back? Yup."

 "Is that-"

 "A coin? Yup. She's bouncing her shots off of them and somehow its punching harder than any bullet from that gun should."

 "What the fuck."
