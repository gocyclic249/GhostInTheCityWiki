 Motoko places a white duck wearing a stethoscope down on the ground. 

 Motoko: "His name is Doctor Quack. He can fly to where you are and heal you."

 Hiromi: "... what?"

 Motoko: "I stuffed him with like three medical databases and a learning AI. Don't tell Netwatch!"

 Hiromi: "..."

 Hiromi: "How is he supposed to heal us exactly?"

 Hiromi *falls backwards*: "Jesus!"

 Motoko: "What do you think?"
