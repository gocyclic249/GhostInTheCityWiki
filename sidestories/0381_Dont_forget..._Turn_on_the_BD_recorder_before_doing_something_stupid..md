About forty times of that should do, and then it can upgrade my body as I take a power nap! I may even wake up with powers! I'm that awesome."

 The purple-haired solo merc ninja programmer rockerboy girl brought the needle towards her, but stopped right before it went into her skin, "What am I doing? Was I really going to inject myself with an untested nano swarm just like that? That's crazy! I need to start a BD recording first to save this for posterity!"

 She did just that and quickly injected herself with her creation. 

 "Battle Angel Motoko, here.. I.. "

 She fell asleep, draping herself over her table. 

 Forty A.I. progressive improvements later, the resultant intelligence accessed Motoko's memories. 

 *Scanning reference: Motoko Kunasagi.*

 *Scanning reference: Ghost in the Shell.*

 *Scanning reference: Battle Angel Alita.*

 *Scanning reference: Cosplay.*

 *Found optimal match for new body upgrade.*

 Several hours later, Motoko woke up and saw a small screen with writing. 

 *Good morning, Motoko. I am your new body's A.I. guide. You can call me Blacklight.*

 The girl groaned, "No! I wanted to become Alita, not Alex Mercer!" 

 Then Motoko suddenly realized, "Wait. I can now change shape! I can become Alita and everyone I ever wanted to cosplay, er, I mean emulate! Yes!"

 She sprinted towards the nearest mirror and changed her form into adult looking Motoko. 

 "Excellent," she said in her now appropriate sexy voice. 

 Then she turned into Alita. Then back to regular Motoko. Then into people she knew. Then other characters. This continued for hours. 

 "I'm back," Jun said as he entered their new apartment and froze. 

 "Which do you think is better, Jun? Transforming robot Motoko or Magical catgirl Motoko?" Both Motokos were holding hands, standing next to each other in front of a mirror.

 They both turned into identical twin Motokos, both much younger and wearing identical antique blue dresses.

 "Come play with us, Jun," said the Motoko on the left in a creepy monotone. 

 "Play with us forever," added the other Motoko.

 Jun blinked, opened the front door again, and quickly let himself out. 

 He stared at the obviously drugged XXL Burrito in his hands. Then he slowly lifted it up and took another bite. 

 "I'll just return later when things start to make a little bit more sense," he shrugged and speedily walked away from his sister, who was laughing in stereo behind him.
