Hell on Skates​

 Night City was no stranger to deadly drivers, in fact it was considered odd if anyone actually bothered to follow basic traffic safety laws and the cops were liable to pull you over if you did since what shady lunatic did that?

 Deadly street racers, borged out gangsters on their bikes, corpos in their heavily armored AVs, NCPD with literal APCs on occasion when shit really hit the fan, Edgerunners running and gunning in their supped up muscle cars, Nomads cruising around while jacked into their highly valued rides, and more could be found in the 'freest' city in North America. It was a boring day in Night City if there weren't at least a dozen drive by shootings ever couple hours.

 But in recent times there is a new addition to that list of skilled and deadly drivers to grace Night City's streets... and beyond.

 Because this driver did not roll on wheels in the classical sense, but much more literally on highly motorized skates that many claim have replaced their feet entirely if not built into them.

 They could race any car or bike, scale walls and the labyrinth that was Night City's alleys and buildings with pure speed and skill, dance around their enemies like some kind of killer ballerina, and move so much like the wind some wouldn't even be able to realize what the fuck just happened till it did.

 With a katana in one hand and in pistol in the other this hellion on skates, or Wanyūdō as some say, has become the nightmare of the worst scum Night City had to offer.

 ---

 Petrov could only fire blindly across the rooftops of the series of buildings and apartment levels he and his gang of scavengers had claimed on the outskirts of Nigh City, a prime location for product from various cells across the city to send their spoils that could be then smuggled past the border and sold to other markets for a huge markup. All around him he watched as his fellows fired and tried to hit the blur that was taking them out one by one!

 Some of them, who were more borged out then the rest, seemed like they had the most success but even with their mods had trouble hitting their mark as it kept moving around in ways that shouldn't be possible!

 Petrov saw it himself as the Wanyūdō, cause that was the only one who could possibly be doing this, drove up walls like they weren't totally vertical, slide around gunfire like it was nothing, jumping dozens of meters into the air to crash feet first into people and grind their faces into mulch, and worse! They zoomed around with their sword so fast it was cutting peoples' limbs or even whole bodies in half like butter! The had to be using a smart weapon since no matter what they aimed as as they fired they found their mark despite their constant movement, but it hit like power or tech weapon as it punched through armor and subdermals like they weren't there.

 He even watched as the Wanyūdō grabbed some poor gonk, rode up into the air with them, and then dropped them head first back to the ground, screaming all the while until it was cut off with a wet splat.

 At this point Petrov was totally done and decided to get the hell out of there. Nothing was worth this!

 So he made his way over to one of the construction lifts he and his crew had installed to move easily move up and down the various buildings and quickly hit the button, barely seeing the demon on wheels turn in his direction before the lift start rapidly going down.

 As he waited for the lift to make it down to the ground floor he slowed his breathing and brought up his car controls to come pick him up so he could drive for the border and never look back. Shit had been crazy over recent years since more and more scavs cells were being directly hunted down, more than usual at least, and after today it just encouraged him to set up again elsewhere since clearly Night City wasn't for him.

 However, before he could think much more about how to build his new life and operation elsewhere he heard a motor sound that didn't come from the lift, turned to his right, and stared wide-eyed at the Wanyūdō driving alongside him straight down on the side of the fucking building! They stared at them through their helmet and seemed to burn into his very soul.

 Patrov quickly brought up his gun and opened fire! This close he had to hit!

 Only he didn't as the Wanyūdō literally danced around his shots, zipping and zooming along the wall before driving around and before his life could reach the ground sliced through the cable with their katana.

 As gravity, that harsh rule of the world, took hold he could only scream as he rapidly reached the ground.

 ---

 "Heh, must have a reinforced skeleton or something," Motoko, the 'Wanyūdō', one of many nicknames she had gained over time but still preferred the Major most of all, said as he watched the barely alive and thoroughly broken Scav yet still somehow conscious Scav attempt to drag himself to his vehicle that was waiting for him not far from the now crashed lift.

 Granted the fall wasn't _that far,_ only a little over a dozen floors, so survivable for people with the right cyberware and knew how to soften their landing as much as they could, which to the Scav's credit he did try.

 Motoko much more preferred falling with style, redirecting gravity right before she hit the ground and using the speed gained to keep on riding.

 Speaking of which, she idly rolled around the Scav as he kept on crawling, confirming that this was her main target.

 She could take him alive for some extra eddies... but she knew that the client would likely prolong the man's suffering if she did. Oh sure he might deserve it, almost certainly even, but Motoko wasn't one to drag things out unnecessarily. She was a professional before anything else.

 Therefore she just rolled up on him, locked her skates to stop moving, stamped on his back, earning a scream of pain, and looped off his head with a quick flick.

 Hey, this guy happily ripped the cyberware out of _kids._ Some karmic justice was alright.

 She quickly confirmed his death, looted his body for anything useful, and then proceeded to call up her crew to let them know she had cleared out most of the Scavs and to start looting the hideout.

 Custom skate mods were expensive, the magnetic wheels were especially pricey, so momma needed steady income to keep on working them over like the precious assets they were!

 Besides, she and the tachikomas just loved roller derby night and she needed every edge she could get!
