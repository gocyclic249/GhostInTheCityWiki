 Night City Today News Interview:

 Reporter: "Night City Today News is here for you live on site of todays Mass hacking incident. Here we have the only alleged witness of todays event. Did you see the hacker of today Garry?"

 Garry The Prophet says ominously: "I saw it jumping about with inhuman grace, keeping in the shadows without making a sound!! No emotion on its face that couldnt be of our race!! I'am telling you it was a VAMPIRE hunting after its preys!!"

 Reporter whispers to the cameraman "More focus on him, this is gonna be amazing!"

 Cameraman focussing in on Garry who is gesticulating vigorously.

 Reporter:"The quick hack which shut down the optics was never seen before. Do you think it was developed by the perpetrator?"

 Garry The Prophet preaching:"It was only a distraction! They already started to influence our brains and memories!! They want to blind us from the truth!! This was only the beginning I'am telling you!! They can already see through our eyes and now they even take our sight!! We have to wake up or it will be too late!! For the Eternal Night is coming!! Mark my words!!"

 Reporter:"Well thats all pretty scary. What do you think of the laughing man logo and its text? "I thought what I'd do was, I'd pretend I was one of those deaf-mutes"."

 Garry The Porphet says with conviction:"It is a message, NO a threat! They want to silence me, so I can no longer preach the truth to you!! But I will not fear I will not cower!! We have to wake up my fellow humans!! We must drag them into the light so that the darkness which plagues this city may be vanquished for that our future generations may live freely once more!! Open your eyes to the truth!!"

 Reporter:"Thanks Garry for your unique insight. And thank you to all our viewers! Night City Today News will allways be there for more live news in Night City for you!"

 Garry The Porphet in the background:" To those who opened their eyes to the truth please provide me a humble donation so I may continue my holy mission!!"
