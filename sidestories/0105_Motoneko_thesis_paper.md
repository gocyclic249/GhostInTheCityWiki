_Why catgirl Motoko is the only option._

 As stated in my previous paper, there are numerous benefits of installing cyberware to bring the general body-shape similar to that of a catgirl. But what of the other considerations?

 It is the sad truth of the world that Perfect Cosplay is an ever-distant utopia. However, to achieve True Cosplay in the traditional meaning of the world, a perfect 1-to-1 replica is not necessary. What is needed is a translation of the spirit of Cosplay into our humble base reality.

 What is a Motoko, if not an ideal anime girl? Corollary, what is an ideal anime girl, if not a catgirl?

 Therefore, to achieve the closest approximation to perfect cosplay, one must deal with the idealized version of what they wish to cosplay as. Not close approximations of physical replicas, but the ideals made manifest.

 Therefore, the perfect Motoko cosplay, is Catgirl Motoko cosplay.

 This cosplay draws from the original source, the Motoko, and elevates it through the medium of Anime into its idealized self, the Catgirl Motoko.

 And why not a cat-girl?

 Cats are one of the- if not THE most efficient land hunters in the world. The humble house cat is responsible for extinction of numerous species of small birds and rodents. Are you going to deny that Motoko will be responsible for the extinction of numerous measly gangs in one city?

 Cats are silent, and sneaky, attacking their prey with cruel efficiency. Is that not what Motoko is?

 And yet, cats are natural born fighters. Punch a baby, it cries. Kick a puppy, it whimpers and hides in a corner. Try to take food from a kitten, it growls and fights back with its claws. Does that sound like someone you might know of?

 They are independent, and unbound by rules of mortal men. A cat does what it wants, when it wants, and how it wants to do it. To try and control a cat is an exercise in futility, to bribe its affection a pointless gamble. Neither a cat's love or violence can be forcefully pointed in a direction it doesn't want to be pointed at, with the exception of the feline's own personal choice to distribute such acts according to its own designs. What kind of human could ever exhibit such traits?

 The nickname is another point of contention. 'The Major', while strong on its own, has no connection to the current Motoko. With no police or military service to tie the title to the person, it's nothing more than an empty boast. So what name should she assume, to appeal to the understanding of the common world?

 Let us look at but a fragment of a single passage about a certain cat-like demon:

_"These yōkai are associated with strange fires and other inexplicable occurrences. The older and more abused a cat is prior to its transformation, the more power the nekomata is said to have. To gain revenge against those who wronged it, the spirit may haunt humans with visitations from their deceased relatives "_

 What is Motoko but an abused cat, on a path to vengeance? What does she do, but visit inexplicable death to her enemies? What is she, if not a spirit of death walking the earth once again?

 What is she, but a _Nekomata_?
