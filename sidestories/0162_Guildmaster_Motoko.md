**CLANG***

**CLANG***

**CLANG***

 The din of hammer meeting steel filled the Smithy that her adventures had lead her to. Getting the location and even a meeting with the rumored Smith had not been an easy task requiring her to risk both life and limb to obtained enough favors for today to happen.

 Now, there remained one last challenge before she could finally, FINALLY obtain the power needed to ensure her Nightmares never see the light of day.

 "So.... You're the adventurer that wants one of my blades? One of the lucky few who managed to survive the gauntlet of Quests that are thrown to those who want to even enter my Smithy to meet me. You understand that this is just a meeting? And that i reserve the right to decide on if i will forge you the blade?"

Lucyna Kushinada couldn't help but blink at the dichotomy. The Legendary Smith. Maker Of Weapons Wielded By The Greatest Adventurers Of The Land. The Jaded Smith Before Her....was a teeny tiny brat that couldn't be over 14 years old.

 "Yes, but you just need to name your price! I swear i'll get you the Eddies! Or if you need me on a Gig i can do that as well. I have a crew i run with that can help with any problems you face."

 "VERY WELL! I Demand your Service for a Year and a Day! You shall now be a member of the Section 9 Guild! Now follow me and i shall introduce you to my brother the hulking Barbarian, my bestie Hiromi the Bard and Nox. He's the gopher."

 ==========

 Still reeling from the abrupt change of tone as well as the sudden introduction to people who all had the same expression of _Pity with a dash of commiseration_ on their faces at her sudden introduction into their Crew. Lucy kept to herself as she tried to figure out what exactly being part of **Section 9** meant.

 What it meant apparently turned out to be agreeing to Motoko's _(Which was apparently the Smith's name)_ random whims, requests and demands. Which wasn't as big a chore as she expected considering Motoko constantly forgot about said requests.

 All in all, her first day as a member of Section 9 went pretty smoothly, something she was pretty glad about as she exited the Smithy.

 .

 .

 .

 .

 .

 Unplugging from her Deep Dive, Lucy felt the world change from the Fantasy shitick ~~The Smith~~ Motoko insisted on for her DataFortress. Back fully in meatspace, she popped out a cigarette and was about to light it up when a message from Maine lit up.

 It was an order for the entire Crew to keep an eye out for Gloria their Chrome Supplier who had just bounced after taking a substantial down-payment from him.

_Well, that's not my problem. Kiwi can handle it._

* * *

 Lucy found herself living a strange double-life in the next few months. In Meatspace, she not only had to tutor this extremely cute gonk who turned out to be Gloria's son while dealing with the absurd requests Faraday was throwing to Maine and the crew, In the Net she found herself for lack of a better word, doing Adventurer's work straight out of Fantasy or RPGs.

_"Hark to my call Guild Members! Today we shall hunt down this most dastardly of villains. For his head, we will be paid with precious metals the world has never seen!"

 Taking pity at her confusion, the Bard Hiromi whispered to her "Motoko means that we're being hired to take down this Netrunner by Netwatch. Once we succeed in doing so, they'll transfer to us some of their proprietary code."_

 It turned out that Motoko was as great a Programmer as rumor claimed and had compile powerful enough Attack Code and ICE that even non-Netrunners like her brother and friends could use them in Data Heists.

 The Breech Program used by Section 9 not only allowed entry in every single target the Crew had hit thus far, it also somehow rendered the defending ICE and DataFortress into Monsters and Dungeons straight out of Fantasy. With the Program basically doing all the work, all the Section 9 members needed to do was direct the Programs in question to do the work.

 Something that suspiciously took the form of their Digital Avatars swinging weapons and physically opening "Treasure Chests" with the loot within being proprietary code of Corporations, Genius Netrunners and in one memorable instance, Scrap Code from an A.I beyond the Blackwall.

_"left! Left! Minotaur to your left!"_

_Dodging desperately, Lucy managed to side-step the charging monster while angling her blade so that it ran itself through. Taking a brief breather, she watched as the Barbarian Jun swung his greatsword against the daemon guarding the Treasure Chest before them, their swords colliding in a cacophony of shrieking steel. 

 Behind him, the Bard Hiromi launched arrow after arrow at the daemon, gradually chipping away at it when all of a sudden the fight was interrupted by a loud shout

 " YES! GOT IT UNLOCKED! TAKE THAT MILTECH!"

 Revealing a now exposed Motoko opening the Treasure Chest. Something that caused the Daemon to roar with rage before turning back towards her, an action that the rest of the Guild attempted to prevent via spamming as many attacks as they could before it fell with a groan of despair._

_"STUPID IMOUTO! The plan was for you to open the chest without drawing attention!"_

 And with each and every Data Heist, her promised blade gradually took form, built from code and algorithms obtained from their adventures...

 Still,it was frustrating to Lucy that despite knowing that Motoko was adding and reworking code right in front of her, all her Digital Self saw was Motoko hammering iron over an anvil into a shape of a blade. Still, she could admit that the Data Heists were fun and more in line of playing a game then actual work.

* * *

 Time continued to flow and things started to go wrong in MeatSpace. First it was Pillar's death at the hands of a Cyberpsycho, then it was David being kidnapped and tortured by an XBD producer before the culmination of Maine's Last Stand and the dissolvement of the Crew.

 Lucy had enough. She washed her hands off Meatspace and threw herself into the Net and Section 9 work, not just participating actively in the Data Heists but also suggesting targets for the crew to hit. And if said targets happened to have people investigating about Her David...well, that was just killing two birds with one stone.

 Yet despite her best efforts, Night City's influence could not be avoided as everything came crashing down once more....Now as she looked at her David battling with the Butcher Of Arasaka, a situation that he was only in because of her...She felt despair sink it's claws deep into her.

 Resolving to do what she could, Lucy was just about to launch a Breech Attempt at Adam Smasher when a message appear:

> Motoko 'Major' Kusanagi said: 
> 
> 
> 
>  Oh hey Lucy! There you are! Man, i was having so much trouble finding you. Anyway, here's your promised blade! Remember to use it responsibly!
> 
> 
> 
>  Kusanagi_GrassCutter_OrochimaruSword.Exe 
> 
> 
> 
> 
> 
> 
> 
> 

 Grasping quickly at the faint hope that appeared, Lucy downloaded the quickhack into her Deck. Ruthlessly purging her Programs, Daemons and even ICE to obtain enough RAM to put it to work.

 Being raised at an Arasaka Facility meant she recognize the names attached to the Quickhack. From what she knew of Motoko, a Quickhack named after a divine weapon would be something worthy of said name.

 Compiling the Quickhack and feeling her deck heat up with the strain on doing so, Lucy focused oncemore on Adam Smasher and the moment the words "**KUSANAGI PROTOCOL**" lit up, she launched the quickhack at him while praying desperately that Motoko would not let her down.

 The best BlackICE Arasaka eddies could buy appeared to stopped her blade, yet they proved to be naught but mere obstacles that barely slowed down her thrust. ICE after ICE each of which could serve as walls to any other Program were Paper before the **KUSANAGI PROTOCOL** as it pierced through them to reach it's target.

 In Meatspace, Lucy watched as Adam Smasher seized up. Parts of his Borg body locking up while other parts moved, causing them to shatter under the stress being forced on them. Under the disbelieving eyes of the public, Adam Smasher Boogeyman of Night City tore himself up.

* * *

 Weeks passed as Night City dealt with the loss of one of Night City's Legends. It was rumored that there were parties celebrating Adam Smasher's death almost everyday, while a new drink was added to Afterlife's menu.

 Yet for Lucy, all that mattered was that she had her moon back with her.

 "Come on David, let me show you something."

 "Are we going to your Moon BD again?"

 " No, not today. Today i'll like you to meet my Guild."

 "Guild?"

 "Yes David, let's go on an adventure together."

 .

 .

 .

 .

 .

As the Fantasyesque theme of Motoko's DataFortress took shape around them, Lucy clutched David's hand tightly and strolled to the Smithy in front of them.

 "Hoh Guildmaster! I bring an aspiring Warrior who seeks to join our Guild and participate in our adventures! He requires the finest Armor you can craft."

 " EEEEEEE YOU'RE PLAYING ALON- I mean.. that is not a light request. To truly forge such armor, you will need to traverse great distances, descend into the deepest labyrinths and slay the fiercest beasts!"

 " That is no trouble at all Guildmaster! We're ready for the challenge, right David?"

 "Er.. yeah! We shall complete the Quests presented before us and let the Realm ring with the name of our Guild!"

 "YOU! I LIKE YOU! LUCY YOU MADE AN EXECELLENT CHOICE IN RECRUITING THIS WARRIOR! NOW COME, ADVENTURE AWAITS!"
