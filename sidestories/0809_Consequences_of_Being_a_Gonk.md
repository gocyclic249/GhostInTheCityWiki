**Consequences of Being a Gonk**

 "So," Vik started as he finished putting the last stitch in his most frequent customer. "Mind telling me how you managed to get a split bullet in your gut."

 "Training accident," was Motoko's reply, but it sounded more as a question than an actual explanation.

 "... Elaborate," Vik ordered, all traces of humor absent in his voice.

 "Well..." Motoko drawled. "I'd just perfected this awesome new technique that lets me deflect bullets with my sword and I wanted to practice it, so I asked my friends to shoot at m- Don't give me that look! It was going perfectly well. I was able to stop every shot they fired at me. It's just that I figured I could kick it up a notch and perfectly slice the bullets straight out of the air. I just forgot one minor detail..."

 "That a cut bullet would still maintain its trajectory and significant forward momentum," Vik deadpanned as he finished her sentence for her.

 "...Yes," Was the girl's reply, her face beet-red in embarrassment.

 "For the record," Hiromi piped in from where she'd been sitting. "I told them all it was an absolutely gonk-brained idea."

 Vik said nothing. He simply got up out of his chair, walked over to his bookshelf, grabbed the heaviest hardcover he owned, walked back over to Motoko, and slammed it down hard on her head.
