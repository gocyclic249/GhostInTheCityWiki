 *You feel it. The slight tingle up your spine that promised the XBD was about to bring you that high once more. You feel yourself turning only to be confronted with the sight of two Animal Gangoons swinging basebats at your head with terminal velocity. A part of you wonders what will happen next? Will you block the attacks? Dodge them? Or counter-attack?  
   
 As your anticipation builds to the peak, the XBD freezes with a sepia filter overlaid on it. Just as you were about to panic on if the Mox had given you a bad batch, a series of options light up before you.*

The bats slow to a crawl right before your eyes as you relax from your pose and raise your fists before breaking out into a flurry of punches that move almost to fast to be seen as your mouth shouts out in a battle cry.*  

*"MudaMudaMudaMuda!"

 *You feel your mouth open and coolly proclaim "Time Alter: Double Accel" and feel the familiar ice-cold sensation flow through you while the world slows to a crawl.   
   
 Despite the bats inching towards your face, there was no panic or fear. Only smooth sure purpose as your hands glide to your shoulder holster and draw your Burya before leveling it at the gangoons before you and opening fire.  
   
 Each pull of the trigger sends a bullet flying out of the Revolver, yet the world forces each round to slow down enough that despite reloading and firing again, not a single one of the bullets arrived at their target.   
   
 Walking away from the soon to be dead men, you feel yourself holstering the Burya before drawing out a cigarette and lighting it up. And as you watch the first exhale of smoke slowly climb to the heavens, you feel time resume it's course as the meaty thuds of bullets meeting flesh fill the air behind you.*

*You feel your mouth open and quietly but firmly say "i know Kung-Fu." as the speed of the approaching bats slow to a snail's pace. Your head instinctively moves to avoid the bats, your back bending backwards till it was parallel to the ground.  
   
 ![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)   
   
 Before springing up again and launching in a leg sweep that sends one of the unbalanced gangoons flying into the air

 before falling with the same snail pace, giving you more then enough time to jump and launch a vicious head kick at the other remaining gangoon.  
   
 ![](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7)   
   
 As time resumes it's pace, both fall with simultaneous thuds that echo throughout the alley.

You feel your eyes close and your mouth open to mumble out "Ichi no kata: " as time comes to a standstill. Almost casually, hands move towards the knives holstered on your hips and back and close around their hilts.   
   
 Your body drops into a runner's crouch and you feel your muscles tensing. Like a spring you wind yourself tighter and tighter containing all that energy before you break into a dash.

 "Hekireki Issen."

  
 As you fully recovered from the rush, you feel a sense of surety and confidence in your choice before the XBD ends.*

 Jun watched in horror as the streets of Night City were filled with people strolling around in trenchcoats, Gold jackets and Yellow Hakamas. Motoko's latest XBD had proved to be a Viral Hit that had spawned a legion of memes and fans imitating the chunibyo words she used to activate the Sandevistan in the XBDs.  
   
 To make matters worst, the AR clothes she had the XBD overlay on the user while the Sandevistan was active proved to be incredibly popular, to the point that she had spawned an entire subculture of posers focused on imitating the styles in the XBD.  
   
 Matters were not helped by Motoko also doing a Lore Breakdown on each of the Persona in the XBD.  
   
 Night City had gone crazy and now Jun found himself having to avoid Swole Gymbros participating in pose-off battles while surrounded by fans in jackets shouting " ZA WARUDO! ZA WARUDO! ZA WARUDO! "  
   
 Wannabe Netrunners greeting each other with "Do you know Kung-Fu?" before launching into mock duels using skillchip enabled Martial Arts. Flashy and stylish looking but utterly useless in an actual fight. All thanks to Motoko's offhand comment of "oh he was a nerd until he had every martial art in existence downloaded into his mind and running on his deck"  
   
 But that was only the tip of the Iceburg, what really caused the whole thing to explode in popularity was Corporate backing. Mainly thanks to Hiromi Chan's Father, Arasaka Night City found itself clearing out it's leftover stock of *Solo Wolf and Bot Mono-Katanas* after slapping on a dash of Yellow paint and selling them to the posers making a mockery of Swordplay.  
   
 Sensei had gone so apoplectic with rage that the entire Kamikaze crew were recalled back to hold him back from massacring the Posers making a mockery of Kenjutsu.  
   
 And as Jun walked around yet another group of posers in cheap Hakamas smacking at each other with fake katanas to cries of "*Hekireki Issen.!"* He couldn't help but lament out loud.  
   
 "I should have just kept the Sandy.."
