 I'm not Seras but here's my attempt on what the XBD will be like  
   
 

---

  
 "Hey Minion! Here you can have this as a bonus. I'm off now, make sure you clean up our stall before leaving"  
   
 David seethe in envy as ~~The BITCH~~ ~~Corpo Brat~~ ~~Hiromi~~ **BOSS** walked to the waiting Quadra before getting in with a squeal that only increased in volume as the growl of the Quadra's engine rose to a Roar before it sped off into the sunset.  
   
 While life had definitely become better since he started working under her and that he was bringing more money via the salary she paid him, it still irritated him that he like the rest of Night City had to bow down to the Mighty Eddie.  
   
 Letting out a sigh and looking at what she tossed at him, David found himself staring at a XBD chip. In that moment, the world stood still and the one sole redeeming perk of being her minion raised its head.  
   
 EARLY ACCESS to the preemest XBD series he had ever came across. A series that elevate XBDs to levels he did not know was possible and at a standard that made other XBDs feel lacking.  
   
 The previous slaugterfest set to a bitching song had being a mindblowing experience and now, he held in his hand the next one in the series. Moving like greased lightning David cleaned up the store in a flash and drove off any of the ones left within with the excuse of needing to do a Stock check before sprinting for home with his prize clutched in his hands.  
   
 =================  
   
 The journey home was a blur to David, driven as he was with the NEED to see the latest masterpiece. Slamming open the door, David sprinted to his bedroom and his waiting wreath, he slotted in the XBD Chip and waited for the magic to happen.  
   
   
 *As the blackness faded away, David found himself staring at a Room labeled 1237 before he strolled forward and opened it. A part of him reveled in the sense of assurance and coldness that were a core part of the EDGERUNNER ACE series as he made his way through the domicile doing quick professional scans of the kitchen and bloodstained Ripper Lab.  
   
 The scene before his eyes suddenly flashed before a laser beam appeared in his view leading to a mine waiting in the hallway to be triggered. Most XBDs David experienced before would only have found the mines via them exploding and in fact usually served as the ending of said XBDs, which was why the nonchalant way he just strode over the mine as if it was a childish prank had him letting out a breath of admiration on the utter lack of panic or fear being experienced.  
   
 Making is way to the outside porch, David found himself throwing a ping at a Scav and watched as the ping blossomed into a web showing him the list of targets waiting within. A amount of foes large enough that David started wondering if this was going to be a collab where the members of her team revealed in the Boat Heist took a starring role as well.  
   
 To his slight disappointment, all that happened was David found himself moving along the outside until he reach a porch where there was a Scav smoking a cigarette. Just as he was about to wonder how the Scav would be eliminated, his body moved and David found himself kicking the Scav off the building accompanied by a trailing scream as if it was something out of the campy Action Vids his momma loved.  
   
 But before he could even savor the moment, the next Action Beat in the XBD happened. David watched in awe as his hands blurred like lightning as they drew the ever-present Burya and a Lexington in a mismatched pair that start spitting lead at the Scavs who had gathered at the sound of the scream.  
   
 **\*Bang, Bang, Bang, Bang,   
   
 \*BAAM**  
   
 The staccato beats of the Lexington found themselves punctuated by the drumroll of the Burya as they create a symphony of death from the instruments in David's hands. Scav after Scav met their end to the rhythmic beat as David experienced what it was like to be a Maestro in the Art Of Dealing Death.  
   
 As the Scavs lay dead around him and as the room descended into utter silence, David readied himself for the XBD to come to an end. His mind already composing a draft of his review to be uploaded as soon as he could get to his computer.   
   
 However, to his surprise the XBD continued onwards and he found himself exiting the room and make his way to one of the doors of the home when he felt an electric tingle up his spine as if someone was calling his name. Turning his head, David found himself confronted by the Tyger Claw Oni leading a posse of chrome Claws staring menacingly at him.  
   
 "Heeey Oni." his mouth said. The cool confident tone that was ever an hallmark of the Series coming through despite facing a Murder Squad of Claws all ready to rip him into shreds.  
   
 "You?" The low breathed out reply of the Oni sent shivers up David's spine. It sounded like the Oni knew the Edgerunner recording the XBD and the low quiet reply had a menace to it that sent a fission of fear into David despite the XBD's feelings of Utter Calmness and Professionalism.  
   
 "YOUUUU!" The Oni's roar sent David into a fizzle as his own feelings of fear found itself compounded with the slight spike of fear that cut into the XBD at the Oni's roar.   
   
 As the Oni charged at him, David found himself hastily turning back and sprinting through the rooms he cleared. The adrenaline from the chasing cutting through the usual Coolness and Professionalism that were usually steadfast throughout the XBD.  
   
 So amped up on fear and adrenaline that David forgot about the awaiting mines until his arm drew the Burya and send a shot triggering it into a large explosion amidst the Oni's roars of rage.  
   
 Making his way to the patio, David skidded to a halt as the Oni approached.   
   
 "What do you want Oni? I'm kinda busy." The non-sequitur apparently threw the Oni off because the look of rage on his face twisted into confusion right before David's eyes as the Oni confronted him.  
   
 "This is dangerous..." The unspoken threat on who it would be **dangerous** for rang warning bells in David's head, as the Oni continued with his threats.  
   
 " You. Are. Coming. With. Us. " The way the Oni bit at the words told David that without a doubt, this was not a choice but a demand with the full weight of the Tyger Claws behind the request.  
   
 " We...can- YOU!" Yet, to David's surprise he felt a sense of amusement and giddy joy build up in the XBD before he found himself cutting off the Oni mid-speech as he took flying leap off the patio.  
   
 A sense of adrenaline and exhilaration filled him as he soared through the skies. The freedom of the air combined with the knowledge of having proved himself the better of the Oni turned into a sense of rapture that David had never experienced before in any XBD thus far.  
   
 Sticking the landing with ease, David turned his head and found himself locking eyes with the Oni who was glaring at him with impotent rage.   
   
 "No."  
   
 And with the One-liner given to the Oni's **request** to come with the Claws. David found himself casually leaping off the building and descending it in a series of hops and jumps that were utterly effortless before landing on the sidewalk of the building in a crouch.  
   
 Standing fully upright, David found himself looking at the building where everything had happened before the view of the XBD zoomed in towards a car parked outside of the building with special focus paid to the splattered Scav embedded into the hood of the vehicle.  
   
 A re-apperance so to speak of the First Kill of the XBD before the scene before him fades to black.*  
   
   
 Yanking the BD Wreath off his head, David found his entire body drenched in sweat from the adrenaline highs the XBD brought him. Ejecting the XBD chip with trembling hands, David took his first close look at the Chip before him.  
   

Inscribed in Gold Letters on a Purple Chip was the title of the XBD:  
   
 EDGERUNNER ACE:   
 *To Steal From The Mouth Of A Tyger* ​

 Because the way i see it, Judy would totally have focused on the Action Beats when editing and also play around with the tones of the words to convey the emotions needed to make the XBD a HIT.  
   
 I actually wanted to just do the XBD bit, but needed someone to run the XBD and finally decided on David which did in-fact allowed me to elaborate abit more using him as the POV character and how his takeaway can be so different from Motoko
