 Jun was tired. Another day of being on the lookout, while one of the Tiger's managers hashed out details of flipping some corner businesses from Maelstrom to Claws. It was a done deal, and manager was just figuring out the small details of schedule, codes, and other minutiae. Could've been an encrypted call, in Jun's opinion, but nobody asked.

 Well, at least Motoko was not making much trouble in the past week, mostly fiddling with her guitar and whatever tech she tried to connect to it.

 Right, Motoko, not making trouble. She was going to blow up a whole living complex, or something equally stupid. He just hoped it wasn't their own complex, or they were not inside at the time.

 - I'm home, sis, - he called out as usual, even though she was almost always too deep in her work to notice him. Crazy shinobi or not, she could get surprisingly distracted.

 Except, this time, she was ready for him, and she was not alone.

 Three girls stood up from their couch, all wearing Motoko's merc outfit, and the same exact hairstyle. He recognized Motoko and Hiromi immediately, but third girl was an unknown. He just stood there, unsure what to do. He was entirely unprepared for what came next.

 All three girls advanced on him, and, as if on command, greeted him with that infernal voice:

 - Welcome home, Oni-chan~

 Something popped in his back. It was probably a pressure regulator, or something like that, as he suddenly felt very-lightheaded.

 - Come on, onii-chan, we were looking forward to playing with you~

 Nope, he wasn't doing this.

 With a dull thud, Jun fell unconscious.

 Quick round of breaching showed that her brother would be fine. She was almost worried there for a second.
