One wonders what possible use you could even have for Cool above ten.

 There is a point where too much of something honestly becomes a bad thing, particularly if it just makes you more flippant about things.

 Motoko: "Oh right! Thanks Jun, that's another song I need to ~~rema~~-rewrite!"

 Jun: "I will tie you to my bike if I have to Motoko!"
