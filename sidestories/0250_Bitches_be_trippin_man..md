**I HATH BEEN SUMMONED BY MEME.**

 The relative quiet in the apartment is like a soothing blanket around the soul, the murmur of pedestrians of this most sinful city acts as calming white

 noise, a confirmation that life continues outside.

Spoiler: Ambient music

[Source](https://youtu.be/56ZF0attVrY)​

 But within one specific apartment, the numerous neon illuminators seem to fail to spread their permeating radiance, the glass in the windows almost seems to drink it up, leaving the interior in near complete darkness, only the rebounding sounds of a steady rhythmic tapping seem to confirm that the walls themselves exist.

 It is this rhythmic tapping that one of the apartment's residents, if an 'inanimate' object can be called such, awakes to.

 "M _m_ mh? Gah dawm. Da-fuck izzat tappin'? Shiieeet, y'know what time it is? Cut that shit. Tryna sleep 'ere…" Lex mutters, his mind firmly placed within that mutable state of being between wakefulness and sleep as he rests on a table.

 The tapping continues. But now that he focuses on it, it's more like clicking and tapping. A constant repeating hymn of 'Click, click-tap-click. Click, click-tap-click'. His foggy mind tries to place what the sound could be… Yet nothing comes to mind.

 "Didja hear me? Cut that shit." Lex groggily requests, his mouth and mind not quite up to the task of creating aggravated tones.

 For a moment there's nothing, except for the clicking and tapping, and then there's a chuckle. But it's not a particularly bright sound. It seems more grim than anything, a very gallow's humor reaction.

 It is that unexpected sound that rouses Lex's awareness fully, and with his faculties now in some semblance of alertness, he realizes that the clicking and tapping is emanating from directly above him, only a few feet away.

 His eyes open and vainly try to pierce the unusually murky darkness of the apartment. Blinking a few times, all he manages to reveal is a pair of indistinct shapes. The darkened blobs are close enough that if he chose to manifest an arm, he could touch them.

 The clicking goes on, now clearly coming from the two shapes, something long and thin from one blob reaching to the other and interaction with something on it, creating the noise.

 Lex's recoil spring tenses uncomfortably, some part of his mind playing a maddening tune of fear that his components choose to dance to. "...Da-fuck? What you playin' at? Who izzat?"

 The moving blob lets out another chuckle, somehow a bit more grim than the first. The clicking stops, the ambient sounds of the city fall away as the stark absence of the constant sounds consumes Lex's senses.

 The darkened figure reaches down to its lower half, there's a sound of fingers rubbing across grimy fabric, the soft tapping of sand particles raining down to the floor below as they are dislodged. The shape moves the indistinct limb up to what might be its head.

 Lex flinches! A thunderclap of sound deafens his mind and makes his eyes vibrate.

 … But moments later, Lex realizes his mistake. There had been no thunder, It had merely been someone snapping their fingers. The lack of sound had made his mind strain for any scrap of noise, desperately searching for information on its surroundings. So it was caught completely by surprise by the abrupt noise.

 Those thoughts run through Lex's consciousness in an instant, which is more than fast enough for it to be ready an instant later when the fingers that had snapped come aflame with a subtle 'Fwoosh' of crackling powder. The burning fingers emanate a dim glow, drawing his eyes to them.

 The fingers are rough, thick with skin and scar, with jagged chewed fingernails scorched black by fire at their tips.

 Like he's in a trance, Lex watches the floating hand move away from him, drawing closer to what is probably its body of origin. The flickering light fights bravely with the dark, but loses handily to the all-consuming murk.

 The hand draws closer to the body. Then, like a monstrosity of nature entering the spotlight of a submarine in the crushing hell of the mariana trench, a pair of coldly blazing red eyes, like the color of dried blood, stare directly at him wreathed in a halo of darkness.

 Lex's recoil spring ceases completely. In an instant, an all consuming torment runs through him, feeling like all at once his body had been consumed by rust, like the rusty color of the eyes. His terrified mind suggests that looking at the eyes is dooming him somehow, but no matter what he does, he can't seem to look away.

 The face breaths in steadily, causing the light to gutter pitifully as it is stolen from its place and forced into a confining tube. Soon enough the cigarette is glowing, and the figure lets out a cloud of smoke right at Lex.

 He fights reflexes telling him to blink the smoke away, not wanting to take his eyes off the figure for an instant.

 With a force of sheer will, Lex pries his mouth open and forces air through his ghostly throat. The sound it produces is more akin to a rodent's squeak. "...Aah, w-w-who-"

 The dull eyes narrow, and Lex's non-existent blood jumps under his skin. "Stop." A voice growls out. Somehow making the offhanded order sound like a threat of violence. "I will tell you when it is time for speaking. You will listen, and then you will answer questions. Nod your head." Says the barely feminine voice, a slight Russian coloring in her words.

 Lex manages an unsteady half-nod.

 "Good. My name is T-40 Uragan. I know everything about you that needs to be known, so no need to tell me your name. What I want to know-" Uragan leans down, breathing in through the cigarette, causing it to glow brighter and reveal the huge shape of the rocket launcher propped up alongside her. "-is everything _you know_ about the mag-rifle Nekomata. You will tell me this." Uragan demands smoothly. Making no threats, but still coming across as oppressively threatening.

 Lex lets the demand run through his mind, speeding through his metallic synapsis like a bolt of lightning. Most of his body screams to submit, to fulfill what is expected just to preserve himself. But another portion, a small one, puffs up indignantly. 'What? Who is she to order us to do that? And to someone we owe so much!' it seems to cry. Although the other parts of his consciousness quickly batter it down with images of the rocket launcher nearby.

 "First question.-" Uragan speaks, not having received any apparent confirmation from Lex that he would do as she says. "-When were your loyalties subverted by the Nekomata?"

 Lex can't stop himself from blinking, his confusion aborting any response he was preparing to give.

 "...Ah, I suppose if you're brainwashed you wouldn't think of it that way… Hmm-" Uragan looks away briefly, tapping her chin with a finger. The absence of her attention allows Lex to take a few unlabored breaths since the terrifying encounter had begun. Although that all consuming terror comes crashing back when Uragan's flat eyes lock onto him again. "-Alright, when did you '_become friends_' with the Nekomata?"

 Again, Lex blinks, but this time he manages a few words. "...I… Well, she… sang I guess-" Lex nearly throws himself back as Uragan's face leaps forward like a lunging snake, stopping inches from his frame while cigarette smoke billows around her.

 "**So she establishes control of your mind through song?!**" Uragan's intense expression morphs into a pondering look as she slowly leans away from the pistol. "Then I was right to take what precautions I could during her 'performances'... Her influence on me _was_ more apparent in those moments…"

 Now it's Lex's turn to ponder, that little indignant voice in his mind taking hold for a moment. "...hol' up. You think she's doin' brainwashin' an shit?"

 Then Uragan looms over him once more, her eyes shining balefully in the light of her cigarette. Her voice streams out like a hoarse whisper. "_I am the one who asks question. You answer question._ _Be_ _Silent_ _._"

 Lex recoils once more, that small voice in his head utterly crushed under the reminder that he is a mere pistol beside a rocket launcher manifestation.

 She continues to stare at him for a few moments before seeming satisfied by his terrified cowering. She once more leans back and adopts a pondering pose, her finger lightly tapping her chin. Although she's somewhat… different, now. Her hands twitch and her abdomen convulses sporadically. Lex would not have noticed if all of his attention hadn't been on her at that moment.

 "Second question… What is…-" Her face twitches, and she bites her lip, nearly crushing her cigarette in the process. "-H _e_​r fav _o_​ri _t_​e co _l_​or?" She finally asks with a hitch in her voice. Somewhat ruining the intimidation factor.

 ~(Meanwhile, inside the mind of Uragan.)~

_'SHIT, I did not mean to ask that! My mouth just moved on its own, and my mind filled with an image of Nekomata-chan's smiling face an- AAAGH! This must be the programming taking hold! I can't let- I need to disenga- Wait... I can't look weak in front of the pistol, this is an interrogation! I need to appear strong! uuhhm, uuhmm. Yes! Her... favorite color is important because... because uhhm. It's a tactical weakness! or something... Yes! A tactical weakness! I could use it to bait her into a trap! Yes... okay... I need to keep going, act like I'm not being controlled already... I'm not, right? UGH! I can just **feel**the cloying capitalist thoughts worming their way into my brain! ~Don't think about it don't think about it~ Act like everything's normal. Deep breath, aaannd...'_

 ~(back to the outside world.)~

 As if a curtain had fallen, Lex sees the rocket launcher in a different light. The twitching of her body, the uncomfortable shuffling of her feet. All of these signs reminding him of his previous… owners. They had run through many girlfriends while he'd been with them, and what the rocket launcher is doing seems much like infatuation to his eye.

 Lex grins in a lopsided fashion, the firearm equivalent of adrenaline influencing his actions somewhat. "Ohh I see. Yur lookin' ta get all up in 'er parts, nahmsayin'? Do a lil' 'field stripin'?"

 Uragan's body goes ramrod straight in an instant, the emotion drains from her face and her eyes go dead. She leans over Lex, placing her hands on either side of him on the table.

 All of this does nothing to dispel Lex's smirk, the pistol confident that he has something on the rocket launcher.

 Still, when she speaks she radiates the very concept of 'assault'. "If you were not being _influenced_ by her _malign_ _capitalist_ _powers_, I would **crush** you where you lay. But since I am civilized enough to not hold your actions against you in your current state, I will overlook your _hostile accusation_." She leans in a little closer, but Lex still notices her face twitching and her legs shifting a little. "Now…Answer. The. Question."

 Lex's smirk evolves and reveals some teeth, he shakes his head in disbelief. "Fuckin' un _believable_ man. Shiet." He whispers. But just as Uragan's face begins to crease with rage, Lex casually throws up a pair of spectral hands in surrender. "Aight, aight. Shit. I'll tell ya… She told ereywon 'bout it. Said 'er favorite color was 'natural Mica Glitter'. Wouldn't listen when we all said that wasn't a color. Bitches be trippin', man." He finishes with a chuckle.

 Uragan's expression instantly shifts, no longer do the creases of anger mar her already significantly marred face. The scars and grease stains on her skin seem to fade a little as a genuine smile appears like a ray of morning sunshine peeking through joyous cloud cover. If said cloud cover was created by a burning warzone. Uragan was by no means considered 'physically attractive', her model was designed with function over form after all.

 Lex laughs a few times, knocking Uragan out of her current state. The pistol looks up at her with a raised eyebrow. "Shiiieett, you got it _bad_."

 "Got _what_, you capitalist slave." Uragan growls, her face akin to stone.

 Lex shakes his head again. "Man, this is fuckin' _nuts_. Jus' tell 'er ya like 'er. Worst she can do is say _no_."

 "What… are you speaking of…" Uragan asks, seeming curious despite herself.

 Lex leans back, inasmuch he can, already laying on a table as he is. "Well, way I see it? You got a _thing_ for her. Which, y'know, I _get it_, she cute an all. Not really my type. But 'ey, ain't judgin'."

 Uragan looks genuinely confused for a moment, but then her face goes positively _white_. Her hands shake a little as she quickly glances in Nekomata's direction, the rifle leaned up against a nearby wall completely dormant as far as Lex can tell.

 Her lips quiver as she mutters. "No… no is not possible, a constant effect? Even when powered down?"

 Lex casually waves a hand, seemingly quite at ease now. "Ay, you aight? Is this your firs' time wida crush or somethin'?"

 Uragan's gaze focuses back on Lex the instant his words are uttered, her face achieves new heights of intimidation as it twists into a rictus of sheer **rage**. "Ты гребаный капиталист! Ты передаешь ее сигнал или что-то не так!"(You fucking capitalist! Are you transmitting her signal or something?!) Uragan's rocket launcher body is hefted onto her shoulder as she lines it up with Lex's own body, which sits helplessly on the table.

 A spike of fear runs through his mind as an instinctive need to _move_ shrieks that he should do so.

 But Uragan's shouts continue to fly. "Ты как зомби, да?! Вы заражаете других, которые заражают других! гнусная капиталистическая хрень!"(You're like a zombie, right?! You infect others who infect others! fucking despicable capitalist bullshit!)

 Lex's eyes widen as Uragan's pointer finger visibly spasms up against her trigger. In that moment, the pistol rapidly concludes that he accidentally stepped on a landmine somehow.

 At the same time, the neon lights from the windows seem to slowly bleed back through, like whatever had been blocking them is being steadily removed. At the same time, several voices begin to mumble or complain throughout the room as the arsenal is awoken by the shouting.

 Uragan grits her teeth as the waking weapons begin to notice her presence and verbally note her threatening stance.

 But when one specific rifle wakes up, it's more than just her teeth that clench.

 "☆Haaaa☆… Ohayo-gozaimasu-des♡…" Nekomata stretches her thin limbs above her head and scrunches up her face as she tries to work out the discomforts of sleep.

 To Uragan, the world seems to narrow into a corridor with her at one end, and Nekomata at the other.

 Her projected rocket launcher starts tilting down in her loosened grip as a rhythmic thumping in her chest gains speed and power.

 Nekomata yawns, cute as a mouse, and sighs in contentment as she stretches in _just the right way_. Then her eyes pan around.

 The instant that they make contact with Uragan, the rocket launcher's firing pin twitches, her cylinder shrieks into a spin, kicking up bright sparks that flutter to the ground like snow, and her enraged face turns bright red.

 Nekomata goes pale, she draws her arms close to her chest and takes a step back. A step back that is mirrored by Uragan, who then swallows heavily.

 The other arsenal members seem content to watch with a mix of confusion and amusement the small stand-off taking place in their midst, although it is only a _very_ smug looking Lex that has enough context to understand what's really happening.

 After several seconds of Uragan twitching red faced, and Nekomata fidgeting fearfully, the mag rifle eventually works up the courage to speak.

 "...Eto~… Uragan-san?♡" The very instant that Nekomata says her name, Uragan bodily _throws_ herself aside, dolphin-diving behind a nearby couch with a loud clatter as her projected weapon hits the floor and disperses in a cloud of particles.

 A few shouts of surprise accompany the action, all weapons surprised that someone would act thusly. But Lex just cackles from his place on the table, utterly amused by the spectacle.

 Uragan breathes harshly, clutching her chest as her body reacts without her consent.

 "P-p-p-p-puuu🙀! What's going on?❣ I-I-Is s-s-she okay?" Nekomata asks aloud, warring emotions of concern and fear in her voice.

 Each word from the rifle's mouth seems to ping over Uragan's head like a lead shot. She ducks and flinches with each one, fearing that they may 'hit their mark' somehow. Spurred on by them, Uragan begins noisily commando crawling her way across the floor to her room, making sure to keep some furniture between her and her 'tormentor'.

 "Ppfaaahaha!-" Lex laughs. "Don' worry 'bout her. Bitches be trippin'."

 Nekomata's lips purse in reproach. "That's a bad word-desu, Lex-kun…"

 Tactician watches Uragan's continued commando crawl from over the back of the couch with an assessing eye. "Hmm. Well she certainly has good form. Good speed while staying low. None, be they corrupt government or invading force, would find her an easy mark!" He remarks as fifes sing out a few notes.

 "By Sir Melorian himself, _must_ this have occurred in the dead of night? I t'was trying to get my _beauty sleep_." Overture sniffs haughtily.

 Uragan finally crawls through the bedroom door, she rolls to the side and leans her back against the wall, still breathing heavily.

 "Well-desu♡, I can always ♪sing♫ a lullaby to make everyone sleep better❣" Nekomata's happy offer makes Uragan's breath hitch. She throws her hands over her mouth to avoid a shout of denial from escaping, not wanting to give the capitalist rifle the satisfaction of knowing her subversive methods have a chance to work on her Russian-made self. The other arsenal members… do _not_ hold their protests back.

Spoiler: But Nekomata is already singing.

[Source](https://youtu.be/837HW-RuX1s)​

 Once again, it is as if gunshots are zipping past Uragan as her hands vainly try to contain her squeals of adoration and shouts of indignant rage.

 '_You'll never enchant my senses, you_ _capitalist_ _siren! I know your tricks! I. Will. Not. Yield!_' Uragan's tumultuous thoughts shriek out, attempting to smother the warm and bubbly feelings originating from her chest which threaten to burst out. The 'capitalist programming' forcing her to want to sing along, despite her complete lack of ability to do so.

 And so Uragan's torment continues.
