Sasha The Nyanrunner

 Sasha, seeing the data moat slamming down, felt a pang of panic well up inside. Kitten! She quickly focused, her experience telling her even if she was with the annoying spider hag, she should be okay. She'd shown she could keep up, even if she was still a new.

 The deamons quickly started spawning. She uploaded her ICE for defense, while she started sending off offensive quick hacks to take out the deamons. She calmed down a bit; this wouldn't take long. The spider's hags apprentice was here as well, glancing at her while grinning.

 "Nyan nyan. My kawaii kitten got away from me. Now I am all alone!"

 Lucy looked at Sasha, and her face scrunched up. She looked like she was about to spit something fowl. Instead, she just sighed and activated stealth quick hacks and started breaching.

 "I guess you're here as well, spider apprentice." Sasha said, grinning as she continued fighting off the incoming deamons .

 Lucy gave a disdainful look towards Sasha.

 "Later." Lucy said and breached through the firewall and disappeared from sight.

 Sasha is sad! The stray left! Wait, there's still deamons left! Now she'd had to do it all on her own! Damn that small spider. Wait. If Sasha was alone. Then she was the stray? No, no, not cool!

 Luckily, her heavy hack was finally ready. Super neko deamon destroying punch! Fire consumed the area and cleared out the deamons. Inferno_mod_v3 really did its job well. She rapidly considered what to do next.

 Kitten should be following the spider hag or go for the server core alone. She would just go in that direction as well. Regardless, dealing with the Netrunner would fix the current predicament. Even if the competition was a mess by now. Plan decided, she went towards the core.

 She was going to load a heavy breaching hack when she noticed she was low on RAM. "Nooo." She would have to wait, damn that spider would find this funny.. Stupid inferno_mod_v3..

 No! She would just push through it. But she had to check the deck first.

 Custom OS: PAWS-OS 0.3 (=^・^=)

 ✧ Temp: 39c (=ↀωↀ=) 

 ✧ RAM: 5/28 (´；ω ；`)

 ✧ ICE: 100% (=ↀωↀ=) 

 Ah yes, this is perfect, Sasha thought. The ice bath would keep the Nyanrunner cool! She got to work. She continued on. Breaching and taking out the deamons started attacking her; it was rote work for her. Nothing really challenging. Most annoying part was she'd likely be late to arrive at the server core.

 Grumbling to herself, her to mind wandered. Breaching was an intensive affair and soon she thought about food. When she was done with this challenge, she would eat one of her favorite snacks. Sasha always would get it from outside of Randy's trailer. He had the best stuff in the city. She wasn't sure how he did it, but it's the best. And she had tried everywhere! Second best was Kabuki market! We don't talk about the third!

 "Nyan, nyan" another firewall was done, and stealth to avoid the paw-trols. Shouldn't be too long now.

 Smiling to herself, she recalled when she first found her favorite snack. Back to that one cold, rainy, fateful day. She was carrying around the cat food as normal. But no cats around, but she was hungry. Looking around quickly, there was nobody she recognised. Just a nibble, she decided. Noone would know, so might as well try it out.

 "Wow!" she shouted out loud on the street, making some people around her jerk and cringe in surprise. Giving her glares. She ignored them.

 The cat food was purr-etty good! That should have been no surprise to her, as cats knew their stuff!

 Too bad she couldn't find any cats to actually give the cat food to. Not that it would stop her, she would keep looking. Cat pictures in her Netrunner lobby were good, but she wanted an actual cat! But it wasn't all bad. She had the best snack in NC now!

 Breaching through another firewall, it finally broke, and she flew through.

 "I am still going strong! Sasha always lands her legs regardless of the fall!"

 She was still somewhat worried about Kitten-chan, fretting about it. Cats should stay together! When we feel like it! Kitten-chan is too cold.

 Sasha perked up as the final firewall was in sight. She could see past the wall, the rest of them were there battling with the server Netrunner. Nyanrunner just had to breach it! She couldn't wait to get her claws into that Netrunner! ruining our competition and endangering kitten! Neko-mama will give this bastard a catnap, permanently!

 The three of them quickly took care of the Netrunner. They had beaten the Netrunner within an inch of catnapping. But Kitten wanted to spare him!

 "Nyaaa! She can't be furr-real! Kitten is too soft pawed!"

 Strike while the rat is down, otherwise he might get up and do something annoying! Netrunners are in fact not all part of a all happy furr-amily, all being choombattas and all that.

 This was so frustrating to watch; It distracted her breaching! Sasha grumbled inside as she continued working. She finally relented; this was okay. Even strays have a code. She wouldn't nag at Kitten. But she would joke around some more to get her frustration out. Finally, she finished breaching and flew through the firewall.

 "Sasha is here!"

 Come here Kitten, Shasha thought. Bet you were lonely without Neko-mama! Wait, why are you looking at Sasha like you've seen a bakeneko!

 Mokoto gave Sasha an exasperated look and rushed off.

 So shy! Just like when we entered ice baths! Time to tease er about it! Wait, Saha knew something even better!

 She would ask Kitten to show me her Nekomata! It's not a cat, but it's as close as it gets to in the sniper department! She was sure she'd heard that she owned one.

 Then she would show her the best hits from Nekobeats radio! Few know about this channel, but it's the best!

 Or maybe, just maybe. She would do something even more amazing for Kitten; she did well today! Only the greatest of awards would be fitting. It would be a pretty big step to give away such a deep secret of NC. She doubted many knew what the best snacks were here. Neko-mama nodded sagely to herself.

 Only the best present for Kitten-chan! She would give her some of her most delicious cat food!
