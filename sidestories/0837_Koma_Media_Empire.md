Motoko was feeling good all things considered, she was really glad about her advances in her Tachikoma. Which led to improvements she could use when she went full borg.

 Alice was also excited because of the Song Bird Implant she managed to help create, allowing someone to not only talk naturally, but use the voice modulation her perk allowed her to do.

 So she can still do her Sexy Major voice, and keep using her tricks when she finally made the transition.

 Though maybe she should think more about why those Tachikoma wanted instruments they could use, why they wanted voice modules that are as real as human voices, and... well installing the programming that allowed them to become such music critics...

 But then that brought up questions of allowing them to look through the static archive she made of her own mind and memories, cause it could be kind of cool to have a cyberbrain instead of a bio-pod...

 "You gonk," Motoko said to herself. "next thing you will question how Preem being a full borg would be!"

 ____________________________________

 "I will buy them from you." Denny told Hiromi with all seriousness.

 "Not for sale," Hiromi replied with all seriousness, and hiding the glee she felt. "the songs and recordings on the other hand..."

 "...All for one, and all for love!" The three jamming Tachikoma sang in a very well done cover of All for One from the Three Musketeers Soundtrack.

 Their sisters playing along waiting for their turns, the ballads set to flow, and the various songs that never got their chance demanding to be released.

 Motoko truly had no idea what she had unleashed...

 Woe is her, the Koma Media Empire was rising...

 Notes: Maybe it is a mistake to unleash quality media into 2077 Cyberpunk, yet something is pushing the Tachikoma...

 Anime, Disney, and other forms of media demanding their release!
