".....so i reached for my blasting rod and launched a wide-scale Overheat targeted at their hands to get them to drop the guns before launching a focused one at the sole ganger charging me with a knife. So i count it as 1 dead from burns and 4 injured badly enough to no longer be a threat. But the meeting your husband set-up was very much a trap.

 I ain't a lawyer, but i advise you milk him of all his worth. "

 " I...I don't know how to thank you Malcom San! Not only did you gather the evidence needed for me to be able to claim a breeching of our Marriage Contract, you went above and beyond in answering my plea for help even after the job is done. I'll be sure to wire you the rest of the eddies once i sue that bastard for all his worth!"

 As Mrs well Miss now Rially left my office, i let my weary exhausted body slump into my comfortable chair. To think that i decided to stop running with Section 9 to be a Private Investigator because i wanted to do something safer and less violent. Shows how naive i was, violence is non-avoidable in Night City.

 I..just rest my eyes for a minute or two.

* * *

 My nap was rudely interrupted by the BANG of my office door being slammed opened. Squinting at the silhouette standing in my doorway, i instantly knew the Lady before me was TROUBLE the moment my eyes rested on her.

 And no. This wasn't me being misogynistic or playing up the whole P.I thing. I knew she was trouble because i recognized the silhouette to be Motoko 'The Minor' Kusanagi, Head of Section 9, Little Sister to the Oni the _Wakashigara_ of the Tyger Claws and to my great displeasure, my close choom.

 "What do you want Motoko? I had a really long day, my body's exhausted and aching from my last gig and i would really really like to rest right now."

 " HEY! That's no way to speak to a Client Malcom! i'm gonna have to dock you points for your lack of customer service! "

 Oh Kamisama. Motoko was in Full Gremlin mode. Shit. This was the worst Motoko to deal with because unlike Major Mode Motoko or Yuurei Mode Motoko, Gremlin Motoko had something fixated in her head and would go through Hell and Highwater to pull it off.

 " Besides, you owe me Malcom! How's the Blackstaff treating you hmmm? Pretty preem at giving you access to a full suite of Combat Quickhacks built by yours truly? Anyway, i'm calling in that Marker you own me for tuning the Blackstaff!"

 "Fuck! Fine. What do you need me to investigate Motoko"

 "EXECELLENT! Here are the deeds Choom of mine. There's this Corpo Rival of Hiromi that we think is doing some shady shit that he plans to pin on her, but unfortunately he knows of her High Table ties with me and Ichi. Which means he's keeping an eye out for both me and the Continental's goon squads

 I mean, i can still totally shadow him without being spotted till i get the info, but that seems really boring. So instead i'm gonna hire you to go be my gophe- i mean INVESTIGATE the target for me."

 "Ok. Fine, i'll do the Gig but i'm still billing you for expenses. I gotta eat."

* * *

 "Stall them she said. You can do it Malcom, i be there in 5."

 Well, it had been 10 minutes since Motoko had updated the Gig requirements on me and I still remained pinned down by the gunfire the Animals were spraying at me.

 Surprisingly, the abandoned warehouse we were having our little fracas in was shockingly well-built considering it had so far stopped any of the rounds from penetrating and reducing me into swiss cheese. As such, the only way they could even hit me would be to use Smart Guns, which lucky for me, the Animals has a whole disdained using.

 That's not to say there weren't any just that they came in small enough quantities and waves that i could run the _**vento riflittum**_ quickhack Motoko had customized for me to spoof the Smart Rounds' targeting soft to the shooters themselves.

 And to think things had started off so well:

_A week since i got shanghaied into this Gig and so far it's been nada, nil. Netrunning to find info on the target was a lost cause considering he was under the Aegis of Arasaka Netrunners, which means i had to rely on the good old tools of the Private Investigator, shadowing, interviewing the neighbors and stakeouts.

 All i could establish was that the Target had a pretty fixed routine of WORK to HOME to WORK with minimal interaction with basically anyone outside of the Arasaka building. On one hand, that made my job really really easy since i was basically being paid a whole day's fee for only the 1 or 2 hours it takes him to leave for work or return from work.

 Of course if that was all that happened, i won't be in my current predicament. Eventually, Motoko's hunch paid off just like always and i had to track the target all the way to this warehouse where he was doing some sort of deal with the Animals.

 Naturally, i reached out to Motoko because my Job was done. Evidence found and Gig closed on my end right?_

_Except Motoko then change the terms of our deal which fine, this wasn't the first time she did this to me, but did she had to always say that bit of ".**..pray i do not alter it any further**" in that deep Euro voice whenever she springs this on me?_

 In any case, here i remain. Taking cover being a pillar and firing potshots at the Animals with my Overture while the Blackstaff works at shredding the defensive ICE the Animals have so i can launch a simultaneous takedown of everyone within range.

 Frankly speaking, the Blackstaff was a monster of a custom-build considering the number of Cyberdecks Motoko had gutted to build it for me. The sheer amount of RAM i had access to with it meant that despite not being a particularly skilled Netrunner, i could brute-force my way through most personal ICE to land some of the refined Quickhacks Motoko had provided to me.

 That certainly made me a "thug" among the Netrunnner community since proper Netrunners could run rings around me, but i'm a Private Investigator. I don't really need to be able to go toe to toe with actual Edgerunners.

 Long story short though, what this meant for the group of Animals currently facing me is that now that the Blackstuff was done chewing through their ICE, the Short Circuit i just launched had not only electrocuted them, it had also turn their firearms into mere lumps of metal.

 Stepping away from my nice safe pillar, i approached the target with the intent of binding the suit so i can throw him at Motoko when she finally arrive only to be interrupted by what felt like a wrecking ball hitting me in the side and launching me towards my good friend the Pillar.

 FYI, something that can stand up to bullet rounds is really really hard, and hitting it face first hurts like hell.

 Still feeling woozy from the impact, i managed to turn my head to look for the source of said impact and found myself confronted by a very very CUT Animal Ganger who had fists larger then my head.

 "So. You're the Blackstaff. I heard about you. Word on the street is you're some kinda of Private Investigator. Someone who likes to butt his nose into matters that are not his business.

 Yeaaah. I heard you're somekind of Genius Netrunner eh? Able to pierce through any ICE in seconds to launch a Quickhack? Well i'm all MEAT baby. Grade A Bioware with no Cyberware for you to get your claws in and a tough enough hide to shrug off that peashooter in your hand.

 What you're going to do now Mr Blackstaff? I'm gonna beat you to death and rip whatever deck you got in your noggin out with my bare hands! Any last words?"

 Still feeling half-concussed from the hit and looking at the grandstanding Animal ganger before me, i knew i should just use my Ace In the Hole. But Motoko must have influenced me more then i thought because almost inadvertently i found myself voicing aloud the command word while pointing the Blackstaff at the arrogant fool before me:

**"FUEGO!"**

 Through my Smart Link, i feel the Blackstaff subtly changing as the Command word worked it's magic. Parts of the tip receded revealing a nozzle that spat out a lance of compressed flame, the [Kendachi Dragon](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Kendachi_Dragon) within modified by Motoko into a cutting torch that scorched a hole through the poor fool.

 End result? Dead fool and ammunition exploding from the residual heat of the Kendachi Dragon and setting the warehouse alight. Technically speaking, the building was on fire, and it wasn't my fault. i certainly didn't bring the ammunition nor store them near combustible materials after all.

 That being said, until Motoko gets here i couldn't afford to let the Target go up to the big prairie in the sky. Stumbling to where he laid twitching on the ground from the aftereffects of my Short Circuit, i dragged him out of the warehouse and onto the side of the road as the night was lighted up by the lights and sirens of the Night City Fire Brigade.

* * *

 It took about another half-hour of watching the Fire Brigade tussle with the growing flame before Motoko finally showed up in her beast of a car.

 " I'M SO SORRY MALCOM! I totally forgot that my Quadra was in the shop and i had to go all the way there to get it. And then when i was making my way here the road was totally jammed with cars and then i had to give way because there was Fire Engines blaring their sirens.

 I mean sure i was the only one who made way, but the nerve of the other drivers! Anyway here i am! Where's that warehouse and the Target? Are they still having their meeting?"

 "The warehouse is currently the center of that nice fire over there, the people the Target was meeting are dead and you're currently standing on the Target. I took him down fast enough that he probably didn't have enough time to delete anything. And now, i'm heading home to bed and you own me another Quickhack for not being here in 5 mins.

 I'm a Private Investigator, not a Edgerunner Motoko! now Good Night!"

_"The world is a dystopian shithole, and Corpos are getting stronger and stronger every single day. Things are spinning around faster and faster, and threatening to go completely awry. Falcons and falconers. The center cannot hold.

 But in Night City, I'm trying to do what i can. I don't want to live in a world where blood and violence are the only routes available. I'd like to do what i can for those who need help. 

 My name is Malcom Kuroishi Deresuden. Conjure by it at your own risk. When things get strange, when what goes bump in the night flicks on the lights, when no one else can help you, give me a call. I'm in the book."_​
