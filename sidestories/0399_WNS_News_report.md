# WNS News report

*Source: https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-1122#post-94775182*
*Words: ~190*

---

"Hello, I'm Arif Iqbal for WNS News. We're now live in front of the Night City Hall where a still growing crowd of protestors is currently congregating in front of the City Hall gates. These protestors, apparently all Scavengers and led by the NC Scavengers Association, are voicing their grievances and protesting to Mayor Rhyne for his and the NCPD's alleged utter ineptitude and failure of stopping the on-going campaign of extermination against them by the Tyger Claws gang and their employed mercenary. We have Mr. Pavel Andreyev, spokesperson for NC Scavengers Association, with us this evening. Tell us Mr. Andreyev, how many Scavengers are coming and how long will this protest last?"  
   
 *\*Burly Scavenger lowering a large placard with **"WE HAVE RIGHT TO LIVE TOO! WE DEMAND JUSTICE!"** written on it speaks into the microphone with a thick accent\**  
   
 Andreyev: All of us! And we're going to be here until that useless *mudak* Rhyne gets off his fat ass and orders the NCPD to do something! We're being massacred here! We're citizens too, damn it!"  
   
 *\*gets distracted by other Scav protestors and turns back to the gate shouting\**
