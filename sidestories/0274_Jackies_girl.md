"So they didn't even fire you?" Tactician murmurs, his tone rife with disappointment.

 HMG-chan nods, pulling her projected legs closer to herself. "Yeah… I mean, it wasn't _Her_ or anything, it was just some other guy… a friend of hers, I guess? So it wouldn't have been the same if they _had_ anyway… It's just-"

 Overture scoffs loudly, throwing her long hair in a wide arc that would have whapped Carnage in the face if that were possible. "It is hardly a surprise, darling. It seems most of our little cadre is kept for moral support most days… Though that isn't to say I would tolerate being used to _uncouthly_'heave lead' at a **gang** of _snot-nosed ingrates_. Pah! To think, I, appearing in such a place, with such a purpose. **Laughable**."

 Tactician stares flatly at Overture, his eyes just peeking over the edge of the table while overshadowed by his brown tricorn hat. "...Yes, _hilarious_." He mutters flatly, then swings his eyes back over to where HMG's projection rests against a far wall. "What were you going to say?"

 HMG blinks rapidly, then looks away. An expression of shame and confusion on her ghostly face. "Oh… it's… it's nothing… I just- I wish we hadn't… been in a _car_-"

 Jun, who had been napping on the couch, suddenly snaps awake, causing HMG-chan to silence her quivering voice.

 The borged-boy-brother swings his legs from the couch with an uncanny smoothness and stands up in a single motion. His feet continue moving, carrying him to the front door just in time for it to slide open an instant later, revealing a quite dichomatic pair.

 The ever-familiar, to the weapons at least, Motoko. Her short stature proving completely inadequate concealment for the towering form of the heavily muscled man with either sun-kissed skin, or chrome enough to simulate such, behind her.

 "Ah Jun-Nii,-" Motoko greets offhandedly. "-this is Jackie. Jackie, this is my brother Jun."

 "_Shiiieeet_, she's rollin' wit-da 'Tinos now?" Lexifer notes with a hint of something bloodthirsty in his eyes as he observes Jun's increasingly hostile stance.

 Overture tilts her head and looks the man up and down. "...No, he's not a Valentino… anymore. His aesthetics are all wrong."

 Lex tilts his head back and stares at Overture with widened eyes. "What, an' you some kinda '_Princess Gang-expert_' now an shit?" he suggests mockingly.

 The revolver scoffs loudly, turning up her lip in disgust. "_Nothing of the sort_. I may simply have a specific… curiosity, towards Valentino 'style'. Their sense of fashion is perhaps the _least offensive_ that this deplorable city offers. Though they don't hold a _candle_ to the Vampyres."

 Lex physically _recoils_, causing him to fall from his lounging position across the top of the sofa.

 After a moment of loud fumbling, Lex snaps back up with an exaggerated expression of shock on his face. His hands reach out and seem to 'touch' on invisible surfaces all around him, miming being trapped in a box.

 Overture _narrows_ her eyes dangerously. "Stop that."

 Lex silently flips her the bird.

 There's a fairly loud metallic *Clang* as Motoko tries to bury her metal fist into her brother's equally metal abdomen. "Don't mind Jun, Jackie. Why don't you take a seat for a bit, we have drinks in the fridge if you're thirsty, I just need to armor up."

 Every weapon watches as Motoko quickly strides through the apartment and into her room, HMG noticeably shying away from her, seemingly in shame, as she unknowingly passes her projection by.

 There's a beat of silence as they all stare at the door for a moment, but then they simultaneously seem to decide that's very boring and they turn their eyes back to the two most interesting people in the room.

 However, as they try to acquire front-row seats to the possible brawl that is about to occur, another figure stands between the weapon projections and the two males speaking in low tones.

 This new figure is fairly short, likely an influence from her asian genes, though the visible muscles on every one of her limbs immediately dispels any illusion of the 'small asian girl' stereotype. Her slick black hair is done up in a loose geisha style bun, though lacking the typical ornamentation, accentuating her rather impressively large forehead and aggressive brown eyes. A simple white tank top with a cartoon golden skull on it hangs loosely from her shoulders, with a pair of gray shorts poking out beneath it.

 Traveling down, her legs are bare, and equally muscled as her arms, and her feet are only protected by a pair of worn single-strap sandals, which she is tapping impatiently. For some reason a metal *click* accompanies each tap.

 "Well? If you _idiotas_ are done checking me out-" The figure growls, grimacing and showing off a single fang that pokes out from behind her lip. "-Then it's time for me to ask you, **WHAT YOUR INTENTIONS ARE TOWARDS MY BOY!**" She shouts, a finger thrust accusingly in the general direction of the arsenal's lounging projections.

 The loud question seems to generate a small gust of wind that blows back Overture's hair, who was the closest, and leaves in its wake a slight ringing sound.

 By mere chance, Lex is the first to recover. "...Huh?" He responds.

 The girl turns on him in a flash. "**YOU HEARD ME!** It's my job to look after my Jackie-kun, and I'm not going to let some _suspicious_ looking _pistolas_ even **look at** my boy wrong!"

 Lex once again recoils, leaning away from the projection in a reflexive self-preservation move.

 In the few moments of relative silence that follow, Jun and Jackie's quiet conversation being ignored by all, there's a soft beeping sound from a far wall.

 Nekomata blearily summons her projection, stretching her thin arms high into the air with her eyes closed in satisfaction. "Hey guys~❣ I was just on the forums and I've got some news! According to Denim-kun Motoko has a boyfrien-" Nekomata opens her eyes and immediately cuts herself off mid-word with a gasp. She excitedly points a finger at Jackie. "Motoko's Boyfriend! 😻 EEEEEEE~!♡"

 There's practically no reaction from the other guns, but the so-far-unnamed figure screws up her face in revulsion. "Si crees que esa niña es lo suficientemente buena para mi Jackie,"(If you think that little girl is good enough for my Jackie) Mid-rant, the short girl lifts one of her legs up and removes a [sandal](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=knVPvrdhIg0&ab_channel=TDiamonds-Topic), wielding it like a weapon and revealing the thick metal plate installed on the bottom near the toes. "¡tienes otra cosa por venir!"(Then you’ve got another thing coming!)

 Nekomata throws herself back against the wall with a squeak of fear, holding her hands high in the air as the footwear is brandished in her direction.

 A sudden gust of cold wind _plows_ through the room, bringing along with it the crackling sound of burning gunpowder. A tall shadowy shape with the glowing ember of a lit cigarette looms up behind the still-unnamed figure.

"Я только что слышал, что ты угрожал ей. Это не разрешено."(I just heard that you threatened her. This is not allowed.) Uragan entones grimly, her rocket launcher-self appearing on her shoulder to slowly lower and point at the back of the short girl's head. Who has yet to turn around. "Винтовка может представлять угрозу безопасности. Но ты не можешь причинить ей вред."(The rifle may pose a safety hazard. But you can't hurt her.)

 Uragan's face is blank, her eyes are hollow. She seems to not merely look _at_ the little violent girl, but _through_.

 As her finger slowly squeezes down on her rocket launcher's trigger, the sandal wielding girl raises an eyebrow, not even looking at the person behind her.

 No one breathes as Uragan's blank psychopathic eyes harden themselves to the violence that is about to take place, the glassy orbs already seeing numerous scenes of explosive destruction from days gone by.

 The short girl says nothing.

 There's a click from the rocket launcher as the trigger approaches the cusp of releasing the hammer within.

 The short girl _moves_.

 A flash, a deafening sound like a gunshot, a rush of wind that stings the eye.

 Uragan is _gone_.

 HMG-chan leans to the side to peer through the door to Motoko's room. "A-are you okay?"

 Uragan's pained groan from within the room is the only response, her rapid flight and subsequent impact affecting her speech.

 Numerous eyes, much wider than before, now turn to look at the sandal wielding girl.

 With a simple nod, she replaces the now slightly smoking sandal to her foot.

 She says nothing.

 "Okay! All set, let's hit the road! We got gonks to deal with." Motoko chirps out, nearly skipping through her bedroom door.

 "Sure Hermanita, let's… Go." Is Jackie's reply.

 As he backs out the door, the short girl projection takes a small running start and _throws_ herself onto Jackie. Swinging around his neck, she slides herself into a comfortable piggie-back. Just before the door closes behind them, she throws a final 'I'm watching you' gesture in the general direction of the gobsmacked arsenal.

 The door shuts, Jun remains standing in place for a moment, seemingly lost in thought.

 Still somewhat stunned, Lex is the first to put words to his confusion. "HOW DAFUQ SHE DO THAT?! I THOUGHT WE CAN'T TOUCH EACH OTHER!" The pistol wails, then frantically makes an effort to poke out Tactician's eyes. Though his fingers pass right through his head.

 "HEY! STOP THAT! I HAVE RIGHTS!"

 Everyone else seems far too distracted by their own thoughts to care.

_Later that very same chapter._

 Loud discordant shouts from numerous blind men echo behind Motoko and Jackie as they hoist the body of a _mostly_ unconscious man over a fence. He lands with a groan, now just plain unconscious.

 "Did they not see us?"

 "Kind of hard to see anything when your whole group's optics turn off one after another. C'mon let's get out of here before they can see again."

 As the two leap over the fence after their quarry, a pair of projections are on the ground to meet them, though they don't know that.

 Burya blinks slowly, a visible cloud of cold air leaving his lips as he stares back at where the 6th st. goons continue to flail in confusion out of sight.

 There's a creaking sound of low quality metal as a step-stool is unfolded next to the tall russian. A few short steps, and the short girl smiles eye-to-eye with the russian. "Buen chico."(Good boy) She compliments while reaching up to pat him on the head. "My name's Tsunami Nue, you looked after my Jackie-kun, so we'll look after you too."

 Burya blinks slowly once more as Nue walks down the step-stool. He reaches into his great-coat and pulls out his wooden pipe, preparing to load it with tobacco.

 *Smack*

 A sandal knocks the pipe from his hand.

"No Fumar"(No smoking)

 Burya lets his hands hang loosely at his sides.

 If one were to look remarkably close, they might just see the barest hint of a grin beneath his mustache.
