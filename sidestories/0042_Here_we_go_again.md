Here we go again

 -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 Normally seeing someone charging a Border Patrol Station on foot is seen as a suicide run of an idiot, right now Jackie Welles was not a fan of conventional wisdom. Especially since it was his ass on the line if his smuggler failed, the fact that the Smuggler had the same name as his Childhood Friend V and even the same voice was not helping.

 Why the hell was he in this car and not coming up with a better plan, why does this car smell weird, and shiny new almost? What did she mean her brother dumped her in some junk and stole her ride, and why did that pendejo in the red car take out the Mechanic and Sheriff while offing himself?

 If he asked V she would of told him that the Pendejo was her brother, that he was incompetent, and that because of an accident he had her piloting his body using a Doll implant so that he could seem competent. Turns out he had arranged the accident that almost killed her, if it wasn't for a Lost Soul Nomad she would have lost her life, and then she cut ties with the Bakkers and Snake Nation. After shooting then dumping her in a bunch of wrecked cars he bragged about how he had a way back in, just had to have proof he killed her.

 Now if he asked the Border Patrol that were facing V expecting an easy kill, well they would briefly repent their sins and ask for heavy back up...

 ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 "What the fuck!?" The Border Security Officer who had been having a typical day shouted as what could only be a Cyber Psycho started making mincemeat out of his men, after tearing the armored door out of its mount, and killing anyone armed in a Border Security Uniform.

 To be fair V was actually crippling them non-lethally, she only killed the ones shooting through the people to try and kill her, and unluckily for the Security Officer... He decided to shoot through a teenage girl with a Assault Rfile, the girl he had picked up off the floor to shove at her.

 The last thing to pass through his mind besides the metal clad fist, was the thought that he wasn't trained for this.

 After ensuring the blood was off her fist, V opened the border, and disabled the communication array physically. Finally allowing her to turn off her jammer.

 Surprisingly the guy who needed her to smuggle him over was waiting with the car.

 -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 "What kind of Linear Frame is that, chica?" Jackie asked the crazy bitch that just went up against Border Security and won.

 As parts of her face opened up in order for her visor to retract into just basic cyberware marks, other parts of her body extended a inch in order to recall the nanites, and store the deployable armor plating. Leaving her a normal looking Nomad in a tank top, vest, and pants... just with patches of cyberware and what resembled tattoos.

 "A Hybrid." V stated calmly as she got in the passenger seat, sadly they still ran into Corpo Security, but managed to still form a partnership.

 Less than a week later she met V... a fellow relic/victim of the Virtuous Citizen Project.

 ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 -NOTE- 

 The Virtuous Citizen Project is my reasoning that there can be three V, namely it was a project to create baseline people that they could program from infants to be citizens that would join the Military, and stand against their competitors. It failed in the first iteration, they tried five times to seed the population before giving up, and the various V's are part of the Fifth and Final Iteration.

 A side effect rendered them immune to things like Cyber Psychosis, a strong loyalty to certain people that gain their trust, and worst of all... Many of them are unwilling to die or sacrifice themselves for the "Greater Good" , though there are exceptions. Most will hold onto a chance to survive even to absurdity...

 -Again my way of explaining why Miltech and MACUSA saw them as a failure and scrubbed the project, while also providing reasoning for each ending.-

 NOTE The Sequel!

 Nomad Female V looks like Demo V outside black markings at the joints that look nothing more than tats of some kind, black markings dotting her spine, and two black diamond like marks on her temples. These are armored nanite clusters that deploy similar to a symbiote from Marvel, during this time certain features will form in her body, and her form will take one of numerous templates.

 Think of it like a fusion of the bodies from Ghost in the Shell, the Gemini Full Body Conversion, and a normal body. Her body is basically a synthetic organic fusion, her brain has a built in cyberdeck that can be upgraded with the consumption of a higher tier deck, and essentially she can "eat" cyberware to add it to her bodies abilities.

 Due to her high Technical Ability when her brother dumped her, the nanites that consumed the car wreck she was dumped in created a new category under Technical called Nano-Forge that leveled it and crafting up like crazy, allowing her to dump Perk points that she had grinded while trying not to feel useless.

 How she discovered the ability was after cutting her hand on something she was trying to fix one handed, digging said hand into the sand, and kind of passing out while lamenting how much easier it would be with one handed tools. She woke with her tool box gone and her arm a mechanical marvel with built in tools, she didn't tell her brother or the Clan because the Bakkers were beginning to join Snake Nation.

 And finally her incompetent brother lost control of her car and crashed into the Garage and managed to kill the Sheriff as well...
