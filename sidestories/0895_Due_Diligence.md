They don't seem to have done it yet but sooner or later someone at the record company is going to do their due diligence and get a full background on the new talent they just signed. And when they get the full lowdown on Motoko, and realize that it's not a case of mistaken identity or whatever they'll think at first because no way they'll immediately believe the teenage musician is also a hardcore mercenary with enough rep to get into the Afterlife, it'll be absolutely hilarious.

_Record Company Account Manager on the phone with a Fixer:_"This background check you sent me on Motoko Kusanagi is wrong. Run it again.

 ...

 What do you mean you ran it three times? I'm talking about the new teenage rockergirl we just signed. I didn't ask about some random merc with the same name.

 ...

 No, they can't be the same person. It says here she's a recognized sword master with strong ties to the Tiger Claws leadership. That she's run missions for most major fixers in the city, including Rogue of the Afterlife. I'm telling you, my client is a teenage girl. Half these stories read like they're about some supersoldier reject from NUSA. It can't be her.

 ...

 You've got pictures of your Motoko in the Afterlife with Rogue? Send it over and once I've seen it and told you it's not her I expect you to redo the background check.

 ...

 Damn. Alright. It's her."
