The "Evil Sound Tech" (trademark, pat. pending) made an ajustment to the reverb, making it sound way too echo-y. Motoko carefully set down her guitar, and stormed over to the sound board, returning the control to it's previous value and glaring daggers at the tech.

 "You touch the fucking reverb again, and I will slit your throat in your sleep." Motoko said. It wasn't the words that made everyone in the room pause for a moment, death threats were commonplace at almost every level of Night City society. It was the matter-of-fact way she said it. 

 Alice was the only one who immediately realized it wasn't a threat. It was the same tone her uncle used, usually shortly before someone was never heard from again.

 The Dead Man Walking just laughed it off, having dealt with many peculiar artists and thinking this was no different. Besides, he lived in City Center, and his appartment building had top notch security. There were turret emplacements and security robots and everything!

 It was thirty minutes later when he absently ajusted the reverb again, and Motoko abruptly called an end to the session for the day.

 ...

 He awoke to a sharp pain in his neck, unable to breathe, blood filling his lungs. The last thing he saw was Motoko standing over him, idly cleaning her knife with his bedsheets. 

 "I told you what I would do if you touched the fucking reverb again. Hopefully the next idiot will listen."
