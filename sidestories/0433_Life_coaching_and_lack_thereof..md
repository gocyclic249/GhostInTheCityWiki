_Life coaching and lack thereof._

 V: "...What is this, some kind of intervention?"

 Jackie: "Well the _Hermana corta_ and I got to talking, and..."

 Motoko: "-We decided that you could really use a life coach."

 V: "...I already have a life coach."

 Jackie: "Those Quantified Satori _Cabrons?_ Everyone knows they're only in it for the eddies, once the account runs dry your left in the dust. No healing in that."

_*Motoko nods.*_

 Motoko: "Which is why Jackie's new, and much improved input helped us set up a healing plan!"

 Jackie: "_Which_ I wish you would stop talking about in the open, Mama Wells has ears all over heywood."

 Motoko: "But she is so much better then Camilla! Your mother will come around eventually, and it's not fair to Misty to keep treating her like some shameful secret!"

 Jackie: "_Sálvame de las mujeres problemáticas._ Listen we're getting off course here, -just tell her what you did."

 V: "What she did? Why do I suddenly get the feeling this is you two asking for forgiveness instead of permission?"

 Motoko: "I uh, may have hacked into the Quantified Satori servers, twisted a few numbers around here and there..."

 V: "What did you do?"

 Jackie: "She changed the registry for your life coach."

 V: "...Changed it to who?"

 Motoko: "To the most healthy and well adjusted individual we know of course! Me!"

 V: "Oh dear god."

 Jackie: "Now it's not as bad as it sounds _Hermana,_ while we learned an important lesson on Motoko's cut off point for alcohol, _we do_ also have a comprehensive chakra healing plan thanks to well... Misty."

 V: "...Why do you do this?"

 Motoko: "Hey! I take no responsibility for drunk Motoko! Besides I even have a certificate of authenticity, and a license to practice... That I stole form Quantified Satori."

 V: "Oh right, the QS C _abrons_ you where just insulting for not having any clue how to do their jobs. I _liked_ my life coach."

 Motoko: "Meh, he would have abandoned you in a second, unlike your new choom Motoko! Here to keep you in a healthy headspace with your chakra's full to bursting with energy."

 V: "There's no way back is there?"

_*Jackie snorts.*_

 Jackie: "With Motoko here? 'Fraid that ship sailed long ago _Hermana._"

 Motoko: "First we need incense, then we need to get you down range with an HMG."

 V: "...I don't have my HMG anymore."

 Motoko: "Oh don't worry! The corporate expense account takes care of all mental health related expenses."

 Jackie: "Uh Motoko? I don't think Misty's healing plan involved 1200 rounds of .50 caliber."

 V: "You know what? I'm starting to think this might actually work out."
