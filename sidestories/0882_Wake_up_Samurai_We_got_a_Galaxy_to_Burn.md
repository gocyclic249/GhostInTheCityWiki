_I mean first time writing a scene in space battles so here goes:_

 "I haven't had real noodles in ages! Thanks Choom." Said the small purple haired cyborg as she devoured the ramen bowl in front of her.

 Daniel Jackson, taking the advice of Jack on bribes, seems to have hit this one right on the money.

 "Wait, there are no noodles on your earth?"

 Motoko was currently preoccupied slurping her ramen that she went silent for a few seconds before saying, "well yes but it's all artificial. Half the stuff we eat is mass produced scop since the corpo wars decreased the amount of farmable land and the farmable land remaining is pretty much used to produce fuel and other more lucrative industries. Only the mega rich can afford real food like beef or fish".

 Daniel, shocked at how bad her alternate Earth ended up, looked at Motoko savoring the broth of her bowl in slight pity and concern.

 ….

 "Okay Four Eyes you clearly want to know something if not you wouldn't be staring, spit it out." Motok said as she slammed her now empty bowl on the table.

 Daniel looked at the girl then sighed, "look, I know we might not be a home for you yet, and based on the fact that you haven't opened up on your personal history, you are still holding out hope on going back home soon."

 Motoko looked on with a sullen look which was frankly impressive in Daniel's eyes with her face being artificial.

 Frantically responding before the depressed child managed to work an even more pitiful facial expression onto her face, Daniel continued "Not to say that it's not possible just that our Earth is still dipping our toes into the stargate. We have only experienced time space anomalies a few times. It's going to take some time before we can figure it out. Of course you were correct when you told Jack that there's other advanced species out there with technology much more advanced than ours that can possibly help. I'd just —

 Cutting in, Motoko exclaimed, "You guys had me at real food honestly. I don't have anything to bargain with advanced races anyway and this Earth is kinda nice. I was just pissed at Jack not letting me touch any weaponry. I'm technically this planet's foremost expert on advanced firearms, cybersecurity, implants, and close combat. I need my toys to function…. Maybe you could help me"

 Motoko at this point was doing her best reenactment of puppy dog eyes.

 Daniel looked at her. He knew Jack was going to get pissed at him for this but truth be told we don't know how old she actually is since her body is artificial.

 "I can't give you clearance on weaponry, I'll have to ask the General to maybe set you up as a weapon expert in addition to a tech consultant so that you can get your hands on weaponry without Jack getting in the way. A job you are already in prime position for, just let me know what you need. This way you'll be on base all the time, see what we're dealing with, and maybe back up an SG team a few times while we work on a solution to get you home."

 Motoko perked up before excitedly moving over the table ending up so close to Daniel's face that he hinged his chair instinctively backwards.

 Her eyes shining, "I want food, a guitar, a Katana, and a workshop to manufacture parts to my specifications. Frankly, that dinosaur of a computer and whatever you got on hand in manufacturing ain't going to cut it for me. I'm also going to need a way to custom order parts to upgrade the fossil tech around here and at least let me go through whatever new discoveries you guys make in real time."

 Daniel trying to maintain balance, "Guitar and Katana?"

 Motoko stared at Daniel with a smile until Daniel's chair finally tipped over too much and began to fall.

 Motoko of course with her reflex 20 was able to catch him by the shirt preventing his fall.

 Motoko doing her best to sound like a certain rockstar "Wake up samurai, we got a galaxy to burn"

 Of course Motoko believes she absolutely nailed that bit. Daniel is just very confused.
