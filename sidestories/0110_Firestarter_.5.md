 "CARL IS DEAD! FUCK WE GO-" His cry was cut off as I leapt at him, knife sinking into his throat as I twisted around him for a moment, using the centrifugal force to rip the knife right back out. He fell like I had cut his strings.

 *500 XP Gained.*

 *Level up achieved!*

 *One Stat Point Gained.*

 *One Skill Point Gained.*

 I grinned despite being caught as a few Maelstrom rushed from whatever they were doing to check out what the yelling was about.

 They found me there. Giving a vicious smile to the horrorstricken Maelstromers, i raised my left hand and snapped my fingers, letting the prepared _Daemon_ do it's work.

**_*Snap_**

 Darkness instantly smothering the entire garage beside a few electronic lights still active, radios and such.

**_*Snap_**

 Even those died out as the garage pettered out into a dead silence only interrupted by the shouts of panicking gangers.

 Now let the Hunt begin. I wonder what they were thinking? When despite being able to see in the dark with their stupid optics, I seemed to disappear into the shadows?

* * *

_William "FrostGuard" Frost_

 FrostGuard was pretty pissed. A fuckin jap was tearing up the boys. Damn Tyger Claws were attacking in the middle of the night with fuckin ninjas!

 He had only seen the one. Just one, and a kid at that. The girl was fast. After Jackshift had called out everyone had grabbed a weapon, but Jack hadn't lived long after that.

 Good kid Jack, bit stupid, but he did right in the end he put out the alarm.

 But now Frost had a fuckin ninja jap killing everyone. There were only six Strom left. Everyone else FrostGuard had called didn't pick up. Hell even Byte wasn't picking up.

 Fuckin Ninja already killed their Netrunner. Fuck. Brick was gonna be pissed. Byte had been important. Not every gonk on the street can act as a serious Netrunner.

 "Stay close!" He yelled out as he forced his Ajax to keep aiming around the dark room. Bitch had disappeared as soon as the boys started calling out for her.

 Then she cut the fucking lights!

 If they were any of the other gangs it might have worked, but the Spyder Optics the Maelstrom chipped into every member could see in the dark just fine.

 Except, somehow she had disappeared. One moment he was staring at her as she raced off into the side of the garage, and then next his optics simply couldn't keep track of her, or more like she had simply shifted into smoke and disappeared.

 Fuckin bullshit.

 Some sort of Optical Camo? He wasn't sure, hadn't seen any of the usual shifting from that.

 "Where the fuck did she go!?" RustProof called out and Frost snorted at the panicked tone as some of the fuckers lost their cool. Firing blindly into the darkness.

 They were panicking. Frost realized right then that if he stuck around he was just as likely to get shot in the back as he was the front. He started shifting back towards the entrance, optics wide to look for her. She had to still be here right?

 As if it was mocking his thoughts, he heard it.

**_*Snap_**

 It echoed in the confines of the garage for a beat or two before the sound of an explosion cut it off.

**BOOOM!!!

 SPLAT.**

 Snapping his head to the source, Frost found himself staring at Stickshift's decapitated body as it collapsed to the ground. The remains of the shattered Satara falling to the ground indicating what had happened.

 "SHIT! Told that idiot he had to do maintenance on his gun! Fucker slacked on that and now the gun gone and blew up when he was shooting it!"

 "Fro-Frost, you think it might be the girl? She cut the lights off when she snapped her fingers and there was that snap before his gun blew up. Yo-you think she's doing some sort of witchcraft?

 Rolling his eyes at RustProof's idiocy, Frost decided to answer the question with the only answer it deserve. A smack around the head until the idiot focused on the matter at hand.

 The sudden quiet was almost oppressive and Frost, Rust, and the other two remaining Maelstrom all quickly gathered together in a group guns out. Even if Frost and his Ajax was the biggest piece among them.

**_*Snap_**

 The sound came again. Frost Moved as if he had a Sandevistan installed and dropped his Ajax to the ground before taking a step back. Looking around him, he saw that the rest of the boys had reacted in the same manner.

 A minute, then two past without any sudden explosions from the guns lying on the ground. A swift jerk of his head got RodLine moving forward reluctantly to pick up his gun. After another few minutes wait and nothing appearing, the rest of the crew hastily gathered their guns when a sudden groan from RustProof rang out.

 "Frost. Frost i- I don't feel so good."

 "Pull yourself together! We having a ninja bitch fucking around with us, this ain't no time to get sick! Now suck it up and fol- HOLY SHIT!"

 Flinching back from the sudden human torch RustProof had turned into, Frost hastily backpedaled away from RustProof's grasping arms and brought his Ajax to bear.

 "Frost. Frost, what's happening to me?? It HURTS! Help me Frost! You have to help me!"

 Yet despite RustProof's pleas, Frost couldn't think of anything he could do. The heat from the flames were getting hotter and hotter and he could see parts of RustProof's chrome start to melt from the heat. Even his pleas were getting garbled.

 "I- I'll do what i can Rust. Ju- Just close your eyes alright?"

 Lifting his Ajax, he held the trigger down and let loose. Sending a full clip into RustProof's head and freeing him from the torment the bitch had put him in. It took him several breaths before he calmed down enough to decide on what to do next.

 "Alright. C'mon stay together you idiots!" He barked as his little group had started splitting apart, but his call brought them back together. "We hold out here. She can't sneak up on us here, we just have to wait for reinforcements. See how this ninja bitch likes trying her hand against forty of us!" He yelled the number. If the Ninja was still here, hopefully the idea of forty men all hunting her would scare her off. Even if it wasn't likely he would receive that kind of response. Hopefully the Jap didn't know that. Then with a few pointed fingers and barked orders, he had everyone in place.

 There were only two ways out of the Garage, well three if you counted the massive roller doors. Otherwise it was the roof, and the ground floor door that they had just walked out of.

 She was trapped in there. All he had to do was keep the place secure until more guns showed up.

 At least that is what he thought.

 He turned around to look over everyone making sure everyone was where they should be.

**_*Snap_**

 It came again. This time the sound of a car starting up followed up on that dreaded sound. Before he could shout any orders, one of the armored vans the gang used as a APC burst forth and slammed RodShift into the wall with a meaty thud.

 Even without checking Frost knew the gonk was dead.

 By his internal clock, it had been less then 15 minutes and 3 of his men were dead without him even catching sight of the bitch as she killed them. Looking around wildly, a camera moving caught his eye.

 She was using the Cameras! The bitch had been running quickhacks on them while using the sound as a misdirection!

 "Shoot the cameras! Shoot all of them!"

 As the steady regular beat of guns firing at full auto burst out taking out the cameras in the Garage, the cacophony was interrupted by the sound of a high caliber gunshot cutting through with a thunderous boom.

 Oilslick, hand still holding down his rifle's trigger, suddenly only had a spray of blood in place of his skull. Slowly he flopped down onto the ground. OilSlick well on his way to being an OilStain as he bled out.

 Just two of his boys left now.

 "Move! We have to find the bitch! And take out all the cameras! Just shoot them all! She's using them to run Quickhacks on us"

 Ignoring their replies, Frost moved towards the roof. He had recognized that gunshot, it had been from TriggerFingers Sniper Rifle.

 Just as he and the boys were about to leave the garage, it came again.

**_*Snap,_

 CRUNCH**

 The ground floor doors had sprung froth with the full might of their pneumatic systems, crushing and tearing GearFist into two halves that fell and painted the ground and walls a bright red. It was as if she was mocking his claim of only being able to run Quickhacks on them via the cameras.

 Before he could even callout to CrankBoost to get back, she did it again.

**_*Snap_**

 That dreaded Snap soon followed by the sounds of multiple explosions as CrankBoost's chrome went dead dropping him to the ground in a tangled heap.

 "Frost, HELP ME! I can't move! i can't feel anything! Are you there Frost! Please Man, you gotta help me! Just pick me up and take me with you! PLEASE!"

 Eyes darting wildly, his Ajax came up and sprayed through the whole garage in wild hopes of catching her if she came down here to gloat. If she was around, he only had to hit her once!

 Going Full Auto had to be enough!

 He flipped his empty magazine out and grabbed his spare from his jacket pocket.

 "Bitch! Come on then! You've cut down these gonks! You think you're hard!? They are chaff! I'll rip you apart!" He roared out as he kept moving searching for her. How does one ninja jap do this? Kill his entire squad?

 He really wished his reinforcements would show up already.

 It was pure luck in the end, the faint hint of something glinting in the corner of his optics he immediately dove into a roll bringing up his Ajax and firing. The shot from the Nekomata missed him by inches. She was back on the roof, hiding in TriggerFingers little shack. He lit it up, not stopping the automatic fire until his mag was empty and he quickly reloaded and started firing again.

 Nothing. He waited, unable to see well if she was still in there, but not willing to climb the ladder that would take him up to the roof.

 He waited, and it was only when his optics spotted blood that he started to relax.

 "Got ya huh bitch?" He called out with a chuckle as he kept his rifle up. Never relax until the body is confirmed dead. Usually with a double tap.

 But with how many rounds he had punched into the little shack she would have to be dead. Surely.

 A gunshot and he grunted back as the round slammed into him, his Subdermal and chest rig barely stopping the round from blowing a hole through him, but it still knocked him on his ass. He rolled instantly rushing for cover.

 How the fuck was she still alive!?

 "How are you still alive!?" He roared rising and once more unloading a magazine into the shack, this time he didn't stay still. Using his gunfire to keep her pinned as he climbed up onto some of the old oil barrels that had been laying around since before they moved in.

**_*Snap_**

 His gunfire came to a sudden halt as messages of a weapon jam started to blast into his optics. Not now! Not when he finally had her pinned! Mind racing quickly, Frost threw his Ajax aside and drew his Overture.

 Yet just as he was raising it up, it came again.

**_*Snap_**

 Frost brought himself to a halt. Senses straining to their limit to find what the bitch was using to target him. However, despite his body coming to a halt, his arm did not. To his surprise, it continued raising his revolver.

 His surprise however turned to horror as his arm smoothly raised the gun not at the now open shack and the smirking bitch within but at his head. She had hacked him! The quickhack this time was at him!

 The feeling of being violated swept through him and warred with his horror of the slowly approaching gun. The bitch was enjoying his horror, he could tell as the revolver in his hand burst open ejecting the bullets within leaving just 1 round before snapping to a close.

 "Please, don't do this! I'm sorry for calling you a bitch! Let me go and i swear i leave the Maelstroms!"

 Ignoring his pleas, his arm spun the cylinder of his Overture before raising it back towards his head.

***Click**

 "I have info! i can give it to you! All the places where we got bases! You can hit them all! Just let me go!"

***Click**

 "Money! I have eddies! I was in the wars and i still have some money from then as well as what i earned when i joined the Stroms!"

***Click**

 "Just tell me what you want! What do you need from me to stop this!"

 Frost saw the Devil smile at that line, his finger came to a halt midway through pulling the trigger as she spoke.

 "Need? Not much, i mainly want you to suffer, but i could spare you if you tell me where Spiders is."

 " I don't know! No one does! He comes to us!"

 "That's a shame, guess we continue then."

***Click**

 "YOU BITCH! You won't get away with this! I Swear! I SWE-"

***BOOM**

 He died before he hit the ground.

* * *

 By the time we hit the gate we both realized what happened.

 Slowing to a stop, surrounding my car and Jun's Bike, both blocking the entrance to the gate, were three Maelstrom trucks.

 Already I could see the red eyed borgs climbing out to look around.

 "Cover me!" I called out as I rushed forward. My Cyberdeck humming slightly as i brought it to bear and quickselected one of my prepared Quickhacks. A brief scan found me appropriate targets in each truck.

 This was the first time i was attempting this, but it was really the perfect moment to prove to Jun that i could handle myself. As i launched the Quickhack, i added my little flair to it once more.

**_*Snap_**

 I couldn't help but laugh as my quickhack worked perfectly. My marionettes reaching and grabbing the grenades at their belt and pulling all the pins out in a musical ting that sent shivers up my spine.

 "SHIT! Are you cra-"

 As the world erupted into Blood and Flames, i couldn't help but laugh and laugh as the mesmerizing flames cleanse the stain that was Maelstrom out of existence. Turning towards Jun, i couldn't help but smile at him.
