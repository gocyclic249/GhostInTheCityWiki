Reporting In​

 V, after a surprisingly pleasant night with Jackie and his newest and surprisingly decent friend, had to smother her emotions as she got ready for another day at work at Arasaka Tower after swinging by her place for a quick shower and change of clothes after escaping from Jackie's place right as he got into a lover's spat with his current Output.

 Honestly, he could do better than a girl who freaked out and assumed the worst right off the bat without even getting all the information.

 Anyway, after a brief moment of freedom and control over her life, despite the danger it posed, V was once again back into the machine that was slowly grinding away at her until there was nothing left, and no doubt she would receive a chewing out since there was no doubt her boss was already made aware at least partly of her involvement in the conflict with Kang Tao last night.

 "Boss wants to see you," Carter informed her not long after she entered the Counter Intelligence Division's main office space, confirming her suspicions. "Sounded pissed."

 V could bet, but luckily she had something that would hopefully... _mitigate_ the worst of that.

 "Thanks for the warning," V nodded as she made her way directly to Jenkins' office, since it was unwise to make him wait when he was upset.

 She walked into his impressive office without fanfare to find the man himself waiting at his desk, drink in hand already despite it being the morning.

 "V! Just the agent I wanted to see!" Jenkins said with fake enthusiasm as he stood from his desk. "So glad you could take time out of your busy schedule to come check in with an old friend! I hope I'm not keeping you from anything important."

 V actually had over a dozen current operations to get status reports on, but Jenkins never liked hearing that he _wasn't_ the most important thing on her list.

 "No sir, nothing that can't wait," V said neutrally.

 "Excellent! Excellent! This is mainly more of a social call anyway, so its not big deal," Jenkins said as he waved his hand. "Just checking in, seeing how you are doing, asking if you if been to anything interesting lately... like an unauthorized attack on a Kang Tao operations depot maybe?"

 The way that Jenkins tone went from friendly and uncaring to serious and pissed was disturbing, and something that V found lots of Arasaka executives to be very good at pulling off.

 "I planned to submit an official report about that, actually, before you called me in," V continued to speak neutrally. "But considering your tone I can guess that you wouldn't want the paperwork and instead just want the why before deciding if I'm a loose end that needs to be cut before it makes Counter Intel and you look bad."

 "That's why I like you V, always get straight to the point. I can respect that," Jenkins said as he took a sip of his drink. "So, tell me why I shouldn't shoot you in the head and dumped in the municipal landfill. Wow me."

 "A consistent and useful agent of mind called in to me about potential intel on Kang Tao that we could use, but only if I acted quickly" V started, speaking only the truth. "Apparently, a small affiliate gang associated with the Valentinos had stolen a Kang Tao executive's car that had something very important inside, so they sent a recovery team to wipe them out and retrieve the stolen property. My agent arrived after the fact and wiped out the cleanup team before calling in an independent netrunner and then me. Said netrunner broke into a secure Kang Tao field operations laptop on sight-"

 "_Without_ firing their brain? How the fuck did they do that?" Jenkins interrupted.

 "I have no idea, I had only just gotten there when they finished," V said truthfully. "We used the laptop to find out where they sent the recovered property for pick up and then I downloaded everything I could from their system before it was flagged and access was cut off."

 With that, V took the shard from its slot on her neck and put it on Jenkin's desk, the man saying nothing as he took it and plugged it into a secure device. Neither of them spoke as he went through the data before he just nodded and sent the data through to another part of Counter Intel.

 "Well, seems like you didn't completely fuck up after all," Jenkins said. "We can use the data to mess with Kang Tao operations in Night City for days, maybe even a week if we are lucky. Lots of ongoing operations that we can... put to better use under Arasaka."

 "My thoughts exactly," V said. "My agent has never given me bad tips before."

 "Which I guess explains why you haven't terminated him yet," Jenkins pointed out.

 "The second he stops being an asset and more of a liability, I will," V said with cold detachment, even if she didn't mean a word of it.

 "Good girl, that kind of thinking applies to _all_ of us in the end," Jenkins said as he took another sip. "So... what happened after _that,_ might I ask?"

 This was going to be the tricky part.

 "After that, seeing an opportunity to further disrupt Kang Tao operations, and get in some points with the Fixer Padre who set up the gig in the first place, I assisted my agent in assaulting the Kang Tao depot that the stolen property was taken to for temporary safekeeping," V started. "We successfully bypassed security and then took out all Kang Tao operatives on site and retrieved the package."

 "Which was...?" Jenkins led.

 "I have no idea, since that was not my primary objective and because I didn't want to risk alienating my agent or Padre trying to take it without good cause," V explained. "Sometimes good connections are worth more in the long run than any piece of tech or intel."

 "Arguable... but without knowing what the package contained and the time crunch you were under, I suppose you made the best decision you could. Besides, I'm sure Padre will put the package, whatever it is on, on sale at some point, so maybe Arasaka will scoop it up depending on how desperate Kang Tao wants it back," Jenkins said. "Which brings us to the _exciting_ part, because whatever it was they sent a armed AV to pick it up... one that ended up destroyed, right?"

 "Indeed," V admitted. "I had no idea that whatever the package was that Kang Tao would devote so much resources to recovery it, nor so quickly."

 "So how did it get taken down, since last I checked you don't make it a habit to carry around anti-vehicle weapons."

 "A contact of my agent he called to assist," V said, which technically the truth, even if she neglected to say it was the same person who broke into the laptop. "They took it down with what appeared to be Techtronika T40 Uragan, targeting the cockpit so that the AV had little time to recover before it crashed."

 "Damn, impressive," Jenkins admitted. "Must be ex-military, maybe a vet from the Unification War, to carry something _that_ around. Fucking crazy too."

 "That was my impression as well," V said with complete honest regarding the last part.

 "Know anything about them?" Jenkins asked.

 "I had never met or even heard of them before last night," V continued honestly.

 "Must be a newish Solo then... maybe an out of towner..." Jenkins muttered. "After that?"

 "We cleared the area, with Kang Tao being none the wiser about who hit them, and then eventually went our separate ways," V finished explaining. "I have nothing else of interest to add, sir."

 "Huh... alright then," Jenkins said. "I'll admit, V, I was quite fucking mad when I heard about this and that you were involved... but from the sounds of it the blowback of this is minimal at best and Kang Tao is left looking the fool, especially with all the bribes they will have to give the NCPD and the city to clean up after themselves. And all with minimal to no investment to Arasaka besides your persona time. You are certainly a go-getter V! I like that!"

 "Thank you sir," V nodded.

 "The company is lucky to have you!" Jenkins said before sighing. "Of course, I can't _officially_ sign off on this since you did act on your own on something that might have bitten the company in the ass. But, instead of terminating your contract, so to speak, I will let you off with a warning... and a couple more additional assignments to keep you busy since _clearly_ you have too much time on your hands if you feel the need to find ways to benefit the company while you are off the clock."

 V kept her face still even as she wanted to scream. She was juggling so many damn assignments as it was!

 "Thank you for the opportunity, sir," V said neutrally instead, as she had learned to do since day one.

 You never say anything to your boss beyond thank you, even as they stabbed you over and over.

 "Of course! I'm always looking out for my friends, after all," Jenkins said with a smile. "Got to look after my number 1 after all!"

 "Is there anything else, sir?" V asked, finding any excuse to leave as soon as possible.

 "Not at this time, you're free to leave," Jenkins said, while V acted on as she turned and began walking out of his office. "Careful in the future though! Not everyone is as understanding as I am!"

 Jenkin's office doors closed behind V as she left and quickly made her way to the bathroom where she proceeded to stress vomit into the sink, since she was nearly sure that Jenkins was going to order her death right then and there.

 Dammit! Fuck! Son of the bitch! AHHHHHH! she internally screamed in her head while keeping the rest of her as in control as possible.

 Jackie and Motoko better fucking appreciate her batting for them, especially the brat!

 Still though... she didn't regret it, since those two gave her the first real night of freedom from this snakepit in awhile... and she hoped there would be a chance to do it again in the future.

 Until then though... it was back to work.
