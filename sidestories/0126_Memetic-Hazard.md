"...Hey Garry"

 "Yeah?"

 "come take a look at this."

 The cheap computer chair squeaks loudly as the plain clothed man stands and approaches his colleague's desk.

 "What's up?"

 "I dunno-" He says while scracting his head. "-I've been seeing these... characters? I guess you'd call em? Emoji's? Something like that. I've been seeing them pop up on this chatboard. and-... garry? Are you okay?"

 Garry is sweating profusely, his eyes grotesquely bugging out of his head, surveying the pixels on the screen. "W-when did you start seeing these?"

 "Uuhhh, a couple weeks ago? Some guy named [The-AnonyM0US3-Otaku](https://forums.spacebattles.com/members/440663/) told this other guy about them and-"

 "Oh fuck, oh shit!" Garry sprints towards a nearby wall and slams his fist into a small glass-fronted cabinet, obtaining several lacerations in order to press the large red button contained within.

[Klaxons](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=W_9KR3mYkUo&ab_channel=LordSandwich) begin to wail through the room, Garry rushes over to his desk and retrieves a standing microphone that he quickly brings up to his mouth. "**THEY HAVE BREACHED CONTAINMENT! REPEAT, THEY HAVE BREACHED CONTAINMENT! THREE CONFIRMED CARRIERS ARE ON THE LOOSE ON SPACEBATTLES, ACTIVATE THE EMERGENCY SHUTDOWN BEFORE IT'S TOO LATE!**"

 "GARRY, WHAT THE HELL IS GOING ON?!" He screams, barely able to think over the noise.

 Garry grabs him by his shirt. "YOU FUCKING IDIOT! WE'RE TRAINED FOR THIS! WHY DIDN'T YOU SOUND THE ALARM SOONER!"

 "G-Garry, the fuck are you talking about!"

 "OUR JOB! OUR FUCKING JOB LUKE! DID YOU NOT HEAR THE TRAINING, IF WE DON'T CONTAIN THIS IT'LL-" A nearly silent sound begins to emanate from Luke's computer. "...no... no no no!"

 Garry throws Luke aside and pulls a double barrel shotgun from under his desk, he screams as both barrels bark flames and shatter Luke's monitor.

 Colors pour out of the sundered remains of the display, and a voice begins to sing from seemingly nowhere.

Spoiler: The wave of color sweeps across the room like an unstoppable cascade of eldritch forces, crushing humanity under its sheer scope.
