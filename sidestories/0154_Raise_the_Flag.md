 "Ok ok, so that didn't go according to plan. I think i'll stay away from G.I.T.S music and the majority of the city's population and keep things low and local in Japantown.

 HERE WE GO!"

" We interrupt this broadcast to bring you the latest breaking news! In a shocking re-enactment of the riot 5 months ago that lead to buildings being torched and millions of rioters dead, a smaller scale riot has just kick off in Japantown."

 " That's horrible Jim! Night City is still recovering from the last riot and you're telling me that another one is starting up?"

 "Afraid so Sally. The MO seems to be the same, one of those _Rockerboys_ using subliminal messages hidden in their music to trigger the masses."

 " How horrible! Do you think the riot will spread like it did before Jim?"

 " We have no idea Sally. However our sources indicate that the music this time around is in Japanese and as such is only affecting those who understand the language or those who have langsoft to translate it."

 " But..But that's all of Night City! "

 " Oh. Well i think i need to sign off now Sally, i got a flight to catch. A vacation in Europe sounds GREAT! This is Jim Harrison, signing off!"

 " Jim! Jim! Take me with yo-"

 " I DON'T UNDERSTAND HIROMI! I JUST WANT TO PLAY MUSIC!"

 Awkwardly patting Motoko on her back as she laid facedown in the ice-cream bowl infront of her. _What exactly can you use to comfort a friend after they accident a Riot for the 2nd time?_ Hiromi wondered.

 After all, techi-... technically this time was partially her fault as well since she convinced Motoko to _keep things local_ and _within familiar grounds_.

 "Freedo-"

 " RAISE YOUR FLAG!!"

 " Be a Soldier, not a Bystander!"

 Wincing slightly at the sounds of the mob rioting and screaming out the lyrics of Motoko's latest work as slogans. Hiromi found herself doing the only thing she could do. Signal for another giant bowl of ice-cream from the sole remaining server within the joint who had not ran out to join the riot.

 As the new bowl of ice-cream was placed on their table, the strangely battle-harden and scarred server spoke.

 " Hey Kid, you did good work. Those songs? They were preem! The voice in my head says you showed what it meant to be the epitome of being a Rockerboy.

 The name's V and on behalf of Jonny Sliverhand, how would you like to form a band with us?"
