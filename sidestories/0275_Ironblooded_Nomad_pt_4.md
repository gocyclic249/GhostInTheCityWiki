Ironblooded Nomad pt 4

 *Ghost: How do you destroy a super star destroyer in a SpaceBattles*

 Motoko stare at her screen HUD silently as she lets Cool keep her expression hopefully neutral. Jackie came through and brought V to this meeting, seems like a Nomad origin.

 *V: An Enterprise travelling at SufficientVelocity*

 Came back the reply. She blink once.... Then twice before fist pumping internally. Finally another sane person not from this dysfunctional cyberpunk world! And no Nomads don't count!

 'Line break'

 Vicky stare at the Major cosplayer as she await a reply. Hoping now that she didn't screw herself over by revealing hints of the other world. Granted she had an easier time of it being with the nomads along with a group of chooms that share a bond with early on.

 *Ghost: got a location we can meet in private?*

 Vicky consider the question, while bring the other SI to her camp would be a calculated risk, she not willing to stake her family safety on the line. Decision made she sent the deets.

 *V: here's the deets, see you in 2 days*

 The Major cosplayer gave a slight nod. Before the communication close though, Vicky received a gif that shows the Major jumping off a building and disappearing with camo. That got a grin of Vicky as she muttered "Weeb".

 'Line Break'

 Two days later, Vicky was tapping her foot impatiently as she wait in a tent with a portable air conditioner in the middle of the desert. She been seeing contacts at the edge of effective range on her HUD from the connected backpack sensor. It wasn't long before something changed. There was a Bleep from the edge of sensor range heading toward the meeting spot. Along with another contact that pop up nearby. "You do know I can see you right?" Ask Vicky as she nonchalantly as she kick back and took a sip from her can of juice no filthy tea leaves drinks for her.

 Motoko shimmers into existence a scowl on her face as her dramatic entry was more or less ruined. She sat down at the other seat popping a can of juice. "So how did you figure out I was here?" Vicky gave a smile and gave a vague reply. "When an eldritch abomination offers you a chance to travel to a new universe, a new life, and a special ability you of course say yes....." But before she can finish, Motoko cuts in "After all, if you are already face to face with an Eldritch creature you are already locked in to having an interesting experience, always better to stay amiable." Vicky raised en eyebrow and was about to ask something before a child like voice interrupted their conversation. " I'm sorry Major! The rest of Section 9 managed to track me and are coming along !" The large car size Tachikoma was panicking outside causing Vicky to sqeal in delight as she rush out to see the blue bot AI panicking outside.

 Motoko was stunned as she observed Vicky trying to scan the Tachikoma with a handheld scanner even as she gleefully interacts with the Tachikoma, she snapped out of it and yelled "Vicky leave the Tachikoma alone for now! We got a bigger problem" Vicky look back and whined "But it's a Tachikoma!!" Mokoto gave her other self a dead pan look "Important discussion now, adoring the Tachikoma later, you gonk." Vicky pouted before grumbling "Fine"

 They went back into the tent to wrap up the discussion. Matoko decided to broach the problem quickly. " Vicky when you started that line about the abomination... Were you the one that came up with it? Vicky gave a blank look as she was wondering where Motoko was going with this before giving a nod. Matoko was flabbergasted at the confirmation "Well when I finished that quote I wasn't doing it as a fan..... I wrote that as well...." Vicky took a while to process that information... Before screaming "Wait..... You mean..... You're me???!!" After she calmed down a bit, she thought of something funny "So that means when you call me a Gonk... Doesn't that mean you call yourself a Gonk?" Motoko can only facepalm at that.
