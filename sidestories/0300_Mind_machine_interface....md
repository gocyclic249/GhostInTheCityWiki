_Mind machine interface..._

 ...This was going to be great, -even with Jun in the room next door. Recording on, music cued, and now to breach... 

 Wire line attached to the door frame I burst into a sprint before shooting out the window in front of me making a running leap from it.

 ...And cue the music.

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=buifCKc9RNU)​

 -My eyes widened and for half a second I was spinning out of control on the wire before physics thankfully worked their magic and I started on the return leg of my journey. It was only Cool that even somewhat allowed me to maintain my focus, helping me even in my surprise to shoot through the window and zero the two scavs on the couch in front of it, with several pump actions.

 Landing in a roll amongst the broken glass and bloodstained pleather, I took a few precious seconds to shake my head. _Why was that playing?_ She had only recorded the one song!

 Slavic shouting just barely rose over the precussive beat of the music in her head. The software dialing in the important details even as she rose up and steadied her shotgun on the plaid. (really plaid?) sofa and let three rounds through the door targeting the highlighted scavs behind it.

 Damn it! Now everything was ruined! How was it even ruined-

 The gamer shard damn it! Somehow the rockerboy perks where interfering with the music shard she had just made. Allowing it to play more then just one song from whatever was in her memory!...But that didn't even make sense! Even if that was possible she'd been thinking of Doom damn it! It should have just played that anyway! Stupid shard, focus on the Brrr!...

 Wait was that it? She had spent quite a lot of time grinding levels in Pokemon, did her mind subconsciously associate 'brrr' with...

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MxFAqXCULI0)​

 ...Mechwarrior now? WTH shard? Was this just a Motoko's greatest hits album? It would make sense if she was actually _in those games!_

 Well at least this one kind of fit, still as she pumped her carnage once more she began to advance towards the door. Kicking the remains down she quickly sprinted into the main operating room. (Which was really several apartments with the walls torn down between them.) Before jumping to the left to avoid the burst of a Burya coming towards her. In return she managed to get several more shots down towards the few remaining scavs in the building. Her weapon jam hack gloriously causing them to stop firing in confusion for a second before she ruthlessly mowed them down, at least that one worked great! One of the scavs even collapsing back after his gun exploded in his hand and falling back into a tub of ice along with his victims.

 Rushing over she made sure he stayed down in that tub for a good few seconds before pointing her carnage down and with another pump chambering a round to finish him off and stain the ice red.

 After which she began to police the area. No point in being sloppy even if the mood was ruined thanks to her shard! Damn it! Next time she would have to isolate it within its own ice before she tried this again. Still there was always another scav den, maybe at the next one she'd actually get some _apropos_ music! Yeah that's right she knew big words! Intelligence FTW! Calling Malcom and Ichi to come up and pick through the remains, she started unlocking door after door as she slowly made her way back upstairs to Jun.

[Source](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8CwFNFGSQV0)​

 ...And stopping after passing by a window on the stairwell, outside it a smog filled and dusky cityscape, complete with an Us Cracks poster floating by.

 ...Sometimes she really hated her mind.
