"Motoko, NO!" Jun cried out as Motoko regarded the shimmering scene that took up the old closet in their apartment.

 "Motoko, YES!" Motoko cried out as she jumped through the shimmering veil.

 ------------------------------------------

_"Major,"_ Major Motoko Kusanagi of Section 9 grunted as she received an unexpected comm signal from the Tachikoma. _"something strange has arrived..."

 "...where are you that you received something, let alone a package?"_ Major Kusanagi asked with a measure of exasperation.

_"...The door in the ceiling,"_ The Tachikoma replied to her bafflement. _"and the little Major is hugging me..."_

 Before she could question this bizarre response, she and the rest of Section 9 were sent a video from the Tachikoma of someone yelling out her name along with a shout of no, and then the camera panning up to a shimmering rectangle in the ceiling. That rippled as what could only be called a younger Motoko with bare mechanical arms dropped through, and proceeded to fling herself onto one Tachikoma to hug it. Then made a circuit of the room inspite of attempts to hinder her, hugging each Tachikoma, and muttering about how they were the best boys...

 Before the intimidating man with intimidating cybernetics dropped down, and started causing a scene. Before the two intruders got into an argument...

 ------------------------------------------------------

 Notes: A comical take on what if a universe/reality collapsed, and an experimental passage between realities got stuck connecting Motoko's apartment to the GTS Universe/reality...

 Kind of a Stargate like thing, maybe randomly showing up throughout their universes to cause Motoko incidents...
