# ACME brand Fully Usable Bodies And Remodeling™ machine

*Source: https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-1490#post-97367227*
*Words: ~340*

---

I'm in no way sorry about this; not now nor ever.  
   
   
   
   
 It was finally time! The long road of Cosplay was near it's end and she will truly be the Major thanks to the ACME brand Fully Usable Bodies And Remodeling™ machine. A quick stay in the pod and she'll come out as THE Motoko Kusanagi. With an eager smile she settled in the machine and didn't fight the immense urge to close her eyes as soon as the lid closed with a final hiss.  
   
 When she came to the pod's lid was already up and she eagerly stepped out ready to admire... *'Why everything has a slight green tint?*' Worriedly she hurried to the full lenght mirror in the wardrobe she placed not too far away and...  
   
 "WHAT THE FUCK!?" She shouted at the image she was seeing. "[IT'S THE WRONG FRANCHISE](https://i.imgur.com/y0XbVNi.jpg)!"\*\*\*\*  
   
 After exhausting the Night City curses vocabulary she dragged herself back to the ACME Fully Usable Bodies And Remodeling™ machine; it was her only shot at fixing this.  
   
 "Hopefully no grimdark this time." She muttered as the machine activated again.  
   

**-O-**​

  
 If looks could do any kind of harm Motoko was fairly certain that blasted machine would already be free floating atoms. "I really hate you right now." Motoko said as both her and [her image](https://i.imgur.com/CKk9sT6.png) in the mirror glared at it.  
   
 "You better work right or there will be sledgehammers involved." She thratened while settling back into the pod.  
   

**-O-**​

  
 She remained silent as she carefully studied her body in the mirror, brushing the hair away from the forehead to better see the symbol on it. "I mean I [don't hate it](https://i.imgur.com/bqx9ON0.gif)? Especially after the other... attempts." She sighed or did the emulation of one considering her current body. "But as cool as I would be [with a trenchcoat](https://i.imgur.com/HUJgIbr.jpg), this still isn't what I wanted."  
   
 "Please," she spoke while the lid started closing. "I just want to be Major Motoko Kusanagi."  
   

**-O-**​

  
 "Well," Motoko said as she twirled in front of the mirror. "[Now we're talking](https://i.imgur.com/TSKUWl8.jpg)."
