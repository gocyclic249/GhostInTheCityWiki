"Hahahahaha!"

 "What the fuck is she doing?" Akari asked one of the Gonks staring at the girl.

 "There..." Said Gonk pointed at the far wall, where a white and blue forearm was skittering across the wall with its fingers, then pausing, shifting on the wall and after exposing some kind of lens on the back of the hand. The arm part of the forearm deployed a barrel that fired something that sounded like a high caliber round, putting a neat and large hole through the head of a reinforced dummy.

 "Let's see them escape my reach now!?" Motoko laughed as she worked her new drone arms, sadly she couldn't get the whole rocket punch thing down... But she was close! "Soon they will fear me as I kill them from afar, and Jun can never scold me because I won't leave this building!"

 "MOTOKO!" Jun's voice shouted.

 "Damnit!" Motoko cursed as she fled her upset brother.

 --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 "What the fuck?" Akari murmured as she watched a large black spiderish robot try and sneak out of the building, with a black cyber arm with some white decorations on it, and the arm was poking out of a fitted recess in the cylinder. Almost like a missile or something...

 Hearing a scuttling noise, she slowly turned around to find...spiders everywhere...with arms...

 She did the only thing she could think of doing...

 "Aaaaaaaaaaaahhhhhh!" She shrieked like a little girl and ran to get sensei, her new found arachnophobia sending her on a panicked retreat, and willing to throw anyone in her way between her and the skittering horde.

 A few minutes later...

 "MOTOKO!" Jun shouted angrily.

 "IT WASN"T ME!" Motoko shouted right back. "I NEVER EVEN SAW A SPIDER BEFORE!"

 "HOW DID YOU KNOW IT WAS A SPIDER!?" Jun shouted.

 "...ANTS!" Motoko shouted before the sound of someone running echoed through the halls, mainly by the loud thudding... It was Jun chasing his oddly silent sister.

 ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 "Aaaargh!" A Maelstrom lieutenant screamed in terror as the razor sharp fingers tore into his face, while around him hell was let loose.

 "I just got that!!!" Shrieked a Maelstrom borg that was running from a spider drone that was connected to him by a hand and forearm that had a hold of something sensitive, and stabbing him with retractable blades in the forearm.

 "I told you that the spiders would rise up in steel to avenge themselves!" A Maelstrom member shrieked as she let loose the built in flamethrowers in her arms, and firing the launchers built into her legs everywhere as fast as she could load them.

 "They're in my mind!!!" Shrieked one of several netrunners that tried to hack the spiders, only to find a nest of spiders in their own minds. Train Spiders, Nightmare Train Spiders, Nightmare Cartoon Spiders, Cute Train Spiders, and just every deranged spider you can imagine.

 "What kind of monster does this..." Brick muttered in shock as he cradled his gun and hoped and prayed... that the bad spiders go away...
