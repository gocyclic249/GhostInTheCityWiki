_Some time later..._

 TC member: "Is that the purple death?"

 TC Lieutenant: "Shhh, quiet down! I heard she beat up her brother the Oni in a straight fist fight the other day before shoving a burrito down his throat!"

 TC ganger: "I thought my family life was messed up after my dad did one too many braindances, but could you imagine the life you would need to live to turn into a monster like that?"

 Motoko creeps up using her 'adult voice': "Oh you know~ three meals a day, plenty of protein, some exercise and a steady knife hand to deal with the dozen or so wannabe gangsters who annoy me."

 TC Lieutenant: "O-of course Kusanagi-sama thank you for your sage a-advice we'll leave you alone now!"

 Motoko: _'Sweet! Directional mic for the win! And I got to use one of those lines I've been practicing! Today is looking up!'

 *_ Motoko fist pumps. before humming a song to herself.*
