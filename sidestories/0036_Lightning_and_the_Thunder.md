So.... The whole Action sequence came to mind when i wanted to write Jun dual-wielding Shigens to shoot everyone down John Woo style. I also wanted to make the Appleseed Vibes stronger hence the introduction of the Hecatonchires System as a Smartlink.

 I originally had a plan about the _Wakagashira_ revealing the Arasaka Connections and getting a prototype Smartlink system that he gives to Jun when i realize that Miltech names their shit with Greek Names. So that changed things.....

 Anyway yeah. played up more of the evilness of the Tyger Claws and how the rank & file are just fodder for the Men at the top to achieve things. I didn't want to make the Hecatonchires too OP and not to invasive a Cyberware hence why the bunny ear look now is due to his customized helmet.

 I do think that complete control over all your Smart Link bullets is pretty good though. Since i imagined it as not just control over Flight Paths but also speed via deciding how the fuel in the "bullet" is utilized.
