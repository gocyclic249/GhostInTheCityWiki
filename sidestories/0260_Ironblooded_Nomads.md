 This will be part one, mainly for Vicky's back story.

 *Two years before Matoko woke up from her coma*

 "Dammit Saul, she still too young to go out on her own. And you know the choice she made was the right one!" Yell Panam as she glare furiously at the leader of her Nomad group. She was furious that much was certain, but she can hopefully convince Saul to see reason.

 "Right choice for who? Her conscious or our family? Regardless, you think I like this idea?" Growled Saul as he tiredly rub his eyes. " She brought down a pack of Wraith on our head because she couldn't leave one of the city folks to their fate, now we lost multiple vehicles and family in the last month. Some of the group is talking about leaving because of her!"

 "Still doesn't make it right. We Aldecaldos stick together through thick or thin. How many times has she done right by us with her mother's caches Saul? HOW MANY?" Scream Panam

 "Too many to count." Muttered Saul under his breath " But it's out of my hands now the decision has been made. I will have to follow through, our family can't survive another disaster like this. The most I can do is give her some extra supplies but that's the best I can do." Saul finally sighed before turning away knowing how Panam will take it.... But the survival of the family comes first even if it means kicking out a young girl from the group damn it for making him make this decision.

 "You ungrateful bastard!" Yell Panam as she stormed out of his trailer walking right into the young girl she was defending outside. She calmed down and quickly pull the girl into a tight hug "I'm sorry Vicky..... He wouldn't listen.... If only.." The young girl, Vicky, barely more than 15 years of age hugs back " It's okay Panam, I understand his decision the family has to come first. Still I don't regret my decision, what the point of living if we can't even stand up for what we believe in? Besides it was fun blowing up that that group of Wraith with the Basilisk! So what did Saul say?"

 Panam quietly wipe away her tears before replying "Your exile is still fixed, Saul won't budge on it. He does agree to give you extra supplies for the trip for what's it worth. I would come along with you if I could but some one gotta stay here and keep that idiot from running us into the ground" Vicky gave a sad smile " Well I expected this to happen, but it's fine Panam. Tell Saul thanks for the supply." With that she quickly started packing her stuff, including the odd little tablet device left to her by her mother. It was night time before one of the modified Behemoth rolled out of the camp. Panam was there to see Vicky off, still wondering how Vicky managed to get that armored truck from Saul, turning around only see the man in question.

 "Thought he said to only give her extra supplies there Saul?" Her response was softer than usual. Saul gave a slight Shurgs "Technically, that truck was found by her. I considered it as us borrowing it until she could drive it still don't know how the hell she found a convoy of pristine Behemoth abandoned out in the desert like that. She has her mother sense for loot I guess." Just as they were about to enter the camp, Mitch came up with a slight crumpled note in his hand. " Well that's another 5 family members gone...." Grumbled Mitch as he shoved the note into Saul's hand.

 Saul and Panam shared a confused look before reading the note. " Sorry Uncle Saul, me and the other orphans don't want to leave Vicky alone so we'll be going with her. Don't worry we got our own supplies packed. Signed Gauge." "DAMNIT VICKY!" Roared Saul as he stomped into his trailer to fume. Panam just shoke her head sadly before pocketing the note. "Take care you guys, good luck."
