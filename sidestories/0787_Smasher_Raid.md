_Some time in the future, particularly after Motoko gets her hands on the good Sandy and levels up a few more time._

 Motoko to Lucy after she joins: Yeeah so this is our lair, over here we plan our gigs, that is where I record my music, those are all the food deliver services we can order from, this is the sign up sheet for our weekly Adam Smasher raid.

 Lucy stops dead cold and turns to Motoko: What do you mean weekly Adam Smasher raids?

 Motoko taps her finger on her lip in thought: Well we hadn't beaten him yet but that is only because they keep patching him all the time. It's a dead simple raid. First our netrunners hack into Arasaka and start stealing data. The first wave are just some Saka security. We never have trouble with those anymore. Then the second wave are the ninjas. We sometimes fail at this point but when we get past them Adam Smasher spawns in. Unfortunately I have to disconect from the net to fight him since I am both the tank and main dps which slows the raid down significantly. The few times I managed to get some progress on the fight they patched the method away and the next time we got to him his attack patterns were different. That's why I recruited you, with you helping me netrun the first part we should more consistently reach the third wave and I will get s lot more practice against Smasher so hopefully we'll get the first server kill.

 At the same time at the Arasaka HQ: Adam Smasher didn't know who the murder munchkins were that kept attacking every Saturday night, or why Saburo-dono decided to let it continue after seeing their main solo and calling it a security check but he has to admit that this is the first time he is having fun since his fight with Blackhand.
