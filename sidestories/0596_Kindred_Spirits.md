Kindred Spirits

 Motoko: "Okay chooms, I have a chore I need to attend to and I could use some help…"

 Section 9: "Well, what is it?"

 Motoko: "It's super dangerous and will likely involve a lot of bullets and body bags…"

 Hiromi and Ichi: "Uhh…well…"

 Malcolm: *Loads up a SMG with lethal intent* "Say no more, let's do it!"

 Motoko: "That's what I like to hear!"

 Hiromi: "Holy shit Mal, let's hear what the mission is first!"

 ---

 Motoko: "Okay chooms, my brother and his Kamikaze coworkers need help with something and I already volunteered but I think we might need more hands. Do any of you have some free time?"

 Section 9: "Well, what is it?"

 Motoko: "Well it involves robbing a bank…"

 Hiromi and Ichi: *Very nervous* "Motoko, maybe you and your brother shouldn't…"

 Malcolm: *Already putting on armor and clown mask* "So when's the heist chooms?"

 Motoko: "That's the spirit!"

 Hiromi and Ichi: "CAN WE DISCUSS THIS FIRST?!"

 ---

 Motoko: "Okay chooms, so recently I got into contact with the ghost of Alt Cunningham and she told me about this awesome cache of Bartmoss hacks that's stored in a secret server deep in the old net. I'm going to attempt to reach it. After I cross the Blackwall, can you guys watch my back when Netwatch and all the Datakrash viruses inevitably try to stop me?"

 Hiromi and Ichi: *Screaming* "CAN WE JUST NOT?!"

 Malcolm: *Drags a beefy Assistant-class netdeck mainframe into the room with a braindance wreath extension*

 Malcolm: "I'm here to chew gum and German suplex RABIDS…and I'm all out of gum..."

 Motoko: "You're my hero Best Boy!"

 Hiromi and Ichi: "WHAT IS EVEN HAPPENING ANYMORE?!"
