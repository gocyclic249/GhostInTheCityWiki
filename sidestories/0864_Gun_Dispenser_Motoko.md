**Gun Dispenser Motoko**

 It wasn't like I wanted to go do another gig at a club with the band, no way! I was just doing this for the eddies and the catharsis! That's right! And also, because Hiromi kept bugging me about expanding her reach in the music scene. Turns out Rockerboys had a lot of damn reach, something that tickled my memory of a distant life. They were pretty much the closest similarity to celebrities of my old world and came with the similar synthetic culture that cropped up around it, celebrity worship.

 "You ready?" Alice quipped from beside me; I turned to look at the Rocker~~boy~~girl and frowned internally. While Alice might not be the most musically inclined _yet,_ she had the look down. She had a pretty nice white leather (probably synth) open jacket on with a strobing effect stitched into the collar that synced to her guitar, under that was her white and gold strappy front bralette that also had micro led wires woven into the thing that did something similar, _anddd_ also showed off her pretty ~~impressive~~ stupid cleavage. Her pants were shiny white leather (synth?) and hugged her very curvy legs. I stopped myself from staring at her figure forlornly, I would grow!.... Eventually!

 "Fineee" I groused out, tearing my eyes away from her stupid nice calves, mine were better! In your face!

 "Seems like we got some… older patrons compared to our last show." She pointed out towards the crowd; I saw it instantly.

 3 people clustered near the front and to the right of the stage, each one kitted out with various chrome. The older one looked like he was in his mid-forties while the two women with him were at least a decade younger. One was a blonde netrunner in a leotard (good taste!) and the other Middle Eastern (I think) one looked like support or overwatch judging from her high end Kiroshi's and her habit of checking the rear. The man was at least 6 foot tall and stood around a head taller than the women, mantis blades and a Militech sandy were visible, the sandy looked new while the blades were several generations old.

 I was about to comment when another pair caught my eye. 2 men, much younger and way closer to David's age than the other three. Their chrome was a few generations old as well but it was all pretty standard for basic merc work. Low end netdeck, ballistic compressor, gorilla arms… More and more people in the crowd kept having similar profiles.

 "This feels like a setup" I told Alice, watching as her eyes went wide.

 "You sure? My uncle is here and there are Tyger Claws all over the club"

 She was right… there was a crap-ton of Tyger Claws all around and especially in the private booth. What the hell was going on….?

 "Mokoto! We are charting!" Hiromi barreled in excitedly into the backstage room.

 "Hiromi, get ready to exfil. We may have a problem." I grabbed her arm and whispered close as I pinged all the suspicious people in the club to share with the team on our LAN.

 "Ohh! Yeah, forgot to tell you. You're getting popular in all the merc hangouts too" My best choom said as she waved away this very real and serious suspicion I had.

 "That still doesn't explain why a bunch of mid to low end mercs are at our venue. This feels like a trap!" I hissed uncharacteristically; my worry was increasing the more Hiromi acted so nonchalant.

 Hiromi simply quirked an eyebrow and me and then her stare became deadpan when she realized I was very serious about this whole situation.

 "…Mokoto, do you remember the last song you came up with? The one called Paper Planes?"

 "Yeah?" I briefly confused why that song became such a hit in a such a short amount of time until I realized the lyrics were pretty spot on to life in Night City. It was practically tranquil compared to the frenetic nature of most of my songs.

 "You used a vintage Arasaka Tamayura in the song because according to you it had the 'cleanest' bang?" Hiromi finished with air quotes towards the end and a questioning tone. The was rude! And I was right! It did have a clean sound!

 "Okayyyy, not really sure what that has to do with the current situation.." I trailed off when her expression became incredulous.

 "You used a Chao as a substitute for a drum line in another version Faint and then in-" Hiromi started listing off all the guns I've been using in my songs. I still wasn't following her, it was a good gimmick. Do the song, throw the gun into the crowd. Get some fans. It worked; I already had a few people coming back to multiple shows with the gun in hand. Empty of course, since security was pretty tight in the more expensive establishments.

 "-Then you used a Dian during that dual guitar solo with Alice for 'Through the Fire and Flames'" Oh, she was still going on. Her eyes snapped to me sharply when she noticed my slightly glazed expression.

 "Mokoto, you have a whole pile of them ready to be set up on the stage table" Hiromi waved her hand in my face and pointed at the small arsenal loaded into a road case with the top popped open.

 "Yeah, I need them for the show Hiromi" I told her with some exasperation.

 Alice fortunately interjected, picking up a gold Senkoh LX that was jutting out from the case. She was careful enough to keep her finger away from the trigger and handled it with some semblance of competence that indicated she was at least trained in how to handle guns from a basic level. She flipped the gun around and felt the leather handle grip, whistling as she realized it wasn't synth.

 "Fucking hell, this is the preemest gun I've ever seen, its so light! And that design! How much do these go for?" Alice asked as she ran a finger down the barrel, that gun was good but for the price it wasn't really that special.

 "Dunno. How much was it, Hiromi?" I turned to my best choom as she slapped a hand on her face.

 "Mokoto… that gun costs 10,000 Eddies." She told me slowly as she sat back onto the couch and sighed.

 "Oh, that's a lot" I didn't really know what else to say, that was way overpriced! I mean, not for me but still!

 "Yes, it is a lot. I'm glad you're getting it" Hiromi continued, she paused when she took in my befuddled expression and narrowed her eyes.

 "Apparently not…" She pinched the bridge of her nose and looked like she was on the verge of screaming.

 "Oh fuck… Motoko, what do you normally do with the guns when you finish a song?" Alice asked as her eyes suddenly widen in some sort of realization.

 "I… kinda just throw them into the crowd, usually to whoever looks like they're having the best time" I told her plainly.

 "Yes! You throw them into the crowd!" Hiromi jumped up and pointed at the open road case.

 "And for some reason that escapes my understanding you choose 7 of the most over-engineered and expensive guns to use in your songs!" Hiromi was getting louder, she grabbed a Militech Achilles and lifted it up and showed it to Alice.

 "This genius thought it would be a good idea to incorporate the high-pitched whine when the electromagnetic coils are charged up. She's using a gun so expensive that only a handful of mercs in the entire city can actually afford the 40k price tag."

 "Wait, 40,000 eddies for this fucking thing?" Alice said with a gasp, taking the gun from Hiromi and almost dropping it when she suddenly found out it was a lot heavier than it looked.

 "You can get it for free it you raid a few Raffen camps!" I protested and then tried to avoid the judgy stares.

 "…Okay, I don't think she's joking" Alice said softly, letting the front of the Achilles rest on the floor.

 "She's not" Hiromi's tone was more flat.

 "Mokoto… all those mercs out there? They're waiting for you to finish the song and throw the gun." Alice said slowly, lifting up the Achilles and resting it on the coffee table.

 "Eh?" I was so very eloquent in my puzzlement.

 "You're giving out free high-end and expensive-as-fuck guns, all the fresh mercs looking for the best gear are hoping to get a preem piece from you! Goddd, you are such a gonk at times! Arrghhhh!" Hiromi finished her little rant with a growl.

 "Oh… oh no"

 "Yeah, too late for 'oh noes' now" Was my best chooms wry reply.

 "You're like a free expensive gun dispenser, I think this might become an issue for us in future shows" Alice added in, bending down to look through the road case.

 "S-Should I just… keep them?" I was getting nervous now.

 "Hiromi has a point, it's too late now" Alice replied as she pulled out a Kappa and started comparing the color of it to her jacket.

 "Just play the songs and throw the guns, gun-dispenser Mokoto" Hiromi said with a snort and plopped herself on the couch to thumb through her agent. Rood! Jun must never hear of this new nickname…

 "Hey, can I keep this one?" Alice popped her head from the case, waving a white and gold Senkoh LX. "It goes with my jacket."
