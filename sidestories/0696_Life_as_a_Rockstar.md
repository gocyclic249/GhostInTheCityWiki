_Motoko two months down the rabbit hole of being a Rockstar..._

 Motoko: "Alright, the penthouse studio has been pretty nice, but I think I'm done with this for now. How is a girl supposed to get any stealth kills when there are groupies shouting her name out on the street!"

 Malcolm: "Or that one time you where about to murder that gonk before he started dancing to 'Come as you are' on the radio?"

 Motoko: "-It just seemed kind of awkward to kill him during that."

 Hiromi: "...I'm still upset you murdered our client from the other week."

 Motoko: "Entirely different case! He had a shrine to me Hiromi,_In his bedroom!_ It's the principle of the whole thing. I can't work for someone like that! Even if they don't know who exactly they've hired."

 Ichi: "Well that's because you always dye your hair and change your eye color before a gig-"

 Hiromi: "Shhshshhsh! _We like_ red hair, purple eyes Motoko."
