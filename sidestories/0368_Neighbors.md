Assuming Jun and her decide to move out of their twice-attacked home, would they stay in Japantown or would Motoko use her meta knowledge to get them to relocate somewhere else with more plot relevance?

 Honestly I'm just considering how funny it would be if the siblings move to Chinatown and get a apartment in Megablock H10 - preferably as close as possible to V's starting location at Apartment 0716 lol.

 Loud knocks echoed in the apartment.

 V's eyes slowly opened as agonizing sunlight seeped through her window shutters. Her new apartment was a utter quagmire with recently acquired used furniture, guns, and empty liquor bottles scattered about - it was a total mess which was oddly fitting considering that her own life was also a mess.

 The obnoxious knocking continued and V was going to _absolutely kill_ whoever was at the door.

 She felt like crap, she looked like crap, and she's pretty sure she smelled like crap. And the really sad part is that she was _much_ worse just a few months ago. Good lord, being forced to go cold turkey by getting cut off from all her Arasaka mandated performance drugs was one of the most excruciating experiences of her entire life. It was only recently that her substantially de-chromed body finally recovered enough for her to be able to walk around and act like a relatively normal person again.

 She might also be currently using near-lethal levels of alcohol as a cheap way to compensate.

 The latest series of knocks finally roused her to action. She crawled out of her bed pod and hissed miserably to herself as she snuggled against the cold tiles of her living room floor. After gritting her teeth for a few seconds, she finally stood and wobbled to the front door, but not before snatching a revolver from a nearby table. She wasn't a complete gonk after all.

 As far as she knows, only Jackie should be aware of her new apartment and he would call her beforehand if he wanted to stop by so already she was in a foul mood.

 V carefully looked through the peephole of her front door and instantly paled in dread…oh no. Not now.

 How did she…oh right, she's a "super elite-level netrunner" as she puts it. Of course she tracked her down.

 More knocks roused V from her thoughts and she reluctantly opened the door with a sigh.

 Motoko, the absolute prick, stood in front of her with a beaming smile on her face like she was having the best day of her life. "Hiya V! It's great to see you!" Her hair was sparkling clean, her clothing seemed fancier than normal, and she even went out of her way to put on perfume. She looked like the living avatar of joy and hospitality.

 V stared at her.

 Motoko beamed back.

 Yeah, V couldn't handle this.

 She slowly rubbed her eyes. "Yeah, it's way too early for this so I'm going back to bed…"

 Motoko's smile slightly faulted "...but it's 1 PM?"

 "Yep, definitely too early. Bye." V hurriedly moved to shut the door but the Japanese brat had the sheer _audacity_ to block the door with her foot.

 V's sudden visage of incandescent rage had little effect against Motoko's mighty grin. Couldn't the stupid kid just take a hint that now was a bad time?

 "Kusanagi, move your foot." she angrily growled out with lethal intent. Whenever she used that specific tone of voice, the instinct of even the stupidest of gonks was alerted to the fact that they were playing with fire and had to choose their next actions carefully.

 So of course her "death voice" had no effect on Kusanagi because of course it didn't.

 Motoko rolled her eyes and puffed out her cheeks in a massive pout. "Alright, I can see that you probably had a rough night so I'll let you rest."

 V let out a sigh of intense relief.

 "But first you have to take my welcome present!" She happily stated before holding out a large paper box with both hands.

 V absently nodded her head before the words registered in her brain. "...A what now?"

 "A welcome present! Back in the olden days people in the neighborhood would greet new neighbors with a gift of Jello so that's what I did!"

 She gracefully removed the top of the package and the remaining four walls of the box fell downwards revealing a large scowling figure of V made of Jello, complete with strands of strawberry jelly on the right side of her quivering jelly head. Right next to her V mold was a smiling figurine that clearly resembled Motoko made with purple grape-flavored gelatin. The two figures were holding hands and seemed to be laying on a field of green jelly that was probably supposed to represent grass.

 On the bottom of the paper frame was the words "~~BEST~~ SECOND BEST FRIENDS 4EVER"

 "I promised Hiromi that she would always be my ultimate best friend but being second place is nothing to sneeze at!"

 Now, V's frayed mind made several rapid deductions about this absolute _catastrophe_ that was in front of her but one thought stood out like a blaring siren.

 "...Neighbors?" She whispered in horror.

 "Yep! Me and Jun-ni live just next door to you. You know what that means?"

 Motoko leaned in with a slightly demented grin on her face. "That means I can easily visit you everyday. Every. Single. Day. I can hang out at your place all day long, we can ride together, kill Scavs together, game together, watch anime BD's together, hang out with Jackie together…"

 V calmly held her hand up as she made an emergency call with her internal agent. "Hi Jackie. Are you busy? Nah nothing's wrong…Yeah I'm good. Listen, I might need your help moving later because my new place is a total no go. I can't go into specifics right now but let's just say I would rather jump from the roof of the block than live here for another second of my life."

 "So Rude!"
