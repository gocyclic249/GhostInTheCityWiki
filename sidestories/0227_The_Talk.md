The Talk​

 For the upcoming plan against the Netrunner and his small army of minor muscle was to work Motoko had to think of ways to cover all the angles and reduce the risk as much as possible for herself and her chooms. So while the plan to isolate said poser gang on the roof was near foolproof and she knew the exit her targets would take one should always prepare for the worst case scenario, and have the proper hardware to deal with it. Thankfully even she knew that the Uragan would be overkill right now, so that was still safely stashed under her bed at home, but there was one little lady that had just recovered and would love to be out in the field again.

 Sadly, Motoko's part of the mission meant that she couldn't wield her herself, so she had to rely on her team to use and take good care of her. But like all mothers, she needed to make sure just how precious she was before she let them take her for a spin.

 "This is a Militech Mk. 31 HMG, HMG-chan for all of you, and the most beautiful lady you guys or gal will every lay their eyes and hands on," Motoko started off by showing off her baby to her friends, Ichi and Malcolm suitable enraptured by her while Hiromi just snorted. Philistine! "She was designed to be mounted on turrets, combat vehicles, mechs/robots, and carried only by those with the muscles or chrome to wield her effectively. She isn't fat, she's _thicc_. She needs wielders who can handle her kick and direct her the right way. She is a classy and high class lady, so reload speed is a bit slow, but the vast majority of the time once you run out of ammo you won't even need it since everything you will have shot at is likely already chunky salsa. She recently just got all fixed up after a scrap with some Wraiths in the Badlands so you better treat her right!" Motoko warned.

 No one argued as she walked them through how to set her up in the back of Ichi's truck and fire her from a prone position should the need come up and they need to fill the lobby of the apartment complex with enough holes to make cheese jealous.

 She didn't like the look the boys were sending her, would have to keep an eye on them to make sure they didn't try to steal her baby away. She couldn't totally blame them though, who wouldn't fall in love at first sight?

 Seeing HMG-chan being put back into use did bring a tear to her eye though, and Motoko promised she would find wonderful reason to let her truly unleash her fury in some future job sometime soon.

 Maybe she should look into finding her some LMG little sisters too? Make sure she isn't lonely at all.

 Sure she and Uragan bonded, but their personality differences made it hard to truly connect, you know?

 Either way, her family of heavy firepower would grow in time and she would love them all!
