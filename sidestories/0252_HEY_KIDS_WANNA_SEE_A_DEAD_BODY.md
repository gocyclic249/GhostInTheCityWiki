# “HEY KIDS, WANNA SEE A DEAD BODY?”

*Source: https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-776#post-92485143*
*Words: ~380*

---

Benevolent Ghost overlord might be pretty fun. Something like:  
   
 \*400 years into the future\*  
 \*The Night City Shinto Shrine is ablaze, it's Tyger Claws guards slain\*  
 \*Two small figures run deeper into a cordoned off portion of the shrine, bleeding as they make their way past some strangely spider shaped Koma-inu\*  
 \*They finally tumble to a stop infront of a large mirror\*  
 \*The heavy steps of their Borg pursuer slows in the background, as it realizes that it's prey is trapped\*  
 "Gonk, this is a dead end, we are fuuuuuuucked"  
 "Mother told told me, that if ever all hope was lost, I should come to this shrine, and pray to our ancestors, and they would protect me."  
 "I can't believe we are chooms, your mother was obviously mumbo jumbo bullshitting you"  
 \*The doors behind them burst open, as a baleful set of red optics peers within, as both of the kids go pale\*  
 "Whats wrong, you look like you've seen a Ghost", It chuckles darkly, as it raises it's mantis blades, "Well whatever, the Kusanagi bloodline ends tonight, any last words?"  
 \*It fails to notice the spiderlike Koma-inu turn behind it in the mirror\*  
 "Yeah Scopshitter, Motoko Motoko Motoko"  
 \*Angered, the Borg prepares to pounce, failing to realize that what appears to be a dust cloud behind it is slowly getting increasingly solid\*  
 "Motoko has been dead for over 200 years, even she can't save you now, heh, I bet she was just some small time whore your family hyped up, didn't she deal with the Mox after all? Any way, time to-"  
 "HEY KIDS, WANNA SEE A DEAD BODY?"  
 \*It's the second to last thing the Borg hears, as suddenly it's world begin to tumble and spin, before coming to a sudden stop, as it gazes with horror at the fact that the body slumping onto the ground infront of it's slowly dimming eyes is it's own, and that standing with one foot firmly planted on the body's back is a shapely woman with purple hair and red eyes, dressed in a leotard and jeans. The as it dies, it hears one last taunt\*  
 "What's wrong, seen a Ghost?"
