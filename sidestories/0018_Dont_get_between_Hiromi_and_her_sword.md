The fact that Adam Smasher had the same type of Sandevistan is because he gets all the bleeding edge gear, it is also unknown how long that Sandevistan was in the military before David got it, and then their was the time since he got it. It is very likely that by the time the fight with Adam Smasher happened is when the first of those Warp Dancers were starting to hit the market, or at least on the verge of being more commonly sold by the right people with the right connections.

 Basically when Maine wanted it, it was still bleeding edge and impossible to get, and by the end of David's life it was somewhat uncommon. Mainly because of the rampant state of competitive development between the Corps, within months they will have a competitor to the newest thing to come out and working on the next thing. Worse for David is that he had the bad luck to get a exoskeleton that relied on big and obvious weak points, that Adam Smasher easily identified...

 Now if it was something like the Rhino from Marvel, well he could beat the piss out of Adam Smasher.

 _______________________________________________________________________________________________________

 "Motoko..." Hiromi muttered as she stared at the sight on tv, of Adam Smasher vs... a Ninja?

 "It fucking worked!?" Motoko cried out in glee, obviously happy to see her creation getting in a real fight. "Man, I can't wait to see who wins... Smasher or the Cyber Ninja!!!"

 Hiromi just gaped in shock as the ninja and Smasher started...brawling, while three people including the girl who Adam Smasher nearly landed on just watched in shock. Seriously they were just throwing hands like it was a boxing match or something.

 "...What the fuck is this!?" Motoko exclaimed angrily. " I didn't give him that just in case so he could act like he was a fucking boxer!? This is not a fucking boxing match!? Where is the blood and drama!?"

 "Listen Motoko..." Hiromi started trying to calm Motoko until she saw it... he threw her baby, well at least it should have been her baby. But Motoko said she couldn't break up the set, and he just tossed it like it was junk! "...get my bike, and the shard for the suit you said didn't fit your theme..."

 "Really?" Motoko asked before looking into her eyes, and then she grinned so widely that it looked almost maniacal. "PREEM!"

 ------------------------------------------

 "You stupid Gonk!" Rebecca hissed as they drove the van away from Corpo Plaza, while glaring back at David who wasn't really in a position to protest. Namely because he was dealing with the fact he was disarmed, literally. "You had a chance to Zero ADAM SMASHER! And you wasted it with a contest between who has the better CHROME!?"

 "... I just wanted to buy time for you guys..?" David answered in an uncertain tone.

 "Are you asking us or telling us?" Falco asked as he gave David an incredulous look, obviously not believing a word of it.

 "I was beating him though!" David responded vehemently in a sulky tone, while glaring at his cut off arms...

 "You two were boxing like a bunch of Gonks for like twenty minutes before the actual Ninja showed up on that Bike and took up that Preem sword you kept treating like trash!" Rebecca snorted in disdain as she glared at him, mainly because after messing around with the sword that came with his new body... She started wanting that Motoko girl to make her a Preem Blade for when she wanted to get up close with a Gonk.

 "Now you and Adam Smasher will be known as the two Gonks who had a Chrome measuring contest only to be cut down in seconds..." Falco spoke up as he looked in the mirror to check on Lucy, who was poking David's cut off legs. "That fucking Borg is going to be a Legend, while you will just be the Gonk who could of defeated Adam Smasher."

 ------------------------------------------

 "I told you that you would be mine..." Hiromi whispered to the HF Blade she named Muramasa, the baby that the Gonk had mistreated. "No one shall take you from me again..."

 "Sorry..." Motoko muttered from the corner, where she was lamenting her errors in giving her baby to such a Gonk that couldn't appreciate its value. "What if I customize the suit for you?"

 "That would be a start..." Hiromi replied with a smug smile, sure she could never take the credit for zeroing Adam Smasher, but her baby was hers again.
