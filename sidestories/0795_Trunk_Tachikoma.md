**Trunk Tachikoma**

_Mitchell "Wheelz" Norman_

 "This is a really bad idea Mitch..." he heard Will say to his right on lookout, distracting him away from the Car Cracker working on breaking into the preem Quadra they were posted up by. Black, with purple highlights, modded all to hell. Serious eddies for any respectable booster, such as himself. "I heard rumours about this car, choom. All sorts of gonks ended up flatlined, messing with it." Will continued on, damn gonk just needed to keep an eye out and not ruin his vibe here.

 The screen on the Cracker fritzed for a second, before continuing its work. "Ah shove it, ain't no way your dumbass heard anything about one car out of the whole city. Just a fuckin' story to try and scare guys like us from a big payday." Any second now, the shard would finish burning and they'd be burning rubber on the way to the chop shop across town. If he were a gonk brained moron, he'd use one of the closer establishments. But Mitch "Wheelz" Norman wasn't no gonk, he was as professional as they came. His Output even said so.

 "Mitch, I'm fucking serious." Will, actually sounding scared, the little bitch kept blabbering "I heard what I heard, ain't no other car in Night City like this one. Gonks have tried this before, and they never get heard of after their celebration call to chooms. Sometimes the shop they go to just goes fuckin' quiet." The Cracker's screen flashed green, and the shard popped free. Hot and ready to go, he slotted it into his port and started unlocking the car "Then people started saying guys never even pulled away with the wheels they'd just-" Will's mouth snapped shut as he whirled on him, stupid gonk wasn't even watching out anymore.

 "Stow it you fuckin' whiner, we're in. Eddies a rolling, as soon as we roll this preem ass ride into Sander's shop." He sent the command, both passenger and driver side doors opening with a smooth hiss, and climbed in while motioning for Will to get his ass 'round the other side already. He'd leave him here if it meant he didn't have to listen to his bitching, but his Choom was his backup right now. Not for this job, easy as it was, but Sander's grease vultures could get awfully violent. If they thought they could get away with it.

_Damn, fuckin' nova interior_, he thought as soon as he sank into the driver's seat. Had to admire the Black synth leather seats and purple trimming everywhere, _you had good taste choom, shame that I need the eddies more than you. Gotta be a rich one to get something like this, probably be driving your second fuckin' car to whine to the NCPD tommorow._ Will clambered in, slamming his door shut, just as the engine roared to life. He noticed the box Will had practially stepped on when getting himself situated, _might have to check that out on the way,_"Get ready Choom, I wanna see what this baby can do before we hand her off!" with that declaration, he shifted into first and went to dump some serious acceleration onto the road. Only, the car didn't shoot off like he'd expected.

 No sound of burning rubber and an engine being let loose. Instead, the damn door locks echoed with a thunk throughout the interior. "Wait, what the fuck!?" he shouted out, trying to get the damn thing moving, feet maniplulating pedals with a frantic energy. "I fuckin' told you Choom, this car was bad fuckin' news!" Will yelled back, desperatley trying to get his door open, damn near pulling his iron to try and blow the 'window' out. He was about to tell the gonk where to shove his bad fuckin news when he heard it, and a twitch of his eye caught just what it was.

 The space between the seats had hissed, a little hatch sliding open. There, looking scary as all hell for how fuckin' small it was, was a blue spider-like drone. Optics whirring, a white sphere with three red pinpoints tracking him, the damn thing tilted over like it was a fuckin dog trying to figure out who the hell you were. _Not that he'd seen it in real life, but he heard that the ones on TV were based on real Dogs_. Then, before he could even register, the big thing on it's back flipped up and open. Last thing that went through his mind, before the bullet, was the thought _maybe I shoulda' listened to fuckin' Will for once._

_Tachikoma 01_

 The naughty intruders were neutralised, just like the Major had tasked her to do if anyone tried to steal the Major's car. She did a little dance for her success, gun abdomen wiggling away as her internal logs flashed with little animated stars and sparkles. _The Major will be so happy, Tachikoma only had to fire 2 rounds for mission success this time._ The incident with her internal 9mm weapon going full auto, spraying down the interior of the Quadra flashed through her logs for a brief moment, stilling her victory wiggle for a split second. But then she started up again as she sent a communication request to the Major, _Oh, maybe the Major will let me play online again! I cannot wait to race against other Human's, proving the superiority of the Majors work over their pitiful attempts._ Agent ringing away, the Tachikoma bobbed in a fascimile of a nod once, _yes, today has been a good day._

_Motoko Kusanagi

 Good job, Tachi-chan._ she thought, as she ended the call with her cute little bot. _Try and steal her babies would they?_ she started making her way back to the Quadra, today had seen here spending quite a while going over some stuff at the Roundabout in person, mentally groaning over how many times this had happened now. _Well, witness the power of this fully automated car security system, Trunk Tachikoma!_ she smirked to herself, before stuttering on a step "Damn, I'm going to be cleaning Gonk juice out of my car all afternoon!_"_ she yelled in exasperation, grumbling all the way back. _Ah well, least I'll have my cute little Tachi-chan to talk to while I work._
