 Motoko: "David, your mom was badly injured, but we managed to stabilize her. 

 David: "..."

 Motoko: "Most of her."

 David: "..."

 Motoko: "Mostly, her brain. But we can rebuild her. Make her better, stronger, faster."

 David: "..."

 Motoko: "Until then, I put a headset that allows her to control my Minotaur and a range extender I cobbled together so she can do it from our ripperdoc's office."

 Gloria: "I'm not in pain and am actually getting used to being in this thing, David."

 David: "..."

 Motoko: "Now I understand you have questions, right?"

 David: "..." _nods_

 Motoko: "Is it about the dress?"

 David: "..." _nods_

 Gloria: "Well, I'm not going to stomp around town without wearing anything. It's indecent. Now here's your lunch, honey. Don't be late to class."

_David_ _takes his lunch from Motoko's hands and zombie walks into his academy while all his classmates silently stare at the mech stomping around wearing a polka-dotted dress and a wig and a crudely drawn smiling face on it; happily waving one of it's large gun arms._
