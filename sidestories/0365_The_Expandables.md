 It's 2:30am, Seras. I hate that this popped into my head.

 Omake: The Exp _a_ ndables

 Kusanagi Jun -- The Oni -- chewed thoughtfully in silence. For years, he knew something had been missing. Ever since his beloved sister woke from her coma. Ever since he began cutting costs to pay for her care and recovery. Ever since every eddie mattered.

 From before he was The Oni.

 That feeling, the wrongness, it never left him. But the puzzle pieces never fit quite right. There was always that one piece missing, within his incredibly observant mind. Until his sister, his oddball, irritatingly lethal sister, tried and failed to bully him the day previous.

 "You know the worst part about those things? It isn't even that they suck, because they DO, Jun," she'd said, out of nowhere. "It's that they lie. They aren't an XXL anything! It's all a lie! _They're lying to you_. [It's just a normal sized burrito](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=POEwEhT-9ig)."

 One simple statement. An innocent fact, conjured from nothing, to fill the silence. Jun's world changed.

 "I need to plan a heist," said Jun.

 --

 Jun sat, some time later, still brooding. Planning a heist was difficult. He couldn't recruit a team full of Kamikaze. While it might be cathartic in the extreme, it wasn't what he needed. Jun needed a team with focus. With the same drive for justice. With.. a wider array of skills than he himself possessed.

 His eyes slid over to his sister. Who immediately stood up and walked off into her room, before returning with.. a physical NCPD badge and a stock Unity.

 "Here, hold these for a sec," she said, passing the two items over. Jun simply held them, clearly confused about what was hap--

 "YOU'RE WAY OUTSIDE THE LAW ON THIS ONE, KUSANAGI," his demon sibling yelled. "TURN IN YOUR BADGE AND GUN, THEN GET THE HELL OUT OF MY OFFICE!"

 Then she snatched them both back and stomped back into her room. Less than a second later a handful of contacts appeared on his internal HUD. Well. According to those dossiers, he suddenly had access to most of a team. Time to fill it out.

 --

 Jun stood on a rooftop across the street from BurritoCorp's main offices, looking upon his team with.. pride? Yes, pride. Here, a group of mostly unrelated individuals, all united in common cause. Or being paid exceedingly well. That was basically the same thing.

 He. Kusanagi Jun. The Oni. Close and ranged combat expert. Hyperlethal designation. Unstoppable. Hungry.

 Akari. His second in command. Potentially insane. Excellent fighter. Fully willing to kill anyone and anything. He assumed she loved burritos as much as he did -- it was probably why she spent so much time with him. Common interests.

 Rita. Mox bouncer. Great with a baseball bat. Better at reading people. If their little team had a face, she could be it. Likely also a burrito lover, given how many times she's brought up dinner around him.

 Gloria Martinez. The medic. An experienced pro. Also a more recent acquaintance, but she and Jun found themselves texting back and forth pretty frequently. The first time they met was a chance encounter, but he _had_ been eating a burrito at the time. He could trust her.

 A good core group. But this wasn't the kind of job that could leave anything to chance. So he reached out to the contacts provided by his sister. He knew she knew people, and if they were half as skilled as she? This job was a milk run.

[Owen Evans](https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-862#post-93228807). A solid Netrunner his sister ran across once. Not the greatest dancer on the stage, but certainly not the worst. Primarily experienced in Corpo defense, but anyone good at that kind of work needs to know how to work the other side of the coin. Slightly twitchy, but that's to be expected. Some people find it tough to be near The Oni, and his burrito preferences were entirely unknown.

 Kotoko Musanagi. Teenager. Probably his sister's age. Apparently a highly skilled all-rounder, according to her dossier. Odd choice in fashion -- in a world of cybernetic eyes, who needed big thick black glasses [like those](https://c8.alamy.com/comp/R2RF2R/-R2RF2R.jpg)? An unfortunately large nose, too. Perhaps that's the explanation for the glasses, to draw attention away from the nose. Known to hate burritos, but willing to fight for love and justice. Fond of staring at Owen for some reason. Menacingly. Some prior history, maybe.

 Uzumakissei Naruhyoudou. Kotoko's.. partner? student? Something like that. Perfectly average looking at first glance, but something about him.. is wrong. Off-putting. He's signed on as extra firepower. He and Kotoko are planning to work slightly detached. It's probably for the best. He doesn't need to be anywhere near the other girls.

 "This is an unbeatable team," said Jun. "You all know your jobs. This is a precision strike. We get in, make our changes, and get out. When all goes well, the next time the vending machines get restocked? We'll have massive burritos. We'll have paradise. Let's.. let's get to work."

 --

 "Welcome to N-54 News, I'm Gillean Jordan. We begin now with your latest local news. The entire office building of BurritoCorp has been obliterated in what spokespersons call, 'A planned demolition.' According to a statement sent out minutes after the building appeared to come apart at the seams, the change in office location is a moved time to coincide with the launch of a new flagship product -- The Double XXL Burrito. This new, oversized food item is launching in select locations, before a more widespread rollout to the rest of Night City and beyond. For a look at the former site of the BurritoCorp HQ and an updated casualty count, we go live to.."

 Jun flicked the television off. He stood up and stepped over to the apartment's vending machine, as the seconds ticked down to noon. At precisely noon the displays fritzed for the briefest second, before proudly displaying a new purchasing option to coincide with the updated delivery software.

 The Double XXL Burrito.

 Jun savored the taste of victory. It tasted.. vaguely spicy. No. Wait. He'd already pressed the button. That was the taste of his very first Double XXL Burrito. And it was huge. He sat back down on the sofa, in a rare moment of whimsy leaving the burrito hang out the side of his mouth. 

 "[I love it when a plan comes together.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7GL6LH6ufhM)"
