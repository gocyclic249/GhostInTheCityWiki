**HERE, THERE, EVERYWHERE ALL AT ONCE**

 (sometime after the gig)

 Jackie: Hermanita was good, right? We can get Motoko for future gigs!

 V: She ain't bad, but I'll think about it.

 (in a bar after getting fired from Arasaka)

 V: You're the kid that helped us before.

 Motoko: Yep! Wanna get a gig together from Padre here?

 V: No.

 Motoko: Awww!

 (after the Arasaka gig)

 Dexter: Sorry V. (then he gets shot alongside his goons)

 Motoko: Wassup V! Good thing I tailed you and Jackie! I was the one who nuked the side of the building so you can slip away! I also dropped him off Vik's by the way.

 V: Bwuh? Ugh, fine, thanks.

 Motoko: So can we go gigging now?

 V: Bring me to Vik first and I'll think about it.

 (some days later by Wakako's Parlor)

 V: What's the gig -- what the?!

 Motoko: 'sup V!

 Wakako: She's your netrunner and stealth partner.

 V: ...Fine.

 Motoko: Yay! V subconsciously admits that I'm a good cyberninja!

 V: Don't read too much into it!

 (sometime later at Moxie's)

 V: I need a drink. Oh hey Judy. You don't go here often.

 Judy: Hey V. Just waiting for my choom. She wants some girls' night out.

 Motoko: Wassup Judy! Wassup V!

 V: WTF, are you stalking me?!

 Motoko: What? No! I record my merc work and Judy here makes them BDs! Wanna come with us tonight?

 V: Ugh. (drinks her drink in one go then stands up). Bye.

 Motoko: Aww!

 (sometime later at the cultic-like sidemission)

 V: Who killed these Maelstrom fuc-- you!

 Motoko: Me!

 V: I thought you don't do other people's gigs! And what is that?!

 Motoko: I didn't know this was a gig! I have a personal vendetta against these gonks so when I finally tracked them I killed them all! Haha! Oh and this is Tachikoma! Say hi!

 Tachikoma: Hi!

 V: Why a giant spider?

 Motoko: Well when you have a gun fight, sometimes you need more and bigger guns! The same principles apply with these spider gonks!

 V: Huh.

 Motoko: Want to eat?

 V: Get a shower first.

 (sometime later)

 Brendan: Thank you so much for listening and treating me nicely!

 V: Yeah, yeah, you're welcome.

 Brendan: The only other one who treats me well is Motoko-chan!

 V: What.

 (V gets a summary of how they meet and how Motoko promised to put Brendan in a spider-body)

 (V leaves and hails a taxi)

 V: Why is she everywhere?!

 Johnny: Must be a figment of your imagination.

 V: (curses a lot)

 Delamain: Is there something wrong?

 V: Ugh, nothing. Just a pain in the ass.

 Delamain: Talking about it might help. Miss Motoko tells me that verbalizing can help with--

 V: I'm getting off.

 (sometime later)

 V: We're finally here. Now stop nagging me Johnny and shut up for the rest of time.

 Johnny: Not often some band can match the Samurais. And the fact that they're in a band battle against Us-Cracks? Once in a lifetime!

 V: Heh, you sound like an excited kid.

 (the bands enter the stage. Us-Cracks gather at one side while at the other end was Section 9)

 V & Johnny: What?!

 Motoko: WE ARE SECTION NINE AND WE'RE HERE TO MAKE YOU THINK ABOUT DEATH AND GET SAD AND STUFF!

 (sometime later in the badlands)

 V: Surely this is far enough that I won't run into her? (munches) This barbeque is pretty good.

 Panem: Told ya. Huh, where is that girl? She can scarf down barbeque like no tomorrow.

 Motoko: I am here!

 V: Seriously?!

 (sometime later)

 Rogue: We need more firepower if we're storming Arasaka. I called someone reliable.

 V: Why not?

 Motoko: Why not indeed!

 V: Why.

 V: I'm sorry, Jun, but I can't do this anymore!

 Jun: What, why?!

 Fully-converted Motoko: (appears on screen) What?
