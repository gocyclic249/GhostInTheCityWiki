 -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------**What Rests Within A Blade?**----------------------------------------------------------

 The world grew dark as she put the kendo helmet on. The light coming through the mask just enough to show her the opponent standing before her with his shinai held in [Seigan No Kamae](https://www.akban.org/wiki/Seigan_No_Kamae,_Japanese_Sword_stance).

 --------------------------

_" Man, that's such a square stance. It's totally useless on the street. You gotta have a stance of your own ya'know? One you feel comfortable in. Trust me, my dad's a preem Edgerunner who uses a Katana for his job."

 --------------------------_

 She heard the mutters from the observers of their bout as she got into her stance. Shinai raised exactly how Motoko had taught her, feet placed apart in the [Te Ura Gasumi No Kamae](https://www.akban.org/wiki/Te_Ura_Gasumi_No_Kamae,_Japanese_Sword_stance).

 "HAJIME!"

 She moved. Dodging the obvious _Men strike_ with a simple sidestep to the right while channeling the momentum of the move into a vicious cut at her opponent's head.

 "IPPON! Winner, Hiromi San! NEXT ONE!"

 Back into stance once more and watch as the next opponent goes into yet another _Seigan No Kamae_. This one seemed to have learnt from the past bouts and decided to wait her out instead of making an attack.

 --------------------------

_" What do i do if they won't attack me?"

 " That's easy, you do this with your feet and make a feint attack. Most of the time, they'll rely on watching your feet to determine if it's a feint or not since people using feints have a tendency to forget to shift their weight to sell the bluff."_

 --------------------------

 Just like what Motoko had told her. The hasty slash he sent her way was easily nudged out of the way before she countered his strike with a deadly _Tsuki_ to the throat leaving him chocking on the ground and grabbing at this throat.

 "IPPON! Winner, Hiromi San. I deem this 10 man Kumite challenge complete. "

 Sweaty hands placed the shinai on the floor of the dojo before they worked their way around the knots keeping the Kendo Gear on her body. It took trembling fingers five to ten minutes to remove the gear before she could make her way to a hot shower.

 The one good thing about being slowly with removing her gear meant that she had the shower room all to herself, as well as a empty Dojo as she made her way to the exit.

 " Hiromi San? A word if you please. With the completion of the 10 man Kumite, you have cleared the Kendo Elective on the curriculum. Yet I will be a lacking instructor if i did not remind you that your usage of _Oji Wazas_ while masterfully done are only part of Kenjutsu as a whole. I highly recommend adding some _Shikake waza_ to your repertoire, in fact i run a dojo outside where you're more then welc ome tooooo

 --------------------------

_" Motoko, why are you only teaching me defensive stances and how to counter-attack? Since i'm learning how to use a katana, shouldn't I ya'll know actually learn how to cut with it? "

 " What! But if i teach you those whose going to be the new training post i can practice my attacks on? Ichi the rube who can barely hold a katana without it wobbling? No way! You're destined to remain as my training post. Now get that stance back up! "

 " MOTOKO!"

 "Fine, fine. I only taught you Te Ura Gasumi No Kamae and \_Oji Wazas\_ because you have too many things weighing down your sword."

 " YOU TOOK THAT FROM BUSHIDO IN NIGHT CITY! I WATCHED THAT BD WITH YOU!"

 " Hey! Just because it's from a BD doesn't mean it's false! Lemme explain what i mean. Your bladework has to many things resting on it. Your desire to spite your mother, your dissatisfaction with Arasaka Academy, My friendship with you, the ambition to be as cool as that female lead in Bushido In Night City etc. All of these have turned into chains that bind and slow your blade down."

 " That's gonk shit Motoko. You going to tell me that your blade has nothing but the desire to cut?"

 " Nope. Life ain't a BD Hiromi. My blade contains nothing but my Ambition. My Ambition to prove to Jun nii-san that i'm no longer just his Imouto, my Ambition to make the Kusanagi Name one of the Named Clans of the Tyger Claws, my Ambition to live up to Tousan and Kaasan's reputation as Edgerunners and my Ambition to raise to Kabun or higher in rank among the Claws.

 I can only achieve all that with my blade Hiromi. That's what is resting on my blade when i cut. Tousan told me once when i was young, using a Katana in a world full of guns takes a special kind of person. Someone willing to bet their life with each swing of the blade, rolling the dice against death with each cut and the more things you have resting on your blade, the more you hesitate, the more you fear to commit to the cut. 

 That can get you killed Hiromi. You have too many things resting on your blade, you can't commit to a cut and it's effects. To see and feel the slicing of flesh and cleaving of bone with a indecisive mind will cause you to freeze., to hesitate and thus die. So learn the Oji Wazas i teach you and commit them to muscle memory, so that when you finally use them they'll be enough to finish the fight so that if you freeze after seeing the results of your cut, it be after the enemy is dead.

 But have no fear choom, you have me as you bestie after all! Hmmm, I think we should end things for today. Heard that some Scavs were spotted around this area. I'll walk you to the Metro."

 " What about you Motoko?" 

 " Me? Lemme at em! I'll show them what the next Oyabun of the Tyger Claws can do!"

 " Go get em Motoko! I'll see you tomorrow?"

 " Yeah, see you tomorrow choom. Oh before you go though, since you're my bestie i'll share with you the one Ambition i told no one else about. You know how my surname Kusanagi is also the name of a Sword in Japan?"

 " Yes, it's one of the three imperial regalia...?"

 " Yep. That's my dream. To achieve so much with my sword that when people mention Kusanagi, it's me they think about and not that dusty old relic."

 " Sounds like someone wants to be a Night City Legend."

 " Well, there are worse ways to leave a legacy" . 

 " See ya you gonk. I'll make sure to practice what you taught me today as well!"

 " Make sure you do! The next time we meet, i'll check again and see if there's still so many things resting on your blade! I'll teach ya'll how to cut once you get em down to just 1 or 2!"_

 "Sorry Sensei, feeling giddy from the Kumite. I'll get back to you later if i'm interested."

 The Joyride was fun, and as she got off Motoko's preem new ride and took the elevator, the distracting effect of Motoko's diversion started to fade and she was once more confronted with the fact that her bestie got into another dangerous situation without her.

 It hurt. Knowing that she was once again unable to help her choom, too weak and useless to be deemed useful in a fight. Poor wannabe Tyger Claw Hiromi. The rich corpo kid slumming it with the riff-raf. The same dark feelings that flowed within her when she saw Motoko again after their last kenjutsu lesson.

 "Enter."

 "Well. What do you need this time Hiromi? That's usually the only reason you be willing to enter my office."

He was clearly trying to get me to sign up for his Dojo outside of the school, but i decided it be more efficient if i just get the Training Sims and Tactical Strategies that are being used by the Company over some bastardized swordplay from a Kenjutsu Instructor based in Night City."
