 Surfin' U.S.A.

 I ducked under another haymaker, dragging along the unfortunate 6th Street ganger I currently had in a headlock. I was about to punch him in the face again when the rooftop suddenly lit up with red and blue lights from an approaching AV. Everyone froze.

 "Cheese it! It's the Fuzz!" I yelled, and everyone scrambled as I ran forward to the edge of the rooftop. It was only as I was bracing to jump off the roof that I realized I'd been dragging along the guy in the headlock, and that he'd been screaming about being afraid of heights.

 Oops?

 His screaming turned incoherent as we soared across the street. The AV closing in on the rooftop we'd left behind. Hm, I don't think we're going to make this. But maybe...

 "Spread your arms," I yelled over the sound of rushing air and sirens.

 I let him out of the headlock and used my hands to flip up over his shoulder onto his back. Using my legs to nudge him towards a dumpster I'd spotted, we glided our way closer. Come on... Coooome on!

 Wham! We hit just inside the rim of the dumpster, the piles of trash bags cushioning the force, I rolled forward as we hit, tumbling over the edge of the pile and flipping through the air, before landing in a perfect dismount stance. Cool bled through me in approval.

 "Ha! That was great Choom! Did you see us? I mean we totally should have flipped off the AV on the way down but otherwise it was"—I looked up at the ganger's legs flailing around out of the trash he was buried in—"Oh. Right. Let me help you."

 Climbing back up the trash mountain, I grabbed the back of the ganger's shirt and pulled. He spit out a thrown away burrito XXL that'd gotten lodged in his mouth. Gross.

 "III THOUGHT I WAS G-GONNA DIIIIIIIIE!" he sobbed out. I gave him a gentle pat on the shoulders as I helped him down.

 "Hey, relax Choom, we landed safely. And what a party, huh? I really needed that, I mean you would NOT believe what this asshole has been trying to do at my record company lately. I have a manager but he will just. not. take. the. hint. I think I'm gonna have to kill him. Anyway we should get out of here before the NCPD shows up. Hey here's my number, call me when a new location's picked okay? I'll show up again!"

 Giving a cheery wave while I walked away, I stepped out of the alley and joined back into foot traffic and disappeared into the night. The ganger's final words following after me.

 "Please don't"
