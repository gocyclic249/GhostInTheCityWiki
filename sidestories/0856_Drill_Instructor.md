Motoko: "Don't worry guys! I suddenly had a great idea and found us a drill instructor on the cheap!"

 Malcolm: "Why do I have a bad feeling about this?"

 Hiromi: "Motoko, what did I tell you about hiring someone without telling me?"

 Motoko: "Errr, not to do it?"

 Hiromi: "Exactly so can I meet them and politely explain that we won't be able to afford their no doubt highly skilled, _and expensive_ services."

_The boys and Rebecca shoot Hiromi a grateful look._

 Motoko: "Oh right yeah, one second."

_Everyone's vision suddenly begins to glitch out._

 Ichi: "The hell kind of quick hack is it this time Motoko?"

 Stranger in aviators: "Here's~ Johnny!"

 Ichi: "What the fuck!"

 Malcolm: "What the fuck?"

 Hiromi: "Holy Shit!"

 Rebecca: "Holy Shit! Is that Keanu Reeves?"

 Motoko: "I found him in the Ara- in a dumpster behind the Arasaka building."

 Hiromi: "Motoko. What. The. Hell?"

 Rebecca: "Why was Keanu Reeves in a dumpster behind the Arasaka building!?"

 Johnny Silverhand: "Kids these days, don't they teach you anything in those corpo indoctrination camps? Names Johnny, Johnny Silverhand."

 The group: "..."

 Hiromi: "Motoko I will ask again. What. The. Hell? Why am I staring at a terrorist?"

 Rebecca: "...No, that doesn't add up, my father had a poster of him. He was blonde."

 Motoko: "He'll be our new drill instructor Hiromi, you wouldn't believe how cheap his rates are."

 Hiromi: "Motoko he's a terrorist!"

 Rebecca: "Are we sure it's the same guy and not a long lost son or something? Have to be hundreds of those running around by now right?"

 Malcolm: "...No it's digital, like that whole 'save your soul' thing going up on billboards all the time right?"

 Johnny: "Give the kid a cookie."

 Ichi: "You said a dumpster behind Arasaka? Not something you stole from Arasaka itself right?"

 Motoko: "Who? Me? Of course not! You think I'd break into the C-Suite of a corporate high rise to steal the digital ghost of the most famous rockerboy of all time just to get you all interested in training day? Do I seem like the kind of person who would do that?"

 Hiromi: "...Idiot, you're an idiot. I just need two pieces of bread and I could make a sandwich."

 Motoko: "...Didn't you want to hear what his rates where?"

 Hiromi: "At this point I'm trying to figure out the best way to beg counter intel for our lives Motoko!"

 Johnny: "-A new body, and the low, low, price of the life of Saburo Arasaka, payable in the next couple of months."

_Hiromi passes out. Malcom and Ichi suddenly take off running into the desert._

 Motoko: "You weren't supposed to say that last part out loud."

_Johnny doesn't look sorry at all, and is instead smiling._

 Johnny: "Sorry."

 Rebecca: "...So why did you dye your hair?"
