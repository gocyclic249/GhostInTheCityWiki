 Alexei looked at his buds as they moved towards the lone girl walking between the towering blocks of the Corpo District. Working the Corpo District was a risky role, but the cyberware from the sheep there were usually preem and thus the best way to raise up the ranks of the Scavengers.

 Today was looking good with how they spotted this chick strolling in the alleys of the Corpo district without any signs of a bodyguard or security team. Gesturing to Peteriovich to turn on the Jammer, Alexei and the rest of the crew moved towards the sheep ready to knock her out and bring her back to the den to get the Preem Cyberware the Corpo brats usually had.

 They had the whole abduction down to a art, with a nod, the crew rushed towards the girl each member already knowing where to grab to prevent the victim from getting any leverage to use any inbuilt Cyberware they had. Yet as they approached, Alexei noticing with trepidation that the girl seemed to have known of their presence as she turned to face the rushing group of Scavengers with a cheeky smile on her face.

 "Abo-"

 *CLICK

 Despite the usual hectic sounds of the Corpo district, the panting of his breath and Alexei's shout to abort, the mild CLICK stood out. It was out of place and sudden yet it seemed to have subsumed everything.

 "-rt!"

 As the call to abort left his lips, Alexei moved to put his words to action and turned to flee only find to his horror that the sheer act of turning had caused his body to fall apart into cleanly severed parts. As his head begun it's descent to the ground, Alexei saw with his darkening sight the severed heads of his friends all with the same dawning look of horror in their eyes, and as his hearing begun to fade he heard a quietly whispered

 " _Ichi no kata: Hekireki Issen "

 The fuck did that mea-_ was the last thought he had before he fell into the darkness' embrace.

 Motoko looked with satisfaction at yet another successful gig. Her new Sandy was working great and the new Katana she got for Hiromi's birthday worked exactly as advertised!
