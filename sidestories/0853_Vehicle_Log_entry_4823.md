**Vehicle Log entry:** #4823

 As I walked on the sidewalk after the mission well done, I fiddled with the shard I nabbed. I was curious. What actually was on this thing? It gave this nagging feeling at the back of my mind. What kind of scheme was Dino actually going for here? It was unprofessional to look, and perhaps even detrimental. Still.. My curiosity got the better of me.

 "A tiny peek wouldn't hurt, wouldn't it?"

 I slotted the shard into my neck port, quickly checking for viruses. Finding none, I cracked the encryption and started to skim through the contents. Humming to myself as I read through it. My curiosity turning into confusion. Before finally to regret.

 "What?"

 My eye twitched as I realised I did this gig to remove that recording with his cousin and save him from paying for the Caliburn. I wanted to save the recording because it was pretty funny. But I wouldn't go that far, looking at the shard was one thing. Saving it another.

 I sighed. Guess you can't choose your family. Recalling Jun and his stupid face. Maybe loving XXL burritos maybe isn't so bad..

 Then I recalled him eating them every single day, and the smell they spread all over the apartment. The different flavours all leaving that peculiar weird smell behind. The turquesa burritos with their blue color and strong cheese odor. Rosado burritos, that smells like curry and meat but doesn't taste like it.. The list goes on.

 "I am not so sure anymore." I blanched.
