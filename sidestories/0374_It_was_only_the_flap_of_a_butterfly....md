It was only the flap of a butterfly...​

*Silence*  
   
 Silence stretched across Yarinobu's office while he contemplated the words that had been spoken to him. Rising his head from the datapad that he was reading while he deposited it on the desk, he looked directly into the man in front of him.  
   
 And he was not impressed, because in front of him was just another cog in the grand Arasaka machine, a corpo, a man who was trying to look calm, but was failing thoroughly if the slight trembling in his hand was a sign and the small drop of sweat that was running down a very rigid face.  
   
 He wanted to take credit for causing that kind of reaction, but he knew it was not the case. They were not alone in the room after all.  
   
 He broke the silence at last. "Are you banning me from sending more teams on missions?"   
   
 "N-Never!" the man said while trembling hands moved to accommodate his glasses, which almost fell after he heard the question. "After the… retaliation against the Neo Kurozoku gang, Arasaka-sama just wants to review and then approve these kinds of missions."  
   
 Yorinobu tried to look into the eyes of the man sitting across his desk, but he couldn't because the man was purposely avoiding eye contact.  
   
 "I see…" He sighed and told the man to leave. There's nothing more to talk about. If the order came directly from Yorinobu's father, then the man in front of him couldn't do anything to change it, and continuing the conversation would serve no purpose.  
   
 He watched as the man got up from the chair and deliberately kept looking at the floor while quickly retreating, as if staying more than was necessary would make the other entity in the room take offense.  
   
 Once the double doors that functioned as the office entrance were closed, he spoke. "What do you think?"  
   
 After the worlds left Yorinobu's lips, a black monolith started moving from the shadows that shrouded a corner of the room. Its steps were like falling rocks against the wooden floor.  
   
 Into the light a black chrome monster came, only a few parts of what it passed as its face revealed its past as a human being, but long forgotten by now.  
   
 And a strong and robotic voice answered, a sound that no vocal cords could ever replicate. "I told you that your nostalgia for those nobodies would bring you trouble."  
   
 Yorinobu raised his head to look at the face of his bodyguard. "Yes, you did." He didn't regret ordering the eradication of the gang, they were no better than the Wraiths that roamed the badlands outside Night City, but he regretted using Arasaka personnel for the task, he knew people would investigate why the order was given, yet he didn't expect for them to connect it to the Steel Dragons, the gang he founded and used to lead.  
   
 Yorinobu frowned, he knew that with his father taking a more direct approach while not restricted, his actions would be analyzed and that means that he won't be able to move forward with some plans that he has been working on, at least for the time being.  
   
 As was habit, he opened one of the many drawers in his desk and took what seemed to be a generic datapad, but once it was turned on, one would notice that it was not a normal datapad. They designed it to Yorinobu's specifications, with its own ICE and capable of only connecting to a single network, one he fully controlled and the ones who had access to it could be counted with one hand.  
   
 Passing the security checks, he tapped into it so he could access a list of candidates, edge runners, mercenaries, disposable assets that could be used and discarded if needed. An agent that he knew updated the list bi-weekly.  
   
 Looking through the list, he slightly shook his head. There were no new names.   
   
 He was turning off the datapad when an alert popped up. A new name was added to the list. Curious, he tapped on the alert and a new window opened.   
   
 It was a [Person of Interest](https://youtu.be/WYDWSNMTauQ?t=3) [report](https://forums.spacebattles.com/threads/ghost-in-the-city-cyberpunk-gamer-si.1046809/page-978#post-94153846), about a young woman with some affiliation with the gang known as Tiger Claws, but that was not what took his interest, it was her apparent skill in a long list of areas, too many for what it seemed to be a teenager.   
   
 He was still dubious about the report, but there was an easy way to confirm it.  
   
 "Smasher, do you know Fujimura of the Tiger Claws?" he said while he continued to read the report.   
   
 "...yes,"  
   
 "Good, I have a mission for you in Night City." Yorinobu told his bodyguard, already a plan forming in his head while an array of questions floated inside his head.  
   
 'Who are you, Motoko Kusanagi?'  
   
 'And are you capable of helping me get closer to my dream?'
