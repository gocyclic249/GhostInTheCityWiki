 Jackie nervously glanced over his shoulder before wincing and hurriedly going back to his noodles in badly feigned calm. V raised an immaculate eyebrow at her partner's nervous behavior as she tensely stirred her own synth noodles.

 "Okay spill. What's gotten you so rattled Jackie? Are we being stalked?" She finally demanded.

 Jackie muttered to himself as he put his half-eaten noodles on the stall counter. "Look V, it's…kinda stupid."

 V raised her right eyebrow slightly higher. Jackie _really_ didn't like it when she did that. Whenever V's eyebrows reached a certain height, gonks tended to die _real_ quick. Sometimes that was helpful but not in this situation.

 "Okay so, you know how I've been working with that sneaky muchacha on some of my gigs lately?" Jackie finally started.

 V noticeably relaxed with that. If he was starting with that topic, then the situation was probably not as bad as she had feared.

 The former Corpo smirked and nodded her head. "Yeah, the oh so mysterious wannabe cyberninja you're always going on about. Not like it's any of my business or anything, but will you ever introduce her to me? I have to admit that I'm curious about the woman that saved your ass almost as many times as I did."

 Jackie briefly considered correcting her on two counts - he was pretty sure he's still higher on the "who saved who" score between the two of them last time he checked and he was also sure V was under a misconception on how old she thought Motoko was.

 In either case, Jackie decided to not say anything. It would be more fun for V to discover that on her own.

 "Eh, it's not on purpose or anything V. The moment just hasn't come up yet. I'm sure you'll meet each other eventually since you're both so active. But anyway, we're getting sidetracked. The issue isn't hermanita but her hermano who _really_ doesn't like me."

 V's smile faded into a confused frown. "...Jackie, are you and Misty having…relationship issues?"

 Jackie's cyber eyes bulged out in horror. "Oh mi Madre no! No V! It's not like that!" He rubbed his forehead tiredly. "...we're just gig partners but her brother seems to have his doubts. Motoko wants us to get along but I don't think that's going to happen anytime soon. Too awkward. He sees me as a…bad influence on her I guess."

 If anything, it was the opposite situation but who the hell would believe that?

 V burst out a laugh. "Ah, I get it now. He's the overprotective brother type. He sees you and thinks this gangbanger is corrupting his pure, precious sister." She didn't even try to hide her curiosity and looked over her shoulder at the afternoon crowd while cracking her knuckles. "So where's the paranoid asshole? I can talk him straight if you want."

 She ignored Jackie's panicked sputtering and her eyes quickly scanned the moving population for anyone blatantly spying on them…then noticed someone.

 "...Jackie?" V asked him in a forcibly measured voice. "Why is there an armed Kamikaze borg staring at us like we killed his puppy from on top of that streetlight?"

 Jackie let out an exhausted moan and allowed his head to drop on the stall counter.
