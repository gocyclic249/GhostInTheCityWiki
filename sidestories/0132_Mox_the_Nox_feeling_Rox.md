Mox the Nox feeling Rox!​

 After leaving Lizzie's and Motoko behind Nox headed back to his apartment to stash the BD shard, couldn't have it on him in case someone tried to klep it off him, and most of all have some privacy so he could put on his wreath to test out the BDs themselves.

 A good supplier always tested out the product so that they could give their customers the best sales pitch after all... and cause he really wanted to experience what Motoko did.

 She was just so preem and... Nox felt his face heat up as he quickly put on the wreath and slotted in the BD file on her first heist gig. He was thankful that the week time limit that Motoko's fixer had imposed had passed since he was really excited to see a real gig in action and he wasn't sure he could have waited to view it if he had it on him early. Not even the threat of whatever the fixer did could scare him away... at least he thought.

 Anyway, he settled down on his couch as he like the BD play and... well, it wasn't _horrible_ or anything, but it was kinda boring, something he picked up from Motoko's emotions in the BD as she literally just watched into a dude's apartment and klepped his shard while he none the wiser while watching his own BD on his own couch.

 ...Nox idly raised his wreath and looked around his apartment, checking to make sure the front door was still locked, before slowly putting his wreath back on.

_'Motoko did mention how easy it was to sneak up on people while they were watching BDs before she...'_ Nox cut off that line of thought quickly with a shudder.

 He would most likely price this BD pretty low, market it as a 'taste' of what the others held, and showing how even the baddest edgerunners still took regular gigs for regular income. A down to earth kinda feel. Maybe get a few laughs at easy job too.

 Once the first BD was finished he slotted in the next one and instantly became more invested since _this_ one involved 6th Street, one of the top gangs in Night City full of ex-vets who _knew_ how to kill.

 Motoko went into serious stealth mode as she hoped a fence, crouching low as she surveyed her surroundings before infiltrating the gang safe house.

 The feeling of control was amazing as she easily entered the house, quickly dodged the ganger by hiding in a closest, and finally locate her objective. The feeling of tenseness jacked up as she plugged into the computer and worked hard to crack the ICE, all the while on alert since any of the gangers could show up and blow her cover.

 Minutes passed and each one ratcheted up tension since this was the usual part in a movie or studio BD the action started. But nothing like that happened as Motoko finally broke through the ICE, confirmed her objective, and made a stealthy escape without none the wiser.

 Nox had to take off the wreath for a minute to calm his breathing, 'oh yeah, people will like that.'

 He quickly put the wreath back on and slotted the final heist gig. This one was apparently focused on an art theft from a corpo, something that was sure to interest people.

 Motoko started out staking out a mid-high rise building before moving around the back and easily breaking in, seemingly disappointed about just how easy.

 She easily rode the elevator up to her target's apartment, more like a house side wise though, and slipped in, hearing the sounds of a man and a woman. Nox, given his experience, instantly knew that Motoko's target must have picked up a joytoy from the scraps of convo she could make out.

 Corpos were scum, but they could be good business.

 Nox watched as Motoko thoroughly scoped out the apartment for her target, not making a single sound, checking each room and every corner until she was interrupted.

 "C'mon Hellena, let me show you something," the man's voice rang out.

 "Fuck," Motoko cursed as she dived into a bedroom closet and hunkered down.

 She kept an eye open through the crack in the door as the man and joytoy came into the bedroom giggling and... well, Nox knew where this was going. He knew more than a few customers who would be into this kinda BD.

 Though Nox did giggle a bit at Motoko's annoyance as the two got going, but thankfully didn't last long at all.

 Either the joytoy was a pro or the corpo was just really backed up.

 After they went to the bathroom to wash up Motoko made her way out, quickly found her objective, some expensive and tacky statuette before deltaing.

 It was well done, with the tension throughout the whole thing with even some comedy and sex appeal. Nox could certainly sell this BD.

 Now, the grand finale, the BD on the Maelstrom raid.

 Nox looked forward to it as he slotted it in.

 ---

 Nox breathed heavily as the BD ended, taking off the wreath as he felt how sweaty he was after experiencing the whole thing.

 That was... that was...

 Nox felt his face heat up again, unrelated to the BD, as he just thought about Motoko and just how _preem_ she really was. Just so out of his league in every way.

 It was crazy comparing the version of her he knew and the one he just saw, both amazing in different ways, and how she still wanted to help him and considered him a friend.

 It was very humbling and... Nox just knew this was gonna be a huge hit.

 The NCPD better put a bounty on his head, cause he just made the next best high!

 ---

 "-and after you get a feel of this you will be _demanding_ for a sequel," Nox told his friend/regular client.

 "...and you sure this ain't some gory run trash?" his friend questioned again.

 "Nothing so base," Nox assured him. "This is a pure professional, a real edgerunner who is mission first always, the preemist of the preem, someone who-"

 "Alright alright, I'll give it a watch," his friend said with raised hands. "Jeez, never seen you so wired about a BD before."

 "Not just the BD, but the recorder. She's gonna be a legend one day, I just know it," Nox said with certainity.

 "Heh, sure. But yeah, will give it a look and spread word around if I like it," he said as he sent over the eddies. "I will ask for a refund if it doesn't live up to hype though."

 "Oh trust me, it will," Nox said with a smile.
