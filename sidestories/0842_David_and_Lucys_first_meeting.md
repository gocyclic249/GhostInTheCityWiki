David and Lucy's first meeting in this timeline.

* * *

 "So I guess I'd say that, this is what my life has been missing you know? Feels like for once I'm actually going somewhere with it you know? Like I'm actually moving forward." He leaned back on the bench clasping the beer. (That his mother could never find out about.) Motoko had done a lot to win her over despite their rocky first meeting, but there where some details you just, left out. And even the fabled Ghost knew when it was better to lie by omission.

 She'd throttle him if she knew half of the things he'd done today. Jacking a car, speeding down the highway, dodging gunfire! So Preem! It was a strange thing to know that those Trauma team AV's flying by where on the way to an accident that _you_ had helped cause a few miles back. He hoped everyone got out of that okay.

 Then there was of course his partner for the gig.

 "Yeah, I think I understand what you mean." The white haired girl, Lucy spoke up answering his previous words. "...Sometimes you just feel stuck. Like your spinning around in someone else's wheel." He couldn't help but grin. Finally someone who understood! And her of all people! She wasn't _the ghost_ of course, but he was confident with putting her up on the list of people who where totally nova with her!

 It was _nice_ to finally have some real friends. Hiromi and Malcom might give him scop from time to time, but this was one of the first times he really felt like he belonged. He leaned forward still clutching the bottle between his hands when he remembered something. "Oh right! The stick thing you gave me to hack the rayfield, you probably want it back right?" He awkwardly went to unzip the backpack he'd brought with him. Beer bottle still held between his hands.

 "Keep it, it's no big-" Her words stopped as she stared down at his his hands as she gained a faraway look in her eyes.

 "-No, no I couldn't possibly." He cut himself off stopping to look up at her even as his hand still blindly fished around the jacket inside of his bag. Before noticing she was gone. "What the hell? Lucy?"

 There was no response, almost as if she'd never even been there. Where the hell had she gone while he was looking away? Sighing at his luck he went to stuff his Arasaka academy jacket back into the bag. Weird he thought that conversation had been going pretty well?

 Suddenly he was rocked from his thoughts as the bench shifted and he was joined by Motoko of all people. A bottle of something else in her own hands. "Enjoying the party, David?"

 Was this a normal thing at parties like this? Did people just rotate around to talk to each other like it was musical chairs? Maybe he should just ask? But like in a preem way.

 "...Well I think I just got ghosted?" Oh crap, _the pun._ She's going to think you're so lame now David. Damn it, there goes all of the points you've built up. However instead of mocking him, she simply turned towards him on the bench before staring at him for a moment in silence.

 Shit it was so bad she's speechless! Quick think of something-

_-The Ghost just patted him on the back._

 "That's rough buddy."

* * *

Short, but it's late here so you get what you get. Here we have Motoko trying to make sure David meets his match. Unfortunately for him, he still goes to Arasaka academy, and anything Arasaka adjacent is a big red flag for Lucy. Well at least until she actually gets to know him. Shame that's going to be a lot harder in this timeline.
