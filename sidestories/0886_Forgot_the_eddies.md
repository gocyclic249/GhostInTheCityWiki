It really wasn't supposed to be anything outside of heathens, drawing in the crowd, singing to the person who caught my eye the most. I would be lying to myself if I didn't see a pattern with who was winning at that. But now I have a small following of 'shotgun girls'! Very attractive little heathens who tried to make every show. One of them got a hold of Hiromi somehow who passed on their message and.. well, who am I to turn down my most busty err, loyal fans!

 _______________________________________

 Tonight was the night! New stage, new hook, and best of all, new song! If it was just the song it would have gone later in the set. But it wasn't just the song!

 The guitar riff starts fast and Klein with Jared opens the vocals "HA HA HAAAAAAAAAAA"

 "Pump it!" I yell into the mic and all around the club my shotgun girls wrack their slides, ooooh yis half the crowd jumps while the other half roar with approval! The boy's keep up with the Ha's and I keep singing "Pump it!"

 Next to me Alice joins in with a "Louder!" I keep going and the girls time their guns with my "Pump it!"

 "Dudes wanna hate on us? Dudes be envious! And I know why they hating on us!" I Run my hands down my body then point at all the girls who moved to the front of the stage. "Cause that's so fabulous!" I make eye contact with all the girls holding their guns giving me some veeeeerrrrrrry, cough, happy looks! Letting my body move across the stage I work the crowd and they are all chanting "pump it" with me. The rest of the band nails the back up vocals, Alice joins me walking the front and I let her close us out.

 "Blast your stereo! Right now, uhh! The joint is fizzlin' it's sizzlin' riiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiiight!"

 ______________________________

 We head up to the VIP area and the security animals just look done but let us up. When we open the door I fully understand the poor bouncers, there is tens of pretty young women with carnage's squealing and bouncing and bouncing and boooooun, shaking my head. I walk over and pull a chair into the corner. "You all did super great! I'm so happy you reached out!" I pause for more happy squeals. "Now let's party!"

 ______________________________

 It was pretty late when I got home but Jun was still up, he had been at the show but didn't stay for after party, having to do something other than 'watch girls throw themselves at his kid sister' and yeah, fair. Mistakes was made. "Hey Toko, you ok? Looking a bit out of it." For once he wasn't eating a burrito so I sit next to him.

 "Ugh, I'm fine... just... after playing a few songs the girls asked if I could sign their guns, then others asked if I could sign shirts.... and others to autograph their uh not shirts...." I place my head into my hands. "It turned into a whole thing. But think everyone liked the new song so that's nice."

 Jun pulls me into a half hug and laughs. "Don't worry I won't tell Hiromi." His tone is very teasing.

 "Uuuuuugh she's going to be so mad I didn't ask for eddies for signings isn't she?" Jun snorts, yeah he knows how much she likes eddies.

 "Yeah, that's the problem. Get some sleep, think you're going to need it." I return his hug and get up. I don't think I'll need to auto sleep tonight. Maybe I shouldn't.... I could have some of the breast dreams.... Nope 8 hours go!
