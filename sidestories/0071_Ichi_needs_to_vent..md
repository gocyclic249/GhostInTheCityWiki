Jun slouched over the surface of the bar, sat half-upright in the barstool. Not out of any particular drunkness, though that was certainly a goal of the night. No, this was a time for self-recrimination, and given that trying to blot it out through victory had almost gotten Motoko...

 Well, he had a choice between the bar or the battlefield. Both made his heart go tight in his chest, and both times it because he hadn't been good enough, but only one had been because he'd been an idiot.

 "How did she even get out alive, Ichi?" Jun whispered, still trying to decide if he'd taste the alcohol rather than the bile.

 Ichi shrugged. "Well, someone posted a video of her tearing a knife from her body and ripping a guy's throat out with it," he said dully, and threw back his glass- Jun could feel the burn in his throat just from smelling it. "Gorilla arms. Motoko needs you, but she doesn't need a bodyguard. Just... you, Jun. That's all she needs."

 They'd had to drag him back to the bar when Motoko had needed to stay at the hospital overnight- said he'd just be stewing in his own head if they left him there. (Which he was doing here anyway.) Hiromi had volunteered to be there in case she woke up early.

 "I thought she'd just been playing with that knife for fun," said Jun. "Didn't think she'd have to use it. Not... not so soon." He picked up his drink. "If I hadn't been such a... a piece of shit," he said, forcing the words out, "then maybe..."

 "She'd have done that regardless," said Ichi. "You remember the time she killed three guys that time we screwed up?"

 He grimaced. He'd... he'd actually forgotten about that. It had been in that time between saving Motoko and failing to save Kasumi, and it'd just been... too raw to think about it.

 "Or that time she killed an entire den of scavs?" added Ichi, bringing up a hand to count on his fingers.

 "...Wait, what?" said Jun, his self-flagellation temporarily set aside in favour of trying to figure out what the hell that was supposed to mean..

 "And then stole their heavy machine gun, called us in to klep all their shit, and gave most of it to sad children for free?" he continued.

 "What?" Okay, the last bit was nice to hear, but every other word he'd just heard-

 "Or that time she drove into the desert, fired her new heavy machine gun out of an open car door, got left behind after her client got kidnapped, then killed every single one of the Wraith who'd taken the guy, including one guy who got stabbed through the brain with a screwdriver, and stole their car for good measure?"

 "Ichi, for fuck's sake-"

 "Or just before you visited, when she leapt off a building to dropkick a guy, stole another car to shoot a Maelstrom borg point-blank in the face with a shotgun- which she also stole?" heedlessly rambled Ichi. "So, y'know, I should be surprised that she killed a guy by ripping his throat out with a knife held in her teeth after having both her hands cut off-"

 "Ichi what the fuck are you talking about?!" Jun finally interrupted.

 Ichi stopped. He stared.

 Then, wordlessly, he drained the entire glass of whatever the fuck kind of paint-stripper he was drinking.
