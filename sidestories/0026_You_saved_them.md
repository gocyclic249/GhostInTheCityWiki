Motoko dashed back and forth at the scene of the fighting between Tiger Claws Vs Scavs Vs Maelstrom.

 She kicked over a vending machine and started ripping out the electronics on the spot, rotating and dashing back over the road and swiping a Food Cart and dragging it over to the poor injured soul, she was desperately trying to save.

 She ignored the continuing battle, the gunshots flying past overhead close by and when a Scav leaped over a car hood hurtling towards her, she thrust a broken drain pipe through their throat with barely a glance.

 She yanked the spasming and twitching Scav closer and cut him open, ripping out Chrome both visible externally as well as implants hidden within.

 Treating the scumbag like a Cyberware Pinata, she swiftly began using the Tech to plug the gaps in her Patients flesh, using tubing from the food cart and syrupy sauces like a set of IV's to keep them alive as she performed amateur surgery.

 Many hours later, Motoko still sat in the street holding her Patient on her lap, a tight hold upon their form as they continued breathing.

 Hiromi walked over to Motoko whose eyes were drifting shut, almost falling asleep on the street.

 "It's gonna be fine you gonk! You saved them!" Said Hiromi with a big grin.

 Motoko smiled softly and nodded before leaning her head against Hiromi's leg while still on her knees.

 They both looked to the slowly stirring form in receipt of a Toko lap pillow.

 "Such a Kawaii Kitty!" Cried out both Girls happily as they cuddled a now Cyborg Neko.
