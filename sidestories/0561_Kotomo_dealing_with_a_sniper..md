 I'd been watching him all night. A building across from the rapid setup event Hiromi had talked me into covering for Kotomo. 

 Now I was standing on the top of a bus singing my heart out, microphone stand in my hands, and I knew it was coming. My little buddies had moved in to watch the sniper. So out of the corner of one eye I was literally watching the back of his head as I sang.

 It was coming. He had been narrowing in the targeting data this whole time. Trying to punch through my ECM, and he was getting close.

 I sighed. This was so annoying. I couldn't break character! Hiromi would kill me! And someone would kill Kotomo!

 So there was only one option.

 Be a badass bitch!

 I timed it. Lowering the quality of my ECM so his Ashura Smart Sniper would finally get it's lock right at the correct moment.

 He fired.

 I turned in my dance move, and activated my sandy.

 For a moment. The world stood still. Nothing moved. A droplet of my sweat shone in the light, and then I let it out, A single spin, the bottom of the microphone stand I had been flinging around slammed into the homing round and squashed it as I slammed it into the bus I was standing on.

 Sorry Hiromi, knew she was going to yell at me about the insurance on the rented bus again.

 But at the same time I my sandy cut out and I shifted, leaning forward practically tipping the microphone out over the crowd, the crowd now reaching up to touch me.

 And my left hand was in the shape of a gun pointed straight down the sites of the man that just shot me.

 "*Bang.*" I finished the song. At the exact moment Kitten fired into the back of the snipers head.

 I threw a wink down at the crowd directly under me, people that had no idea what they had just missed out on. 

 Just another day in the life of Motoko Kusanagi!
