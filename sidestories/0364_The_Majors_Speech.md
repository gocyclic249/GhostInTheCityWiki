**"The Major's Speech":**

 Gentlemen, chooms, agents and colleagues of all types.

 Some of you have come to believe that I like it when numbers go up.

 I wish to DASH these rumors. 

 I LOVE it when Numbers go BRRRRRT.

 Through my life, I have discovered a plethora of different types of Numbers:

 You sit in your nine-to-five job and are bored to death, so you turn on your phone to play a game with funny animals that get stronger the longer you kill stuff. Efford Values.

 Then your Boss fires you because you are not doing your job and so YOU fire HIM from your guild which costs you the next big fight. Karma Meter!

 Now you try to unwind and fire up your Dating Sim to forget the crushing loneliness of reality, but you offload all your feelings onto a cold calculating machine and she dumps you. Relationship Values…

 Now you just want to feel something nice again and turn on your grinding RPG, but the RNG gods do not smile upon you and only common level loot is dropping. Stat Blocks.

 This finally costs you the game because you played on Hardcore and flew too close to the Boss. Health Points… _chuckle_.

 What I am telling you, my army of friends and acquaintances, is that I am a connoisseur of Numbers. And with our work over the years, you all saw me get better at pretty much anything I decided to focus on. 

 You see, I don't distinguish between Numbers if they actually go up. I want Efford Values and Karma. I crave Relationship Points and bigger Stat Blocks on my loot. I salivate at the thought of my Health Points being so high that they break the Health Bar. But what I understand most of all is that each and every number is tied up and governed by the humble and basic Experience Point.

 AND I WANT EVERY LAST ONE OF THEM TO GO...

 BRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRT!!!!!!!
