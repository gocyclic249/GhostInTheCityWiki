**Hiromi Ittō-ryū (Hiromi One-Sword Style)**

"So, you want to learn how to kill? No, don't deny it. By choosing this Training Sim, you've shown the spirit of your sword one of a **Satsujin-ken**. Not for you the elegance of a sword dance, the zen mediation of the sword among nature. No, for you the Sword is a weapon to Kill, Maim and murder. It is a return to what the sword is meant for.

 Welcome to **Jigen-ryū** (示現流) and it's teachings. By the end of the training sim you will be instilled with the essence of this school of Kenjutsu. Know that the First Strike is all, a second strike is not even to be considered for when you swing your blade, you swing it with the conviction to live or die by it's results. 

 We will now begin, follow the diagram and take the stance shown.... "

 No. She won't give up. She can't give up._

 " Once more "

 The steel bokken found itself raised once more into a _tonbo-no-kamae_. Hiromi breathed out and swung.

_1.

 3._

" The **Ittō-ryū** (一刀流) is about utilizing the _Kiri-otoshi_ to strike. First we shall begin with mastering the _Kiri-otoshi_, please take the stanc- Apologies, records indicate that you have completed the **Jigen-ryū** (示現流) Training Sim, re-optimizing **Ittō-ryū** (一刀流) Training Sim.

 Re-Optimization Complete.

 Records indicate that you have learnt how to cut. As such, this Training Sim will focus on imparting you with the 1500 techniques of the **Ittō-ryū** (一刀流) so that regardless of the foe you face, you will be able to Cut. The philosophy of the **Ittō-ryū** (一刀流)is**One sword gives rise to ten thousand swords(一刀即万刀)**, as such this Training Sim will work to provide you with the variations needed to form your own sword. 

 We will start with drilling the techniques into your body. Please standby for TARGET.EXE to be generated...."

* * *

 *BZZZT

 The sudden shock from the Arasaka Training Suit blazed through her veins. It took all of her focus to ensure that she only let out a muffled shout as she struggled to prevent herself from falling.

 "The previous _Kata_ was off by 2 degrees, please re-do the technique"

 Forcing her aching muscles to move through the Kata once more, Hiromi ignored her trembling hands, her spasming muscles and the pounding of her lungs.

 " _Kata_ 382 of 1500 complete. Moving on to Practical Usage. Now generating TARGET.EXE"

 As the Training Target spawned and charged towards her, it was all Hiromi could do to take her stance. Yet, the hours of sweat and pain paid off as muscles moved almost by instinct to deflect the Target's lunge before swinging down in the exact manner of _Kata_ 382.

 " PASS. Moving to _Kata_ 383. Please take your stance."

* * *

" You have stepped on the path of the **Satsujin-Ken**. With **Jigen-ryū** (示現流)you have learned how to kill, it is your first step towards the **Satsujin-Ken**. With **Ittō-ryū** (一刀流) you have explored the myriad ways you can kill with your sword and have turned that step into a walk towards the **Satsujin-Ken**. _**Yagyū Shinkage-ryū**_ (柳生新陰流)is the means in which you go beyond being a **Satsujin-Ken** and begin your journey on the road to **T _atsujin-ken_—the sword of an accomplished man, an Expert.**

 This training sim will impart to you postures and offensive techniques, designed to win at any price as well as how to account and adapt to the opponent's weaponry, the geography of the terrain, the hour of battle, and other parameters. By the end of this training sim, you will master the "power of adaptability", much as a hunting hawk, which must constantly reconsider the best trajectory in which to strike effectively. 

 We will begin with Terrain Adaption. Please hold as TERRAIN ONE.EXE is generated. "

* * *

 "Scenario 71 Generated."

 As the generated scenario of the Training Sim form itself around her, Hiromi's Mind raced as her training came into play with a brief snapshot of key details in the span of the second before the generated foes attack.

_Narrow Alleyway. Dumpster 3 steps away. Ladder 1 meter above. One light source, street light 10 steps away from me. 5 enemies spotted. 2 near the dumpster, 3 near the street light. Weapons in question, 2 baseball bats and 3 D5 Copperheads._

**> time lapsed since scenario start: 0.00s**

 She moved.

 Closing the distance towards the duo with baseball bats with her 1st step.

 Kicking off the ground with a jump on her 2nd step as her hands flow naturally to the Katana sheathed on her back

_"Hachi no kata: Takitsubo"_

 Landing on the ground with her 3rd step in a crouch as blood drips down her katana and the two halves of one of the baseball bat duo falls with a splat.

**> time lapsed since scenario start: 0.10s**

 Springing up from her crouch and slashing her katana upwards

 "_Ku no kata: Suiryū Shibuki - Matataki"_

 Ignoring the blood from the bisected corpse of the second baseball bat wielder and letting the rest of the momentum carry her on top of the dumpster.

 Kicking off the dumpster, placing her right foot on the wall and using it to launch herself towards the ladder. Almost nonchantly avoiding the spray of rifle fire that hit the dumpster and wall she was mere seconds ago.

**> time lapsed since scenario start: 0.30s**

 Darting up the ladder and onto the ledge it leads to as the trio below continue to spray the dumpster with bullets.

 *CLICK

 *CLICK

 *CLICK

 As the 3 copperheads ran dry, Hiromi made her move.

 A leap downwards, a swing of her blade. Followed by a muttered.

_"Ichi no kata: Minamo Giri"_

 A decapitated head leaps into the sky as the body drops holding it's rifle.

**> time lapsed since scenario start: 0.55s**

 Letting the remainder of the momentum from her jump assist her in kicking off from the ground once more in a dash with katana held up at the two remainder gunmen who were frantically reloading their rifles.

 "_San no kata: Ryūryū Mai"_

 The sword in her hand flows like water as her feet dance a graceful unpredictable path. Chopping legs and cleaving arms before ending with one final spin that sees the other two heads join their comrade in the sky as gravity takes hold.

**> time lapsed since scenario start: 1.00 mins**

 *Thud

 *Thud, Thud

 So yeah. Hiromi basically spent oodles of time and lots of her mom's resources to access Training Sims of Sword Schools with over half a millennia of History just so she can win a bet with PAST Motoko.

 Also Arasaka No Yaiba (The Blades of Arasaka) because in Corpo hands, Tanjiro is a proud enforcer of the Arasaka Ways. This is also my attempt at stealthly making Hiromi a Kensei. Cus despite doing this to win a bet, she did legitimately do the training to create her own Sword Style. If you take away the weebness of her moves, in order they're actually:

 - A straight diagonal slash

 - A reverse diagonal slash cutting upwards

 - A horizontal slice at neck level

 - A straight cut at legs before moving the blade directly upwards to cut off the arms

 - A spinning cut at neck level for both heads

 - Throwing her sword like a Javelin which is apparently a legit move in _**Yagyū Shinkage-ryū**_
