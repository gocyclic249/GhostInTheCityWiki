 It started among the street gangs first. Word of a Short Circuit quickhack that could leave a whole crew spasming on the ground vomiting their XXL Burritos out in the span of seconds.

 Like all rumors of such Super Quickhacks, they were easily dismissed as mere heresay and some ganggoon's attempt to make his failed robbery sound more impressive. But unlike the past, the rumor refused to die, with tales of it's victims escalating in number and soon it made the leap from rumor to fact when the News reported a Biotechnica Break-In that left all the security within it jittering on the floor till sedatives were brought to bear.

 For something with such an effect, it was soon agreed that this Quickhack was no Short Circuit, no Short Circuit indeed! Yet the lack of identifying factor beyond the side-effect meant that it was hard to notice the Quickhack being used or the user for that matter...

 However, soon a name started to spread. A name whispered in shadows and hitting the ears and lips of Gang goons throughout the city... A name learnt from hard experience via interacting with the Netrunner using the Quickhack. Interactions in the form of being struck repeatedly by said Netrunner until the magic words were spoken allowing the netrunner to depart with feline satisfaction.

 And now you, Anton Eza find yourself confronted by the Netrunner in question. Your crew lie spasming on the ground as electricity burns their muscles and nerves to a crisp. Your only hope to survive is to choose the right dialogue to make the netrunner leave. Which would you pick:
