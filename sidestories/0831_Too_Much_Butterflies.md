**Too Much Butterflies**

 I didn't think much on the cyberpsycho attack on Arasaka Tower until Hiromi burst into my apartment, crying. The cyberpsycho had destroyed several floors and departments; Hiromi's parents were part of the casualties.

 Hiromi wanted vengeance. Section 9 of course obliged. 

 But after hours of searching…

 "No…" I whispered, seeing who the culprit was. 

 Bathed in blood with crazed and bloodshot eyes, was V. She didn't recognize me nor Jackie, who came with me. And she was good, too good, that she nearly tore off her choom's head.

 Was this my fault? Was it because of the listening post? Did I made too many butterflies that I made V stay longer in Arasaka? Were those extra months that crucial to her sanity?

 Cool swallowed the anxiety. Cool helped me focus. Cool steadied my finger on the trigger as I saw V glare through the crosshairs.

 I pressed it.

Something quick I had to put out after reading how close to the edge V was in previous chapters. This won't probably happen but it's fun to speculate that Motoko butterflied so many stuff, for good or ill.
