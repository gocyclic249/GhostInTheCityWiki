If we go with the assumption that Arasaka!Motoko and Kusanagi!Motoko co-exist and incarnated at the same time again, A!Motoko just made life a whole lot worse for K!Motoko.

**Hiromi:** Look, Motoko, I don't get why you're so against being associated with the Tyger Claws. It's clear that you're a fan of Arasaka-sama—

**K!Motoko:** I am not! You take that back!

**Hiromi:** It's obvious! You're copying her everything! Her leotard, you suddenly picking up the sword out of nowhere...

**K!Motoko:** I'm not listening! La-la-la-la—hey Hiromi, look! A distraction!

**Hiromi:** That's exactly what I'm talking about! Look! *turns on a nearby TV*

 *ksshht*

**Tokyo News Reporter:**_—now here, live. Where we have just caught up to the notoriously elusive Motoko Arasaka, who has been making waves within the company recently. Madam Arasaka, could we have a few words?_

**A!Motoko:**_Not listening! La-la-la-la! Hey Reporter-san. What's that?!

 *camera turns to follow a pointed finger, then snaps back to reveal empty pavement*_

**Reporter:**_Why did you—?! We've lost her now!_

**K!Motoko:** That means nothing. It's purely coincidental.

**A!Motoko:** (offscreen) _Nin-nin, Reporter-san._

**K!Motoko:** BITCH SAY WHAT?! THAT'S MY LINE!

**Hiromi:** It's okay, Motoko! Everyone has a role model!

**K!Motoko:**(angrish gamer noises)
