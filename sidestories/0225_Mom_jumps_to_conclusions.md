Hiromi wasn't a complete gonk, she knew she had a crush on Motoko. She was, however, too scared to confess. After all, Motoko was her best friend and she didn't want to ruin that for herself. She was happy with how things were for now.

 Everything changed on that day though. "That voice! That stupid fucking sexy voice," she screeched into her pillow. It wasn't fair, everything was going great, they were best friends and she was content with that. That voice tho... She needed M O R E.

 The day after they'd hit the scav ripper's den, she went to talk to her mother. "Mom, I need to go see a ripper," she said to her over breakfast. Her mother was a real stone cold Arasaka corpo bitch, she needed to sell this to her somehow, and she needed to think fast.

 "Why do you need new cyberware? Is this about your "street" friends? You should know this from your classes. Explain how this expense would enhance your... endeavors, going forward. Capital expenditure shouldn't be done on a childish whim," her mother said.

 "Fuck," Hiromi thought. How the fuck was she going to give a corporate spin to wanting to record braindances of her crush...

 "I have a... uhh, associate who I believe has valuable skills. I would like the ability to record her as a means of... uhh... assessing her abilities?" Hiromi said. There was no way this was going to work, fucking fuck she was such a gonk.

 "Is this about that friend of yours who came around the other day? Hmm, she seemed like a capable girl. Very well, let's go," her mother said.
