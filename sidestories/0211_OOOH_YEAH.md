_Eww._ That was the thought running through Motoko's head after reading the brief Regina sent her. Still, the client's the client and as an Edgerunner it was a matter of professional pride that she fulfilled the Gig as stated.

_This is going to be challenging though...Luckily Scorpion owns me a favor!_

* * *

 Reaching the bar and casually breaking through the pathetic ICE present, Motoko hijacked the available cameras and verified the locations of each and every person in the bar till she finally spotted her target.

 It must have been a slow day for the Ripper, since he was at the counter attempting to hit on the bartender. Marking his position on the Minimap she had generated of the bar, Motoko pinned it to the corner of her eye before getting into the Vehicle she had borrowed from Scorpion.

 The Vehicle had started life as a off-road vehicle of some kind, but over the course of multiple owners each with their own refits the vehicle had started to take the shape of a brutal weapon meant to rip and tear it's way through all that stood in it's way.

![Image 10](https://4.bp.blogspot.com/_5OGIrfjjcc0/TFretQfGGyI/AAAAAAAABUw/pCz8cqtYheM/s320/Picture+1.jpg)​

 In short, it was beautiful and exactly what she needed to do the job at hand!

 Getting into the vehicle and checking her marker once more, Motoko made her move

* * *

 The Lion's Den like all the other bars in Night City had a disposition to play loud music to the extend that those within could barely hear themselves think let along speak. Yet, even the beats from Lizzy WIzzy's latest hit were unable to cover up the roar of a truck moving at full speed.

 Just as the patrons of the bar were about to start wondering if the Lion Den's had somehow gotten an unreleased version of the song, it happened.

![Image 11](https://media0.giphy.com/media/aTx5OMKR7FCGQ/giphy.gif)​

 The patrons watched with bemused horror and shock as with a loud " OH YEAH!!!!!", a truck burst in and out of the bar with such speed that the only casualty that was in it's way turned into a fine red mist,

 Shockingly, despite the speeds involved and the size of the truck, there was only one casualty. As such the rest of the patrons of the bar just shrugged their shoulders and got back to the more important mission of drinking enough to so that the new Liver the dearly departed Ripper had just installed for them was put through it's paces.

* * *

 "What the fuck Motoko? I thought you were a professional! Why the hell did you kill the Ripper? You just needed to get the data from her to hunt down the Target of the Gig! Not splatter them into a Mist!"

 "Huh? But the brief said to go...through her.... Ohhhhhhh! Oopsy! This totally isn't my fault! Er er ENGLISH ISN'T MY FIRST LANGUAGE! It's all my Langsoft's fault! I'll totally go get a brand new one after our meeting Regina!"

 Motoko couldn't help but wince as Regina's face started to take on a more Wakakoish scowl. _Huh, i wonder if this is some secret Fixer knowhow..Regina looks just like Wakako after i told her about going to rescue Scorpion._

 " Er well, i still got the data! So i'm A-Ok to complete the Job! I'm just going to see myself out now. Noneedtosendmeoff!"

 As Motoko scampered off, Regina yanked open the drawer of her desk and took out the wrapped box Wakako had sent her after she reached out to her to verify Motoko's credentials after her little fracas with Ian.

 Unwrapping it and yanking open the bottle of Whisky, Regina raised the bottle to the Fixer Gods above and made a toast.

 "I guess experience comes through once more. Just like what you said Wakako, it's a matter of When i need this bottle of Whisky and not IF. Cheers."
