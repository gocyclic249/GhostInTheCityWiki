Sasha looks at the dancing Maelstrom gang goons [dancing](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AQXVHITd1N4) to some old song.  
 Motoko was trying her hardest to not laugh at them.  
 Mac grins at the display.  
   
 "So you see ladies while all the standard fare can do most jobs and as Motoko has shown can be optimized, the real fun is in custom programs."  
   
 Sasha looks at Motoko.  
 "We have so gotta start doing this!"  
   
 Motoko raises an eyebrow at cat themed netrunner.  
 "What's this we stuff I've been doing this for a while now."  
   
 The music stops and Dumdum starts grabbing his weapon.  
 "God damn it old man how many time doe we gotta tell you to stop pulling that bullshit around here?!"  
   
 Mac smiles at the borg and claps his hands.  
 "One more as always Dumdum."  
   
 The borged out gang goon gives an appropriate oh shit expression as another song plays and they start dancing.  
   
 Motoko blinks and looks at Mac.  
 "Is that the spice girls?"  
   
 Mac gives her a shocked look.  
 "Yes, I'm surprised someone your age even knows about that band since they were a pretty niche group when I was your age.  
 Now if you want to have some real fun what you need is customized decks, not as easy now as in the old days since it's all chipware but with the right tools you can make miracles."  
   
 Sasha frowns at the man.  
 "Fabricating customs chips is a pain, gotta get a chip fab and then the design process, all the hardware testing and it's not cheap."  
   
 Motoko smiles at her.  
 "But if you pull it off it's gotta be pretty nova, hey Mac do you customize your deck?"  
   
 Mac laughs.  
 "Damn right I do, now the off the shelf Netdriver is good but my Fenrir is better, plus if you get a optimized external deck to work in tandem with it you'll never have to worry about RAM."  
   
 Sasha's eyes go wide.  
 "You can actually plug an old external deck into a modern cyberdeck and use it to boost your RAM?!"  
   
 Motoko tilts her head in confusion.  
 "Seriously Sasha, I don't know what has me more frustrated that I didn't think of this solution to the RAM problem or that Sasha is now going to use it to spam her RAM hog programs."  
   
 "Oh don't worry kid when I wasn't much older than you Spider Murphy used to school me all the damn time.  
 Hell the old girl can probably still kick my ass, though I'd like to think I can at least make her have to put in some real effort now."  
   
 Motoko scratches her head at the image of a young Mac getting his butt kicked by the net running legend.  
 "Whatever did happen to Spider Murphy anyway?"  
   
 Mac frowns and shakes his head.  
 "That's not my story to tell, Recluse is the only one who knows all the details and asking her is the quickest way to piss her off.  
 some mysteries prefer to stay buried and don't take well to people trying to dig them up.  
 But enough with this downer Debbie talk, let me show you my chip fab and how to design a custom deck."  
   
 Sasha raises her eyebrow at the man.  
 "And how much are these lessons going to cost us?"  
   
 Mac starts laughing his ass off.  
 "Oh that's easy, you two get to play chaperone to my granddaughter to the upcoming Lizzy Wizzy concert.  
 Traci is easily her biggest fan in Night city and she's got VIP & backstage passes, so just try to keep her out of trouble and protect Lizzy from her enthusiasm."  
   
 Motoko facepalms  
 "Your granddaughter is an obsessive celebrity stalker?"  
   
 "HA no she's not that bad and Lizzy is always happy to chat with her.  
 Lizzy's agent does try to keep her on schedule but well Traci tends to be rather distracting when she acts like a chipmunk high on [Surge](https://cyberpunk.fandom.com/wiki/Surge) ."  
   
 Dumdum falls onto the ground.  
 "Alright already we give up, just please stop already!"  
   
 "Oh right almost forgot about you guys, we can't stop yet we haven't gotten to the [funk of forty thousand years.](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rLMr9CsJHME)"  
   
 "OH GOD NO, NO MORE NO MORE!!"  
   
 Motoko winces as the music begins and the Maelstrom members dance again.  
 "Damn I'm almost starting to feel bad for these idiots, aren't you worried they'll come after you for this?"  
   
 Mac shakes his head.  
 "Nope not really, this was done with bricks approval, seems Dumdum and friends fucked up and needed to be taught a lesson."  
   
 Shasha grins and pipes up.  
 "Think we can make them do an US Cracks routine?"  
   
 Motoko winces.  
 "That is just inhumane, no one deserves US Cracks."
