"Laughing man.... Ghost." the sprite figure approached me and Yoko. A nod passes between them while I am looking around.

 The rest of the Dewdrop Inn had gone back to normal, albeit with more dancing 'toons around the place, an awful cacophony of different styles of music blarring around them as they danced like nobody was watching.

 Near complete annominity had it's perks.

 "Ghost," Yoko gestured, bringing my attention to the person before me once more. "This is Collector. If you are looking for something more.... _Mundane_, then he is your go-to gonk."

 Collector nodded, "I have an offer I would like to make for your program... Sending you the deets."

 Back in the fleshverse my eyes glowed briefly as I took in the data packet. It was... it was.... _perfection._

 Hiromi looked down on my collection with a slight look of disgust, "And.... What exactly are you going to do with all that?"

 "Do?" I responded, sitting on the last of the fifty-three totes that lined the walls of my basement haven. "I am going to educate myself! I'm going to read them all!"

 "Fifty-three totes of 80's and 90's manga, you couldn't just look it up on the net?" she said, grabbing one labeled 'Kishimoto'.

 "Nope!" I popped the P as I opened my first read. "It was all the old net and nobody kept this stuff before the krash.... well, almost nobody..." Masamune Shirow hadn't written GitS, but hell if I hoped this wasn't the next best thing.
