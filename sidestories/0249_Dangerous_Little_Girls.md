Dangerous Little Girls​

 Guadalupe Alejandra Welles, or more commonly known as Mama Welles, was a woman who loved her son despite the many questionable decisions with his life he made.

 Her Jackie was a dreamer, and she knew along with most old timers that Night City liked crushing dreamers under its boot. He was a good boy who wanted to make a name for himself with his own two hands, even if said hands got bloody along the way. At least he tried not to fight when he didn't have too... though her Jackie tended to leap a lot before he looked or even thought.

 Guadalupe would feel much better and happier if he settled down with a nice girl, raised a family, and took over the bar for her. It wouldn't be an exciting life, but it would be a long and happy one.

 But Jackie was a man now, she accepted that. His life was his own to live, even when she made it clear when she disagreed with aspects of it, and all she could do was love and be there for him.

 Which especially meant if he was gonna continue to live under her roof he was gonna be home for dinner _ever_ night if possible, work permitting, and tell her all about his day.

 "She is _how_ old?" she asked in astonishment.

 "I think 14 or 15? Not even legal driving age!" Jackie laughed.

 "And you were when you started?" Guadalupe gave him a raised brow and a smirk.

 "You know what I mean! Girls her age should be getting up to minor trouble at best, not merc work," Jackie grumbled. "But, apparently she has the goods and skills."

 "She got one over on you, didn't she?" Guadalupe pointed out with a smile.

 "I thought she was just posing!" Jackie defended himself. "I mean, who expects a girl barely into her teens to be as hardcore as she claims?"

 "Don't go underestimating women, Jackie. We can be quite dangerous when we want to be," Guadalupe warned her son.

 "Of course, Mama. Those _pendejos_ who tried to stick you up last month learned that the hard way before your regular clientele stepped in."

 "Damn straight," Guadalupe said with a smirk becoming serious again. "And she says she wants to work with you?"

 "Yeah. Apparently she is one of my Fixer's stealth and infiltration pros, but she wants someone big and tough on hand if things ever get hot," Jackie explained before lifting an arm. "I'm quite flattered she knows high quality when she sees it, especially since she isn't no pushover herself. She trained a bit with me and Vik not long ago, and she has good hooks in her."

 "Oh? Viktor trains her?" Guadalupe asked curiously, recalling Jackie mentioning how he first met the girl at her office. The former boxing pro turned Ripperdoc was an old friend and didn't just train anybody. Helping train and channel Jackie's fighting spirit after her son threw her ex-husband out had been partly a favor from her.

 "A little. Point is, she is a tiger, that girl," Jackie said. "Didn't say yes right away. Gotta give the idea a trial run and see if any good. If it is might be regular partners on gigs."

 "Well... if she passes your little test you best bring her over for dinner. I like to know my son's friends and those who will be watching his back after all."

 "Oh, Mama..." Jackie whined.

 "No room for compromise here, Jackie! You best invite her over or else I'll do it for you," Guadalupe put her foot down.

 "Fine... but maybe don't open up with the baby pictures this time?" Jackie practically begged.

 "I am your mother and it is my god given right to embarrass you in front of your friends any time I damn well please," she said with a smile.

 "Yes Mama," Jackie said in a defeated tone.

 The two netrunners watched as Motoko jacked out and then once making sure she was really out of the private server talked to one another.

 "And how long have you been teaching her?" Walk3r asked in an incredulous tone.

 "Been coming into my shop in meat space occasional for a few months, but actual Net diving? A couple of days." Yoko answered

 "Bullshit! No green as grass baby runner could have survived that old server, let alone do as well as she did. I know decent runners who might not have done half as well!"

 "I told you she was good."

 "I _thought_ you were trying to teach her the common sense of getting in and out once she realized she was in over her head! The rate of skill growth you are describing is unreal! Not even having V3L's klepped gear should have closed the gap this fast."

 "I know... her mother, Natasha Kusanagi, was a good runner from the info dives I took, but pretty sure she her daughter is gonna be way better before the year is even up. _That's_ why I'm helping her out."

 "To get a lot of solid favors out of her?"

 "To stay on her good side once she becomes a true monster on Net."

 "Don't you think you are overblowing things?" Walk3r asked in a tone that was both in disbelief and maybe a bit of nervousness. "She's just a kid after all."

 "Its the fact she is a kid that partly sells it for me, its still growing and adapting after all. Its like neuroplasticity at its finest. Her brain should have been fried if not forced out during that attack, I'm talking at least minor brain damage, but she took it like it was nothing. If she comes back soon no worse for wear you know she'll be the real deal."

 "Wow, never seen you have _this much_ faith in someone before, Yoko."

 "Not faith... _instinct_. That girl is gonna make the Net her playground someday if she lives long enough, and if she does we all got to hope by then that we are her playmates and not her toys."
