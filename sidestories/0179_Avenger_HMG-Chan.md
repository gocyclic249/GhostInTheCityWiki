**Avenger HMG-Chan!**

 "Aaaaaaaaagh!" Jun shrieked at an octave that would make an Opera singer jealous, and sounded like the shrieks of a little girl or a full grown man discovering a fist sized tarantula in their cereal bowl. Loud, high pitched, and piercing. Laying at the feet of his frantically was a burrito, oddly what was visible on its wrapping were the words Diablo La Muerte, or in other words Devil the Death.

 "Kukukuku!" Chittered what looked to be a large black and white spider, from the large protrusion on its back emerged what looked like the barrel of an HMG. "Call my behind fat will you..."

 "Where are they!?" Motoko demanded as she sprung from her bed, the ninja inspired night wear fluttering, and the purple scarf trailing behind her as she grabbed her Burya and stumbled through the door to fight whatever Scavs who decided to end their lives with this attack.

 "To Battle Comrade!" Burya called out as they went.

 "Hmmm..." HMG-chan hummed as she consulted her other half. "maybe we shouldn't have sabotaged so many vending machines..."

 "We have brought down the oppressors..." Chitin replied as she glared at the pile of humanity that moaned and suffered, from eating or drinking pure spice disguised as their favorite innocent snacks and drinks.

 "I am going to squash that bug!!!!" Jun's hoarse scream sounded out, as a loud thud shook the floor.

 "Die!" Jun roared as he stabbed. slashed, and then beat the unlucky and deranged Tachikoma prototype into a flat pile of materials.

 "HMG-Chan lives on!" HMG-Chan cried out, her plan to only tamper with the burritos paid off. Thankfully Chitin the traitor was willing to sacrifice themselves for her.

 "Now for you!" Jun cried reaching for the Stupendous Magical Gun HMG-Chan, filling her innocent heart with fear.

 "Nin-po Burrito Shuriken!" Motoko cried as she pelted Jun with multiple opened burritos, bringing agony to his eyes and sinuses. "Ninja Escape!"

 "MOTOKO!!!" Jun cried out as Motoko ran, carrying her HMG that she had used the quick detach on the gunpod to retrieve.

 "I win..." HMG-Chan murmured as she relished being held by her owner.
