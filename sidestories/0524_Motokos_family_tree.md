**Motoko's family tree**

 Motoko felt her eyes roll as Hiromi pull her along, after learning that she was tossed out of the poor girl had been nearly went catatonic.

 Nearly being the key word.

 She had fussed over her like a Miltitech grunt fusses over their rifle, and for those not knowing how much that is?_too much!_

 And so Motoko had asked the question '_what will it take to not coddle me like Arasaka coddles its stock investors?_'.

 And that was this, Hiromi dragging her down the street in the more fancy Corpo district.

 "Where are we going Hiromi? You seemed weird when I asked?"

 Hiromi with a smile said "To this shop that can look at your genes to find out more about you family history! Its kinda a big thing to a number of the higher-rank Arasaka members, something about work dynasty's and traditions. I thought it be pree-, _cool_ to see if you how your family tree is, perhaps one of your gramps worked with Arasaka at one point. _Then maybe I'll get mum and dads approval..._"

 "What was that last part?"

 "Nothing important! Oh! Here's the the Shop."

 Motoko looked at the shop, although smaller than the other Corpo department stores, was better decorated with a water fountain in front of it as well a a few real and organic trees. As she looked at the shop she shrugged, it reminded her of her old world ancestor finder survives, just without the do-it-at-home kits.

 So, yeah seemed okay, either it would probably just be a swab test that tells her that she comes from a long line of nobodies.

 What could possibly go wrong?

* * *

_**Everything went wrong!!!**_

 Mokoto drove at speed she'd never usually drive at in Section 9's truck, Ichi was controlling the gun as he gave directions Malcolm was checking on Jun's wounds after picking him up and Hiromi was frothing at the mouth _actually catatonic_!

 All of this thanks to a DNA test!

 "Mokoto!" shouted her brother "What the hell is going on?!"

 Before she could say anything Ichi interrupted "Arasaka AV! it's gaining!"

 "Then shoot it!" she shouted back to Ichi, with her eye glued to the road she began to explain to her brother. "One of our grandma's was a bad bitch and decided to fuck and have the kid of the one guy that Suburo would kill an entire city to kill again!"

 "What?! Are you saying that our-"

 "Yes! Our granddad is _**Johnny-Fucking-Silverhand!!!**_" She had thought her instinct to blow up Arasaka tower had been from her playing the game, _NOPE!_ Just lame-ass grampa's genes!

 Damn you Silverhand! You're not cool! She'll blow up Arasaka ten times better after she got away from-

 She saw a figure in the rear view mirror. No, it couldn't be...

**"GET BACK HERE YOU FUCKING MEAT! I WANNA HAVE SOME FUN!!!"**

 It was! Adam Smasher!

_DAMN YOU JOHNNY!!!_
