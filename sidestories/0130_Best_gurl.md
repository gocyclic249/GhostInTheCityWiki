**Best gurl**

 The young girl took off her BD wreath with a big smile on her lips. "Wohoo!" She shouted as she raised her wreath above her head and shook her head so that her two green pigtails flailed about her. "That was soooo preeeeem!"

 Rita looked over at the young girl with a smirk "Liked that didn't ya?" She said.

 "Yeah they were soo chilled." The young girl said. Her eyes were big like saucers. "Who was that? They must be chromed up the gills. Felt almost like taking dorphs it was so chilled."

 "Not that much chrome actually, and it's from an edgerunner Nox wants to stuffit with." Rita said.

 "Nox? Huh? Wut? Him? Doing the old-in-out-in-out with this guy? Girl?" The girl said. She looked at her wreath "Naaah that ain't gonna happen." She shook her head. "Nox wouldn't know what or how to do it with a girl this preem." She said.

 Rita gave a small laugh. "Eh, she's a bit weird, so I dunno, maybe. Nox … eh.. yeah maybe you're right." She said.

 "So, so whoisit! Please-pl-pl-pleasetellmeRitaI'lldoanythingifyoutellmeI'llevenpolishyourbat." The girl said. Her eyes were now bigger then saucers. Rita's resistance to the dreaded puppy eyes quickly crumbled.

 "So it's this new edgerunner, name's Motoko. She'll prolly swing by later today." Rita said.

 The girl hugged her tightly. "Thank you!" She said.

 Later that day.

 Motoko pulled up in her black painted car at Lizzies. It was still quite early so there were a few parking spots still available. She pumped her fist. "A-yeah parking space! Lit!" She said. 

 As she walked up to the bouncer Rita, Motoko noticed that Rita's face was twitching, almost as if she was struggling to not smile for some reason. "Judy in?" She said to Rita.

 "Yeah usual place." Rita said. "go right ahead." She stepped aside to let Motoko past. "And for what's it's worth: I'm sorry." Rita said as Motoko walked past her.

 "Sorry? For what?" Motoko said. Rita shrugged and smiled.

 Motoko shook her head as she walked through the club to get to Judy's workstation. "That was weird, were the virtues that bad? Am I not getting any eddies?" She said to herself.

 As Motoko, worried about eddies, came down the stairs she saw a young girl running towards her, green hair flaring behind her. The girl came to a stop right in front of Motoko. "You her? You Motoko?" The girl said. Her eyes were big and shiny.

 "Um yeah?" Motoko said.

 The girl went down on her knees in front of Motoko with her arms stretched up towards Motoko and her eyes was possibly even bigger then before. "Please teach me your Gun-Fu Senpai!" The girl said.

 "Whaa..."

 "I saw your BD Senpai, please teach me I want to be like you when I grow up." The girl said.

 "Eeeeh...."

 "Please, please, please, please!" The girl said. Her eyes kept growing bigger.

 The girl used puppy eyes on Motoko, it was super effective.

 "Well I suppose I can show you a couple of..." Motoko said.

 The girl was up and hugging Motoko tightly around her belly so fast that she surely had a sandevistan installed. "Thank you, thank you, thank you." The girl said.

 The hugging was becoming a bit awkward for Motoko, who tried to gently extricate herself from the girl. "So ummm... what's your name?" Motoko said.

 The girl loosened her death grip around Motoko a bit and looked up at her, eyes shiny with tears and a big smile on her lips. "Oh I'm Rebecca, but call me Becky!" Rebecca said.

 "Oh okay, wait you don't happen to have a brother named Pilar do you?" Motoko said.

 Rebecca's eyes narrowed. "You know my bro?" She said.

 "Umm no, just heard of him." Motoko said.

 "Oh, okay, this is gonna be preem! We're going to best chooms! Hey can you teach me how to use a machinegun in each hand?" Rebecca said.

 It was at this moment Motoko knew, she fucked up.
