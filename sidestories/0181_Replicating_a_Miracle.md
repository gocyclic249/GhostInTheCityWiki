**Replicating a Miracle**

 ---

 It had been a few hours since Motoko had left, and her three friends sat quietly in one of the Tyger Claw bars, simply thinking.

 Malcolm slowly shook his head in awe, replaying in his mind what he'd seen Motoko do. "It's just crazy that-"

 "Yes Malcolm," Hiromi groaned, "we know. Motoko is amazing."

 "But it doesn't even make sense!" Malcolm whined. "I mean aren't you supposed to _di_ e if you get a bad brain injury?"

 Ichi opened his mouth to give a (hopefully) snappy comeback, but paused as a thought slowly came to him.

 "Hey Ichi," Hiromi said quietly, her own mind racing, "are you thinking what I'm thinking?"

 Ichi slowly nodded. "I... I think so. Good idea Malcolm."

 "What?" the other boy said as his head rapidly turned to look between them in increasing concern.

 He _knew_ that look they had. And it was one of the worrying ones.

 "Don't worry," Hiromi said with slowly growing confidence. "I think this might just work."

 ---

 Malcolm stared flatly at his two chooms, both Ichi and Hiromi leaning against the concrete walls of the garage.

 "Wha- We almlossst got it that tiiiiiime!" Hiromi slurred out, eyes tracking all eight Ichi's that were around her.

 "Yyyyyyyyyup," Ichi droned out as he gripped onto a nearby table to stop himself from falling into the sky.

 Malcolm shook his head before striding out of the room. He was getting Vic.

 "Come on then!" Hiromi said with a sudden burst of confidence. "I can almost _feel_ the knowledge! I bet I could shoot a... I could shoot a gun! With a gun!"

 Ichi nodded. "Yeah. That would be preem. Shooting a gun. So the gun was a gun."

 "Aw yeah," Hiromi cackled. "Let's go!"

 With that, the two staggered back upright, faced each other, and ran straight forwards as fast as they could.

 Then their foreheads collided with a worrying 'crack', before the two fell backwards to the ground once again.

 "Wwwwe're n-n-not dead," Hiromi sung, "so it's alllllll good! Ima get me some singing-ing-ing-ing!"

 Ichi continued lying on the ground, his face contorting into several different expressions. "I think I peed a little again again. Ewwww. Hehe."

 With that mutual confirmation, the two knew that they were getting closer. They were both alive after all. They would definitely figure out how to replicate what happened to Motoko!

 The optimal amount of brain-damage was within their grasp!
