Nooo~ I dun wanna go sand-swimming. It's bad for mah helth-GHAH!?

 Something poking me tears me from a… less than logical dream.

 Allowing the view of Motoko's room to snap into clarity shows nothing out of place. Aside from the human staring directly at me from two feet away!

Spoiler: M1911

![Image 6](https://gamepress.gg/girlsfrontline/sites/girlsfrontline/files/2018-06/2.png)

 "Hmm." The odd little human girl grumbles, clearly stressing her voice to its lowest pitch in a futile attempt to sound old and wise. "I told ye. Mmmhmm. Shouldn'ta gotten attached. Now look'et ya. Sittin' on a shelf."

 "Wha- do I know this human?" I ask aloud incredulously. Knowing full well the human won't understand me anyway.

 Shockingly, the human nods as if she heard me. "Eeeyup. Metcha back at thuh desert I did. Got a mite bit sandy afterwards."

 Whuh?! But the only other one there was-

 Another human steps through Motoko's bedroom door, this one draped in cold-weather clothing.

Spoiler: Makarov

![Image 8](https://gamepress.gg/girlsfrontline/sites/girlsfrontline/files/2018-06/8.png)

"Черт, где я сейчас?!"(Damn, where am I now!?) The white-haired one seemingly questions in frustrated confusion, before looking at me and the odd human beside me. "Oh! Maybe you help me? I'm looking for a-" The person pulls out a small business card looking thing from her pocket. "-Constitutional Arms Unity? I'm supposed to 'preserve her action' or something…" She finishes with a confused shrug.

 The first human looks at the newcomer with weary eyes. "We'll outlast 'em. Yep. Three world wars. Best not to get attached."

 The white-haired one steps up beside the blonde. "Hey, you seem like a парень(guy) that could use a good drink!" She reaches into her coat and withdraws a large bottle of clear liquid. "How about some водка!(Vodka) to calm the nerves?"

 The blonde human nods, her american-flag pattern tie bobbing with the motion. "Mhmm, now, lemmie tell ya. All ya be needin' is seven-plus-one forteh-fives. If you need more than that, well ya need more time at the range. Mmhmm."

 The white-haired one laughs loudly. She withdraws a small pistol from her pocket and uses a bottle-opener built into the base of the grip to [pop](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=gY4ybMc2wms&ab_channel=LuisaPool) the bottle open. "Aaahh~! Enough talk _,_ is time for drink!"

 Huh, is that?.. "Hey. PPSST, HAY! Elder Makarov? What the hell is wrong with these humans?"

 The pistol held expertly in the human's hand doesn't respond, but the girl looks at me with an amused expression for some reason.

 Suddenly, the floor begins to shake. Both of the human's expressions switch to professional masks. The white-haired one drops the bottle, which bounces across the floor and begins spilling its contents with loud *Glug-glug-glug*s.

 Quick as lighting, the blonde human reaches for a pair of holsters that I hadn't noticed on her hips. Dual 1911s find themselves pointed to a spot on Motoko's bedroom floor, alongside the Makarov wielded by the now cold-faced human beside her.

 The shuddering in the floor builds, growing louder and louder, shaking the floor more and more.

 The walls themselves begin to rattle and the vibrations even begin to slowly walk me across my shelf.

 A small section of the metallic floor begins to bulge upwards, repeated heavy *clangs* echoing around the room all the while.

 Finally, the tortured floor gives way to the assault. The long black gun protrudes from the hole, heat-ribs run along its length, and a small mechanical piece near the end of the barrel flaps back and forth with the sound of gunfire.

 Then the noise abruptly stops, the metal piece on the gun goes still. The weapon retracts through the now two foot wide hole and a pair of slender hands reach up from the darkness and feel around the jagged edges of the opening.

 "Ush-sho~" A soft voice mutters as the human pulls herself up. The light of the bedroom reveals their form, covered in fabric belts of ammunition and a long sort of skirt-thing?

Spoiler: M1895 CB

![Image 10](https://gamepress.gg/girlsfrontline/sites/girlsfrontline/files/2021-04/pic_m1895mg.png)

 As soon as the human pulls herself from the hole, she sniffs the air with her nose twitching animatedly. "Huu~ huu~ I smell potato…"

 … wat

"Cука!"(Bitch!) The white haired one exclaims while diving for the bottle of alcohol gluggin away on the floor. She manages to prevent about half of the concoction from pouring out. "глупый пулемет!(Stupid gun!) You made me spill водка!(Vodka)Дебил."(Moron)

 Seemingly not concerned that a person had just tunneled up through the floor with a relic M1895 Colt-Browning! The other humans holster their weapons in an unconcerned fashion.

 "Hmm. nine-milla-meter aint got enough stoppin' power. Mmnope." The blonde one mutters.

 "Хaxaxaxaxa! Well, three is a party! Come on! Let's drink!" the suddenly cheerful girl bearing the bottle cheers.

 "Huuu~ Huuu~ Potato…" mutters the last dreamily.

 I can't help but gape as the three humans sit down on Motoko's bed and begin taking swigs from the bottle, the floor now covered in a rank liquid that slowly dribbles down into the large gash in it.

 I try to ask them what the hell is happening, but it ends up coming out as, "Wha- How do- Gheh- EEugghgh-! EEUUAAAAAA!?"

 The new-comer's eyes light up in a star pattern as she seems to notice me for the first time. "Hah~!" She grunts airily. Her hands fly to the grip of her M1895 as she stands up from the bed. "I know you… you will not take first place!"

 HUH??!! "HUH??!!"

 The girl quickly loads a fresh belt into her weapon and points it towards the floor. "...I am the best hole-digger in the world." As if the statement was announcing the start of a race, she pulls the trigger and the m1895 begins chattering out rounds. The mad human presses the swinging mechanism of the gun into the floor and tunnels into it like a jackhammer, quickly boring yet another hole into Motoko's bedroom.

 The walls shake even more than last time, items around the room rattling and falling from shelves. The white-haired human laughs uproariously, in between taking long gulps from her soon-to-be-empty bottle. The blonde one seems even grumpier somehow as she and her partner begin to bounce on the bed from the aggressive vibrations.

 My vision begins to blur as the rumbling gets worse and worse.

 I can feel myself involuntarily shuffle across the shelf towards the end, getting closer by the second.

 While my thoughts are still rattling just as bad as the room around me, the floor disappears and I tumble from the shelf, the colors of the room blurring together as I pitch forward and fall right into one of the suddenly huge holes in the ground.

 The darkness seems to go on forever, just falling.

 Falling.

Falling. 

Falling.

 "EUHGAH!" I shout against my will as the floor appears below me.

 Despite lacking any lungs, it feels like my frame is tempting to eject them with how heavy I'm breathing.

 My eyes dart around my surroundings, and I find I'm back in Motoko's bedroom.

 There's no holes in the floor, no insane alcohol drinking girls who use firearms like demolition equipment.

 Just me on a shelf.

 Oh thank the ancestors, it was a dream.

 …

 I'm glad you're still here with me dust motes.

 What would I do without you?

 *sigh*

 I'm glad that's over.
