 Mac looks over the young man at his desk.

 "David Martinez, Top of your class in Arasaka Acadamy, despite what my granddaughter believes you never sold black lace...."

 Davids eyes bug out

 "Wait what, I never sold that crap!"

 Mac chuckles at the boys reaction.

 "Oh I know that, you sold XBD's and did it like a creepy gonk.

 I see Hiromi taught you how to be a more effective salesman, so what brings you to my office?"

 "I was told you're a fixer and one of the one to go to if you want to break into the Edgerunner business."

 The older man laughs at the comment.

 "True true I do tend to get a disproportionate share of the rookies, personally I blame Wakako and Dino for that one.

 Still I'm probably your best bet, Padre is usually more willing to recruit fresh talent than most of the big names but most of the basic jobs go to Valentinos.

 You could go to Hiromi for a job but she's got you stuck on XBD distribution and S9 is a little over your skill level, unless you want to work for Loot Goblin.

 Just do yourself a favor and never take a job from Kirk or Faraday"

 Davids eyes glaze over for a second as the older man lists off various fixers.

 "What's wrong with those two?"

 "Kirk isn't even a real fixer just a gonk who thinks he's a bigger deal than he is and

 Faraday is a backstabbing asshole and control freak."

 David nods at his advice.

 "So I don't suppose you have any jobs I can do?"

 Mac shakes his head.

 "Alas nothing as exciting as killing scavs that stole a car or raiding Arasaka tower.

 No you my friend start at the bottom, deliver a package to my client unopened and undamaged.

 The Client will give you a shard and you bring that back to me."

 "Wait that's it, I'm a delivery boy?"

 "This is your first job David, you don't have a rep nor any iron or chrome, prove you can do the simple jobs,

 deliveries, collection pickups, shill jobs, bit of simple klepping and leg breaking low level targets.

 Once you get some experience under your belt and dirt under your fingernails we'll move on to bigger jobs.

 "So where am I taking this?"

 "The Afterlife."

 "Wait really, NOVA!!"

 "Hold your horses kid, just because the package is going there doesn't mean that anyone will give you the time of day.

 just go there, tell the doorman you got a package for Nix and that you have to hand it to him in person.

 Nix will probably thank you, give you the shard and shoo you out, if he likes you he might even give you a tip."

 David looks over the box.

 "So what is it?"

 Mac lets out a laugh and shakes his head.

 "First rule about deliveries, never ask what's in the package.

 You can ask if it's fragile, volatile, has specific requirements for transport & storage but never ever ask what's in the box.

 Second Rule is to never open the box."

 One last thing kid, do you even know how to use a gun?"

 David Shrugs.

 "Kinda"

 Mac laughs his ass off.

 "Jesus christ kid, alright go deliver the package and get back here, when you get back I'll outfit you with some basic iron and shooting lessons.

 Wants to be an edgerunner in Night city and doesn't even have a gun or know how to shoot, HA!"
