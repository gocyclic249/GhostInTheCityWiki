My barrel sparks and barks, my body chatters and clanks, somehow the sounds fail to match the din of uncontrollable joy spilling from my receiver. My ammunition chain disassembles itself as I go, falling to pieces, much like the targets ahead of me, as me and my beloved companion unite in glorious purpose!

 Oh what a joyous day that was! My boring life prior to the girl's interference had been nothing but watching an elevator door. My barrel cold and untouched by smoke or cinders, the stagnant air of the building permeated my recoil spring. I could feel the rust beginning to set in, a slow painful death.

 But then **She**came! She held me with such loving respect, the way she caressed my paneling with thoughts of the destruction we would wreak filling her mind. I know that they were, because it was all I could think of as well! In that moment, I foresaw our time together. The years of faithful service, the millions of **_bodies_**. And all of it we would achieve with a jubilant smile. That smile...

 Like the grin on her face even now as we race through the sands! I'm certain that is my future, a premonition given to me by the Dakka lords above! Praise be to Elder Maxim! Praise be to Mother Deuce!

 I can _feel_ the moment one of my rounds penetrates the 'Raffen' car's front differential and tears it to jagged pieces. The mechanical component, still under significant speed and strain, immediately eats itself, throwing out great chunks of metal and dropping the drive shaft into the sand, which catches on something, and forces the vehicle to flip! Parts are thrown in all directions, some of them organic, and **still**we fire!

 My bullets just keep coming! This is the happiest day of my li-

**CRASH!**

Sand...

 all I see is sand.

 It is in my chamber, it is down my barrel, in my magazine. Sand everywhere.

 But that isn't all that bad, I'll still function regardless of obstruction. I am a descendant of Mother Deuce and uncle Kalashnikov after all!

 No, what's worse is that there is no slender hand wielding me.

 No companion to spread destruction and sound.

**She**... is gone.

 ...

 I'm not sure how long I've been laying here, expecting her to pull me from the grains any moment.

 I'm sure we just had a bit of a tumble! She'll be back for me!

 ...Right?

 Well... maybe she got hurt or something. I can't expect her to carry me if her arms fell off now can I? That would be unreasonable!

 Well! If she's lost her arms and can no longer wield me, then I'll just have to get my own arms and go to **Her**. It can't be that hard.

 I pull and stretch, grind and click, the screws in my forward frame come loose, spilling out into the sand. The carbon-alloy-mesh wrapped gas tubes of my recoil system spring out from my body with a snap!

 I pump the new four inch long 'arms' furiously, digging at the sand and clawing my way out of the dune. I cheer silently as my digital aiming optic reveals the blue sky through the particles of rock.

 HAHA! No unrefined metals can contain my engineered form!

 The sun warms my black casing as I feel a phantom smile spread across my frame.

 Now, all I have to do is get back to **Her**apartment. I remember exactly where it is! It's in night city! Which is that way!

 Not being able to 'speak' in my preferred explosive manner, I access the small speakers that usually only beep in warning when I'm empty, or there's a rare jam.

 My voice comes out high pitched and crackly, but I simply can't stay quiet! "[I'm on my waaaaay~! I'm on my WAAAAAY!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mo_IQUmmTvI&ab_channel=CartoonNetworkUK)"
