Motoko got the car. She got the girl. Now she just needs to head to the drive-in so she can force Hiromi to watch anime with her.

* * *

"Hey Johnny. Someone's already here."

 V and Rogue are coming though from the projector room. I guess it's Johnny right now though. Kind of annoyed I forgot about that plot point. Having a date night is good, but they're interrupting our date night now.

 "Oh thank you." Hiromi whispers before she turns around and says, much louder, "well choom, it was fun, but it looks like we got company. Why don't we let the... Holy shit."

 Hiromi is looking flustered. Eyes darting between Rogue and me. "Motoko, is the queen bitch of the Afterlife on a date... with a rando?"

 "That's not nice Hiromi." I say, before I address our two new guests, "Looking good Rogue. Cute outfit. And Johnny. Look at you. You're looking a new man." Haha. Maybe that was a lame joke, but I always wanted to say that.

 Rogue is looking confused. Bet she thought that no one's touched this old place in years. Which is true in game. But getting the chance to have an entire drive-in to myself, well myself and Hiromi was too good to pass up. Plus. Hiromi couldn't ditch me since I was the one who drove.

 Before they could even get a word in edgewise, Hiromi started dragging me to the entrance, well. I guess we've had our fun. I wave bye to the two of them, still having not said a word, being utterly baffled by our presence. It's like they haven't seen teenagers sneak into a movie theater for a date night.

 "How did you now", is the last thing I hear before they're fully out of view. Playing with the radio as Hiromi guns it out of the parking lot. I come to a realization. I'm dumb. I just let it slip that I knew about the whole V is Johnny thing. Dang it. I'm just too funny for my own good. That gets me a smack from Hiromi.

 "I don't know what, but I can tell you're thinking something really dumb right now you gonk."
