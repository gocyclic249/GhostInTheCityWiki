Omake: Tachi-what-ma?

 In a museum in Night City two Tachikomas were moving along the historical displays.

 "And so it happened that the first of us came to be!" Tachikoma #117 was explaining their origin to the newest member of the family.

 "So THIS must be the Tachikoma #1?" Tachikoma #314 asked while stopping before a display with an actual, full-sized Tachikoma inside it.

 "Well, yes." Tachikoma #117 answered. "But we don't actually call her by the number."

 "Really? So by what designation do you call her?"

 "Tachi-grand-ma."
