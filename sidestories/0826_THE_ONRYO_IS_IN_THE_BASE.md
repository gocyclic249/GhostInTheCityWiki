[INTRUDER ALERT: THE ONRYO IS IN THE BASE!]

 "What? The Ghost is in the fucking base?"

 Brick was not a masterful tactician or suped-up-enough of a borg to feel sure about the general alert, so he did what felt most wise and began to sprint towards the Netrunner den in the basement.

 Running along the sleeping quarters Brick kept shouting to wake up all the others and hoping it would be enough to save their sorry hides. It was strange, though, it felt as if there was someone following him out of the edges of his vision. Brick tried to not let it distract him.

 "A little help here, i don't want to die!"

 Someone's Exotic doll was pathetically clawing along the card reader slot to the Netrunners door and Brick showed her aside without care.

 "Outta the way, braindead!"

 Positioning his elbow just right, Brick was about to activate the scanner and let himself into the den, when Shithouse, his thiccest choom, turned down the corridor and slammed his considerable borg weight right through the framing, thankfully not impaling him on the giant HMG he was carrying.

 "YO SPYDER, the Ghost is in the base!!!"

 Spyder, their dedicated Netrunner contorted himself out of the icebath, looking like an emaciated gimp, pressed a button to close a secondary security shutter for the broken door and examined all three of his visitors:

 "And who do you think, you absolute fucknuggets, activated the bloody Intruder Alert? But let me ask you something easier: Did anyone manage to see that shitty Ghost on your way here? "

 "Uh, no?"

 "Then you are as useless as all of our scrap Digital Warfare hardware."

 Shithouse cradled his HMG closer to his body and tried to express bravado, which was honestly more emotions that he was capable of in a good week:

 "The Ghost is just a shitty Edgerunner, ain't she? We've killed plenty of gonks like her in the past. She'll just run around, quickhack and then'll get flatlined, like you!"

 The balaclava style headgear of his wetsuit accentuated the eyebrow lift, but Spyder did not rise to the insult.

 "If you managed to kill a Runner, they'd be nothing like me, and nothing, NOTHING like that flatliner Ghost loose inside our base."

 "What are you, wage-slave of their fucking Corpo?"

 "No-" the runner narrowed his Kiroshi, a quickhack flashed through his eyes and the doll's head was blasted open by a ceiling mounted turret "-that would be whoever dead meat who's doll that was! Her eyes were transmitting everything that was happening here, fucking security leak!

 So listen up, gonks, or the XBD starring your insides will be the next big hit right after 'The only thing they fear is you!'!"

 Turning to his setup, the Netrunner pulled up a number of cameras that were strategically scattered throughout the base. Many of them were out of order and all three scavs could see more and more turning off by the minute. Static was filling the room.

 "The Ghost already breached our defences."

 One of the cameras zoomed onto a wraith-like silhouette only to be break into more static whitenoise. Another was focussing on a Scav trying to activate a turret that seemed to be in some kind of funk, at the last moment the turret spun up, turned and shredded the one trying to activate it.

 "You've seen what she's done to all our comrades."

 Another camera, fisheyed to cover the entirety of a ripper's den, caught her hastily working on a half-eviscerated borg. A flash of superheated steel that seemed to cut the fisheye in half and running footage was replaced with a glitching stillshot of ripper table, borg and ripper falling apart, cut diagonally with just one stroke.

 Something exploded and dust rained from the ceiling, panicked eyes searching every dark corner, Brick and Shithouse began losing their leftover cool.

 "And worst of all, she could hide anywhere from us! She could be in this fucking room!"

 Manic eyes twitched between the three scavs and tension rose, biomons subtly whining, warning their users of untold amounts of adrenalin being released into their bloodstreams.

 "She could be that HMG, she could be that doll! She could even be…"

 Two roars followed by weapons discharge exploded in the small room, revebrating through the walls until the HMG dried out. Keeling over from a giant crater in his gut, Spyder knew that he had seconds to live, his only satisfaction being that he knew that he finally got that fucker. Having planned beforehand he had jailbroken his Kiroshi to completely reflash their drives, deleting everything installed onto them, so now that he was hemorraging all over the scenery, his eyes finally rebooted and he was greeted with chunky salsa leftovers from his monowire cutting. It was Brick and Shithouse, the two idiotic meatbags had shot themselves in rage whilst he must have gotten penetrated due to the machine gun still firing after it was detached from the borg's hands.

 "There was no Ghost. There… was… no… Ghost…"

 "Where… wa…"

 The last thing Spyder saw was the lens of his ceiling mounted Turret slowly focussing on him, as if it was a curious doll programmed to be inquisitive and innocent…

 -

 A block away, Motoko slowly exhaled after swallowing the last drops of a bowl of ramen broth. She uploaded her Onryo about fifteen minutes ago, might be right about time to call her friends with the Behemoth. She was not keen on cleaning up dried blood from all the salvage.

 It was a hot day.
