Scorpion's Plus One​

 "Not sure if I should be proud of ya or haul your ass to the city to get locked up," Mitch, along with most of the camp, joked as he moved some gear past Scorpion.

 "Y'all are not nearly as funny as you think you are," Scorpion grumbled for what must have been the 100th time already.

 The whole camp was giving him shit ever since he invited Motoko to their BBQ the next time she had the chance, saying stuff like that the whole time just to mess with him.

 Things like:

 'The beautiful prince finally inviting the dashing princess for a home dinner.'

 'Finally acting on his feelings for his savior ever since that fateful day.'

 'Scorpion likes them young, short, and deadly.'

 'Better call the cops cause Badlands has its latest perv.'

 'His crush finally coming over to officially meet the family.'

 'Looking forward to meeting his sugar momma.'

 Nevermind the fact that Motoko, despite her talent as a solo, was still just 14 years old and Scorpion preferred his ladies on the mature side. Nevermind how their relationship was purely professional and at most friend-like after she saved his ass. Nevermind that Saul himself wanted her over to discuss the clan's debts to her. Nevermind how they all wanted to cultivate a good working relationship with a capable city dwelling, no one missed out on the chance to screw with him for laughs.

 He didn't mind, truly. They were family after all, and family likes ripping into each other for fun. But it was getting annoying just how common it was becoming.

 Especially since Panama was the worst offender.

 Still, the teasing would continue and eventually peter out in time so he would just have to grin and bare it. It helped that he was able to mostly ignore it thanks to working on his newest ride, something his family was happy to point out was a gift from his 'pint-sized input' after taking out a small Wraiths camp.

 Raffen and proper nomad clans may have been natural enemies, but both of them loved their vehicles so Scorpion loved his newest Quadra. The one he claimed from the collection of cars they collected from the abandoned farm Motoko lead them too looked like it wasn't far from factory fresh, something he could appreciate as he repainted it, keyed it to himself, and started modifying to his liking.

 "So Scorpion, what you think your admirer is up to?" Panam called out from where she was working at another former Wraith car. "Think she is thinking about you?" she teased with a smirk.

 "Sure she has much more important things to think about," Scorpion causally answered, not rising to the bait or else Panam would yank him in, as she usually did.

 "Of course, storming gang hideouts and wiping them all out. Maybe a corpo office and fighting off hitsquads. Or maybe she's hijacking an AV," Panam listed off.

 "I'm sure even she has normal days." Scorpion waved her off.

 ---

 "Hi Ciri!" was Motoko's first words the second she saw the [figure](https://oyster.ignimgs.com/mediawiki/apis.ign.com/the-witcher-3/3/3f/Ciri_in_the_witcher_3_wild_hunt-wide.jpg) in question walking and looking around transfixed at Night City and her people as she stumbled out of a nearby alley not long after Motoko noticed a flash of light and muted boom.

 Something most would likely in NC would say was a grenade going off or something become continuing on with their day. NC was used to weird shit.

 Even her medieval clothing, sword, and lack of chrome would raise more than a few eyebrows for most, except maybe later bit.

 "Wha...? Who are you? How do you know me?" She said in shock as her hand went to her sword as she kept looking around. "Where am I?"

 "All great questions that involve the multiverse as a whole and that I'm _ecstatic_ to finally talk to someone about who would totally get it!" I said as I causally looped my arm into hers before she could react and started dragging her along. "We can talk all about it at my favorite ramen place!"

 "Ramen?" Ciri asked in confusion.

 "Oh, you are in for a treat!"
