 "SURPISE!"

 Motoko moved. She knew she only had seconds before Jun reacted and stopped her. She had this planned and rehearsed to the exact second! Popping out of the vent, she lunged at the standing target facing the Big ass TV

_Huh, Hiromi must have been working out. She looks really really buffed. No, no more thought, just SMASH_

 Just as the cake left her hand, something about her target caught her attention. The 20 points in reflexes + her new Sandevistan _(Thanks David, may you rest in peace.)_ started working started working and slowed time down to a crawl allowing her to actually look at her target.

_wrinkled face wearing sunglasses in a rictus of rage that was turning to one of confusion, the guy on the commerative Arasaka Eddie bill being choked by said face.

 OH SHIT! This was the Prologue bit where Yorinobu was killing his Pops!_

 In the infinite Matrix mode that was Motoko's current perception of time, her hand clawed desperately at the cake inching it's way through the air hoping that somehow her Gamer System would activate a Telekinesis DLC so she could Force Grab the Cake. 

 Alas, the world did not collaborate and all she could do was stare at Yorinobu's face as he realized what was about to happen.

![Image 32](https://mystickermania.com/cdn/stickers/memes/sticker_2134-512x512.png?t=09102020)​

_Huh. He kinda looks like that Shocked Pikachu meme._

 Fueled by years of Gamer Instinct, Motoko instinctively saved a picture of the face with her Kiroshis and started compressing it into a emoticon.

***SMUSHED.**

 The awkward silence that followed after the Cake's impact was soon broken by a wheezing gasp.

 "Edge...Runner, i have a....Gig. Save my....Life and i will give you....Everything you want."

 The vocal request from Saburo echoed through the air and triggered the **The Major E-Contract App** Motoko had customed made and installed to handle the pesky requests that a renowned Solo like her would get from strangers.

![Image 33](https://www.powerpyx.com/wp-content/uploads/cyberpunk-2077-stanislavskis-method.jpg)​

 It looking One to One to Cyberpunk2077's Dialogue tree was pure coincidence and not Motoko being a weeb. ![Image 34: ;)](https://forums.spacebattles.com/styles/sbforums/smilies/wink.gif)

 Quickly accepting the Gig and thus sending a copy of the contract to both the Client's mailbox and Hiromi's, Motoko got to work. Hands moving like lightning punched a nerve cluster on the Assailant's arm causing the arm to spasm and involuntarily release the Client. 

 A followup leg sweep sent Yorinobu tumbling before steel arms locked him into a hold where a nerve pinch then send him into dreamlands.

_Sorry, hope you have better luck in your dreams._

 Just as Motoko moved to help the coughing Saburo up, the doors to the suite burst open as Hiromi darted into the room at a sprint. The sounds of combat and gunfire revealing how she got through while flashes of blood spurting through the air revealed Jun's presence.

 "MOTOKO, WHAT DID YOU DO! WHAT DO YOU MEAN YOU GOT A GIG FROM THE CHAIRMAN TO SAVE HIS LIF- APOLOGIES! ARASAKA SAMA, I DID NOT SEE YOU! I APOLOGIZE FOR WHATEVER MOTOKO DID!"

 "Wow so rude Hiromi!"

 "It's fine. *cough, your friend here saved my life from a coup attempt by my son. It seems like you're her Fixer, it's good to see Arasaka still has some up-and-coming talents. Now young Solo, i will fufil my promise, what do you want."

 "I want a department established under Arasaka Japan's Corporate structure and the position of it's Head to be given to my friend over there!"

 "Done. Is that all you want? Nothing else for you?"

 "Nope, that's all i need at the moment. Happy Birthday Hiromi!"

 As Hiromi burst into tears and pounced at Motoko with a bear hug, Motoko couldn't help but feel fuzzy inside and proud that she managed to turn what was a disaster into the BEST PRESENT EVER!

_All that gunfire and pleas for mercy are kinda ruining my feels though..OH RIGHT JUN! He's still cutting his way through the security troops guarding the Penthouse!_

 "Oh oh, Arasaka Sama! If it's Ok, could you also get the security troops to standdown so my brother can stop butchering them? We'll like to get back to Hiromi's Birthday Party. Maybe also have a Cake sent up from Konpeki Plaza's kitchen as well?"

 "HAH! Very Well young Solo! Hiromi kun, congratulations on your Birthday, i see a bright future for the Youngest Department Head in Arasaka!"
