_3 months after having met Motoko..._

 "You know the plan we go into the Militech warehouse, kill them all and get the sandy. We need to be fast, no cameras in the vicinity or building, only one car entered, so there are at most five militech operatives. Get ready." V said coldly.

 At her command the other three Arasaka operatives left the car. No answer, silent as allways, as they should be.

 She finally found the sandy Jankins wanted her to get.

 The fucking slave driver.

 Four fucking months working her ass off with nothing to show and then finally a clue, it was almost to good to be true.

 If the Arasaka Netrunners hadnt confirmed the camera tape to be real, she would have thought it was a trap.

 But hey sometimes you just got to be lucky, it happened more than once on her gigs with Jackie.

 She would have smiled at her memories of him if not for the drugs.

 They surrounded the door.

 A operative put on the explosives.

 She began to count down 3.... 2.... 1....

 Boom!!!

 the door flung open and the operatives went in.

 To her suprise there was no shooting, was the intel false again?!

 When she made her first step through the door, being the last person, her optics went out.

 Fuck! They had a netrunner and he could get through their ice without them noticing!

 Then she heard three shots.

 Comically slow, as if they took their time executing them.

 Neither her Shingen or Kenshin seemed to work when she tried to fire them.

 None of her programms or quick hacks worked, she was helpless, even her chemiclas stopped being administered, so she could now feel the panick, the fear and then just calm sadness.

 She wouldnt see Jackie again...

 She would even miss the crazy kid...

 Then her optics worked again, blinding her shortly, because the warehouse was now brightly lit.

 "SURPRISE!!!" She heard people shout.

 Thats when she saw Motoko still holding her burya which was smoking, as well as Jackie, Jun she got to know a short while ago, Vik who helped her with her new chrome, Misty who was kinda weird but also nice, as well as a few other kids she saw around Motoko.

 What surprised her the most was the huge garland which spelled happy birthday, even in her favorite collor, red.

 "HAPPY BIRTHDAY V!!" They shouted again, completely ignoring the dead Arasaka operatives at her feet, who lost half their heads.

 "WHAT THE FUCK ARE YOU DOING?!" she almost screamed more roughly than she wished, but she thought she would die 5 seconds ago.

 "Its your birthday and because you never stop working we wanted to give you a surpirse party!" said Motoko as if it was the most normal thing in the world, having a bright grin on her face.

 Crazy girl.

 "We made a cake Hermanita, Motoko even got real strawberries and cream!" said Jackie with a huge smile on his face as well.

 She had to chuckle, those gonks.

 "You are gonna get me fired" She said with a small smile, stepping over the dead operatives to hug her friends.
