Why...

 Am I a Scavenger?

 I can't be...I just...can't...

 "Look at this boys!" A mocking voice called out in the darkness, causing her heart to freeze. "Looks like someone threw away someone who already had her chrome ripped out!"

 "Aw man," A hard and dangerous sounding voice spoke up. "why the hell would someone throw an already broken-"

 Broken...shards flying...glittering...sneers...mocking laughter...useless.

 ___

_"Looks like your brought a fragile hunk of steel to a gun fight," The man with a large mechanical arm sneered as the shards of her mother's sword rained down, broken and shattered. "I wonder if you will do any better..."

 Flashes of pain, darkness, and a man with clamps on his fingers reaching forward and obscuring her vision... Then pain and suddenly she could only see out of her left eye.

 "Man you are already passing out and we ain't even really got started yet..." The man who broke her...sword sneered, even as his friends broke her...like her sword. "I think we need to make this more...special. Bring in that shit you Gonks used on that cop!"

 Her face on fire as a loud sound echoed in the darkness, something grabbing her head, and then something forced into the void in her head. Suddenly she could see out of her left eye, she wished she couldn't...

 They broke her like the Sword, why did they want her to see her broken body!?

 "I want you to see this Choom," A Scav with goggle like optics and metal teeth purred as he held up a syringe with a bubbling jade liquid in it. "this shit is going to fucking hurt, and I want you to know what it is going to do. It is going to make you heal, but not like regrow your eyes or some shit. No this shit is going to force out all of that chrome you still have slowly and oh so painfully, and I am going to watch with glee..."

 She recognized him then, she had cut his hand off...when she was still whole, and when she thought her Sword was...invincible. Then he jabbed it forward, pain flashed through her neck alongside a kind of soothing feeling, and then it became burning, agonizing pain!

 \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\__

 "-at the fuck was that shit!!!" The first voice screamed in a voice that sounded...nauseous.

 "I don't know what you did bitch-" The second voice growled angrily, but she found she couldn't focus on his voice.

 Rather she found herself stunned by the box that filled the darkness that she had been trapped in.

**Due to repeated actions the Skill Technopathy has been created.

 Technopathy: The Ability to Communicate with Technology and Manipulate Technology with Mana.

 Technopathy Level 1: Passive: Allows User to Communicate with those who have Comm Devices or Radios.

 Active: Allows User to Control A Single Device for 50 Mana a Minute.**

 Suddenly light was returned to her as she could see a young girl with dark hair, laying on the ground, and looking like sallow and thin. Around her and stomping towards her were men with obvious chrome, dressed in white tank tops, and looking like generic Scavs like in the memories now floating in her mind.

 She knew even as one drew out a gun that they were going to kill the girl, and that by the feel of the ground and how the girls hand scraped against a sharp rock... That she was the girl and she was about to see herself killed.

 "Nooo!" She shouted in defiance as her hand came up in the direction of the men she could see from afar, and she pulled desperately on what she could feel around her and shoved it at the ones who wanted to kill her.

 Suddenly the box was back, her vision was gone, and she felt like something was dancing along her skin and through her veins.

**A New Skill Has Been Created: Nooo!

 Noo! Absorbs Nearby Power in Order to Generate a Blast of Electrical Energy. Damage Scales by How Long and How Much Energy Users Channels from Electrical Sources.

 Nooo! Level 1: Can Absorb Up to 200 points of Energy that can then be channeled into lightning, can target up to three enermies in the same area, or spread throughout a cone of up to three yards in front of the user.**

 "What is happening to me?" Motoko muttered as she passed out on the ground, leaving two dead Scavs smoking in front of her.

 "Motoko..?" Hiromi murmured in wide eyed shock as she numbly slid down the wall of the alley she had slipped into when she saw the Scavs walking towards the person who she had found out had stolen her best friend's body, that was until she was suddenly bombarded with memories... Memories of her friend being beaten by Scavs, her sword broken, and then tortured.

 Suddenly she was no longer sure that the Motoko that had been returned to Jun was the real Motoko, her not knowing how she had been caught by the Scavs, and her seeming hostile regard for her taking on a more sinister light.

 Notes: Here is a more organic Gamer Motoko, or at least the best I can do.

 Quick explanations.

 -She was injected with the essence of Gaia, some kind of chemical that some Gonk found in the ground and forced out his Cyberware. He was found by Scavs before it could really work on him and grant him some kind of insane abilities. The essence of Gaia was trying to produce a way to counter the uncontrolled proliferation of tech, but was too weak to make more than a small amount that was stumbled upon. It is essentially a virus.

 -Motoko ran into Scavs who shattered her Sword after she got cocky after cutting the hand off of one of them who had Gorilla Arms, mentally she equates the broken sword to herself. That she broke like her sword, that she was as useless as her sword, and that she couldn't overcome her own weakness. During her Coma she tried to rebuild herself with her own memories, sadly the memories were so intertwined with her sword and swordsmanship that she rejected them because of the pain it brought. Eventually the Amnesiac being she had become surrounded by painful memories dug into her own mind, dug deeper and deeper, and eventually dug into the memories of who she had been before.

 -The other Motoko was made when they experimented on whether the Soul Killer could be used to save someone infected by the Gaia Virus/Essence, namely they loaded her up with Cyberware in order to slow it's speed of rejection so they could successfully use the Soul Killer on her. Eventually the engram defragmented into a shockingly complete mind, the mind being Motoko before the Scavs defeated her. Essentially the parts of her that were rejected and thus hates the other Motoko, quietly resents Jun, and loathes Hiromi for the attack that caused her to lose her life. Rejecting essentially that any of it was her fault, that it was her own arrogance that made her think she could cut down the Scavs, and of course her sheltered over confidence.

 -Hiromi basically went to hunt down the "Fake" Motoko to try and earn forgiveness from Motoko, not realizing that the "real" Motoko is basically made up of the resentment and rage she felt that she failed to kill the Scavs, and the almost psychotic hate for Hiromi who she chose to blame even though Hiromi had warned her about the Scav Activity there and asked her not to go. Now she has been exposed to the memories of Motoko who she and Jun were led to believe was an engram from a Scav traitor that was uploaded into multiple victims, while the victims actual minds were stored somewhere else so they could torture them and steal any info they have.

 -Jun basically loses his girl like canon, which causes him to bury his misgivings about his supposedly real sister, and the fact she acts unnerving in her Gemini derived Borg Body. Essentially she is secretly spying on the Tyger Claws and plans to ruin Hiromi and her family, planning to pretend to still be her friend and... forgive her. In the end she hopes to ruin Jun for failing to protect her, cripple the Tyger Claws for not saving her in time, and ruin and kill Hiromi and her old friends who she blames for everything going wrong.
