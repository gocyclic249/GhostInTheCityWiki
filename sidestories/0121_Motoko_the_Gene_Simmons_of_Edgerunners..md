 "You...are...the...best!" Hiromi squealed in glee.

 "Motoko," Judy spoke up as she stood in the middle of the room to receive her next BD. "we need to talk about this..."

 "About what?" Motoko replied as she absently took in the slight changes to the room, it actually looked a little more secure. In fact the whole place has been looking more secure, and the Mox seemed to be better at being aware than before.

 "Look at the monitor," Judy pointed to one of the security feeds where an Edgerunner was drawing and holstering her twin pistols, her clothes were kind of gaudy, and she looked like she was barely fifteen. But the way she moved showed familiarity with the guns and knowledge of how to use them. "that girl a few days ago was barred from coming in after she tried to show off and shot one of the patrons in the foot, and now after buying your "How to Be Preem" BD's..."

 "She became Preem?" Motoko asked even as she realized that the girl was pulling tricks that she hadn't tried yet, and became determined to try as soon as she was done dropping off the new BD, though why Hiromi had wanted her to climb and sneak like a Preem Ninja was beyond her. But like always she would get the uncut BD for her best Choom.

 "She is fourteen and people are starting to think she is an actual Edgerunner instead of a Street Brat wannabe..." Judy stated while rubbing the back of her neck. "They are starting to use your BD's with new recruits, and even some weird cyber leg Tyger Claw came in here to buy your new series! Muttering something about rubbing it in on someone named Jun!"

 "Wait!" Motoko suddenly demanded with fire in her eyes, and a serious mien that scared Judy. Leaving her on the verge of calling security, largely because of how she had seen Motoko deal with people in her BD's. "My brother knows I am a BD star!?"

 "What..?" Judy murmured as the information churned in her mind, coming to an obvious conclusion...
