Finally after all this time it was done...

 Not only was HMG Chan repaired... She was improved!

 As much of the original was kept as possible, but to compare her to her previous form would almost be a disservice to her current one. _Almost._ Her barrel was bent beyond repair and was thussly tossed in the trash. And thanks to her new lengthened and fluted barrel, it was more accurate and lighter! Plus it looked freaking _badass._ Though fitting the new one on to the reciprocating mechanism was.... uh, _troublesome._

 Anywhere possible weight was reduced to the bare minimum, where steel was too heavy titanium and other light metals were used instead. No expense was spared for her girl!

 The biggest mechanical change was the loading mechanism, the old one was bulky cumbersome and just impractical to reload solo in combat. That and it was trashed _anyway._ So I built my own!

 Basically, I took a bunch of those snazzy mags from a Defender, took'em apart, and rebuilt them to use HMG Chan's fifty caliber rounds. Reloads much faster and even has one of the snazzy belt grabber things! The only real down side is that you can't really fit all that many fifty caliber anti-material rounds into a tube that size, she only fits fifty two rounds in a mag.

 It was an acceptable cost so that HMG Chan would no longer be fat. (heh)

 I even added a little rubber pad on her but, so that I could brace her against me if I had to.

 And with that I christened her HMG Chan 2.0!

 Well almost.

 I may have uh... kinda sorta... made her into a AI.

 -------------

 "What did you just say?" The bemused smile had fallen from Hiromi's face. She may have heard the AI bit.

 "But she's just a little one, a sweet one really. I mean its me! Do I look like some one that would create a crazy murderous AI?" I replied with as much emphasis as possible. I mean really.

 Hiromi's face went as white as a sheet. Was she okay? Was her sugar low?

 "Hey do you need a snack or something? Your face is really pale." I said with a bit of worry in my voice.

 "Huh? Don't distract me. Show me the murder bot!" She replied right back, casting my worry aside. That's kind of hurtful you know? Wait what was that last bit?

 "She is not a murder bot! She my pure little angel, and you will take that back." I seethed, no one insults my little angel like that! Especially not my best friend.

 "If it's so innocent then why isn't it here, huh? You're hiding it because your embarrassed you made a murder bot and didn't want me to see." She announced.

 "Nuh uh, she is uh..."

 "See" She said in the tone of a person convinced of their victory.

 "No she's just..." I denied somewhat weakly. My little angel wasn't a murder bot. She just wasn't quite ready for other people yet.

 "Listen how about you show me the murder bot thing, then you don't have to get worked up about it." She stated diplomatically and somewhat smugly.

 "Fine. I'll show her to you. Not a murder bot." I acquiesced sullenly. She's sweet damn it!

 "Awesome! I get my very own murder bot!" She gushed.

 "She's not a murder bot!" I snapped. I could tell if I didn't put my foot down that this would be a thing.

 "So does that mean I can keep it?" She teased. Running for my workshop.

 "Wait what!? She is mine and you can't have her! Also quite calling her an an it!" I growled.

 But she was already gone.

 -------------

 Hiromi stared at HMG Chan.

 I stared at at Hiromi.

 HMG Chan watched TV.

 "Your gun is watching cartoons." Hiromi observed.

 "She likes them." I agreed.

 "Why is your gun watching cartoons?" Hiromi continued.

 "She likes them." I repeated.

 Hiromi gave me a very _complicated_ look just then. I could tell she wanted a bit more than that, so I motioned for her to follow me out, and away from prying ears... Er microphones?

 Anyway, I sat Hiromi down at the kitchen table and told her how this happened, at least as far as I could tell. Which really wasn't much.

 Basically the AI I created from all the crafting knowledge downloaded into the brrr machine I call a brain, is a highly adaptive learning program. That is at its core supposed to be a simulacrum of a human mind... Kinda. Computers aren't really built for that.

 But with enough CRAFTING you can _make_ them.

 So that's what I did.

 It was fun.

 At least until...

 "What is my purpose Creator?" Those were the first words out of HMG Chan's mouth... Er speaker, yeah. Anyway this presented me with a problem as I really hadn't thought that far ahead, and just kinda did it without thinking. Again.

 So I didn't really have an answer. And worst yet I couldn't tell her that. Like what was I supposed to say? You exist to sit there and watch as I use you to blow people to bits? Oh my god I'm an idiot.

 Then the other _questions_ started.

 Creator what is this? Creator what is that? Why do you do that creator? Creator what are you doing. Creator what is the purpose of that.

 Eventually I snapped and told her to stop calling me that.

 I had ten... _TEN_ seconds of blissful silence.

_Mistress_ why are you shaking?

 Nope, nope, not doing that.

 I told her _never_ to call me that again, set her in front of the TV with the least fucked up thing i could find, and went to bed.

 -------------

 "Okay, and?" Hiromi questioned.

 "It would be easier to just show you..." I sighed.

 I brought us back to the workshop with the all the enthusiasm of the walking dead.

 HMG Chan was still there (duh) and something princessy was now on.

 "HMG Chan i'm back..." I announced.

 "MOTHER! You're back! Please sit, I have so many new questions!" An aggressively peppy and downright girly voice replied back with all the volume control of a tornado.

 I was then verbally assaulted/interrogated about anything and everything I knew about... Unicorns. Are they real? Can they fly? Are they bullet resistant? Was she cooler than a unicorn? And finally if she could have one.

 Hiromi watched in slowly dawning horror as she realized the depths of the _**sin**_ I have committed.

 My name is Motoko Kusanagi, and I am a teenage mother.
