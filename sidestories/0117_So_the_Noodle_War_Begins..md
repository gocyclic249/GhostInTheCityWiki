I hope it's just something super lowkey. So uneventful that Motoko doesn't even realize who she ran into into hours later when she's at home and all of the sudden realizes that she ran into the plot.

* * *

 Have you ever seen Home Alone? That scene where Kevin's mom realizes that she left Kevin at home and just yells at the top of her lungs. Cause that's me right now.

 Started off as a normal day, well normal for normal people. It was a pretty dull day for me. All I did was run around town hoping to get the last bit of xp for a athletics level up. No such luck. Feels like I've finally reached the soft cap and now xp is harder to come by than real meat in this city.

 Hiromi was busy. Jun of all people had a date. Even Ichi wasn't around to hang out with. Wakako had no jobs available so it was up to me to figure out what to do with myself. And I came up with the perfect plan. Grab lunch and watch tv. Hey, don't judge, as crazy as this city can get, it isn't crazy all the time.

 So here I am, all by my lonesome, not a thought of murder in my head, when I see a short mox carrying a dozen take out containers away from my favorite noodle stand. I didn't even get to say a word when the old man running the stand just shakes his head and says, "sorry, short girl just cleaned me out of all my number 7s."

 My disappointment was immeasurable, and my day was ruined. I stomped my way back home and had to accept that this was gonna be an XXL burrito kind of day. Jun didn't even look like he cared when I told him what happened. The gonk didn't see why a burrito day was bad. God, I hope he didn't get his date any burritos, she'd leave him the second she found out he loved those things more than her.

 I just wanted the day to end. Call it all a wash. Maybe I'll give Nox a call and ask him to be on the look out for any mini-moxs around. Stealing all my number 7s. The nerve of some people.

 Maybe I should have gotten a better look at her. She definitely looked like a mox even if she was short. Green hair, her clothes definitely looked kitsch. In fact, she kind of... looked like... "REBECCA! HOLY SHIT! YOU STOLE MY NOODLES! I THOUGHT YOU WERE SUPPOSE TO BE COOL!"

 And that's how Rebecca and I entered the noodle cold war. Buying out number 7s every day to stop the other from having access. Not gonna lie though. I've eaten way too many noodles, I might need to recruit some more members onto my side. I'll check to see if Scorpion and co are interested.
