 "Leave? Leave the apartment? Night City?"

 "Civilization," Motoko expounded, "I can't even go to the Nomads for this. No one who knows me must know where I am."

 Jun rubbed his temple, "What did you do?"

 "Okay, this is totally not my fault. You know how I was learning netrunning? Well, I started a daemon farm, but left the time dilation on full and they all evolved.."

 "Motoko."

 "..and they kind of escaped into the Net and are now multiplying exponentially and.."

 "Motoko! What did you do? Five words or less."

 "Um..," then Motoko spoke, "DataKrash Two: Electric Boogaloo."
