"You were upset at how the NCPD performed so you spent the last week.. crafting?" asked Hiromi's mom.

 "Last few weeks. Yep!" Motoko nodded, "I figured I can build these and create a rival company to police this city much better."

 Hiromi's dad spoke up, "And how are you planning to fund this company? What kind of income streams are you looking at?"

 Motoko shrugged, "I figured anyone we save or help can pay as much or as little as they want. Whatever they feel is good."

 "What!?" said Hiromi's parents. And Hiromi. And Jun.

 "Also, we'd get first pick of the loot from all the bad guys, and there are a lot of bad guys in this city."

 Hiromi's dad hummed non-committingly.

 "Plus, most of the patrolling will be done by drones like the ones I made, and they are very effective while not being very expensive."

 "So you can save on life insurance premiums and keep repair costs down. Go on," Hiromi's mom nodded.

 "Right," Motoko said, "first up is the Tachikoma spider drone. Their AI isn't too complex yet, but they are air-gapped and can follow simple verbal orders from recognized users. They can also scale the sides of buildings and parkour and have a wide variety of weapon load out options. That large bin in the back can store a person or a bunch of loot."

 "Next up is the ED-209 enforcement drone heavy mecha. Cheap, easily mass produced, and capable of taking down most borged out individuals. I also made sure they can run and climb up and down stairs without issues. While the Tachikoma make for great scouts and skirmishers, EDs provide the muscle and firepower."

 "And you want to call your new company?" Hiromi's mom asked.

 "Omni Consumer Products," Motoko said, "It's a name that is good for future expansion."

 "Right. Of course," Hiromi sighed. She remembered visiting Motoko during the last few weeks, who just made her watch ancient movies while she tinkered.

 "And last, but certainly not least, my brother, Jun, is modeling the R-Cop Linear Frame I made. Heavy armor matched with mobility. Add a variable weapon system with quick reload function and shoulder mounted guided missile launcher and plenty of tiny silent flying stealth drones for oversight, and this linear frame can help keep the user alive and act as oversight for the ED and Tahikoma drones."

 "It's less than what MaxTac uses," commented Hiromi's mom.

 "Well, yes. I'll leave all the borged out cyberpsychos to MaxTac. OCP police are there to handle regular crime.. and Maelstroms. The R-Cop linear frame should be enough to keep the user alive while retreating from any heavy threats. Any common criminal will be severly outmatched. Now say the line, Jun."

 "No," Jun refused.

 "Say it or I'll send them embarrassing pictures of you!"

 "You're bluffing. You don't have any such pictures, Motoko."

 "Am I? Am I really? Say the line."

 "Ugh, fine," Jun cleared his throat and activated the loudspeaker which changed his voice to sound more intimidating, and robotic for some reason, "Dead or alive, you're coming with me!"

 Motoko squeed in delight.

 Hiromi sighed, "You are such a gonk! Both of you."
