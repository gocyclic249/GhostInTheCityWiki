**_Truman Technical solutions regional offices 

 City center_**

 Wallace unplugs his tablet from the offices mainframe.

 "Well I got bad news and worse news, Bad news is you're going to have to replace a good deal of your hardware because whomever hit you slagged it to hell.

 Worse news is given the skill level and aggressiveness of this attack you should consider all data on these servers to be out in the public."

 The corporate drone looks over the report on the tablet handed to him and winces.

 "What level of security would we need to prevent another attack of this magnitude?"

 Wallace sighs and then shows the Corporate rep a new window in the tablet causing their face to pale.

 "Significant changes to hardware, a multi layered ICE system for each node using at a minimum Triple-6 grade encryption,

 a dedicated administrative AI and no less than five netrunners per security shift.

 In all honestly Mr. Johnson given the type of work your company does and the data you claim to store it's simply more expense than it's worth.

 My investigation show this was a massive case of catastrophic bad luck that you were targeted for a dick measuring contest among black hat netrunners."

 "Why in gods name did they pick us?"

 "Simply put you were big enough to be a valid game field but small enough not to be able to send kill teams after them.

 Just be glad H4x47h0n isn't a thing anymore, the 40's & 50's were some crazy shit."

 Mr. Johnson sags in defeat.

 "So there's nothing we can do?

 Wallace pats the man on the shoulder.

 "Go with some upgraded ICE and Triple-6 encryption, getting the AI just doesn't fit your cost benefit margins.

 Having a second security runner per shift might not be a bad idea either to improve response time."

 Johnson takes down some notes.

 "I'll have to run these figures by management but I don't think there will be a problem."

 "Sure take your time since I have to wait till you replace the mainframe and effected servers before I can do program installations.

 Word of advice however, see if you can get your bosses to approve the purchase of the new Arasaka pagoda K-7's.

 I can swing a discount since I got a few friends in their sales department."

 Mr Johnson adds that to his notes.

 "Well I suppose that will be all for today?"

 Wallace hands him another tablet.

 "Just need you to authorize a data release for so I can forward my investigation data to netwatch.

 I got a few old buddies in the investigation branch so I can probably get them to bump this to the top of the pile."

 "Ah yes I do suppose that would help things along for the investigators we hired."

 Wallace raises an eyebrow.

 "Oh, who did you hire?"

 "That would be us, good afternoon Mr. Johnson I am Lisa Kensington and I will be your liaison with Danger Gal.

 I am familiar with Mr McNamara's work so I can assume it's safe to say he's already done a full sweep of your system for any traces of the the belligerents."

 Mr. Johnson shakes the woman's hand and shows her the tablet Wallace gave him.

 "Pleasure to meet you Mrs Kensington, yes Mr McNamara has already compiled the data and was just about to send a copy to Netwatch's investigation branch.

 "Excellent if you could forward it to my team as well we can analyze the data and begin our investigation and search for the assailants."

[Another woman](https://images-wixmp-ed30a86b8c4ca887773594c2.wixmp.com/f/ffde16f2-8bcb-4614-8888-a98b6f87de42/deai9yj-c42fb0e7-bd21-44b5-914b-02dfb15429fa.jpg/v1/fit/w_828,h_1172,q_70,strp/cyberpunk_girl_by_boniichan21_deai9yj-414w-2x.jpg?token=eyJ0eXAiOiJKV1QiLCJhbGciOiJIUzI1NiJ9.eyJzdWIiOiJ1cm46YXBwOjdlMGQxODg5ODIyNjQzNzNhNWYwZDQxNWVhMGQyNmUwIiwiaXNzIjoidXJuOmFwcDo3ZTBkMTg4OTgyMjY0MzczYTVmMGQ0MTVlYTBkMjZlMCIsIm9iaiI6W1t7ImhlaWdodCI6Ijw9MTgxMSIsInBhdGgiOiJcL2ZcL2ZmZGUxNmYyLThiY2ItNDYxNC04ODg4LWE5OGI2Zjg3ZGU0MlwvZGVhaTl5ai1jNDJmYjBlNy1iZDIxLTQ0YjUtOTE0Yi0wMmRmYjE1NDI5ZmEuanBnIiwid2lkdGgiOiI8PTEyODAifV1dLCJhdWQiOlsidXJuOnNlcnZpY2U6aW1hZ2Uub3BlcmF0aW9ucyJdfQ.RhiOBKbKqbU9AYc1nHXIubWje9wRGWyHSNP0sL2yi04) enters the room.

 "This is of course assuming pops doesn't already have an idea as to who did this."

 Lisa sighs and pinches her nose while Wallace laughs

 "Mr. Johnson this is Sarah McNamara and she will be the lead on the net investigation team."

 Mr. Johnson looks between the women and the older man.

 "I'm sorry to ask but you two look very similar and given her name I assume you're related to Mr McNamara?"

 Wallace finally stops laughing to address the man.

 "Yes Lisa and Sarah are my daughters and the finest netrunners the kitty cat commandos..."

 Lisa glares at him in a manner that very much reminds Wallace of her mother while Lisa just rolls her eyes.

 "...Danger Gal has in their employ and yes Sarah I have some leads on who might be behind this, they are included in the data package."

 Lisa smiles at Wallace.

 "Well thank you for your assistance dad but if you don't need to do anything else we need to secure the room.

 Oh and could you pick up Traci after school tomorrow, I'm going to be busy with this and the weekly managers meeting in the afternoon."

 "Sure thing pumpkin."

 Sarah grins at Wallace.

 "Think you two can keep out of trouble, because if not I want pictures."

 Lisa sighs at her sisters comment and Wallace laughs again.

 Mr. Johnson Looks over toward the Danger Gal liaison.

 "A very interesting family you have Mrs Kensington."

 "Yes well while my father and sister could stand to be more professional they are very good at what they do."

 "Well besides giving you and mom ulcers."

 "Yes Sarah that too, now don't you have a data package to analyze?"

 Sarah takes a seat and plugs in a datashard.

 Wallace shakes his head at his daughters antics.

 "Well Mr. Johnson you're in good hands and I will be on my way, please let me know what your managers decide and if you want me to get those Pagoda's for you."

 As Mr Johnson and Wallace leave Lisa looks over to her sister.

 "So what did dad leave out?"

 Sarah unplug the datashard.

 "Actually he was pretty through this time though if you read between the lines of his notes at least one of the netrunners is an associate of his.

 Can't be one he's overly close to since he was going to give netwatch enough to get a good track on them.

 Probably because one of the runners tired to flatline the security guy, you know how dad gets about that kind of thing."

 Lisa shakes her head.

 "Yes well lets do a full sweep of the mainframe anyway, I doubt he'd miss anything but you know how Michiko gets if we skip a step."

 "Just because your father is some ace former netcop and already screened the device doesn't mean you can ignore standard procedure,

 then has us do remedial training assignments and calisthenics with Zaburo-sensei for a month."

 "Yes all the more reason to go over the mainframe."
