Jun took a quick glance away from the show when he heard the door open, and was hit with a dilemma.

 He chose nearly instantly. Motoko was more important than Teenage Cyber Pirate Ninjas... even if the team's netrunner was hiding the fact that he'd been bitten by a zombie process during a dive.

 Jun put down a few eddies to pause the show and stood up. "What happened? Are you OK?"

 "Jun... I found out today that I'm adopted."

 He rushed over and gently grabbed her shoulders in a hug.

 "Motoko; we have baby pictures to prove it... or, is this about the amnesia again? I told you; it doesn't matter. You'll always be my little sister."

 "I double-checked and asked to be sure, Jun. You're adopted too."

 He stumbled back against the counter. "But... Mom and Dad... we look so much like them and everything?"

 "They're still our parents. We just have more than we expected to ever have."

 "Who is it? Anyone we know?"

 "Wakako. She was really happy about the gig's result I guess, and gets a bit odd with backpay when she's sentimental? We can officially call her Grandma now."
