_It's called "**marketing**"!_

 So, after the bloodbath in the Scav den I was heading back to my Quadra when I remembered something important.

 "Oh fuck!"

 "What?" Jun asked, quickly looking around.

 "The loot! Almost forgot about the loot!" I told him. I quickly called Ichi and let him know where he can find the latest "unattended"Scav loot stash to loot.

 "Gonk." Jun muttered under his breath, but I heard him! "Also, You gonna drive home in Your car while soaked in Scav blood and guts?"

 "Shit!" Hadn't thought of that... Wonder if there are any conveniently located showers around here? I did NOT want the interior of my sweet Quadra smelling like a slaughterhouse. Unfortunately no showers mysteriously appeared so I was forced to grab a few plastic sheets that the Scavs had plenty of and basically cover my car's interior with them.

 After getting back home and taking a long shower I was looking more like a teenage girl again instead of an extra from a gory horror movie. Just in time to get a call from Hiromi.

 *Hey Motoko! I just got a call from Ichi and Malcolm! They're puking their guts out at that Scav den You sent them to. What the fuck happened there?!*

 *Hey Hiromi! Not much? Just played some music and zeroed some Scavs I guess?*

 *Then why is Ichi saying the Scav den looked like - and I'm gonna quote Ichi - the inside of a slaughterhouse where someone has set off a bomb and painted the floor, walls and ceiling with blood and guts?!*

 *Guess...the music made me a tiny bit enthusiastic?*

 *Enthusiastic? That's what You gonna call it? Ichi sent me a few pictures and even _that_ made me almost throw up in my mouth. Say, did You scroll a BD of it?*

 *Yes, why? You want a copy?*

 *Of course I want a copy, gonkbrain! I want a copy of every BD You make, sealed and signed so it will have crazy collectible value in the future where we'll all be ultrarich and famous! But I'm mostly worried about if it should get to the police and they pronounce you a cyberpsycho on the spot and send MaxTac on your gonk ass!*

 *That would suck... You got any ideas how to help your best choom?*

 *Hmmm...Let me think of something. I'll call You back. Hold on to the BD until then, don't want You scaring the shit outta the Mox and everyone else in Night City until I come up with an idea of how to spin this.*

 *Kay, talk to You later!*

**<One week later>**

 "Hello and good evening! This is N54 News coming to You with the latest from Night City!"

 "The City has been taken by storm by latest BrainDance released by the Mox gang. Advertised as *the latest in Anti-Scav self-defense tactics _slash_ Scav survivor psychological self-help guide* it at first glance looks like a horror-gore XBD picturing a textbook cyberpsychosis episode, but statistics show that viewers of the product actually DO significally better against scav attacks or have shown marked improvement in their psychological state if viewed by the survivors of Scav attacks. Just yesterday in Westbrook a crew of five scavs tried to abduct and no doubt kill a local resident for his cyberware, but encountered a surprisingly effective resistance. According to the lucky Westbrook resident he had gotten his hands on the rare BD and did as he had learned from it - Ripped and Teared until the Scavs were all dead. And on the other side, the survivors of previous Scav attacks have said that the BD has given them a cathartic release from their fears and helped them move on with their lives, no longer afraid to even exit their homes!"

 "So, that's the latest from Night City, which according to statistics shows a marked drop in Scav activities, a total of 9 percent compared to last year!"

<>

 "Wow," I muttered, turning off the TV. "Hiromi must have really aced her marketing class in the Academy..."

 "Heh! Well done Motoko!" Jun huffed amusedly next to me and gave me a headpat.

 *Eh, worth it.* I thought and continued on my next musical masterpiece.
