_Hayato_

 Hayato was having a bad week, small things kept happening to her. Nothing that really hurt her, a stubbed toe here. A few glasses falling out of a cupboard there. And to top it off this morning she couldn't find her favorite hairpin her father had given her a few years back. It turned up after some searching but she was running late for her ~~date~~ spar with Motoko, her choom? To make matters worse her bodyguard was late as well, saying her car needed a tire change. When she walked into the Dojo she saw her rival already working with a shinai. Her form was beautiful as always. She dragged her eyes away and went to change. Her feet once out of her shoes felt much better, it could be time for a size up. Even if they are new, better to remove discomfort by replacing than hoping to break them in. Once changed she approached Motoko in the space she had claimed.

 "I apologize for the wait. Are you prepared?" She asked while wrapping her hands. Motoko stood and smiled.

 "As soon as you say I am the bestest assassin we can start Hayato-chan!" I sent a small glare.

 "I refuse, you are not an assassin! A hitman at best but you are far from stealthy!"

 "Really? Well, guess I've been too sneaky." Her smile changes to a smirk. "That is a lovely hairpin you came in with, shouldn't leave it next to your kitchen sink." No I gasp but she continues on. "You didn't even see the package I left on the floorboard of your car did you?" The flat. She used that time to put something in the car and no one saw? "I've moved your plates, glasses, silverware and food in your fridge around at least four times this week as well. I even switched your night stands on what side of the bed they go."
