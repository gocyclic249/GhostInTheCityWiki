_Consequences..._

 Motoko was really feeling like it was a lazy day today. Seriously she had done good over the last few months if she said so herself. Even managing to save her very own cat girl! Not that Sasha really appreciated that title. Preferring instead to go by the 'less demeaning' title of exotic. Tomato, tomato, and all of that, a cat girl was a cat girl, just ask any FFXIV player! Oh wait those were Mi'qote... Still what she called herself didn't matter, cat girl get!

 Sasha had even been hanging around the apartment from time to time listening in as Motoko remade tracks! (not that she knew) From her past life. Watching T.V. and talking with Jun over dinner... Jun who in all of his denseness was actually out rather late tonight.

 That wasn't _too_ unusual. He often sent texts, texts! of all things when Fujimara sent him on a mission that would keep him all night. Operational security thy name isn't Junichiro Kusanagi, his parents, _our parents_ would not be proud. Still she hadn't received any word from him and his agent went straight to voicemail when she tried to call, It was actually starting to get worrying...

 As if to spite all of my growing fears the door was opened to a six foot tall brooding teenager with a scowl that seemed etched onto his face. Not one of his usual daily scowls but something more similar to Sasha when left out in the rain. Concern rose within me. "Jun are you okay?"

 "It's gone." was all he grunted in response stalking into our living place before sitting down in a white plastic chair of all things that he had scavenged from who knows where. Hearing the emptiness of his voice I immediately rushed to his side. This wasn't Jun the perpetually worrying big brother I was dealing with, but instead Jun the potential Cyberpsycho, what the hell had Fujimara had him doing!? I grabbed his arm.

 "Jun listen to me, it's going to be okay. Whatever this is about we'll get through this." Yet his gaze was empty as he looked into my eyes.

 "Biotechnica's stock took a hit a few weeks ago. Some stupid scandal about 'improperly labeled medications'" Those last three words where spoken with such sarcasm that he was seriously lucky Sasha wasn't here to hear them... Also that was me Jun! Don't speak ill of the deeds your little sister does in service of a better planet! And cat girls! Still none of this was actually _the point._

 "Jun what happened?" I gave him my best attempt at a caring yet stern face as I gripped his hands to get him to keep looking at me and talking.

 "T-they have to sell some off some subsidiaries they own... All foods was one of them." _All foods?_ wasn't that the place you go shooting up Maelstrom with Jackie in? Good times, _maybe I should give him a call and see if we can get that going ahead of time..._ Jun continued to speak, his voice even more shaky. "The owners are downsizing, cutting off the brands that aren't doing as well to prepare it for sale." Okay seriously what did this have to do with anything? "The Burrito XXL! They're discontinuing it!" He looked like a man who had just learned he had to sacrifice his first born to the devil.

 …I flicked my stupid psycho of a brother in the forehead. He deserved it.
