 "Let me see him," Vik stated from the front seat of the modified Delamaine Limo that they somehow set up. "damn this is bad... We need to get him somewhere I can operate..."

 "We have a secure clinic set up where Arasaka wouldn't look." Hiromi stated as she helped shift the seats for the medical equipment that had been installed, her face pale, but the look on her face and in her eyes were iron.

 "Probably a good idea..." Vik grunted as he put on his medical glove and worked on Jackie's wounds.

 "Dammit," V growled as she slammed her fist on the seat. "we need to get out of here to save Jackie and deliver this damn chip!"

 "Kind of hard with all these gonks shooting at us..." Vik stated only for the garage door to blow open, and a certain custom car to come roaring through, and followed by an enraged Adam Smasher.

 "Motoko says she has this," Hiromi replied as the Limo suddenly accelerated forward and past the single minded Adam Smasher as he chased Motoko with single minded fury...
