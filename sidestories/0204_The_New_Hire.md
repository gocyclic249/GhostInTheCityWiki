**The New Hire**

 "Hey, Vik?"

 He looked over upon hearing the familiar voice. There stood Motoko, glancing over her shoulder, posture and face nervous.

 "Yes, Motoko?" he replied, standing and stretching from where he had been sorting through her latest delivery. It wasn't a bad haul, per se, but it was... well, he could use it, at least. She turned her head forward, still side-eyeing to her left, before her eyes turned to him.

 "I just had a couple questions. About your... new hire, if you don't mind." Ah. Yes, that would make sense. He had a couple questions himself. Actually, he had more than a couple. He wasn't keen on answering most of them. Still, he'd help her where he could. He gave one last cursory glance to the parts he'd been checking, then turned fully towards her.

 "What do you want to know?" He hoped the new hire wasn't listening... which he probably would be. For both their sakes, he hoped Motoko would at least stray from the more... accusatory kind of questions.

 "Do- don't you think he... I dunno," she starts, raising her shoulder in a shrug while her gaze shifts around, "kinda looks like Adam Smasher?" He'd half expected her to not outright ask. He should have known better.

 He listened for a moment, saying nothing, looking in the direction of his workshop. He couldn't hear anything out of the ordinary. He wasn't sure if that was good or bad.

 "Well, now that you mention it..." he said, slowly crossing his arms and raising his right hand to his chin, rubbing at it, then closing his eyes. "Nah." He opens his eyes again, savoring the sheer bafflement on the reckless Solo's face. "Don't see it."

 "You- what?" she intelligently replies as she slumps, her hair somehow sticking up as if she'd been tugging on it. She shakes her head again, and it goes back to normal. That was... new. "Vik, what do you mean? It's literally Adam Smasher! Wearing a crappy fake mustache!"

**"Oi."** If he hadn't done it himself, he would have appreciated seeing her do a full-body flinch. It was sort of funny. Slowly, stiffly, they both turned. There stood his 'new-hire,' a borg that was very visually similar to Adam Smasher (if it somehow _wasn't_ Adam Smasher, Vik'd probably need to replace some of his own parts), the only difference being the mustache that looked like it had been glued on.

 "Ah, hey Adams," he did his best to ignore Motoko choking (do not laugh, do _not_) as he spoke, "you need something?"

 The borg didn't speak, just staring at the two of them. His head turned towards Motoko, staring her down. **"Who's the half-pint?"** Out of the corner of his eye, he saw Motoko's eye twitch (do _not **laugh**_).

 "Hm? Oh, this is Motoko, a patient of mine." He reached out and patted her on the head, smiling at her somewhat adorable attempts to push his hand away, her face slightly reddening. "She occasionally provides some of the parts we use." The borg said nothing, just staring at her, before snorting.

**"Oi, cosplayer,"** the borg finally spoke, presumably directed at Motoko, who flinched and froze, her eyes wide at his new-hire's form of address. She knew what he was talking about, maybe? He'd have to ask her about that. **"Any visual similarities between myself and the awesome and might Corpo Borg known as Adam Smasher are purely coincidental."**The borg slowly walked towards her. Vik became slightly nervous about this- he really, really hoped this didn't turn into a fight, because he and Motoko were very unlikely to win.

 "I-I see," Motoko awkwardly replied as the borg halted when he was only a foot away from her.

**"And the mustache, this excellent, finely groomed, mustache is not crappy, and is very much real."** The borg lowered himself until he was eye-level with her. "**Got it?"**

 Motoko awkwardly stared, her mouth open as she seemed to fumble for words. She took a breath, then, "Got it, mister..."

**"Crasher. Adams Crasher."** Motoko audibly gulped as she seemed to fight a disbelieving smile off her face.

 "G-Got it, mister Adam Sm- Crasher! Mister Adams Crasher, sir!" she hastily tacked on when the borg's eyes narrowed at her slip-up. He stared for a few moments more, Motoko beginning to sweat nervously, Vik not far from doing the same. Finally, the borg stood and turned.

**"Gettin' back to work now, Doc."** Crasher went back to his workshop, though he paused and turned over his shoulder towards Motoko. **"Seeya 'round, little Major."** The borg stepped out of sight, his footsteps fading as he went back to Vik's shop.

 "Hey Vik?" Motoko spoke after a few seconds, her body slumped in relief, though stiff in some places. Concern about Smasher's parting words?

 "Yes Motoko?" he replied, his voice betraying nothing.

 "I think I'll hold off on any upgrades for a while."

 "Ah, I see." He paused for a moment, before, "So what did he mean by cospl-"

 "NOTHING!"
